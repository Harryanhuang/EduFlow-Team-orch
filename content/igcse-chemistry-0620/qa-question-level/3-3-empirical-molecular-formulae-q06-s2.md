# QQL: 3-3-empirical-molecular-formulae — Q-3.3-06 (F)

**Question ID**: Q-3.3-06
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Topic**: 3.3 Empirical and molecular formulae
**Question**: Glucose has the molecular formula C6H12O6. What is its empirical formula?
**Answer**: CH2O. Divide all subscripts (6, 12, 6) by their highest common factor, which is 6, to get the simplest ratio 1:2:1.
**Explanation**: A common error is giving the molecular formula as the answer. Always simplify to the smallest whole-number ratio.
