# Question: Question Q-1.2-03
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Topic**: 1.2
**Question**: Why does sugar dissolve faster in hot water than in cold water?
**Answer**: Water particles in hot water have more kinetic energy and move faster. They collide with sugar particles more frequently and with greater energy, breaking them apart more quickly.
**Explanation**: Temperature increases particle speed. A common error is saying hot water "is thinner."
