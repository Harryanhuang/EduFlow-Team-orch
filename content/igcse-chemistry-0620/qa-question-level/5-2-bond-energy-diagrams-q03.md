# Question: Question Q-5.2-03
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Topic**: 5.2
**Question**: Explain why increasing the temperature increases the rate of reaction (give two reasons).
**Answer**: (1) Particles have more kinetic energy, so more collisions have energy greater than the activation energy. (2) Particles move faster, so collisions are more frequent. The first reason has the greater effect.
**Explanation**: Energy per collision + frequency. A common error is only mentioning collision frequency.
