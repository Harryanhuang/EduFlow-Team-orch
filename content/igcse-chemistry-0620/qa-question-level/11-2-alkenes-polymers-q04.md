# Question: Question Q-11.2-04
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Topic**: 11.2
**Question**: Why are plastics a problem in the environment?
**Answer**: Most plastics are non-biodegradable (they do not decompose naturally) and persist in the environment for hundreds of years. They accumulate in landfills and oceans, harming wildlife.
**Explanation**: Non-biodegradable + persistent. A common error is saying plastics "dissolve" in water.
**Tags**: plastics, non-biodegradable, environmental
