# Question: Question Q-8.1-08
**Difficulty**: Challenge
**Topic**: 8.1
**Question**: Element X is in Group 1 below sodium. Predict how X reacts with water compared with sodium, and write a general word equation for the reaction.
**Answer**: X reacts more vigorously than sodium because reactivity increases down Group 1. It forms an alkaline solution of the metal hydroxide and hydrogen gas. Word equation: Group 1 metal + water → metal hydroxide + hydrogen.
**Explanation**: Students must apply the group trend to an unfamiliar element. A common error is predicting lower reactivity because the atom is larger.
