# Question: Question Q-12.1-05
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Topic**: 12.1
**Question**: What colour precipitate does copper(II) ion form with aqueous sodium hydroxide?
**Answer**: Blue precipitate of copper(II) hydroxide.
**Explanation**: Cu²⁺ + 2OH⁻ → Cu(OH)₂ (blue). A common error is saying green (that's Fe²⁺).
**Tags**: copper-test, Cu2+, NaOH-test, precipitate
