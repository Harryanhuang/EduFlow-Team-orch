# Question: Question Q-2.1-09
**Difficulty**: Challenge
**Topic**: 2.1
**Question**: An ion X³⁺ has 10 electrons and 14 neutrons. Identify element X and state its mass number.
**Answer**: X³⁺ has lost 3 electrons, so the neutral atom has 10 + 3 = 13 electrons = 13 protons. Atomic number 13 = aluminium (Al). Mass number = 13 + 14 = 27. This is aluminium-27.
**Explanation**: Ion charge tells electron loss/gain. Protons = electrons in neutral atom. A common error is saying the element has 10 protons.
