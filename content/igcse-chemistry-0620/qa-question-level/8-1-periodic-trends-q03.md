# Question: Question Q-8.1-03
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Topic**: 8.1
**Question**: Describe the trend in the melting points of Group 1 metals down the group.
**Answer**: Melting points decrease down the group. Lithium has the highest melting point, and it gets lower through sodium, potassium, rubidium, and caesium.
**Explanation**: Decreases down the group. A common error is saying it increases (confusing with reactivity).
