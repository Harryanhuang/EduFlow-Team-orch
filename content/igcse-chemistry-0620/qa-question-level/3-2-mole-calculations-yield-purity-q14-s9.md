# Question: Question Q-3.2-14
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Topic**: 3.2
**Question**: What volume of oxygen is needed to completely react with 48 dm³ of hydrogen at r.t.p.? (2H₂ + O₂ → 2H₂O)
**Answer**: 24 dm³
**Explanation**: From the equation: 2 mol H₂ : 1 mol O₂. So 48 dm³ H₂ needs 48/2 = 24 dm³ O₂ (same temperature and pressure → volume ratio = mole ratio). A common error is saying 48 dm³.
