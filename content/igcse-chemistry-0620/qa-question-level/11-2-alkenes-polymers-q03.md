# Question: Question Q-11.2-03
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Topic**: 11.2
**Question**: What is addition polymerisation? Write the equation for the polymerisation of ethene.
**Answer**: Addition polymerisation is the process where many small unsaturated molecules (monomers) join together to form a large molecule (polymer) by opening their double bonds. nC₂H₄ → [-CH₂-CH₂-]ₙ. The monomer is ethene; the polymer is poly(ethene).
**Explanation**: Monomers join, double bonds open. A common error is showing the polymer still having double bonds.
