# Question: Question Q-9.3-02
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Topic**: 9.3
**Question**: Describe two methods to prevent rusting and explain how each works.
**Answer**: (1) Barrier methods: paint, oil, grease, or plastic coating — these physically block oxygen and water from reaching the iron surface. (2) Sacrificial protection: attach a more reactive metal (zinc, magnesium) to the iron — the more reactive metal corrodes preferentially, protecting the iron.
**Explanation**: Block contact or sacrifice. A common error is saying galvanising is just a barrier (it's also sacrificial).
