# Question: Question Q-8.1-01
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Topic**: 8.1
**Question**: Why are Group 1 metals called "alkali metals"?
**Answer**: They react with water to form alkaline solutions (metal hydroxides) and hydrogen gas. The hydroxides are soluble and make the solution alkaline (pH > 7).
**Explanation**: Alkaline product. A common error is saying they are called "alkali" because they are "metallic."
