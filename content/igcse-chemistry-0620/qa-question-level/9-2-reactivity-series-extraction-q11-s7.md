# Question: Question Q-9.2-11
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Topic**: 9.2
**Question**: Explain why recycling aluminium saves 95% of the energy compared to extracting it from bauxite.
**Answer**: Extracting aluminium from bauxite requires electrolysis of molten Al₂O₃ at 900°C, which needs enormous amounts of electricity. Recycling only requires melting the already-extracted aluminium, which uses far less energy.
**Explanation**: Electrolysis vs. simple melting. A common error is saying recycling "requires no energy."
