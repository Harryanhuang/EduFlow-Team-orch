# Question: Question Q-9.3-01
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Topic**: 9.3
**Question**: What are the two essential conditions for iron to rust?
**Answer**: Iron rusts only when both oxygen (from air) and water are present. The chemical reaction is: 4Fe + 3O₂ + 2xH₂O → 2Fe₂O₃·xH₂O (hydrated iron(III) oxide).
**Explanation**: Both air AND water needed. A common error is saying only water is needed.
