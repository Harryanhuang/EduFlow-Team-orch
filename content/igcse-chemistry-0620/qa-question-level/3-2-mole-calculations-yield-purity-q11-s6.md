# Question: Question Q-3.2-11
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Topic**: 3.2
**Question**: Why should you rinse the burette with the acid it will contain, not with water?
**Answer**: Rinsing with water would dilute the acid, changing its concentration and giving inaccurate titration results. Rinsing with the acid ensures the concentration is not affected.
**Explanation**: Avoid dilution. A common error is saying water "reacts" with the acid.
