# Question: Question Q-2.4-09
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Topic**: 2.4
**Question**: Both diamond and graphite are forms of carbon. What is the term for different forms of the same element?
**Answer**: Allotropes. Diamond and graphite are allotropes of carbon — different structural forms of the same element in the same physical state.
**Explanation**: Same element, different structure. A common error is saying "isotopes."
