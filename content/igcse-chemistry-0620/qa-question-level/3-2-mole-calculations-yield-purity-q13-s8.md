# Question: Question Q-3.2-13
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Topic**: 3.2
**Question**: Calculate the volume of carbon dioxide produced when 10 g of calcium carbonate reacts with excess HCl at r.t.p. (CaCO₃ + 2HCl → CaCl₂ + H₂O + CO₂; molar volume = 24 dm³/mol)
**Answer**: 2.4 dm³
**Explanation**: Mᵣ of CaCO₃ = 100. Moles = 10/100 = 0.1 mol. 1:1 ratio → 0.1 mol CO₂. Volume = 0.1 × 24 = 2.4 dm³. A common error is using the wrong molar mass.
