# Question: Question Q-2.3-01
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Topic**: 2.3
**Question**: What is ionic bonding?
**Answer**: Ionic bonding is the electrostatic attraction between oppositely charged ions, formed when one or more electrons are transferred from a metal atom to a non-metal atom.
**Explanation**: Transfer → ions → electrostatic attraction. A common error is saying electrons are "shared" in ionic bonds.
