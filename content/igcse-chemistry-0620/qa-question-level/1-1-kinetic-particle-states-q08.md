# Question: Question Q-1.1-08
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Topic**: 1.1
**Question**: How can you use melting point to determine whether a sample of ice is pure or impure?
**Answer**: Pure ice melts at exactly 0°C. If the sample melts over a range of temperatures or below 0°C, it is impure. Dissolved impurities lower and broaden the melting point.
**Explanation**: Pure substances have sharp, fixed melting points. A common error is saying impurities raise the melting point.
