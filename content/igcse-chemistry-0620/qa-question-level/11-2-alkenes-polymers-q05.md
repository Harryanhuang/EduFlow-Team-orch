# Question: Question Q-11.2-05
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Topic**: 11.2
**Question**: Draw the repeating unit of poly(propene) from the monomer propene (CH₂=CH-CH₃).
**Answer**: -[CH₂-CH(CH₃)]- with single bonds between the carbon atoms and the CH₃ group attached to every other carbon. The double bond of the monomer becomes a single bond in the polymer.
**Explanation**: Double bond → single bond; side group carries through. A common error is keeping the double bond.
