# Question: Question Q-2.2-06
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Topic**: 2.2
**Question**: Describe the trend in reactivity of Group 1 metals (alkali metals) as you go down the group.
**Answer**: Reactivity increases down the group. The outer electron is further from the nucleus and more shielded by inner shells, making it easier to lose.
**Explanation**: Further = easier to lose. A common error is saying reactivity decreases (confusing with halogens).
