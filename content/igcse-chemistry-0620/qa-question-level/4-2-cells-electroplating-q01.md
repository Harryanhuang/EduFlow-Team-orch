# Question: Question Q-4.2-01
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Topic**: 4.2
**Question**: What is a simple electrochemical cell?
**Answer**: A device that converts chemical energy into electrical energy using two different metals (electrodes) immersed in an electrolyte. The more reactive metal acts as the negative electrode (anode) and the less reactive as the positive electrode (cathode).
**Explanation**: Chemical → electrical energy. A common error is saying it "stores" electricity.
