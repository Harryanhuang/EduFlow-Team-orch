# Question: Question Q-4.2-10
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Topic**: 4.2
**Question**: In the electrolysis of brine (concentrated NaCl solution), name the products at each electrode and one use of each product.
**Answer**: Anode: chlorine gas (Cl₂) — used to make bleach, PVC, disinfect water. Cathode: hydrogen gas (H₂) — used to make ammonia, fuel. The remaining solution is sodium hydroxide (NaOH) — used to make soap, paper.
**Explanation**: Cl₂ at anode, H₂ at cathode, NaOH in solution. A common error is saying sodium metal forms (it doesn't in aqueous solution).
