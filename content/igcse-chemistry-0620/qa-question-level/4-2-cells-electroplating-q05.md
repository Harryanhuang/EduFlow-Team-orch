# Question: Question Q-4.2-05
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Topic**: 4.2
**Question**: Why is electroplating with chromium useful for car parts?
**Answer**: Chromium is very hard, resistant to corrosion, and has a shiny appearance. Electroplating car parts with chromium protects them from rusting and gives an attractive finish.
**Explanation**: Hard + shiny + corrosion-resistant. A common error is saying chromium makes the part lighter.
