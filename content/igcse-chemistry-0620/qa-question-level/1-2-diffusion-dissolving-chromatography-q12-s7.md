# Question: Question Q-1.2-12
**Difficulty**: Challenge
**Topic**: 1.2
**Question**: Predict the precipitate formed when barium chloride solution is mixed with sodium sulfate solution. Write the ionic equation.
**Answer**: Barium sulfate (BaSO₄) — a white precipitate. Ionic equation: Ba²⁺(aq) + SO₄²⁻(aq) → BaSO₄(s). Sodium and chloride ions are spectator ions.
**Explanation**: BaSO₄ is insoluble. A common error is saying NaCl precipitates.
