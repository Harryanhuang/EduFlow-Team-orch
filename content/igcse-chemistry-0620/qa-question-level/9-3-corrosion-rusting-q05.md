# Question: Question Q-9.3-05
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Topic**: 9.3
**Question**: What is rust chemically? Give its formula.
**Answer**: Rust is hydrated iron(III) oxide, Fe₂O₃·xH₂O. It is a reddish-brown, flaky substance that forms on the surface of iron and steel when exposed to air and water.
**Explanation**: Hydrated iron(III) oxide. A common error is saying iron(II) oxide.
