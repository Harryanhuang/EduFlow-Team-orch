# Question: Question Q-3.2-10
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Topic**: 3.2
**Question**: What indicator would you use in a titration between a strong acid and a strong alkali?
**Answer**: Phenolphthalein (colourless in acid, pink in alkali) or methyl orange (red in acid, yellow in alkali). Both give sharp colour changes for strong acid-strong alkali titrations.
**Explanation**: Sharp endpoint needed. A common error is saying universal indicator (too gradual).
**Tags**: indicator, titration, phenolphthalein
