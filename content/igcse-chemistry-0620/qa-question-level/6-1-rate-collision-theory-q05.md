# Question: Question Q-6.1-05
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Topic**: 6.1
**Question**: State four factors that affect the rate of a chemical reaction.
**Answer**: (1) Concentration of reactants (or pressure for gases). (2) Temperature. (3) Surface area (particle size) of solid reactants. (4) Presence of a catalyst.
**Explanation**: Concentration, temperature, surface area, catalyst. A common error is including "volume of container."
