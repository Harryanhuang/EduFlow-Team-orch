# Question: Question Q-1.2-07
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Topic**: 1.2
**Question**: Define the terms "solute," "solvent," and "solution."
**Answer**: Solute: the substance that dissolves. Solvent: the liquid that does the dissolving. Solution: the mixture of solute dissolved in solvent.
**Explanation**: Solute + solvent = solution. A common error is mixing up solute and solvent.
