# Question: Question Q-11.1-06
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Topic**: 11.1
**Question**: Name the homologous series to which methane, ethane, and propane belong.
**Answer**: Alkanes.
**Explanation**: Saturated hydrocarbons. A common error is saying "hydrocarbons" (too broad — not a homologous series name).
