# Question: Question Q-8.2-04
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Topic**: 8.2
**Question**: Iodine does not displace chlorine from potassium chloride solution. Explain why.
**Answer**: Iodine is less reactive than chlorine (it is below chlorine in Group 7). A less reactive halogen cannot displace a more reactive halogen from its salt solution.
**Explanation**: Reactivity order: Cl > Br > I. A common error is saying iodine "isn't soluble enough."
