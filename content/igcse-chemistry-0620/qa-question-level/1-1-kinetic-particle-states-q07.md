# Question: Question Q-1.1-07
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Topic**: 1.1
**Question**: A pure substance has a melting point of 0°C and a boiling point of 100°C. What state is it in at 25°C?
**Answer**: Liquid. At 25°C the temperature is between the melting point (0°C) and boiling point (100°C), so the substance is in the liquid state.
**Explanation**: Between melting and boiling = liquid. A common error is saying solid because 25°C is "cold."
