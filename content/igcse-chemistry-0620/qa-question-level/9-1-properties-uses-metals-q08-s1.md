# QQL: 9.1 — Q-9.1-08 (S)

**Question ID**: Q-9.1-08
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Topic**: 9.1 Properties and uses metals
**Question**: Aluminium is used for aircraft bodies and food containers. Explain how the properties of aluminium make it suitable for these uses.
**Answer**: Aluminium has a low density (lightweight — important for aircraft). It is strong (especially when alloyed). It is resistant to corrosion due to a protective oxide layer. It is non-toxic (safe for food contact). It is malleable and can be shaped easily.
**Explanation**: Students must link each property to a specific use. A common error is saying aluminium is "unreactive" — it is reactive but has a protective oxide layer.
