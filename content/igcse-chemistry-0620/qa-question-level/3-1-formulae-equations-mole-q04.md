# Question: Question Q-3.1-04
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Topic**: 3.1
**Question**: What is the formula of the compound formed from magnesium ions (Mg²⁺) and chloride ions (Cl⁻)?
**Answer**: MgCl₂
**Explanation**: Two Cl⁻ ions are needed to balance the 2+ charge of one Mg²⁺ ion. A common error is writing MgCl.
