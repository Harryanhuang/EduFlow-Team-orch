# Question: Question Q-10.1-01
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Topic**: 10.1
**Question**: What are the two main gases in the atmosphere and their approximate percentages?
**Answer**: Nitrogen (~78%) and oxygen (~21%). The remaining ~1% includes argon, carbon dioxide, water vapour, and other trace gases.
**Explanation**: N₂ ≈ 78%, O₂ ≈ 21%. A common error is saying oxygen is the main component.
