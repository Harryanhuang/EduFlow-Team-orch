# Question: Question Q-6.1-02
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Topic**: 6.1
**Question**: Sketch and label a reaction profile showing the effect of a catalyst on an exothermic reaction.
**Answer**: The profile shows reactants at a higher energy level than products (exothermic). The uncatalysed curve has a higher peak (activation energy Ea). The catalysed curve has a lower peak (Ea catalysed). ΔH (overall energy change) is the same for both.
**Explanation**: Lower peak, same start and finish. A common error is changing ΔH for the catalysed path.
