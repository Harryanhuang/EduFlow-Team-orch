# QQL: 5.1 — Q-5.1-01 (F)

**Question ID**: Q-5.1-01
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Topic**: 5.1 Exothermic and endothermic reactions
**Question**: What is an exothermic reaction? Give one example and describe the energy transfer.
**Answer**: An exothermic reaction releases energy to the surroundings (usually as heat). The temperature of the surroundings increases. Example: combustion of methane (CH4 + 2O2 → CO2 + 2H2O). The products have less energy than the reactants.
**Explanation**: Students must state that energy is released and temperature increases. A common error is saying "energy is created" — energy is transferred, not created.
