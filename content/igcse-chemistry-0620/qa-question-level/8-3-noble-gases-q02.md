# Question: Question Q-8.3-02
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Topic**: 8.3
**Question**: Give one use of helium and explain why it is suitable.
**Answer**: Helium is used in weather balloons and party balloons because it is less dense than air and is non-flammable (unlike hydrogen, which is also light but explosive).
**Explanation**: Light + safe. A common error is saying helium is "chemically active."
