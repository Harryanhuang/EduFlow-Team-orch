# Question: Question Q-2.1-05
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Topic**: 2.1
**Question**: State the relative mass and charge of a proton, neutron, and electron.
**Answer**: Proton: relative mass = 1, charge = +1. Neutron: relative mass = 1, charge = 0. Electron: relative mass = 1/1840 (negligible), charge = -1.
**Explanation**: Proton +1/1, neutron 0/1, electron -1/negligible mass. A common error is giving the electron a mass of 0 (it's negligible but not zero).
