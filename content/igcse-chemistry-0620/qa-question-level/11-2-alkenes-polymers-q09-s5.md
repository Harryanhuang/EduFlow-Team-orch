# Question: Question Q-11.2-09
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Topic**: 11.2
**Question**: What are the environmental problems associated with disposal of non-biodegradable polymers?
**Answer**: They do not decompose naturally, leading to landfill accumulation. Burning produces toxic gases. They can harm wildlife when they enter ecosystems as microplastics. Recycling is energy-intensive.
**Explanation**: Non-decomposing + toxic combustion + microplastics. A common error is saying they "dissolve in water."
