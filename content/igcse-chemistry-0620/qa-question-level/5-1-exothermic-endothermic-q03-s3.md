# QQL: 5.1 — Q-5.1-03 (S)

**Question ID**: Q-5.1-03
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Topic**: 5.1 Exothermic and endothermic reactions
**Question**: Explain, in terms of bond breaking and bond making, why some reactions are exothermic and some are endothermic.
**Answer**: Breaking bonds requires energy (endothermic). Making bonds releases energy (exothermic). If more energy is released from bond making than is absorbed in bond breaking, the overall reaction is exothermic. If more energy is absorbed in bond breaking than is released in bond making, the overall reaction is endothermic.
**Explanation**: Students must address both bond breaking AND bond making. A common error is saying "breaking bonds releases energy" — it requires energy.
