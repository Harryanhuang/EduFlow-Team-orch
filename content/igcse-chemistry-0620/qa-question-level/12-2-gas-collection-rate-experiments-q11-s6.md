# Question: Question Q-12.2-11
**Difficulty**: Challenge
**Topic**: 12.2
**Question**: A gas extinguishes a lighted splint and turns limewater milky. Identify the gas.
**Answer**: Carbon dioxide (CO₂). It does not support combustion (extinguishes the splint) and turns limewater milky due to formation of calcium carbonate.
**Explanation**: Does not burn + limewater milky = CO₂. A common error is saying nitrogen (also extinguishes but doesn't affect limewater).
