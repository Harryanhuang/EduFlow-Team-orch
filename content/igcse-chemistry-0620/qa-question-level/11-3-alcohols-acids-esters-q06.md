# Question: Question Q-11.3-06
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Topic**: 11.3
**Question**: How can you identify ethanoic acid (vinegar) from its smell?
**Answer**: Ethanoic acid has a sharp, vinegar-like smell.
**Explanation**: Characteristic smell. A common error is saying it is odourless.
