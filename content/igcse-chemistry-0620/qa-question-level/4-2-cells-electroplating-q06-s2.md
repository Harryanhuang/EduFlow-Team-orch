# Question: Question Q-4.2-06
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Topic**: 4.2
**Question**: Give two uses of electrolysis.
**Answer**: (1) Extraction of reactive metals (e.g., aluminium from aluminium oxide). (2) Electroplating (coating objects with a thin layer of metal). Also: purification of copper, production of chlorine and sodium hydroxide.
**Explanation**: Extraction + plating + purification. A common error is saying electrolysis is used for distillation.
