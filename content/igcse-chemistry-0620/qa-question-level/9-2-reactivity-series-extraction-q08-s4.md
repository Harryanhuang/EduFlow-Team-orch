# Question: Question Q-9.2-08
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Topic**: 9.2
**Question**: A student places strips of metals A, B, and C in dilute hydrochloric acid. A bubbles vigorously, B bubbles slowly, C shows no reaction. Rank them in order of reactivity.
**Answer**: A > B > C (most to least reactive). More vigorous reaction with acid = higher in the reactivity series.
**Explanation**: Acid reaction rate indicates reactivity. A common error is saying C is most reactive because it "doesn't waste itself."
