# Question: Question Q-1.1-04
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Topic**: 1.1
**Question**: What is meant by "changes of state are physical changes"?
**Answer**: A change of state does not alter the chemical composition of the substance — the particles remain the same type. Only the arrangement and energy of the particles change. The process is reversible.
**Explanation**: Physical ≠ chemical. A common error is saying new substances form during melting.
