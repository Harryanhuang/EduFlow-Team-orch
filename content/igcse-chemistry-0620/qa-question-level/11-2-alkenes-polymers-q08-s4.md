# Question: Question Q-11.2-08
**Difficulty**: Challenge
**Topic**: 11.2
**Question**: Nylon is a condensation polymer. Name the two types of monomers used to make nylon and the small molecule released.
**Answer**: A dicarboxylic acid and a diamine. The small molecule released is water (H₂O) — one H from the amine group and an OH from the carboxylic acid group combine.
**Explanation**: Two monomers + water released. A common error is saying amino acids (that's proteins, not nylon).
