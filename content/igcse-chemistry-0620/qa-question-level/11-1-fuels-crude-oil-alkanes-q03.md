# Question: Question Q-11.1-03
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Topic**: 11.1
**Question**: What is the general formula for alkanes?
**Answer**: CₙH₂ₙ₊₂, where n is the number of carbon atoms.
**Explanation**: Saturated hydrocarbons. A common error is CₙH₂ₙ (that's alkenes).
**Tags**: alkanes, general-formula, saturated
