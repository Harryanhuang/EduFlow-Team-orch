# Question: Question Q-12.2-09
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Topic**: 12.2
**Question**: How do you test for ammonia gas?
**Answer**: Hold damp red litmus paper near the gas. Ammonia turns red litmus paper blue (ammonia is alkaline). It also has a characteristic pungent smell.
**Explanation**: Red → blue litmus. A common error is using blue litmus.
