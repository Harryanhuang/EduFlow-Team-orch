# Question: Question Q-1.2-10
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Topic**: 1.2
**Question**: How does temperature affect the solubility of most solids in water?
**Answer**: Increasing temperature generally increases the solubility of solids in water. More solid can dissolve at higher temperatures.
**Explanation**: Hotter = more soluble (for most solids). A common error is saying temperature has no effect.
