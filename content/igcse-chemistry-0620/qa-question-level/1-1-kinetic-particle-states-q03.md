# Question: Question Q-1.1-03
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Topic**: 1.1
**Question**: Explain, in terms of particles, why heating a gas in a sealed container increases its pressure.
**Answer**: Heating gives gas particles more kinetic energy, so they move faster. They collide with the container walls more frequently and with greater force, increasing pressure.
**Explanation**: Must mention both frequency and force of collisions. A common error is only mentioning one.
