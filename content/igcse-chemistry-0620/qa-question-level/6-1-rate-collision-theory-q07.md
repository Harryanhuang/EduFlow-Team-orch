# Question: Question Q-6.1-07
**Difficulty**: Challenge
**Topic**: 6.1
**Question**: A student compares the rate of reaction of magnesium with two different concentrations of hydrochloric acid. The graph of gas volume vs time shows two curves. Which curve represents the higher concentration and why?
**Answer**: The steeper curve represents the higher concentration. Higher concentration means more reactant particles per unit volume, leading to more frequent collisions and a faster rate. The steeper initial slope shows greater rate.
**Explanation**: Steeper = faster = higher concentration. A common error is picking the curve that produces more total gas (same amount of Mg gives same total gas).
