# Question: Question Q-8.2-01
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Topic**: 8.2
**Question**: List the halogens in order and describe the trend in colour and physical state.
**Answer**: Fluorine (pale yellow gas), chlorine (green gas), bromine (red-brown liquid), iodine (grey-black solid). As you go down the group, they become darker in colour and change from gas to liquid to solid.
**Explanation**: Darker + denser down the group. A common error is saying bromine is a gas.
