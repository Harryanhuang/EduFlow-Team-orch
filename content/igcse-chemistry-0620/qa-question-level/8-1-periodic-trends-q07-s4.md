# Question: Question Q-8.1-07
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Topic**: 8.1
**Question**: Write the balanced symbol equation for potassium reacting with water.
**Answer**: 2K(s) + 2H₂O(l) → 2KOH(aq) + H₂(g)
**Explanation**: Group 1 metals form metal hydroxides and hydrogen with water. Common errors include writing K₂O or forgetting to balance hydrogen.
