# Question: Question Q-1.2-09
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Topic**: 1.2
**Question**: Describe how you would obtain pure salt crystals from a salt solution.
**Answer**: (1) Heat the solution to evaporate some water (concentrate). (2) Stop heating when crystals begin to form at the edges (crystallisation point). (3) Leave to cool slowly so large, pure crystals form. (4) Filter, wash with a little cold water, and dry.
**Explanation**: Crystallisation, not evaporation to dryness. A common error is evaporating to complete dryness.
