# Question: Question Q-1.1-01
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Topic**: 1.1
**Question**: Describe the arrangement and movement of particles in a solid.
**Answer**: Particles are arranged in a regular, closely packed pattern. They vibrate about fixed positions but do not move from place to place.
**Explanation**: Solids have fixed shape and volume. A common error is saying particles "don't move at all" — they vibrate.
