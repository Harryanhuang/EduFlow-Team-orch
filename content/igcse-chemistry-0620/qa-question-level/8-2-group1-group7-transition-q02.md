# Question: Question Q-8.2-02
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Topic**: 8.2
**Question**: What is a displacement reaction? Write the equation for chlorine displacing bromide from potassium bromide solution.
**Answer**: A more reactive halogen displaces a less reactive halogen from its salt solution. Cl₂(aq) + 2KBr(aq) → 2KCl(aq) + Br₂(aq). The solution turns orange-brown due to bromine.
**Explanation**: More reactive displaces less reactive. A common error is not balancing the equation.
