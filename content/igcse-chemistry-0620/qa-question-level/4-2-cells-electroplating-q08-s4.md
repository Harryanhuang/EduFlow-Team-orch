# Question: Question Q-4.2-08
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Topic**: 4.2
**Question**: Why is aluminium used for overhead power cables?
**Answer**: Aluminium is a good conductor of electricity and has a low density (lightweight), making it suitable for overhead cables where weight is important. It is also resistant to corrosion due to its oxide layer.
**Explanation**: Conductor + lightweight. A common error is saying it is the "best" conductor (copper is better but heavier).
