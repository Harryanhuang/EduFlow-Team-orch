# Question: Question Q-2.1-08
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Topic**: 2.1
**Question**: Calculate the relative atomic mass of chlorine given that it consists of 75% chlorine-35 and 25% chlorine-37.
**Answer**: Ar = (75 × 35 + 25 × 37) / 100 = (2625 + 925) / 100 = 3550 / 100 = 35.5
**Explanation**: Weighted average of isotope masses by abundance. A common error is taking the simple average (36).
