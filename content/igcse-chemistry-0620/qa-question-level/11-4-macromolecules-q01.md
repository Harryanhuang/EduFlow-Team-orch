# Question: Question Q-11.4-01
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Topic**: 11.4
**Question**: What type of polymer are proteins?
**Answer**: Proteins are natural condensation polymers made from amino acid monomers joined by peptide (amide) links.
**Explanation**: Amino acids → peptide bonds. A common error is saying proteins are made from sugars.
**Tags**: proteins, amino-acids, peptide-link, condensation-polymer
