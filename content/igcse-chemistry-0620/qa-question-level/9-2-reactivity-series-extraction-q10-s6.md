# Question: Question Q-9.2-10
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Topic**: 9.2
**Question**: Give two advantages of recycling metals.
**Answer**: (1) Saves energy — extracting metal from recycled material requires much less energy than from ore. (2) Conserves natural resources — reduces the need to mine new ore. Also: reduces landfill waste and pollution from mining.
**Explanation**: Energy + resource conservation. A common error is saying recycling makes the metal "purer."
