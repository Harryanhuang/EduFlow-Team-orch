# QQL: 5.1 — Q-5.1-05 (S)

**Question ID**: Q-5.1-05
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Topic**: 5.1 Exothermic and endothermic reactions
**Question**: Draw and describe the features of a reaction profile for an exothermic reaction. Label the activation energy and the enthalpy change (ΔH).
**Answer**: The reaction profile shows energy on the y-axis and reaction progress on the x-axis. Reactants start at a higher energy level than products (energy released). The "hump" represents the activation energy (minimum energy for successful collisions). ΔH is the energy difference between reactants and products (negative for exothermic).
**Explanation**: Students must draw and label all features. A common error is drawing products higher than reactants for an exothermic reaction.
