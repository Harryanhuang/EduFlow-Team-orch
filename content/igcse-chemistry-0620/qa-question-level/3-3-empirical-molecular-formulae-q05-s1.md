# QQL: 3-3-empirical-molecular-formulae — Q-3.3-05 (F)

**Question ID**: Q-3.3-05
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Topic**: 3.3 Empirical and molecular formulae
**Question**: What is the empirical formula of a compound?
**Answer**: The empirical formula shows the simplest whole-number ratio of atoms of each element present in a compound.
**Explanation**: Empirical = simplest ratio. A common error is confusing it with the molecular formula, which gives the actual number of atoms.
