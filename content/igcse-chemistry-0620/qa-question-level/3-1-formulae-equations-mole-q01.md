# Question: Question Q-3.1-01
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Topic**: 3.1
**Question**: Calculate the relative molecular mass (Mᵣ) of water (H₂O). (Aᵣ: H = 1, O = 16)
**Answer**: Mᵣ = (2 × 1) + 16 = 18.
**Explanation**: Sum of relative atomic masses of all atoms. A common error is forgetting to multiply hydrogen by 2.
