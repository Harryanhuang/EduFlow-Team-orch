# AP Calculus BC — Production Syllabus

## Course overview
- **Exam**: AP Calculus BC (College Board CED 2024-25)
- **Scope**: All 10 Units (U1-U8 shared with AB; U9-U10 BC-only)
- **Item format**: MCQ, 3 per subtopic (F:1|S:1|C:1), calculator marked where appropriate
- **Directory**: `content/ap-calculus-bc/subtopics/unitN/`

## Unit breakdown

### U1: Limits and Continuity (4 subtopics)
- U1.1.1 极限的直观定义与符号
- U1.1.2 左右极限与极限存在条件
- U1.1.3 极限运算法则
- U1.1.4 极限不存在的三种情形
- U1.2.1 直接代入与因式分解消零
- U1.2.2 有理化与特殊极限 (sin x/x)
- U1.2.3 无穷远处的极限与水平渐近线
- U1.3.1 连续的定义与三类不连续
- U1.3.2 中间值定理 (IVT)

### U2: Differentiation Definition & Basic Rules (4 subtopics)

- U2.4.1 可导性与连续性的关系
