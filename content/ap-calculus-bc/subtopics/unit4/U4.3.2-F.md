What is $\displaystyle\lim_{x\to0}\frac{\sin x}{x}$?

## Options
A) $0$

B) $1$

C) $\infty$

D) Does not exist

## Answer
B

## Explanation
As $x\to0$, $\frac{\sin x}{x}$ has the indeterminate form $\frac{0}{0}$. By L'Hôpital's Rule: $\lim_{x\to0}\frac{\sin x}{x}=\lim_{x\to0}\frac{\cos x}{1}=1$. Option A is $\sin(0)$. Option C would result from incorrectly applying the rule. Option D is false — the limit exists and equals 1.
