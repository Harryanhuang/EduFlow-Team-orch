For $f(x)=x^2$, what is the average rate of change from $x=1$ to $x=3$?

## Options
A) $2$

B) $4$

C) $6$

D) $8$

## Answer
B

## Explanation
Average rate of change $=\frac{f(3)-f(1)}{3-1}=\frac{9-1}{2}=4$. Option A is $f'(1)=2$ (instantaneous, not average). Option C is $f(3)-f(1)=8$ without dividing by interval length. Option D is just $f(3)$.
