Use the local linearization of $f(x)=\sqrt{x}$ at $x=9$ to approximate $\sqrt{9.2}$.

## Options
A) $3.0$

B) $3.1$

C) $3.03$

D) $3.02$

## Answer
C

## Explanation
$f(9)=3$, $f'(x)=\frac{1}{2\sqrt{x}}$, so $f'(9)=\frac{1}{6}$. Linearization: $L(x)=3+\frac{1}{6}(x-9)$. At $x=9.2$: $L(9.2)=3+\frac{1}{6}(0.2)=3+\frac{1}{30}\approx3.033$. Option A is just $f(9)$. Option B overestimates. Option D uses $f'(9)=\frac{1}{5}$ incorrectly.
