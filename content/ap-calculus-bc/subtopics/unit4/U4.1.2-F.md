A particle moves along a straight line with position $s(t)=t^2-4t+3$. What is its velocity at $t=2$?

## Options
A) $0$

B) $2$

C) $4$

D) $-4$

## Answer
A

## Explanation
Velocity is the derivative of position: $v(t)=s'(t)=2t-4$. At $t=2$: $v(2)=4-4=0$. Option B is $s'(0)$. Option C is $s(2)$. Option D is $v(0)$ with wrong sign.
