What is $\dfrac{d}{dx}[\cos x]$?

## Options
A) $\sin x$

B) $-\sin x$

C) $\cos x$

D) $-\cos x$

## Answer
B

## Explanation
The derivative of $\cos x$ is $-\sin x$. This is one of the basic trigonometric derivative formulas. Option A gives the derivative of $\sin x$. Option C says the derivative equals itself (true only for $e^x$). Option D negates the original function, which is not a standard derivative result.
