What is $\dfrac{d}{dx}\left[x^5\right]$?

## Options
A) $5x$

B) $5x^4$

C) $4x^5$

D) $x^4$

## Answer
B

## Explanation
By the power rule, $\frac{d}{dx}[x^n]=nx^{n-1}$. With $n=5$: $\frac{d}{dx}[x^5]=5x^4$. Option A forgets to reduce the exponent. Option C has the wrong exponent on $x$. Option D drops the coefficient.
