The graph of $f$ is a line passing through the points $(0,1)$ and $(2,5)$. What is $f'(1)$?

## Options
A) $1$

B) $2$

C) $3$

D) $5$

## Answer
B

## Explanation
A line has constant slope everywhere. The slope is $\frac{5-1}{2-0}=\frac{4}{2}=2$. So $f'(x)=2$ for all $x$, including $f'(1)=2$. Option A is the $y$-intercept. Option C is the function value $f(1)=3$. Option D is $f(2)=5$.
