If $f(x)=\sin x$, what is $f^{(4)}(x)$ (the fourth derivative)?

## Options
A) $\sin x$

B) $\cos x$

C) $-\sin x$

D) $-\cos x$

## Answer
A

## Explanation
$f'(x)=\cos x$, $f''(x)=-\sin x$, $f'''(x)=-\cos x$, $f^{(4)}(x)=\sin x$. The derivatives of $\sin x$ cycle with period 4: $\sin\to\cos\to-\sin\to-\cos\to\sin$. Option B is $f'(x)$. Option C is $f''(x)$. Option D is $f'''(x)$.
