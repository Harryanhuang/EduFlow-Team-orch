What is $\dfrac{d}{dx}\left[\arcsin x\right]$?

## Options
A) $\frac{1}{\sqrt{1-x^2}}$

B) $-\frac{1}{\sqrt{1-x^2}}$

C) $\frac{1}{1+x^2}$

D) $\frac{1}{\sqrt{x^2-1}}$

## Answer
A

## Explanation
The derivative of $\arcsin x$ is $\frac{1}{\sqrt{1-x^2}}$. Option B is the derivative of $\arccos x$. Option C is the derivative of $\arctan x$. Option D is the derivative of $\text{arccosh }x$, not a standard AP formula.
