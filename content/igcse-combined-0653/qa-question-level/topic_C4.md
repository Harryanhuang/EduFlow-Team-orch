## QA Question Level File

**3. [F]** What is the law of conservation of mass?

**4. [F]** State two examples of physical changes.

**5. [S]** Balance the equation: Fe + O₂ → Fe₂O₃.

**6. [S]** What is the difference between a chemical reaction and a physical change?

**7. [S]** What is a catalyst?

**8. [S]** Balance: CH₄ + O₂ → CO₂ + H₂O.

**11. [S]** What is relative molecular mass (Mr)?

**12. [S]** Calculate the relative molecular mass of calcium carbonate CaCO₃.

**14. [C]** Balance: Ca(OH)₂ + HCl → CaCl₂ + H₂O.

**15. [C]** A 2.4 g sample of magnesium is burned in oxygen, forming 4.0 g of magnesium oxide. Calculate the mass of oxygen that reacted.

**16. [C]** Balance: C₂H₆ + O₂ → CO₂ + H₂O.

**17. [C]** 6 g of carbon is burned completely in 16 g of oxygen. Calculate the mass of carbon dioxide produced and identify which reactant is in excess.

**18. [C]** Evaluate the importance of balancing chemical equations in industrial chemical processes.
