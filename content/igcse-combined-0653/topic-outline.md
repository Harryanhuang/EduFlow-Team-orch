# IGCSE Combined Science (0653) Topic Outline

Based on Cambridge IGCSE Combined Science (0653) syllabus. Covers Biology, Chemistry, and Physics topics assessed across two written papers (Paper 1 Multiple Choice and Paper 2 Short Answer/Structured).

## Assessment Overview
- **Paper 1**: 45 minutes, 40 multiple choice questions (40 marks)
- **Paper 2**: 1 hour 15 minutes, structured questions (80 marks)
- **Total**: 120 marks, weighted across biology/chemistry/physics equally

### B2 — Cell Structure and Organisation
