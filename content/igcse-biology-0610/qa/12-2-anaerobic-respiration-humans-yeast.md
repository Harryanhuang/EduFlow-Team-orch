# QA: 12.2 — Anaerobic respiration in humans and yeast

## Topic 名称
Anaerobic respiration in humans and yeast（无氧呼吸：人类与酵母）

## Topic 定义
掌握人类 (glucose→lactic acid) 与酵母 (glucose→ethanol+CO₂) 两类 anaerobic respiration equation。理解 NAD⁺ regeneration 才是 anaerobic respiration 的核心生化目的——乳酸/乙醇仅是排废产物。掌握 oxygen debt 的 full repament chain。理解酵母发酵的经济应用(cakes/beer/wine/biofuel)及 temperature/enzyme/fermentation rate 的关系。

## 关键知识点
- Human: glucose → lactic acid (+ ~2 ATP), no CO₂
- Yeast: glucose → ethanol + CO₂ (+ ~2 ATP), fermentation
- NAD⁺ regeneration: glycolysis needs NAD⁺; no O₂ → ETC stops → NADH→NAD⁺ via LDH (humans) or alcohol DH (yeast)
- O₂ debt: post-exercise O₂ for lactic acid→pyruvate→CO₂+H₂O, gluconeogenesis, myoglobin refill
- Anaerobic limit: low ATP (2 vs 38), lactic acid ↓pH, slow lactate clearance
- Yeast economic applications: bread (CO₂ rises dough), beer/wine (ethanol), biofuel
- Temperature effect: 35°C → optimum (faster), 50°C → denaturation (zero)

## 常见错误
- 写 human anaerobic equation 含 CO₂（实际只有 yeast）
- 写 yeast fermentation 产 lactic acid（实际 ethanol+CO₂）
- 说 oxygen debt 是"运动中需要的 O₂"（实际是运动后）
- 说 anaerobic respiration 目标是"产生乳酸"（目标是再生 NAD⁺）
- 说 anaerobic respiration "不产能量"
