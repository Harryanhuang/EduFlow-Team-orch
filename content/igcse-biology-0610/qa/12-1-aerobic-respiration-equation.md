# QA: 12.1 — Aerobic respiration: equation and energy

## Topic 名称
Aerobic respiration: equation and energy（有氧呼吸：方程式与能量）

## Topic 定义
掌握 aerobic respiration 的 word + balanced symbol equation (C₆H₁₂O₆ + 6O₂ → 6CO₂ + 6H₂O)。理解 respiration 的能量释放用于 ATP 合成的七类用途。理解 mitochondria 结构与 respiration 的功能适配（cristae 增加 ETC 表面积），以及 high-energy-demand cells → more mitochondria 的对应关系。掌握植物昼夜兼行 respiration 的必然性。能设计并分析 respiration 的温度实验（热量释放-CO2 产出）和 respirometer 速率测量。

## 关键知识点
- Aerobic equation: C₆H₁₂O₆ + 6O₂ → 6CO₂ + 6H₂O (+ ~38 ATP)
- Mitochondria: glycolysis (cytoplasm) → Krebs cycle (matrix) → ETC + ATP synthase (inner membrane/cristae)
- ATP uses: muscle contraction, protein synthesis, active transport, cell division, growth/repair, thermogenesis, nerve impulses
- Plant respiration: 24/7 in all living cells; photosynthesis only in light + chlorophyll-containing cells
- Temperature effect on respiration: enzyme-controlled → Q₁₀≈2 → optimum → denaturation above
- Experiment — heat: vacuum flask + germinating peas + thermometer + dead pea control
- Experiment — CO₂: KOH scrubs inlet CO₂ → limewater milky with respiring organisms; dead control
- Respirometer: KOH absorbs CO₂ → O₂ consumption measured by liquid drop movement in capillary
- Aerobic (38 ATP) vs Anaerobic (2 ATP) — complete vs incomplete breakdown

## 常见错误
- 写反 respiration equation（CO₂ + H₂O → glucose + O₂ 是 photosynthesis）
- 说 plants "only photosynthesise, they don't respire"
- 说 respiration 只发生在 cytoplasm
- 描述温度实验不解释酶机制
- 设计 experiment 忘加 dead control 或 KOH scrub
