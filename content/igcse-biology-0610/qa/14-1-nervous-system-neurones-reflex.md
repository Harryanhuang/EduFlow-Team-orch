## е…ій”®зҹҘиҜҶзӮ№
- CNS (brain + spinal cord = processor) vs PNS (nerves = wiring)
- Three neurone types: Sensory (receptorвҶ’CNS), Relay (within CNS), Motor (CNSвҶ’effector)
- Reflex arc: stimulusвҶ’receptorвҶ’sensoryвҶ’relay (spinal cord)вҶ’motorвҶ’effectorвҶ’response
- Motor neurone structure: cell body, dendrites, axon, myelin (saltatory), nodes of Ranvier, axon terminals
- Synapse: CaВІвҒә influx вҶ’ neurotransmitter vesicle fusion вҶ’ diffusion вҶ’ postsynaptic receptor вҶ’ NaвҒә influx вҶ’ new impulse; one-directional (asymmetric vesicle/receptor)
- Myelin: Schwann cells вҶ’ insulation вҶ’ saltatory conduction at nodes вҶ’ speed 1вҶ’100 m/s; MS = demyelination вҶ’ conduction failure
- Sensory vs motor comparison: cell body position (mid-axon ganglion vs one end in CNS), axon length, terminal target
- Dual pathway: spinal reflex ~0.1s (unconscious) vs cortical sensation ~0.5s (conscious)
