# QA: 9.2 — Blood: components and functions

## Topic 名称
Blood: components and functions（血液：成分与功能）

## Topic 定义
掌握血液四大组分（红血球/白血球/血小板/血浆）的结构、功能与数量比例。理解 phagocytes vs lymphocytes 的非特异性 vs 特异性免疫分工、blood clotting cascade 的酶联反应链、haemophilia 的分子病理、以及 centrifugation 分层原理。能将单组分异常映射到全血功能失调（如 Factor VIII 缺失→血友病）。

## 关键知识点
- 四大组分：RBC (O₂, haemoglobin) / WBC (defence) / Platelets (clotting) / Plasma (transport medium, ~55%)
- WBC 两类：Phagocytes (non-specific, phagocytosis) vs Lymphocytes (specific, antibodies + memory)
- Blood clotting cascade: Platelets→thromboplastin→prothrombin→thrombin→fibrinogen→fibrin→mesh→clot
- Haemophilia: Factor VIII deficiency → cascade broken → no fibrin → no clot
- 离心分层：RBC bottom (~45%), buffy coat middle (<1% WBC+platelets), plasma top (~55%)
- Haematocrit: RBC 体积/全血体积，正常男~45% 女~40%
- Plasma 功能七类：nutrients, waste, urea, hormones, heat, ions, plasma proteins

## 常见错误
- 说 plasma carries oxygen（O₂ by haemoglobin in RBCs）
- 说 all WBCs "eat" pathogens（lymphocytes produce antibodies, don't engulf）
- 说 platelets "are" the clot（platelets trigger cascade, fibrin is the clot mesh）
- 混淆 phagocytes (non-specific) 和 lymphocytes (specific)
- 说 buffy coat is "fat"（实际是 WBCs + platelets）
- 忽略 plasma proteins 的 osmotic + clotting 功能
