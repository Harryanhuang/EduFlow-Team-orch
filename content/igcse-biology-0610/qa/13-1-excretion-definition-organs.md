# QA: 13.1 — Excretion: definition, organs, and urea formation

## Topic 名称
Excretion: definition, organs, and urea formation（排泄：定义、器官与尿素形成）

## Topic 定义
掌握 excretion 的定义与必要性——移除细胞代谢产生的毒性废物以维持体内平衡。区分 excretion vs egestion vs secretion 三个易混概念。掌握四大排泄器官（lungs/kidneys/skin/liver）及其去除物质。理解 urea 的 deamination→ornithine cycle 产制路径，以及 liver-kidney 代谢-滤过轴的协同分工。能解释高蛋白饮食如何通过排泄系统调节维持 homeostasis。

## 关键知识点
- Excretion definition: removal of metabolic waste (toxic by-products of cellular reactions)
- Excretion vs Egestion: metabolic waste (was in cells) vs undigested food (never absorbed)
- Excretory organs: Lungs (CO₂), Kidneys (urea, excess H₂O, ions), Skin (H₂O, salts, urea), Liver (deamination→urea, Hb→bilirubin)
- Urea formation: excess amino acids → liver deamination → NH₃ → urea (ornithine cycle) → blood → kidneys → urine
- CO₂ toxicity: CO₂ + H₂O → H₂CO₃ → H⁺ + HCO₃⁻ → pH↓ → enzyme denaturation → breathing driven by CO₂/pH
- Liver-Kidney axis: liver = metabolic detox; kidneys = filtration/excretion
- Homeostatic links: CO₂→pH, urea→nitrogen balance, H₂O→osmoregulation

## 常见错误
- 说 faeces are "excreted" (they are egested)
- 说 liver "excretes" directly (it processes; kidneys excrete)
- 混淆 excretion, egestion, secretion
- 说 CO₂ is "not toxic"
- 说 urea comes from "digestion" (comes from deamination in liver)
