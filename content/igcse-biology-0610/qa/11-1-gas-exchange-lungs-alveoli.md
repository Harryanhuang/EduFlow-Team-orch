# QA: 11.1 — Gas exchange: lungs and alveoli

## Topic 名称
Gas exchange: lungs and alveoli（气体交换：肺与肺泡）

## Topic 定义
掌握 breathing (ventilation) 与 respiration 的本质区别。理解肺泡五大适应特性如何优化 Fick's Law（表面积/浓度梯度/扩散距离）。掌握吸气（主动）与呼气（静止被动、强制主动）的肌肉-压力-体积机制。掌握吸烟对呼吸系统的三重危害（tar→cilia/emphysema; carcinogens→cancer; CO→O₂运输）。能将 emphysema 的病理变化映射到 Fick's Law 变量下降。

## 关键知识点
- Breathing (mechanical pump, ventilation) vs Respiration (chemical energy release in cells)
- 吸入空气：21% O₂, 0.04% CO₂；呼出空气：16% O₂, 4% CO₂；N₂ 不变
- Alveoli 五适：表面积 ~70 m² / 1-cell-thick wall / rich capillary network / moist surface / good ventilation
- Fick's Law: Rate ∝ (Surface Area × Concentration Gradient) / Diffusion Distance
- Inhalation: diaphragm contracts↓, external intercostals contract→ribs↑out → volume↑ → pressure↓ → air in (ACTIVE)
- Exhalation at rest: diaphragm relaxes↑, intercostals relax→ribs↓in → volume↓ → pressure↑ → air out (PASSIVE, elastic recoil)
- Forced exhalation: internal intercostals + abdominal muscles contract (ACTIVE)
- Trachea: C-shaped cartilage rings (keep open), ciliated epithelium + goblet cells (mucus escalator)
- 烟草三重危害：Tar→cilia 麻痹+alveoli 破→emphysema；Carcinogens→lung cancer；CO→carboxyhaemoglobin→O₂ carrying↓
- Emphysema→Fick's Law: 表面积↓ (alveoli merge) + 梯度↓ (fewer capillaries) → 扩散速率↓

## 常见错误
- 交替使用 breathing/respiration 为同义词
- 说 exhaled air has "no oxygen"（仍有 16%）
- 说 inhalation is passive（实际 active contraction）
- 说 all exhalation is active（静止呼出是 passive elastic recoil）
- 说 cartilage rings are "complete"（C-shaped，食道侧缺）
- 描述 emphysema 不提 Fick's Law 变量

## 可出题方向
- Breathing vs Respiration 辨析
- Alveoli 五适应+Fick's Law 映射解释
- Inhalation/Exhalation 肌肉-压力机制比较 (active/passive)
- 吸烟三重危害详述
- Emphysema→Fick's Law 预测→症状
