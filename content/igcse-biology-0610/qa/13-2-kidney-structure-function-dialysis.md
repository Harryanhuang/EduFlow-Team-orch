# QA: 13.2 — Kidney structure and function, dialysis

## Topic 名称
Kidney structure and function, dialysis（肾脏结构与功能，透析）

## Topic 定义
掌握 kidney 的宏观结构（cortex/medulla/pelvis/renal artery/vein/ureter）及 nephron 各区域的结构-功能适配（glomerulus→PCT→Loop of Henle→collecting duct）。理解 ultrafiltration（被动高压过滤）与 selective reabsorption（主动重吸收）的两步机制。掌握 urine vs plasma 的组成差异及其生理原因。理解 dialysis 作为 kidney failure 管理方案的原理与限制。掌握 ADH 负反馈调控血液水势的完整回路。

## 关键知识点
- Kidney gross: cortex (glomeruli), medulla (LoH + collecting ducts), pelvis (urine collection), renal artery/vein/ureter
- Nephron: ~1 million/kidney; ultrafiltration → selective reabsorption → urine concentration
- Ultrafiltration: afferent > efferent → high glomerular pressure → small molecules forced into Bowman's capsule; proteins/cells retained
- Selective reabsorption: PCT — all glucose (active transport), most water (osmosis), most ions reabsorbed; microvilli + mitochondria
- Urine vs plasma: urea ↑70×, glucose=0, protein=0, cells=0; water ~95% variable
- Dialysis: partially permeable membrane + dialysis fluid with correct gradients; manages not cures
- ADH: hypothalamus osmoreceptors → posterior pituitary → ADH → collecting duct aquaporins → water reabsorption ↑↓ → negative feedback

## 常见错误
- 说 nephron "makes urea" (liver makes)
- 说 proteins pass into filtrate
- 说 glucose is normally in urine
- 说 dialysis "cures" kidney failure
- 说 ADH "adds water" to blood
- 混淆 renal artery/vein 方向
