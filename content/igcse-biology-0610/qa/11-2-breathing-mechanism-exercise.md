# QA: 11.2 — Breathing mechanism and effects of exercise

## Topic 名称
Breathing mechanism and effects of exercise（呼吸机制与运动影响）

## Topic 定义
掌握 exhalation 的 active/passive 机制、intercostal external vs internal 的拮抗分工。理解 CO₂ 驱动的 chemoreceptor 负反馈控制系统（而非 O₂ 驱动）。掌握 exercise → oxygen debt → recovery 的完整代谢链（anaerobic→lactic acid→Cori cycle）。能将 trained vs untrained 的生理差异映射到 breathing rate, vital capacity, recovery time 的实测差异。

## 关键知识点
- Exhalation at rest = passive (elastic recoil, no ATP); forced exhalation = active (internal intercostals + abdominals)
- External intercostals (inhalation) vs Internal intercostals (forced exhalation) — antagonistic pair
- Tidal volume (~0.5 dm³) vs Vital capacity (~3-5 dm³) — spirometer trace
- Exercise trigger: CO₂↑ → H₂CO₃ → pH↓ → medulla oblongata chemoreceptors → rate↑ + depth↑
- Anaerobic respiration: glucose → lactic acid; O₂ debt → post-exercise O₂ for lactic acid oxidation + myoglobin refill
- Recovery 四阶段：rest base → spike → gradual decline → baseline
- Respiratory + Circulatory co-regulation: medulla → SAN + respiratory muscles; Bohr effect (↑T/pH↓ → O₂ offload)
- Athlete vs untrained: larger vital capacity/stroke volume/mitochondria/myoglobin → smaller rate spike, faster recovery

## 常见错误
- 说 exhalation is "always active"（静止呼出 passive elastic recoil）
- 说 exercise breathing 由 O₂ 需求驱动（primary trigger is CO₂）
- 说 anaerobic respiration "replaces" aerobic
- 混淆 tidal volume 和 vital capacity
- 描述 exercise recovery 不解释 oxygen debt 的生化链

- Spirometer trace 四阶段解读
