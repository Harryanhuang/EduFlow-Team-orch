# QA: 10.1 — Pathogens and disease transmission

## Topic 名称
Pathogens and disease transmission（病原体与疾病传播）

## Topic 定义
掌握四种病原体类型（bacteria/virus/fungi/protoctista）及其代表性疾病的传播方式、症状与预防。理解 communicable vs non-communicable 的根本区别。掌握以 malaria (vector-borne protoctist) 和 cholera (waterborne bacterium) 为模型的传播链分析，以及多模式预防策略。能分析人体非特异性屏障（skin/mucus/stomach acid/lysozyme）的协同防御机制。

## 关键知识点
- 四种病原：Bacteria (Salmonella, TB, Vibrio cholerae) / Viruses (HIV, influenza) / Fungi (athlete's foot, rose black spot) / Protoctista (Plasmodium→malaria)
- Communicable (transmissible, e.g., flu, TB, malaria) vs Non-communicable (non-transmissible, e.g., cancer, diabetes)
- HIV 传播：body fluids only（sexual, needles, mother-baby, blood transfusion）；casual contact 不传播
- Malaria：Plasmodium + female Anopheles mosquito vector → 预防策略五层
- Cholera：Vibrio cholerae + faecal-oral water contamination → 预防策略 clean water + sewage
- Salmonella：food poisoning → 污染食物→细菌→毒素→肠道损伤
- 非特异性屏障：Skin (keratin + sebum), Mucus (traps + cilia sweep), Stomach acid (pH 1-2 kills), Lysozyme (tears/saliva → bacterial cell wall)
- 疾病控制八法：hygiene, vaccination, isolation, vector control, water/sewage, antibiotics (bacteria only), food safety, education

## 常见错误
- 混淆四种 pathogen 类型的代表性疾病
- 说 antibiotics work on viruses
- 说 malaria 由 virus 引起
- 说 HIV 可通过 casual contact 传播
- 认为 communicable = "serious"（non-communicable 也可以严重）
- 把 cholera 和 malaria 混为一谈（传播机制根本不同）
