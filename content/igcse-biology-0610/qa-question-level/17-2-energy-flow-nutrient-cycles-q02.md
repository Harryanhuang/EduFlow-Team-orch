# Question: Question Q-17.2-02
**Difficulty**: Challenge
**Question**: Describe the carbon cycle.
**Answer**: Carbon moves between organisms and the environment: (1) Photosynthesis — plants take in CO₂ from the atmosphere and convert it to organic molecules. (2) Feeding — carbon passes along food chains. (3) Respiration — organisms release CO₂ back to the atmosphere. (4) Decomposition — decomposers release CO₂. (5) Combustion — burning fossil fuels releases CO₂.
**Explanation**: Photosynthesis, feeding, respiration, decomposition, combustion. A common error is forgetting combustion.
