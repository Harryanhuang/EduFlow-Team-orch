# Question: Question Q-2.1-02
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: Name two structures found in plant cells but not in animal cells.
**Answer**: Cell wall (made of cellulose, provides support and shape) and chloroplasts (contain chlorophyll for photosynthesis). Plant cells also have a large permanent vacuole containing cell sap.
**Explanation**: Cell wall + chloroplasts. A common error is saying "mitochondria" (those are in both).
