# Question: Question Q-5.2-01
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: What is meant by the "optimum temperature" for an enzyme?
**Answer**: The optimum temperature is the temperature at which the enzyme works at its fastest rate. For most human enzymes, the optimum is about 37°C (body temperature).
**Explanation**: Fastest rate, body temperature. A common error is saying the optimum is "any high temperature."
