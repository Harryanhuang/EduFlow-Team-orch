# Question: Question Q-18.1-06
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Why is a fermenter used in industrial biotechnology?
**Answer**: A fermenter provides controlled conditions for large-scale microbial growth: temperature (cooling jacket), pH (monitored and adjusted), oxygen supply (aeration for aerobic processes), and nutrients (continuous supply). It also prevents contamination by unwanted microorganisms and allows monitoring of the process.
**Explanation**: Controlled conditions, large-scale, sterile. A common error is saying fermenters are "just big containers."
