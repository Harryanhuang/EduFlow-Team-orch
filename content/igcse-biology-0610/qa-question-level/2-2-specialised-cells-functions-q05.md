# Question: Question Q-2.2-05
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: What is a nerve cell (neurone) and how is it adapted for its function?
**Answer**: Nerve cells transmit electrical impulses. Adaptations: long axon for transmitting signals over distances; branched dendrites to connect to other neurones; myelin sheath (in some neurones) to speed up impulse transmission; cell body contains nucleus and organelles.
**Explanation**: Axon, dendrites, myelin. A common error is confusing a nerve cell with a muscle cell.
