# Question: Question Q-19.1-02
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: How does deforestation contribute to climate change?
**Answer**: Deforestation: (1) reduces photosynthesis — fewer trees absorb less CO₂ from the atmosphere. (2) Burning cleared trees releases stored carbon as CO₂. (3) Soil disturbance releases stored carbon. Increased atmospheric CO₂ enhances the greenhouse effect, leading to global warming and climate change.
**Explanation**: Less CO₂ uptake + more CO₂ release. A common error is saying deforestation "only affects local areas."
