# Question: Question Q-2.3-03
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Name three organs in the human digestive system and state the function of each.
**Answer**: Stomach — churns food and mixes with acid and enzymes to begin protein digestion. Small intestine — completes digestion and absorbs nutrients into the blood. Liver — produces bile to emulsify fats. Also: pancreas produces digestive enzymes; large intestine absorbs water.
**Explanation**: Organ + function. A common error is confusing stomach digestion with absorption (absorption is in the small intestine).
