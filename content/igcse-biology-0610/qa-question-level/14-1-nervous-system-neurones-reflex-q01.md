# Question: Question Q-14.1-01
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: Name the three types of neurone and state their function.
**Answer**: Sensory neurone — carries impulses from receptors to CNS. Relay neurone (interneurone) — entirely within CNS, connects sensory to motor. Motor neurone — carries impulses from CNS to effectors (muscles/glands). Reflex arc: stimulus → receptor → sensory → relay → motor → effector → response.
**Explanation**: Sensory → CNS, relay → connects, motor → effectors. A common error is reversing sensory and motor.
