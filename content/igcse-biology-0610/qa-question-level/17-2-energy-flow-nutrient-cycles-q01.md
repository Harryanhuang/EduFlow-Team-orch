# Question: Question Q-17.2-01
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: Describe the nitrogen cycle.
**Answer**: Nitrogen moves through ecosystems: (1) Nitrogen fixation — bacteria convert atmospheric N₂ to nitrates in soil. (2) Plants absorb nitrates to make proteins. (3) Animals eat plants. (4) Decomposers break down dead matter, releasing ammonia (ammonification). (5) Nitrifying bacteria convert ammonia to nitrates. (6) Denitrifying bacteria convert nitrates back to N₂ gas.
**Explanation**: Fixation, absorption, feeding, decomposition, nitrification, denitrification. A common error is forgetting nitrogen fixation.
