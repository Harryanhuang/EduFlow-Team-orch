# Question: Question Q-18.1-03
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: What is biotechnology? Give two examples.
**Answer**: Biotechnology is the use of living organisms (or parts of organisms) to make or modify products for human benefit. Examples: using yeast to make bread and alcohol, using bacteria to make yoghurt and cheese, using genetically modified bacteria to produce insulin, treating sewage with bacteria.
**Explanation**: Living organisms → products. A common error is saying biotechnology is "only genetic engineering."
