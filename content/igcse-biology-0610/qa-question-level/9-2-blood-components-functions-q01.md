# Question: Question Q-9.2-01
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: Name the four components of blood and state one function of each.
**Answer**: Red blood cells — carry oxygen (haemoglobin). White blood cells — defend against pathogens (phagocytosis, antibodies). Platelets — blood clotting (prevent blood loss and pathogen entry). Plasma — transports dissolved substances (glucose, CO₂, urea, hormones, ions).
**Explanation**: RBC, WBC, platelets, plasma. A common error is saying plasma carries oxygen.
