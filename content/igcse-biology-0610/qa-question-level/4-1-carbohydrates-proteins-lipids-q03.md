# Question: Question Q-4.1-03
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: Give two functions of proteins in living organisms.
**Answer**: (1) Enzymes — biological catalysts that speed up chemical reactions. (2) Structural — keratin in hair, collagen in skin. (3) Transport — haemoglobin carries oxygen. (4) Hormones — insulin regulates blood glucose. (5) Antibodies — defend against pathogens.
**Explanation**: Enzymes, structure, transport, hormones, antibodies. A common error is saying proteins are only for "growth."
