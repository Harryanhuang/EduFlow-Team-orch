# Question: Question Q-7.1-02
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: What is the main function of carbohydrates in the diet?
**Answer**: Carbohydrates are the main source of energy for the body. They are broken down into glucose during digestion. Glucose is used in cellular respiration to release energy for all cell activities. Excess glucose is stored as glycogen in the liver and muscles, or converted to fat.
**Explanation**: Main energy source. A common error is saying carbohydrates are "for growth" (that's protein).
