# Question: Question Q-4.1-02
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: What are the building blocks (monomers) of starch, proteins, and fats?
**Answer**: Starch is made of simple sugars (glucose molecules joined together). Proteins are made of amino acids joined by peptide bonds. Fats (lipids) are made of glycerol and three fatty acids.
**Explanation**: Glucose, amino acids, glycerol + fatty acids. A common error is saying proteins are made of glucose.
