# Question: Question Q-14.1-04
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Why are reflex actions important? Give two examples.
**Answer**: Reflexes are rapid, automatic, involuntary — they bypass conscious brain processing for speed. (1) Protection from injury — withdrawal reflex. (2) Homeostasis — pupil reflex (light control), swallowing reflex. (3) Posture — stretch reflex, knee-jerk reflex. (4) Newborn survival — sucking, grasping reflexes. All use the same arc but serve different needs.
**Explanation**: Speed + automation → protection + homeostasis. A common error is saying reflexes "don't involve the nervous system."
