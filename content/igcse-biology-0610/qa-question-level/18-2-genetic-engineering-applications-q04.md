# Question: Question Q-18.2-04
**Difficulty**: Challenge
**Question**: What are the advantages and disadvantages of genetically modified (GM) crops?
**Answer**: Advantages: increased yield, resistance to pests/diseases (reduces pesticide use), improved nutrition (e.g., golden rice with vitamin A), drought resistance. Disadvantages: risk of transferring genes to wild plants, reduced biodiversity, unknown long-term health effects, dependence on seed companies, ethical concerns.
**Explanation**: Pros and cons balanced. A common error is saying GM crops are "definitely safe" or "definitely dangerous."
