# Question: Question Q-19.1-01
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: What are the main causes of habitat destruction?
**Answer**: Deforestation (clearing forests for agriculture, timber, development), urbanisation (building cities and infrastructure), agriculture (converting land for crops and livestock), dam construction (flooding areas), pollution (contaminating habitats), and mining. These activities reduce biodiversity and destroy ecosystems.
**Explanation**: Deforestation, urbanisation, agriculture, dams, pollution, mining. A common error is listing only one cause.
