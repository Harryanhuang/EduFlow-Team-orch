# Question: Question Q-19.2-03
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: What is conservation? Give three methods of conservation.
**Answer**: Conservation is the protection and management of natural resources and biodiversity. Methods: (1) Establishing nature reserves and national parks to protect habitats. (2) Breeding endangered species in captivity and reintroducing them. (3) Creating seed banks to preserve plant genetic diversity. (4) Education programmes. (5) Legislation (bans on hunting, trade restrictions).
**Explanation**: Protection + management, multiple methods. A common error is saying conservation is "just protecting animals."
