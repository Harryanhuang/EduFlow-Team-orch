# Question: Question Q-12.1-03
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: What is the energy released in respiration used for? List six uses.
**Answer**: ATP from respiration powers: (1) Muscle contraction — ATP binds to myosin. (2) Protein synthesis — peptide bond formation on ribosomes. (3) Active transport — Na⁺/K⁺ pump in neurones. (4) Cell division — mitosis/meiosis. (5) Growth and repair — synthesis of new cell components. (6) Maintaining body temperature — heat from respiration in endotherms. All living cells respire continuously.
**Explanation**: Six uses all consuming ATP. A common error is saying energy is "stored."
