# Question: Question Q-17.2-03
**Difficulty**: Challenge
**Question**: Describe the water cycle.
**Answer**: Water evaporates from oceans, lakes, and soil (and transpires from plants) → water vapour rises and cools → condensation forms clouds → precipitation (rain, snow) returns water to land → water runs off into rivers and oceans or soaks into the ground (infiltration) → cycle repeats. Energy from the sun drives the cycle.
**Explanation**: Evaporation, condensation, precipitation, runoff. A common error is leaving out transpiration.
