# Question: Question Q-19.2-05
**Difficulty**: Challenge
**Question**: How can recycling help to conserve resources and reduce environmental impact?
**Answer**: Recycling reduces the need to extract new raw materials (e.g., mining metals, cutting trees for paper). It saves energy (making products from recycled materials uses less energy). It reduces landfill waste and pollution. It conserves finite resources (e.g., fossil fuels, metals) for future generations.
**Explanation**: Less extraction, saves energy, less waste. A common error is saying recycling "eliminates" waste entirely.
**Tags**: recycling, resources, energy, landfill
