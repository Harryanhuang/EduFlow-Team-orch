# Question: Question Q-14.3-05
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Describe an experiment to investigate phototropism in plant shoots. Include a control.
**Answer**: Three sets of oat/cress seedlings: A — overhead light (control, grows straight up). B — unilateral light from one side (bends toward light). C — shoot tips covered with foil caps, unilateral light (grows straight, no bending). Keep all at same temperature/water for 48h. Conclusion: tip detects light; covered tip prevents auxin redistribution → no bending.
**Explanation**: Three conditions with tip-covered control. A common error is omitting the control.
