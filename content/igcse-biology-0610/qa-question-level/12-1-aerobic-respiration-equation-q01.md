# Question: Question Q-12.1-01
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: Write the word equation and balanced symbol equation for aerobic respiration.
**Answer**: Word: glucose + oxygen → carbon dioxide + water (+ energy released). Symbol: C₆H₁₂O₆ + 6O₂ → 6CO₂ + 6H₂O (+ energy). The energy released is used to phosphorylate ADP + Pᵢ → ATP. Aerobic respiration releases approximately 38 ATP per glucose molecule.
**Explanation**: Glucose + O₂ → CO₂ + H₂O + energy. A common error is writing light energy as the output (that's photosynthesis).
