# Question: Question Q-15.2-02
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: Name the main parts of the male reproductive system and state one function of each.
**Answer**: Testes — produce sperm and testosterone. Scrotum — holds the testes outside the body to keep them cooler than body temperature for sperm production. Sperm duct (vas deferens) — carries sperm from the testes to the urethra. Urethra — tube through which semen and urine pass (not at the same time). Penis — places sperm into the vagina during copulation.
**Explanation**: Functions of testes, scrotum, sperm duct, urethra and penis. A common error is saying the testes store sperm.
