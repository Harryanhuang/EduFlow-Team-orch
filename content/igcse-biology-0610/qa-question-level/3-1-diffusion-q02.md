# Question: Question Q-3.1-02
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: What is diffusion?
**Answer**: Diffusion is the net movement of particles from a region of higher concentration to a region of lower concentration, down a concentration gradient. This happens as a result of the random movement of particles.
**Explanation**: High to low concentration, down gradient. A common error is saying particles "move to where they are needed."
