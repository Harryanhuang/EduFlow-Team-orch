# Question: Question Q-19.2-07
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: How can individuals help to conserve ecosystems?
**Answer**: Individuals can: (1) Reduce, reuse, recycle — reduce waste and resource use. (2) Save energy — use public transport, switch off appliances. (3) Use sustainable products — certified wood, sustainable fish. (4) Support conservation organisations. (5) Plant trees. (6) Reduce water usage. (7) Avoid products made from endangered species.
**Explanation**: Practical individual actions. A common error is saying individuals "can't make a difference."
**Tags**: conservation, individual-actions, reduce, recycle
