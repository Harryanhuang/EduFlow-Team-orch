# Question: Question Q-4.2-05
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Describe how to test for the presence of protein in a food sample.
**Answer**: Add Biuret solution (sodium hydroxide + copper sulfate) to the food sample. If protein is present, the colour changes from blue to purple/violet. A negative result remains blue.
**Explanation**: Biuret → purple. A common error is confusing Biuret with Benedict's test.
