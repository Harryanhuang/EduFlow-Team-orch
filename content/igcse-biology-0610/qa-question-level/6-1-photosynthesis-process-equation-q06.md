# Question: Question Q-6.1-06
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: What are the raw materials and products of photosynthesis?
**Answer**: Raw materials: carbon dioxide (from air, enters through stomata) and water (from soil, absorbed by roots). Products: glucose (used for energy and building molecules) and oxygen (released as a waste product through stomata).
**Explanation**: CO₂ + H₂O → glucose + O₂. A common error is saying sunlight is a raw material (it's the energy source).
