# Question: Question Q-2.3-02
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: What is a tissue? Give two examples.
**Answer**: Cell → tissue → organ → organ system. Cells are the basic unit. A tissue is a group of similar cells working together. An organ is made of different tissues working together. An organ system is a group of organs working together.
**Explanation**: Cell-tissue-organ-system. A common error is reversing tissue and organ.
