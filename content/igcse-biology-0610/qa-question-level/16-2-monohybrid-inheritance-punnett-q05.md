# Question: Question Q-16.2-05
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: How can you determine whether a tall pea plant is homozygous (TT) or heterozygous (Tt)?
**Answer**: Perform a test cross — cross the tall plant with a short (homozygous recessive, tt) plant. If all offspring are tall, the parent was TT. If approximately half are tall and half are short, the parent was Tt. The test cross reveals the unknown genotype.
**Explanation**: Test cross with recessive, observe offspring. A common error is crossing with another tall plant.
