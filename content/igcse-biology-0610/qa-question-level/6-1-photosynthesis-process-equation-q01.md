# Question: Question Q-6.1-01
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: Write the word equation and balanced symbol equation for photosynthesis.
**Answer**: Word: carbon dioxide + water → glucose + oxygen (in the presence of light and chlorophyll). Symbol: 6CO₂ + 6H₂O → C₆H₁₂O₆ + 6O₂. Light energy is absorbed by chlorophyll and converted to chemical energy.
**Explanation**: CO₂ + H₂O → glucose + O₂, balanced. A common error is forgetting the 6s or writing H₂ as a product.
