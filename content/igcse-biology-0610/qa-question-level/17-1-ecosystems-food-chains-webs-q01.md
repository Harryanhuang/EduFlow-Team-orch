# Question: Question Q-17.1-01
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: What is a producer, consumer, and decomposer? Give an example of each.
**Answer**: Producer — makes its own food by photosynthesis (e.g., green plant, algae). Consumer — feeds on other organisms (e.g., rabbit, human). Primary consumer eats producers; secondary eats primary. Decomposer — breaks down dead matter (e.g., bacteria, fungi). All three are needed for nutrient cycling.
**Explanation**: Producer makes food, consumer eats, decomposer breaks down. A common error is saying decomposers are "consumers."
