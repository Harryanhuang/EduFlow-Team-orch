# Question: Question Q-19.2-04
**Difficulty**: Challenge
**Question**: What is sustainable development? Give two examples.
**Answer**: Sustainable development meets the needs of the present without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their needs. Examples: sustainable fishing (quotas to prevent overfishing), sustainable forestry (replanting trees after logging), renewable energy (solar, wind instead of fossil fuels), sustainable agriculture (crop rotation, reducing pesticides).
**Explanation**: Present needs without compromising future. A common error is saying sustainable means "not using resources."
