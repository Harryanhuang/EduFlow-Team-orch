# Topic 8.1 — Cyber Security Threats
## QA Question Level File

**1. [F]** What is the difference between a computer virus and a computer worm?

**2. [F]** State two examples of social engineering attacks.

**3. [F]** What is ransomware and how does it typically infect a computer?

**4. [S]** Explain how a brute force attack works and describe one method to defend against it.

**5. [S]** Describe how SQL injection attacks exploit vulnerabilities in web applications.

**6. [S]** What is a zero-day vulnerability and why is it particularly dangerous?

**7. [C]** Compare three types of malware (virus, trojan, ransomware) in terms of how they spread, their primary purpose, and methods of protection against each.

**8. [C]** A company's employees receive an email appearing to be from their CEO requesting an urgent bank transfer. Analyse this social engineering attack and recommend protection measures for the organisation.

**9. [C]** Evaluate the effectiveness of different protection methods against DoS attacks, considering both technical solutions and organisational policies.
