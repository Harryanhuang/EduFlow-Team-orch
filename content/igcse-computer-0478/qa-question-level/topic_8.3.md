# Topic 8.3 — Ethical Issues in Computing
## QA Question Level File

**1. [F]** State what is meant by an ethical issue in computing and give one example.

**2. [F]** What is meant by the digital divide? Give one example of how it affects people.

**3. [F]** Define what is meant by responsible use of technology.

**4. [S]** Explain two ethical concerns related to the use of artificial intelligence in decision-making processes.

**5. [S]** A company uses automated AI systems to screen job applications. Discuss the ethical implications of this approach.

**6. [S]** Describe two ways in which technology companies can help reduce the digital divide.

**7. [C]** Evaluate the ethical implications of using AI-powered surveillance systems in public spaces, considering both security benefits and privacy concerns.

**8. [C]** Discuss the ethical responsibilities of software developers when creating applications that may be used by children or vulnerable users.

**9. [C]** Automation through AI is predicted to replace many jobs. Evaluate the ethical obligations of companies and governments in managing this transition for workers.
