# Topic 8.4 — Environmental Impact of Computing
## QA Question Level File

**1. [F]** What is meant by e-waste and give one example of an electronic item that contributes to it.

**2. [F]** State two ways data centres consume significant amounts of energy.

**3. [F]** Define what is meant by green computing.

**4. [S]** Describe two methods that can help reduce e-waste from electronic devices.

**5. [S]** Explain two initiatives that data centres can implement to reduce their carbon footprint.

**6. [S]** Discuss why the disposal of old smartphones and tablets is an environmental concern.

**7. [C]** Evaluate the environmental impact of streaming video services versus owning physical media such as DVDs, considering the entire lifecycle.

**8. [C]** Many countries lack proper e-waste recycling facilities. Analyse the global consequences of this situation and suggest possible solutions.

**9. [C]** Some technology companies claim to be carbon neutral. Evaluate the effectiveness of carbon offset schemes as a solution to the environmental impact of computing, considering their limitations.
