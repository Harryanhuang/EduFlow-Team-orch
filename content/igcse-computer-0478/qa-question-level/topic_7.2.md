# Topic 7.2 — Network Protocols
## QA Question Level File

**1. [F]** What does HTTP stand for and what is its primary function?

**2. [F]** State two differences between TCP and UDP protocols.

**3. [F]** What is the purpose of DNS in a network?

**4. [S]** Explain the role of DHCP in assigning IP addresses to devices on a network.

**5. [S]** Describe what happens when a user types a URL into a browser, including at least three protocols involved.

**6. [S]** Port 80 and port 443 are commonly used. Explain what these ports are used for and why they are different.

**7. [C]** Compare TCP/IP four-layer model with the OSI seven-layer model, focusing on how they map to each other and their respective advantages.

**8. [C]** FTP uses two connections (control and data). Analyse why this design choice was made and explain the implications for firewall configuration.

**9. [C]** Video streaming services often use UDP rather than TCP. Evaluate why this is the case and discuss the trade-offs involved.
