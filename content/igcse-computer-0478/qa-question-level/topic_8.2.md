# Topic 8.2 — Data Protection
## QA Question Level File

**1. [F]** What is the purpose of the Data Protection Act 2018 in the UK?

**2. [F]** State two rights that individuals have under GDPR regarding their personal data.

**3. [F]** What is a digital footprint?

**4. [S]** Explain the concept of data minimisation as required by data protection regulations.

**5. [S]** Describe what is meant by the "right to be forgotten" and give an example of when it might be exercised.

**6. [S]** How do cookies affect user privacy and what regulations govern their use?

**7. [C]** Compare the UK Data Protection Act 2018 with GDPR in terms of their key differences and how UK organisations must comply with both.

**8. [C]** A social media company collects extensive user data including location, browsing history, and social connections. Analyse the ethical considerations and data protection implications of this practice.

**9. [C]** Evaluate the balance between an individual's right to privacy and the need for organisations to collect personal data for business purposes. Include consideration of data minimisation principles and user consent.
