A $0.500$ kg object initially at rest experiences $F(t) = k t^2$, with $k = 6.00$ N/s$^2$. The object reaches $16.0$ m/s at time $t$.

Find $t$.

## Options
A) $0.794$ s
B) $2.00$ s
C) $1.59$ s
D) $1.26$ s

## Answer
C

## Explanation
Integrate the force over $[0, t]$ to obtain the impulse:

$$J = \int_0^{t} k\tau^2\, d\tau = k\,\frac{t^3}{3} = \frac{6.00\,t^3}{3} = 2.00\,t^3$$

Apply the impulse-momentum theorem with $v_i = 0$:

$$\Delta p = m\,\Delta v \;\Longrightarrow\; 2.00\,t^3 = (0.500)(16.0) = 8.00$$

$$t^3 = \frac{8.00}{2.00} = 4.00 \;\Longrightarrow\; t = \sqrt[3]{4.00} \approx 1.59\text{ s}.$$
