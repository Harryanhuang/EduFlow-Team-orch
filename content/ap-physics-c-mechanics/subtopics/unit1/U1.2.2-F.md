A car accelerates from rest at $a = 3.0 \text{ m/s}^2$ for $4.0 \text{ s}$. How far does it travel?

## Options
A) $12 \text{ m}$
B) $24 \text{ m}$
C) $48 \text{ m}$
D) $96 \text{ m}$

## Answer
B

## Explanation
$x - x_0 = v_0t + \frac{1}{2}at^2 = 0 + \frac{1}{2}(3.0)(16) = 24 \text{ m}$.
