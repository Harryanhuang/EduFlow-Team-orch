Which of the following is a vector quantity?

## Options
A) Temperature
B) Mass
C) Displacement
D) Energy

## Answer
C

## Explanation
Displacement has both magnitude (e.g., $5 \text{ m}$) and direction (e.g., $30°$ north of east). Temperature, mass, and energy are scalars — they are described by a single number (with units) and have no direction.
