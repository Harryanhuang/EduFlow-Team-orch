A projectile is launched at $30°$ above the horizontal with initial speed $20 \text{ m/s}$. What is its initial horizontal velocity?

## Options
A) $10 \text{ m/s}$
B) $17.3 \text{ m/s}$
C) $20 \text{ m/s}$
D) $15 \text{ m/s}$

## Answer
B

## Explanation
$v_{0x} = v_0\cos\theta = 20\cos 30° = 20 \times 0.866 = 17.3 \text{ m/s}$.
