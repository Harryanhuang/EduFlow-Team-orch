A velocity-time graph shows a triangle with $v = 0$ at $t = 0$, $v = 4.0 \text{ m/s}$ at $t = 2.0 \text{ s}$, and $v = 0$ at $t = 4.0 \text{ s}$. What is the displacement over the $4.0 \text{ s}$ interval?

## Options
A) $0 \text{ m}$
B) $4.0 \text{ m}$
C) $8.0 \text{ m}$
D) $16 \text{ m}$

## Answer
C

## Explanation
Area of the triangle: $\frac{1}{2} \times \text{base} \times \text{height} = \frac{1}{2} \times 4.0 \times 4.0 = 8.0 \text{ m}$. The entire area is above the $t$-axis, so displacement = $8.0 \text{ m}$.
