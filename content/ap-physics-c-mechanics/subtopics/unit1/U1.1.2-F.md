A particle's position is given by $x(t) = 3t^2 - 2t + 5 \text{ m}$. What is its instantaneous velocity at $t = 2 \text{ s}$?

## Options
A) $6 \text{ m/s}$
B) $10 \text{ m/s}$
C) $12 \text{ m/s}$
D) $14 \text{ m/s}$

## Answer
B

## Explanation
$v(t) = dx/dt = 6t - 2$. At $t = 2 \text{ s}$: $v = 6(2) - 2 = 10 \text{ m/s}$.
