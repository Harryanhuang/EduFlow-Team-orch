A particle starts at $x_0 = 4.0 \text{ m}$ with $v_0 = 2.0 \text{ m/s}$ at $t = 0$. Its acceleration is $a(t) = 4t \text{ m/s}^2$. What is its position at $t = 3 \text{ s}$?

## Options
A) $13 \text{ m}$
B) $16 \text{ m}$
C) $19 \text{ m}$
D) $28 \text{ m}$

## Answer
D

## Explanation
$v(t) = v_0 + \int_0^t 4t'\,dt' = 2.0 + 2t^2$. $x(t) = x_0 + \int_0^t (2.0 + 2t'^2)\,dt' = 4.0 + 2.0t + \frac{2}{3}t^3$. At $t = 3 \text{ s}$: $x = 4.0 + 2.0(3) + \frac{2}{3}(27) = 4.0 + 6.0 + 18.0 = 28 \text{ m}$.
