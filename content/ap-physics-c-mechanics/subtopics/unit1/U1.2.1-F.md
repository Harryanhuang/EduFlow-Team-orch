On a position-time graph, a straight line with a negative slope represents motion that is:

## Options
A) Speeding up in the positive direction
B) Slowing down in the positive direction
C) Moving at constant velocity in the negative direction
D) At rest

## Answer
C

## Explanation
A straight line on an $x$-$t$ graph means constant slope = constant velocity. Negative slope means the position is decreasing with time = motion in the negative direction.
