A vector has magnitude $10.0 \text{ m/s}$ and points at $30°$ above the positive $x$-axis. What is its $y$-component?

## Options
A) $5.00 \text{ m/s}$
B) $8.66 \text{ m/s}$
C) $10.0 \text{ m/s}$
D) $7.07 \text{ m/s}$

## Answer
A

## Explanation
$A_y = A\sin\theta = 10.0 \times \sin 30° = 10.0 \times 0.5 = 5.00 \text{ m/s}$. The $y$-component uses $\sin\theta$ for the angle measured from the $x$-axis. The $x$-component would be $A\cos 30° = 8.66 \text{ m/s}$.
