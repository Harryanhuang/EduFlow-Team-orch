A projectile is launched at $60°$ with $v_0 = 30 \text{ m/s}$ on level ground ($g = 10 \text{ m/s}^2$). What is its range?

## Options
A) $45 \text{ m}$
B) $78 \text{ m}$
C) $90 \text{ m}$
D) $120 \text{ m}$

## Answer
B

## Explanation
$R = v_0^2\sin 2\theta/g = 900 \times \sin 120° / 10 = 900 \times 0.866 / 10 = 77.9 \text{ m} \approx 78 \text{ m}$.
