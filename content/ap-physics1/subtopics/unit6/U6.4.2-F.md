A mass on a spring oscillates with angular frequency ω = 4 rad/s and amplitude A = 0.05 m. What is the maximum speed?

## Options
A) 0.05 m/s
B) 0.2 m/s
C) 0.8 m/s
D) 4.0 m/s

## Answer
B

## Explanation
Maximum speed occurs at equilibrium: v_max = ωA = (4)(0.05) = 0.20 m/s. Option A (0.05) is just the amplitude. Option C (0.8) uses ω/A or some other incorrect combination. Option D (4.0) is just ω. The maximum speed is the product of angular frequency and amplitude — larger ω or larger A both increase v_max.
