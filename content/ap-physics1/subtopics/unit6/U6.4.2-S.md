An object oscillates in SHM with ω = 5 rad/s and A = 0.10 m. What is its speed when x = 0.08 m?

## Options
A) 0.15 m/s
B) 0.30 m/s
C) 0.50 m/s
D) 0.60 m/s

## Answer
B

## Explanation
v = ω√(A² - x²) = 5 × √(0.10² - 0.08²) = 5 × √(0.01 - 0.0064) = 5 × √(0.0036) = 5 × 0.06 = 0.30 m/s.

Check: v_max = ωA = 5 × 0.10 = 0.50 m/s. At x = 0.08 (80% of amplitude), the speed is 0.30 m/s (60% of v_max). This makes sense — speed decreases as displacement increases. Option A (0.15) might use √(A - x) instead of √(A² - x²). Option C (0.50) is v_max — wrong position. Option D (0.60) exceeds v_max — impossible.
