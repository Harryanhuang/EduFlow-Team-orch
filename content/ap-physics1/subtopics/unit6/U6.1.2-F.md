An object oscillates with a frequency of 5 Hz. What is its period?

## Options
A) 0.2 s
B) 0.5 s
C) 2.0 s
D) 5.0 s

## Answer
A

## Explanation
The period and frequency are reciprocals: T = 1/f = 1/5 = 0.2 s. The period is the time for one complete oscillation. Option B (0.5) uses f/10 or confuses with something else. Option C (2.0) uses 1/(f/10) or inverts incorrectly. Option D (5.0) is the frequency itself, not the period.
