A book is at rest on a horizontal table. Which forces act on the book?

## Options
A) Gravity and the normal force only.
B) Gravity, the normal force, and a "force of rest."
C) Gravity, the normal force, and the table's weight.
D) Gravity only, because the book is not moving.

## Answer
A

## Explanation
Two forces act on the book: (1) gravitational force (Earth pulls the book down) and (2) normal force (table pushes the book up). There is no "force of rest" — rest is simply zero net force. The table's weight is a separate force acting on the table, not the book. Option A is correct.
