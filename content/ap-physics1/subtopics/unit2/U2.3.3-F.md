A block slides down a 30° incline with μ_k = 0.2. What is its acceleration? (g = 10 m/s², sin 30° = 0.5, cos 30° ≈ 0.87)

## Options
A) 3.3 m/s²
B) 5.0 m/s²
C) 6.7 m/s²
D) 10 m/s²

## Answer
A

## Explanation
Net force along slope: mg sin θ − μ_k mg cos θ = ma. So a = g(sin θ − μ_k cos θ) = 10(0.5 − 0.2 × 0.87) = 10(0.5 − 0.174) = 10(0.326) = 3.26 ≈ 3.3 m/s². Option A is correct.
