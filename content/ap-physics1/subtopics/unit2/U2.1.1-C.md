A 2 kg cart and a 10 kg cart are pushed across a frictionless surface with the same net force for the same time. Which statement is correct?

## Options
A) The 10 kg cart gains more momentum.
B) Both carts gain the same momentum.
C) The 2 kg cart gains more momentum.
D) The 10 kg cart has greater acceleration.

## Answer
B

## Explanation
By the impulse-momentum theorem: Δp = FΔt. Since both carts experience the same force for the same time, they gain the same momentum Δp = FΔt. The 2 kg cart has greater acceleration (a = F/m), but the 10 kg cart compensates with larger mass, giving the same Δp. Option B is correct.
