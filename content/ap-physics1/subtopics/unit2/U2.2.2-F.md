Two blocks (2 kg and 3 kg) connected by a light string are pulled across a frictionless surface by a 10 N horizontal force applied to the 2 kg block. What is the acceleration of the system?

## Options
A) 2.0 m/s²
B) 3.3 m/s²
C) 5.0 m/s²
D) 10 m/s²

## Answer
A

## Explanation
Treating both blocks as one system: F_net = 10 N, m_total = 2 + 3 = 5 kg. a = F/m = 10/5 = 2.0 m/s². Option A is correct.
