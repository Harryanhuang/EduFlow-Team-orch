A 2 kg block sliding at 12 m/s on a horizontal surface (μ_k = 0.4, g = 10 m/s²) comes to a stop. What is the stopping distance?

## Options
A) 12 m
B) 18 m
C) 24 m
D) 36 m

## Answer
B

## Explanation
Deceleration: a = μ_k g = 0.4 × 10 = 4 m/s² (magnitude). Using v² = v₀² − 2aΔx with v = 0: 0 = 144 − 2(4)Δx → Δx = 144/8 = 18 m. Option B is correct.
