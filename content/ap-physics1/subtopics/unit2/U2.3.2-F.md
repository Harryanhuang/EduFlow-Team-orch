A block slides on a horizontal surface with μ_k = 0.3. What is its deceleration? (g = 10 m/s²)

## Options
A) 0.3 m/s²
B) 3.0 m/s²
C) 30 m/s²
D) It depends on the block's mass.

## Answer
B

## Explanation
f_k = μ_k N = μ_k mg. By F = ma: −μ_k mg = ma → a = −μ_k g = −0.3 × 10 = −3.0 m/s². The deceleration is 3.0 m/s². Note that mass cancels out — the deceleration is independent of mass. Option B is correct.
