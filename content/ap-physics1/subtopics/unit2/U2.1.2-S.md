A 6 kg block on a frictionless surface is pushed with a horizontal force of 30 N while a 10 N friction force opposes the motion. What is the acceleration?

## Options
A) 1.67 m/s²
B) 3.33 m/s²
C) 5.0 m/s²
D) 6.7 m/s²

## Answer
B

## Explanation
Net force: F_net = 30 − 10 = 20 N (applied force minus friction). Acceleration: a = F_net/m = 20/6 ≈ 3.33 m/s². Option B is correct. Students sometimes forget to subtract friction or use mg instead of m.
