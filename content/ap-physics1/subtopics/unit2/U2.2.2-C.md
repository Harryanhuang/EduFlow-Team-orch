A 2 kg block on a frictionless 30° incline is connected by a light string over a pulley to a 2 kg block hanging vertically. What is the acceleration? (sin 30° = 0.5, g = 10 m/s²)

## Options
A) 0 m/s²
B) 2.5 m/s²
C) 5.0 m/s²
D) 10 m/s²

## Answer
B

## Explanation
For the block on the incline (along slope): T − mg sin 30° = ma. For the hanging block: mg − T = ma. Adding: mg − mg sin 30° = 2ma → mg(1 − 0.5) = 2ma → 0.5mg = 2ma → a = g/4 = 10/4 = 2.5 m/s². Option B is correct.
