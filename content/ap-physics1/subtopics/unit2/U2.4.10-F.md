Two masses m₁ = 3 kg and m₂ = 5 kg are connected by a light string over a light, frictionless pulley (Atwood machine). What is the acceleration of the system? (g = 10 m/s²)

## Options
A) 2.5 m/s²
B) 5.0 m/s²
C) 7.5 m/s²
D) 10 m/s²

## Answer
A

## Explanation
For the Atwood machine, taking m₂ (heavier) accelerating downward and m₁ upward. For m₂: m₂g - T = m₂a. For m₁: T - m₁g = m₁a. Adding: (m₂ - m₁)g = (m₁ + m₂)a. So a = (m₂ - m₁)g/(m₁ + m₂) = (5-3)×10/(3+5) = 20/8 = 2.5 m/s². The heavier mass accelerates downward, the lighter upward, both with the same acceleration magnitude. The tension can be found: T = m₁(g+a) = 3(10+2.5) = 37.5 N.
