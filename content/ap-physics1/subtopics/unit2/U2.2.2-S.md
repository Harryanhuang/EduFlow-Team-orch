Two blocks (m₁ = 3 kg, m₂ = 6 kg) are connected by a light string on a frictionless horizontal surface. A 18 N horizontal force is applied to m₁. What is the tension in the string?

## Options
A) 6 N
B) 9 N
C) 12 N
D) 18 N

## Answer
C

## Explanation
System: a = F/(m₁ + m₂) = 18/9 = 2 m/s². For m₂ alone: T = m₂a = 6 × 2 = 12 N. Alternatively for m₁: F − T = m₁a → 18 − T = 6 → T = 12 N. Option C is correct. Note: tension ≠ applied force (18 N) because the system accelerates.
