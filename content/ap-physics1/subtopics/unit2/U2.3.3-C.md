At what minimum angle θ will a block begin to slide down a rough incline with μ_s = 0.5?

## Options
A) 15°
B) 27°
C) 30°
D) 45°

## Answer
B

## Explanation
Block begins to slide when mg sin θ = μ_s mg cos θ → tan θ = μ_s = 0.5. So θ = arctan(0.5) ≈ 26.6° ≈ 27°. Option B is correct. Note: tan θ = μ_s, not μ_s = sin θ/cos θ used some other way.
