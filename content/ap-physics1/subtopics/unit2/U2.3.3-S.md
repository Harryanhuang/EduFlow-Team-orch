A 5 kg block is placed on a 30° incline (μ_s = 0.8, μ_k = 0.3, g = 10 m/s²). Does the block slide? (sin 30° = 0.5, cos 30° ≈ 0.87)

## Options
A) Yes; mg sin θ = 25 N exceeds f_s_max ≈ 34.8 N.
B) No; f_s_max ≈ 34.8 N exceeds mg sin θ = 25 N.
C) Yes; mg cos θ = 25 N exceeds μ_s N.
D) No; the friction coefficient is too high for motion.

## Answer
B

## Explanation
Component along slope: mg sin 30° = 5(10)(0.5) = 25 N. Maximum static friction: f_s_max = μ_s mg cos 30° = 0.8(5)(10)(0.87) = 34.8 N. Since f_s_max (34.8 N) > mg sin θ (25 N), the block does NOT slide. Static friction adjusts to equal 25 N. Option B is correct.
