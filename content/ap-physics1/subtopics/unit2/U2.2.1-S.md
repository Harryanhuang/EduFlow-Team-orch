A box slides down a rough incline. Which correctly describes the forces in its FBD (with axes parallel and perpendicular to the incline)?

## Options
A) mg sin θ down the slope, mg cos θ into the slope, normal force perpendicular to slope, friction up the slope.
B) mg down, normal force up, friction up the slope.
C) mg sin θ down the slope, normal force up, friction up the slope.
D) mg down, normal force perpendicular to slope, friction down the slope.

## Answer
A

## Explanation
Four forces: gravity (mg, decomposed into mg sin θ parallel and mg cos θ perpendicular to the slope), normal force (perpendicular to the surface, away from the surface), and kinetic friction (parallel to the surface, opposing the sliding direction — so up the slope). Option A correctly lists all four components. Options B, C, D either miss forces or give incorrect directions.
