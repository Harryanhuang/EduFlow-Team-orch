Two boxes (3 kg and 5 kg) are in contact on a frictionless surface. A 16 N horizontal force pushes the 3 kg box toward the 5 kg box. What is the acceleration of the system?

## Options
A) 1.0 m/s²
B) 2.0 m/s²
C) 3.2 m/s²
D) 5.3 m/s²

## Answer
B

## Explanation
Treating both boxes as one system: F_net = 16 N, total mass = 3 + 5 = 8 kg. By Newton's Second Law: a = F_net/m_total = 16/8 = 2.0 m/s². Both boxes accelerate together at the same rate. Option B is correct.
