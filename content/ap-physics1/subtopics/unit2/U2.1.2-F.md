A net force of 12 N acts on a 4 kg object. What is the acceleration?

## Options
A) 3 m/s²
B) 4 m/s²
C) 12 m/s²
D) 48 m/s²

## Answer
A

## Explanation
By Newton's Second Law: a = F_net/m = 12/4 = 3 m/s². Option A is correct.
