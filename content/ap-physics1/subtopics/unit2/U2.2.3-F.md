A block slides down a frictionless incline of angle θ. What is its acceleration?

## Options
A) g
B) g sin θ
C) g cos θ
D) g tan θ

## Answer
B

## Explanation
The component of gravity along the incline is mg sin θ. By F = ma: mg sin θ = ma → a = g sin θ. The perpendicular component is balanced by the normal force. Option B is correct.
