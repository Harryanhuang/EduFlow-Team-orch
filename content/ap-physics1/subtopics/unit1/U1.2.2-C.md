A boat crosses a river that is $800 \text{ m}$ wide. The boat's speed relative to the water is $4 \text{ m/s}$ perpendicular to the banks. The river flows with a current of $3 \text{ m/s}$ parallel to the banks. How far downstream does the boat land from the point directly opposite its starting point, and what is the magnitude of the boat's velocity relative to the ground?

## Options
A) Downstream distance $= 400 \text{ m}$; Ground speed $= 5 \text{ m/s}$
B) Downstream distance $= 600 \text{ m}$; Ground speed $= 5 \text{ m/s}$
C) Downstream distance $= 600 \text{ m}$; Ground speed $= 7 \text{ m/s}$
D) Downstream distance $= 400 \text{ m}$; Ground speed $= 7 \text{ m/s}$

## Answer
B

## Explanation
船相对水的速度：$v_{bw} = 4 \text{ m/s}$（垂直河岸）。水流速度：$v_{wg} = 3 \text{ m/s}$（平行河岸）。过河时间：$t = 800 / 4 = 200 \text{ s}$。下游漂移距离：$d = v_{wg} \times t = 3 \times 200 = 600 \text{ m}$。船对地速度大小：$v_{bg} = \sqrt{v_{bw}^2 + v_{wg}^2} = \sqrt{16 + 9} = 5 \text{ m/s}$。选项 B 正确。常见错误是将两速度直接相加得到 $7 \text{ m/s}$，或误用速度分量关系导致下游距离计算错误。注意 x 和 y 方向运动独立，过河时间仅由垂直分量决定。
