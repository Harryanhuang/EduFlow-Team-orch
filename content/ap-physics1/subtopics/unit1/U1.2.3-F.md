Car A moves east at $20 \text{ m/s}$. Car B moves east at $15 \text{ m/s}$. What is the velocity of Car A relative to Car B?

## Options
A) $5 \text{ m/s}$ east
B) $35 \text{ m/s}$ east
C) $5 \text{ m/s}$ west
D) $35 \text{ m/s}$ west

## Answer
A

## Explanation
相对速度公式：$v_{A/B} = v_A - v_B$。两车速均向东，$v_A = +20 \text{ m/s}$，$v_B = +15 \text{ m/s}$。$v_{A/B} = 20 - 15 = +5 \text{ m/s}$，即 $5 \text{ m/s}$ 向东。选项 A 正确。常见错误是将速度相加得到 $35 \text{ m/s}$，或减法顺序颠倒得到 $-5 \text{ m/s}$（向西）。
