A particle moves along the $x$-axis. During the first $2 \text{ s}$ its velocity is $+6 \text{ m/s}$. During the next $3 \text{ s}$ its velocity is $-4 \text{ m/s}$. During the final $1 \text{ s}$ its velocity is $+2 \text{ m/s}$. Which of the following statements is true?

## Options
A) The average speed for the entire $6 \text{ s}$ is greater than the magnitude of the average velocity.
B) The average speed for the entire $6 \text{ s}$ equals the magnitude of the average velocity.
C) The average speed for the entire $6 \text{ s}$ is less than the magnitude of the average velocity.
D) The average velocity is zero, so the magnitude of the average velocity is undefined.

## Answer
A

## Explanation
计算各段位移：第一段 $\Delta x_1 = 6 \times 2 = +12 \text{ m}$；第二段 $\Delta x_2 = -4 \times 3 = -12 \text{ m}$；第三段 $\Delta x_3 = 2 \times 1 = +2 \text{ m}$。总位移 $\Delta x = 12 - 12 + 2 = +2 \text{ m}$。总路程 $= |12| + |-12| + |2| = 26 \text{ m}$。总时间 $= 6 \text{ s}$。平均速度大小 $= |2| / 6 = 1/3 \approx 0.33 \text{ m/s}$。平均速率 $= 26 / 6 \approx 4.33 \text{ m/s}$。显然平均速率大于平均速度大小，选项 A 正确。本题陷阱在于第二段位移恰好抵消第一段的大部分，使得总位移很小，但路程很大。常见错误是直接将三段速度算术平均得到平均速度，或忽略负号导致位移计算错误。
