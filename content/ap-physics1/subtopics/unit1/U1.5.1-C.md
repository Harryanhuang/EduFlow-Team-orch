The position-time graph for an object moving along a straight line consists of three segments: (1) from $t = 0$ to $t = 2$ s, a straight line from $x = 0$ to $x = 8$ m; (2) from $t = 2$ s to $t = 4$ s, a horizontal line at $x = 8$ m; (3) from $t = 4$ s to $t = 6$ s, a straight line from $x = 8$ m to $x = 2$ m. What is the average velocity for the entire $6$ s interval and the total distance traveled?

## Options
A) Average velocity $= 1/3 \text{ m/s}$; Distance $= 14 \text{ m}$
B) Average velocity $= -1 \text{ m/s}$; Distance $= 14 \text{ m}$
C) Average velocity $= 1/3 \text{ m/s}$; Distance $= 2 \text{ m}$
D) Average velocity $= -1 \text{ m/s}$; Distance $= 2 \text{ m}$

## Answer
A

## Explanation
总位移：$\Delta x = 2 - 0 = 2 \text{ m}$。平均速度：$v_{\text{avg}} = 2/6 = 1/3 \text{ m/s}$。路程：第一段 $8$ m，第二段 $0$ m，第三段 $|2-8| = 6$ m。总路程 $= 8 + 0 + 6 = 14 \text{ m}$。选项 A 正确。常见错误是将平均速度当作平均速率（$14/6$），或忽略第二段的静止状态。注意平均速度是矢量（位移/时间），而平均速率是标量（路程/时间）。
