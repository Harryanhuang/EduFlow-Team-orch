A projectile is launched from level ground with an initial speed of $25 \text{ m/s}$ at an angle of $53°$ above the horizontal. Using $g = 10 \text{ m/s}^2$, what is the total time of flight and the horizontal range?

## Options
A) $T = 4.0 \text{ s}$; $R = 60 \text{ m}$
B) $T = 5.0 \text{ s}$; $R = 75 \text{ m}$
C) $T = 4.0 \text{ s}$; $R = 75 \text{ m}$
D) $T = 5.0 \text{ s}$; $R = 60 \text{ m}$

## Answer
A

## Explanation
初速度分解：$v_{0x} = 25 \cos 53° = 25 \times 0.6 = 15 \text{ m/s}$；$v_{0y} = 25 \sin 53° = 25 \times 0.8 = 20 \text{ m/s}$。飞行时间：$T = 2v_{0y} / g = 2 \times 20 / 10 = 4.0 \text{ s}$。射程：$R = v_{0x} \times T = 15 \times 4 = 60 \text{ m}$。或用射程公式 $R = v_0^2 \sin(2\theta) / g = 625 \times \sin 106° / 10 = 625 \times 0.96 / 10 = 60 \text{ m}$。选项 A 正确。常见错误是飞行时间只计算上升段 $v_{0y}/g = 2$ s，或射程公式中角度用错。
