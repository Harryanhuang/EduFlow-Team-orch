A train moves east at a constant speed of $20 \text{ m/s}$. A passenger on the train walks west (toward the rear of the train) at $2 \text{ m/s}$ relative to the train. Taking the ground as the reference frame with east as positive, what is the velocity of the passenger?

## Options
A) $+22 \text{ m/s}$
B) $+18 \text{ m/s}$
C) $-18 \text{ m/s}$
D) $-22 \text{ m/s}$

## Answer
B

## Explanation
以地面为参考系，向东为正。火车速度 $v_{\text{train}} = +20 \text{ m/s}$。乘客相对火车向西运动，即相对火车速度为 $-2 \text{ m/s}$。乘客对地速度 $v_{\text{passenger}} = v_{\text{train}} + v_{\text{passenger/ train}} = 20 + (-2) = +18 \text{ m/s}$。选项 B 正确。常见错误是将相对速度直接相加得到 $22 \text{ m/s}$（未注意方向相反），或符号处理混乱得到 $-18 \text{ m/s}$。
