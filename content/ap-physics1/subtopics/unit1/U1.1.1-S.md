A car travels along a straight road. It first moves $40 \text{ m}$ in the $+x$ direction at $8 \text{ m/s}$, then immediately turns around and moves $25 \text{ m}$ in the $-x$ direction at $5 \text{ m/s}$. What is the magnitude of the average velocity for the entire trip and the average speed for the entire trip?

## Options
A) Average velocity $= 1.5 \text{ m/s}$; Average speed $= 6.5 \text{ m/s}$
B) Average velocity $= 6.5 \text{ m/s}$; Average speed $= 1.5 \text{ m/s}$
C) Average velocity $= 1.5 \text{ m/s}$; Average speed $= 6.5 \text{ m/s}$ (both magnitudes)
D) Average velocity $= 6.5 \text{ m/s}$; Average speed $= 1.5 \text{ m/s}$ (both magnitudes)

## Answer
C

## Explanation
总位移 $\Delta x = 40 - 25 = 15 \text{ m}$（$+x$ 方向）。总路程 $= 40 + 25 = 65 \text{ m}$。第一段用时 $t_1 = 40/8 = 5 \text{ s}$，第二段用时 $t_2 = 25/5 = 5 \text{ s}$，总时间 $= 10 \text{ s}$。平均速度大小 $= |\Delta x| / t_{\text{total}} = 15 / 10 = 1.5 \text{ m/s}$。平均速率 $= \text{路程} / t_{\text{total}} = 65 / 10 = 6.5 \text{ m/s}$。选项 C 正确。注意平均速度是矢量，其大小为 $1.5 \text{ m/s}$；平均速率是标量，为 $6.5 \text{ m/s}$。常见错误是将平均速度当作速度的平均值 $(8+5)/2$，或忽略方向导致位移计算错误。
