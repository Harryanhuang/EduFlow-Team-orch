A displacement vector has a magnitude of $10 \text{ m}$ and makes an angle of $30°$ above the positive $x$-axis. What are the $x$ and $y$ components of this vector?

## Options
A) $A_x = 5 \text{ m}$, $A_y = 5\sqrt{3} \text{ m}$
B) $A_x = 5\sqrt{3} \text{ m}$, $A_y = 5 \text{ m}$
C) $A_x = 8.7 \text{ m}$, $A_y = 5.0 \text{ m}$
D) $A_x = 10 \text{ m}$, $A_y = 0 \text{ m}$

## Answer
B

## Explanation
矢量分解公式：$A_x = A \cos\theta$，$A_y = A \sin\theta$。代入 $A = 10 \text{ m}$，$\theta = 30°$：$A_x = 10 \cos 30° = 10 \times (\sqrt{3}/2) = 5\sqrt{3} \text{ m}$；$A_y = 10 \sin 30° = 10 \times (1/2) = 5 \text{ m}$。选项 B 正确。常见错误是将 $\sin$ 和 $\cos$ 混淆，或误记特殊角的三角函数值。
