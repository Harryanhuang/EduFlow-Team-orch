A coordinate system is set up along a straight road with the positive direction defined as east. A car is moving west at a speed of $15 \text{ m/s}$. What is the velocity of the car in this coordinate system?

## Options
A) $+15 \text{ m/s}$
B) $-15 \text{ m/s}$
C) $0 \text{ m/s}$
D) The velocity cannot be determined without knowing the reference frame.

## Answer
B

## Explanation
正方向已规定为向东。汽车向西运动，即沿负方向运动，因此速度为 $-15 \text{ m/s}$。选项 B 正确。常见错误是只写速度大小 $15 \text{ m/s}$ 而忽略方向符号，或认为速度总是正值。
