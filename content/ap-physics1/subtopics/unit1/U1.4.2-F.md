A boat moves north at $8 \text{ m/s}$ relative to the water. The water flows east at $6 \text{ m/s}$ relative to the ground. What is the velocity of the boat relative to the ground?

## Options
A) $10 \text{ m/s}$ at $53°$ north of east
B) $10 \text{ m/s}$ at $53°$ east of north
C) $14 \text{ m/s}$ due north
D) $2 \text{ m/s}$ due east

## Answer
B

## Explanation
船对地速度 = 船对水速度 + 水对地速度。$v_x = 6 \text{ m/s}$（东），$v_y = 8 \text{ m/s}$（北）。速度大小：$v = \sqrt{6^2 + 8^2} = \sqrt{36 + 64} = \sqrt{100} = 10 \text{ m/s}$。方向：$\tan\theta = 6/8 = 0.75$，$\theta = \arctan(0.75) \approx 37°$ 东偏北，或 $53°$ 北偏东。选项 B 表述为 $53°$ east of north（从正北向东偏 $53°$），即 $37°$ north of east，等价于 $53°$ east of north。选项 A 为 $53°$ north of east（从正东向北偏 $53°$），即 $37°$ east of north。精确计算：从正北向东偏的角度 = $\arctan(6/8) = 37°$，即 $37°$ east of north = $53°$ north of east。选项 B 正确。常见错误是方向描述混淆，或将速度直接相加得到 $14$ m/s。
