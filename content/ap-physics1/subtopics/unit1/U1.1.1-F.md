A student walks 3 m east, then 4 m north, then 3 m west, and finally 4 m south. Which statement correctly describes the displacement and the total distance traveled?

## Options
A) The displacement is 14 m and the distance is 0 m.
B) The displacement is 0 m and the distance is 14 m.
C) The displacement is 10 m and the distance is 10 m.
D) The displacement is 7 m and the distance is 7 m.

## Answer
B

## Explanation
位移是初末位置的有向线段，与路径无关。该学生先向东 3 m，再向西 3 m，水平位移为 0；先向北 4 m，再向南 4 m，竖直位移也为 0。因此总位移为 0 m。路程是路径总长，即 $3 + 4 + 3 + 4 = 14$ m。选项 B 正确。常见错误是将路程与位移混淆，或认为往返运动后位移等于某一段的剩余距离。
