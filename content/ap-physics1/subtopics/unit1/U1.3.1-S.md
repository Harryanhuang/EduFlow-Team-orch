A ball is thrown from the top of a building with an initial horizontal velocity of $15 \text{ m/s}$. It takes $2 \text{ s}$ to reach the ground. Assuming $g = 10 \text{ m/s}^2$ and air resistance is negligible, what is the horizontal distance traveled by the ball and its vertical velocity just before hitting the ground?

## Options
A) Horizontal distance $= 30 \text{ m}$; Vertical velocity $= -20 \text{ m/s}$
B) Horizontal distance $= 15 \text{ m}$; Vertical velocity $= -10 \text{ m/s}$
C) Horizontal distance $= 30 \text{ m}$; Vertical velocity $= -20 \text{ m/s}$ (downward)
D) Horizontal distance $= 20 \text{ m}$; Vertical velocity $= -30 \text{ m/s}$

## Answer
A

## Explanation
水平方向匀速运动：$x = v_{0x} t = 15 \times 2 = 30 \text{ m}$。竖直方向自由落体：$v_y = v_{0y} - gt = 0 - 10 \times 2 = -20 \text{ m/s}$。负号表示向下。选项 A 正确。常见错误是认为水平方向有减速而距离减小，或竖直方向初速度不为零导致计算错误。注意题目明确给出 $g = 10 \text{ m/s}^2$。
