A swimmer can swim at $1.5 \text{ m/s}$ in still water. She wants to swim directly across a river that flows at $1.0 \text{ m/s}$. At what angle upstream must she aim, and what is her speed relative to the ground?

## Options
A) $42°$ upstream; Speed $= 1.1 \text{ m/s}$
B) $42°$ upstream; Speed $= 1.5 \text{ m/s}$
C) $48°$ upstream; Speed $= 1.1 \text{ m/s}$
D) $48°$ upstream; Speed $= 1.8 \text{ m/s}$

## Answer
A

## Explanation
要直接横渡， swimmer 对地速度的下游分量必须为零。设 swimmer 对水速度为 $v_{sw} = 1.5 \text{ m/s}$，方向与垂直河岸方向夹角为 $\theta$（指向上游）。则 $v_{sw} \sin\theta = v_{wg} = 1.0$（抵消水流）。$\sin\theta = 1.0 / 1.5 = 2/3$，$\theta = \arcsin(2/3) \approx 41.8° \approx 42°$。 swimmer 对地速度大小：$v_{sg} = v_{sw} \cos\theta = 1.5 \times \cos 42° = 1.5 \times 0.745 = 1.12 \text{ m/s}$。或用勾股定理：$v_{sg} = \sqrt{1.5^2 - 1.0^2} = \sqrt{2.25 - 1.0} = \sqrt{1.25} \approx 1.12 \text{ m/s}$。选项 A 正确。常见错误是角度计算错误（用 $\tan$ 而非 $\sin$），或忽略水流影响认为对地速度仍为 $1.5 \text{ m/s}$。
