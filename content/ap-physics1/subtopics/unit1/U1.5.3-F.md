The acceleration-time graph for an object is a horizontal line at $a = 2 \text{ m/s}^2$ for $t = 0$ to $t = 5$ s. If the object starts from rest, what is its velocity at $t = 5$ s?

## Options
A) $2 \text{ m/s}$
B) $5 \text{ m/s}$
C) $10 \text{ m/s}$
D) $25 \text{ m/s}$

## Answer
C

## Explanation
a-t 图像的面积表示速度变化量。矩形面积 = $2 \times 5 = 10 \text{ m/s}$。由于初速度为零，末速度 = $10 \text{ m/s}$。选项 C 正确。常见错误是将加速度值直接当作速度，或错误计算面积。
