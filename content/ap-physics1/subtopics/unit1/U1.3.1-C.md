Two projectiles A and B are launched from the same height above level ground. Projectile A is launched with velocity $v_0$ at angle $\theta$ above the horizontal. Projectile B is launched with the same speed $v_0$ but at angle $\theta$ below the horizontal. Both projectiles land on the ground. Which statement is true about their flight times and horizontal ranges?

## Options
A) A and B have the same flight time and the same range.
B) A has a longer flight time and a longer range than B.
C) A has a longer flight time but the same range as B.
D) A has a longer flight time but a shorter range than B.

## Answer
B

## Explanation
飞行时间分析：Projectile A 有向上的初速度分量 $v_0 \sin\theta$，先上升再下降，飞行时间较长；Projectile B 有向下的初速度分量 $v_0 \sin\theta$（负值），直接向下运动，飞行时间较短。射程分析：水平速度分量相同 $v_0 \cos\theta$，但 A 的飞行时间更长，因此 A 的射程更大。选项 B 正确。常见错误是认为对称性导致射程相同（这只适用于从地面发射并落回地面的情况），或忽略初始高度相同但发射方向不同对飞行时间的影响。注意本题从同一高度（高于地面）发射，而非从地面发射。
