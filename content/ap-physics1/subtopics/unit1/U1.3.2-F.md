A projectile is launched from level ground with an initial speed $v_0$ at an angle of $45°$ above the horizontal. Which expression gives the maximum height reached by the projectile? (Use $g$ for the acceleration due to gravity.)

## Options
A) $v_0^2 / (2g)$
B) $v_0^2 / (4g)$
C) $v_0^2 / g$
D) $v_0^2 \sin^2 45° / (2g)$

## Answer
D

## Explanation
最大高度公式：$H = v_{0y}^2 / (2g) = (v_0 \sin\theta)^2 / (2g) = v_0^2 \sin^2\theta / (2g)$。当 $\theta = 45°$ 时，$\sin 45° = \sqrt{2}/2$，$\sin^2 45° = 1/2$。因此 $H = v_0^2 / (4g)$。选项 D 正确，因为 D 是通用公式，代入 $45°$ 后等于 $v_0^2 / (4g)$。选项 B 虽然数值正确，但 D 是更通用的正确表达式。常见错误是混淆最大高度公式与射程公式，或忘记 $\sin^2 45° = 1/2$ 而选 A。
