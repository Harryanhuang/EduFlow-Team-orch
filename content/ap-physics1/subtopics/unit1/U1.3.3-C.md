A ball is thrown from level ground with initial speed $v_0$ at angle $\theta$ above the horizontal. At a certain point P on the trajectory, the ball's velocity makes an angle of $\theta/2$ with the horizontal. If the speed at point P is $v_P$, which relationship is correct?

## Options
A) $v_P = v_0 \cos(\theta/2)$
B) $v_P = v_0 \cos\theta / \cos(\theta/2)$
C) $v_P = v_0 \cos\theta$
D) $v_P = v_0 \sin\theta / \sin(\theta/2)$

## Answer
B

## Explanation
抛体运动水平速度守恒：$v_x = v_0 \cos\theta$。在点 P，速度方向与水平夹角为 $\theta/2$，因此 $v_x = v_P \cos(\theta/2)$。联立得 $v_P \cos(\theta/2) = v_0 \cos\theta$，即 $v_P = v_0 \cos\theta / \cos(\theta/2)$。选项 B 正确。常见错误是认为速度大小不变（$v_P = v_0$），或错误使用竖直分量关系。本题需要理解水平速度守恒和速度方向与分量的几何关系，属于多步推理题。
