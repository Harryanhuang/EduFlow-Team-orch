The position-time graph for two objects A and B moving along the same straight line is shown. At time $t = 0$, both objects are at the origin. Object A's graph is a straight line with slope $2 \text{ m/s}$. Object B's graph is a parabola opening upward with vertex at $t = 0$ and passing through the point $(4 \text{ s}, 16 \text{ m})$. At what time do the two objects have the same velocity?

## Options
A) $t = 1 \text{ s}$
B) $t = 2 \text{ s}$
C) $t = 3 \text{ s}$
D) $t = 4 \text{ s}$

## Answer
B

## Explanation
Object A 的速度恒定：$v_A = 2 \text{ m/s}$。Object B 的位置函数：抛物线顶点在原点，过 $(4, 16)$，设 $x_B = at^2$。代入得 $16 = a(16)$，$a = 1 \text{ m/s}^2$。因此 $x_B = t^2$。B 的速度：$v_B = dx_B/dt = 2t$。令 $v_A = v_B$：$2 = 2t$，$t = 1 \text{ s}$。选项 A 正确。常见错误是求位置相同的时间（$2t = t^2$，$t = 2$ s），或混淆速度与位置的概念。注意题目问的是速度相同，而非位置相同。
