Rain is falling vertically at $8 \text{ m/s}$ relative to the ground. A car moves horizontally at $6 \text{ m/s}$. At what angle from the vertical do the raindrops appear to fall to a passenger in the car, and what is the apparent speed of the raindrops relative to the car?

## Options
A) $37°$ from vertical; Apparent speed $= 10 \text{ m/s}$
B) $37°$ from vertical; Apparent speed $= 14 \text{ m/s}$
C) $53°$ from vertical; Apparent speed $= 10 \text{ m/s}$
D) $53°$ from vertical; Apparent speed $= 14 \text{ m/s}$

## Answer
A

## Explanation
以地面为参考系：雨滴速度 $\vec{v}_r = (0, -8) \text{ m/s}$（向下为负 y）。汽车速度 $\vec{v}_c = (6, 0) \text{ m/s}$。雨滴相对汽车的速度：$\vec{v}_{r/c} = \vec{v}_r - \vec{v}_c = (0-6, -8-0) = (-6, -8) \text{ m/s}$。相对速度大小：$v_{r/c} = \sqrt{(-6)^2 + (-8)^2} = \sqrt{36 + 64} = \sqrt{100} = 10 \text{ m/s}$。角度：$\tan\theta = |v_x / v_y| = 6/8 = 0.75$，$\theta = \arctan(0.75) \approx 37°$。即雨滴看起来从正前方倾斜落下，与竖直方向夹角 $37°$。选项 A 正确。常见错误是将两速度直接相加得到 $14 \text{ m/s}$，或角度计算错误（用 $\tan^{-1}(8/6)$ 得到 $53°$）。注意相对速度是矢量减法，且角度是相对于竖直方向而非水平方向。
