Ball A is thrown straight upward with an initial velocity of $+12 \text{ m/s}$ (taking upward as positive). Ball B is thrown straight downward from the same height with an initial velocity of $-8 \text{ m/s}$. Both balls are in free fall with acceleration $a = -9.8 \text{ m/s}^2$. At the instant when Ball A reaches its maximum height, which statement about Ball B is correct?

## Options
A) Ball B is still moving downward and has a speed greater than $8 \text{ m/s}$.
B) Ball B has momentarily stopped.
C) Ball B is moving upward.
D) Ball B is moving downward with a speed of exactly $8 \text{ m/s}$.

## Answer
A

## Explanation
Ball A 到达最高点时，其竖直速度减为 0。由 $v = v_0 + at$，对 Ball A：$0 = 12 - 9.8t$，解得 $t = 12 / 9.8 \approx 1.22 \text{ s}$。对 Ball B：初速度 $v_0 = -8 \text{ m/s}$，加速度 $a = -9.8 \text{ m/s}^2$。经过相同时间 $t = 1.22 \text{ s}$，Ball B 的速度 $v_B = -8 + (-9.8)(1.22) = -8 - 12 = -20 \text{ m/s}$（近似值）。负号表示向下运动，速度大小约为 $20 \text{ m/s}$，明显大于 $8 \text{ m/s}$。选项 A 正确。常见错误是认为 Ball B 速度保持不变（忽略重力加速度），或计算时间错误导致结论偏差。
