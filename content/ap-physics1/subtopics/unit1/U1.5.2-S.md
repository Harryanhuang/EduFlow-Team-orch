The velocity-time graph for an object moving along a straight line consists of two segments: (1) from $t = 0$ to $t = 3$ s, velocity increases linearly from $0$ to $6 \text{ m/s}$; (2) from $t = 3$ s to $t = 5$ s, velocity decreases linearly from $6 \text{ m/s}$ to $0$. What is the total displacement and the total distance traveled during the $5$ s interval?

## Options
A) Displacement $= 15 \text{ m}$; Distance $= 15 \text{ m}$
B) Displacement $= 12 \text{ m}$; Distance $= 12 \text{ m}$
C) Displacement $= 15 \text{ m}$; Distance $= 18 \text{ m}$
D) Displacement $= 18 \text{ m}$; Distance $= 15 \text{ m}$

## Answer
A

## Explanation
v-t 图像是一个三角形，底 $5$ s，高 $6$ m/s。面积 = $\frac{1}{2} \times 5 \times 6 = 15 \text{ m}$。由于速度始终为正，位移 = 路程 = $15 \text{ m}$。选项 A 正确。常见错误是将两段分别计算但错误求和，或混淆位移与路程。注意加速度先正后负，但速度始终为正，物体始终向同一方向运动。
