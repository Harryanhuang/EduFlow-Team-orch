Three objects P, Q, and R move along a straight line. Their positions as functions of time are:
- $x_P(t) = 2t$
- $x_Q(t) = 8 - 2t$
- $x_R(t) = 4 + t$

At what time (if any) are all three objects at the same position simultaneously?

## Options
A) $t = 2 \text{ s}$
B) $t = 4 \text{ s}$
C) There is no time when all three are at the same position.
D) $t = 1 \text{ s}$ and $t = 3 \text{ s}$

## Answer
C

## Explanation
先求两两相遇的时间。P 与 Q 相遇：$2t = 8 - 2t$，$4t = 8$，$t = 2 \text{ s}$，此时位置 $x = 4 \text{ m}$。P 与 R 相遇：$2t = 4 + t$，$t = 4 \text{ s}$，此时位置 $x = 8 \text{ m}$。Q 与 R 相遇：$8 - 2t = 4 + t$，$3t = 4$，$t = 4/3 \text{ s}$，此时位置 $x = 16/3 \text{ m}$。三个相遇时间各不相同（$2 \text{ s}$、$4 \text{ s}$、$4/3 \text{ s}$），因此不存在任何时刻三个物体同时位于同一位置。选项 C 正确。常见错误是只计算其中两个物体的相遇时间就假设第三个也同时在场，或未验证第三个物体在相遇时刻的位置。
