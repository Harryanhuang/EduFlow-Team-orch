Two cars A and B are moving on a straight highway. Car A moves at $30 \text{ m/s}$ east. Car B moves at $20 \text{ m/s}$ west. What is the velocity of Car A as measured by a passenger in Car B?

## Options
A) $10 \text{ m/s}$ east
B) $50 \text{ m/s}$ east
C) $10 \text{ m/s}$ west
D) $50 \text{ m/s}$ west

## Answer
B

## Explanation
以地面为参考系，向东为正：$v_A = +30 \text{ m/s}$，$v_B = -20 \text{ m/s}$。Car A 相对 Car B 的速度：$v_{A/B} = v_A - v_B = 30 - (-20) = +50 \text{ m/s}$。即 $50 \text{ m/s}$ 向东。选项 B 正确。常见错误是减法顺序颠倒得到 $-50$ m/s（向西），或将两速度大小相加但方向错误。注意相对速度公式 $v_{A/B} = v_A - v_B$ 中的减法是矢量减法。
