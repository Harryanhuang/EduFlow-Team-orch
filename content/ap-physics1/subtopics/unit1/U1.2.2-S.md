A particle moves in a plane with velocity components $v_x = 4 \text{ m/s}$ and $v_y = -3 \text{ m/s}$. What is the speed of the particle and the angle its velocity vector makes with the positive x-axis?

## Options
A) Speed $= 5 \text{ m/s}$; Angle $= 36.9°$ below the positive x-axis
B) Speed $= 7 \text{ m/s}$; Angle $= 36.9°$ above the positive x-axis
C) Speed $= 5 \text{ m/s}$; Angle $= 53.1°$ below the positive x-axis
D) Speed $= 5 \text{ m/s}$; Angle $= 36.9°$ above the positive x-axis

## Answer
A

## Explanation
速度大小（速率）：$v = \sqrt{v_x^2 + v_y^2} = \sqrt{4^2 + (-3)^2} = \sqrt{16 + 9} = \sqrt{25} = 5 \text{ m/s}$。角度：$\tan\theta = |v_y / v_x| = 3/4 = 0.75$，$\theta = \arctan(0.75) \approx 36.9°$。由于 $v_x > 0$ 且 $v_y < 0$，速度矢量指向第四象限，即正 x 轴下方 $36.9°$。选项 A 正确。常见错误是忽略 $v_y$ 的负号而认为角度在 x 轴上方，或将速度大小误算为 $4 + 3 = 7 \text{ m/s}$。
