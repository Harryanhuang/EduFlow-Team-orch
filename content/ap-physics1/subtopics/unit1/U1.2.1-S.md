Two displacement vectors $\vec{A}$ and $\vec{B}$ have magnitudes $A = 6 \text{ m}$ and $B = 8 \text{ m}$. Vector $\vec{A}$ points $30°$ north of east, and vector $\vec{B}$ points $60°$ north of west. What is the magnitude of the resultant displacement $\vec{R} = \vec{A} + \vec{B}$?

## Options
A) $10 \text{ m}$
B) $14 \text{ m}$
C) $2 \text{ m}$
D) $7.2 \text{ m}$

## Answer
A

## Explanation
建立坐标系：东为 $+x$，北为 $+y$。$\vec{A}$：$A_x = 6 \cos 30° = 6 \times (\sqrt{3}/2) = 3\sqrt{3} \approx 5.20 \text{ m}$，$A_y = 6 \sin 30° = 3 \text{ m}$。$\vec{B}$ 指向西北偏北 $60°$，即与正西方向夹角 $60°$ 向北，或与正北方向夹角 $30°$ 向西。$B_x = -8 \sin 60° = -8 \times (\sqrt{3}/2) = -4\sqrt{3} \approx -6.93 \text{ m}$，$B_y = 8 \cos 60° = 4 \text{ m}$。合分量：$R_x = 3\sqrt{3} - 4\sqrt{3} = -\sqrt{3} \approx -1.73 \text{ m}$，$R_y = 3 + 4 = 7 \text{ m}$。合矢量大小：$R = \sqrt{R_x^2 + R_y^2} = \sqrt{3 + 49} = \sqrt{52} \approx 7.2 \text{ m}$。选项 D 正确。常见错误是直接将两矢量大小相加 $6+8=14$，或错误分解角度导致分量计算错误。注意：如果题目问的是 $\vec{A}$ 和 $\vec{B}$ 垂直的情况，则 $R = 10 \text{ m}$，但本题角度不是 $90°$ 关系。
