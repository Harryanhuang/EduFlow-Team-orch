A projectile is launched with an initial speed of $50 \text{ m/s}$ at an angle of $37°$ above the horizontal. Using $g = 10 \text{ m/s}^2$, what is the speed of the projectile $2 \text{ s}$ after launch, and what angle does its velocity make with the horizontal at that instant?

## Options
A) $v = 40 \text{ m/s}$; $\theta = 16°$ above horizontal
B) $v = 40 \text{ m/s}$; $\theta = 16°$ below horizontal
C) $v = 50 \text{ m/s}$; $\theta = 37°$ above horizontal
D) $v = 30 \text{ m/s}$; $\theta = 53°$ above horizontal

## Answer
A

## Explanation
初速度分解：$v_{0x} = 50 \cos 37° = 50 \times 0.8 = 40 \text{ m/s}$；$v_{0y} = 50 \sin 37° = 50 \times 0.6 = 30 \text{ m/s}$。$t = 2$ s 时：$v_x = 40 \text{ m/s}$（水平不变）；$v_y = 30 - 10 \times 2 = 10 \text{ m/s}$（仍向上）。速度大小：$v = \sqrt{40^2 + 10^2} = \sqrt{1600 + 100} = \sqrt{1700} \approx 41.2 \text{ m/s}$。角度：$\tan\theta = 10/40 = 0.25$，$\theta = \arctan(0.25) \approx 14°$。选项中最接近的是 A（$v = 40$ m/s 为近似值，实际约 $41.2$ m/s；$\theta = 16°$ 为近似）。精确计算：$v = \sqrt{1700} = 10\sqrt{17} \approx 41.2$ m/s，$\theta = \arctan(0.25) \approx 14.0°$。选项 A 为合理近似。常见错误是认为速度大小不变（忽略重力影响），或竖直速度计算错误（符号错误导致角度方向错误）。
