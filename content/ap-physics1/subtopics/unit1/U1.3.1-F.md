A projectile is launched with an initial velocity of $20 \text{ m/s}$ at an angle of $30°$ above the horizontal. What are the horizontal and vertical components of the initial velocity?

## Options
A) $v_{0x} = 10 \text{ m/s}$; $v_{0y} = 10\sqrt{3} \text{ m/s}$
B) $v_{0x} = 10\sqrt{3} \text{ m/s}$; $v_{0y} = 10 \text{ m/s}$
C) $v_{0x} = 20 \text{ m/s}$; $v_{0y} = 0 \text{ m/s}$
D) $v_{0x} = 10 \text{ m/s}$; $v_{0y} = 10 \text{ m/s}$

## Answer
B

## Explanation
抛体运动初速度分解：$v_{0x} = v_0 \cos\theta = 20 \cos 30° = 20 \times (\sqrt{3}/2) = 10\sqrt{3} \text{ m/s}$；$v_{0y} = v_0 \sin\theta = 20 \sin 30° = 20 \times (1/2) = 10 \text{ m/s}$。选项 B 正确。常见错误是将 $\sin$ 和 $\cos$ 混淆，或认为水平分量等于初速度大小。
