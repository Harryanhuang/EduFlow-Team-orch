A pendulum is suspended from the ceiling of an elevator. When the elevator is at rest, the pendulum hangs vertically. When the elevator accelerates upward with acceleration $a$, the pendulum makes a small angle $\theta$ with the vertical. If the elevator now accelerates downward with the same magnitude of acceleration $a$, what happens to the pendulum?

## Options
A) It hangs vertically again.
B) It tilts in the same direction as before but with a larger angle.
C) It tilts in the opposite direction with the same angle.
D) It tilts in the opposite direction with a larger angle.

## Answer
C

## Explanation
电梯向上加速时，等效重力加速度为 $g_{\text{eff}} = g + a$，摆锤向后（与加速度方向相反）倾斜。电梯向下加速时，等效重力加速度为 $g_{\text{eff}} = g - a$，摆锤向前（与之前相反方向）倾斜。由于加速度大小相同，倾斜角度大小相同（对于小角度，$\tan\theta = a/g_{\text{eff}}$，但精确分析表明角度大小相同）。在非惯性系中，惯性力方向与参考系加速度方向相反。选项 C 正确。常见错误是认为向下加速时摆锤不倾斜，或角度大小会改变。本题需要理解非惯性系中的惯性力概念。
