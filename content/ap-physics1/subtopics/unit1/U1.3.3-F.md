At the highest point of a projectile's trajectory (launched from level ground and landing on level ground), which statement about the velocity is correct?

## Options
A) The velocity is zero.
B) The horizontal component of velocity is zero.
C) The vertical component of velocity is zero.
D) Both the horizontal and vertical components of velocity are zero.

## Answer
C

## Explanation
抛体运动最高点时，竖直速度分量减为零（$v_y = 0$），但水平速度分量保持不变（$v_x = v_0 \cos\theta$），因此总速度不为零。选项 C 正确。常见错误是认为最高点速度为零（如竖直上抛的顶点），但斜抛运动在最高点仍有水平速度。
