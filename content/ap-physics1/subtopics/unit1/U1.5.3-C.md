The position-time graph for an object is a parabola opening downward with vertex at $t = 2$ s, $x = 12$ m, and passing through $(0, 8)$ and $(4, 8)$. Which of the following correctly describes the velocity-time and acceleration-time graphs for this motion?

## Options
A) v-t: straight line with negative slope; a-t: horizontal line at negative value.
B) v-t: straight line with positive slope; a-t: horizontal line at positive value.
C) v-t: straight line, zero at $t = 2$ s; a-t: horizontal line at negative value.
D) v-t: straight line, zero at $t = 2$ s; a-t: horizontal line at positive value.

## Answer
C

## Explanation
x-t 图像是开口向下的抛物线，顶点在 $t = 2$ s，说明该时刻速度为零（斜率为零）。抛物线开口向下，说明二次项系数为负，即加速度为负。v-t 图像应为直线（因为 x-t 是抛物线，其导数为直线），斜率为负（加速度为负），且在 $t = 2$ s 时为零。a-t 图像应为水平线（加速度恒定），值为负。选项 C 正确。常见错误是混淆开口方向与加速度符号（开口向下对应负加速度），或认为 v-t 图像也是抛物线。本题需要理解 x-t → v-t → a-t 的逐次求导关系。
