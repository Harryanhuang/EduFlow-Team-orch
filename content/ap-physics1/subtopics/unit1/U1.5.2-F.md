The velocity-time graph for an object is a horizontal line at $v = 5 \text{ m/s}$ for $t = 0$ to $t = 4 \text{ s}$. What is the displacement of the object during this time interval?

## Options
A) $5 \text{ m}$
B) $10 \text{ m}$
C) $20 \text{ m}$
D) $1.25 \text{ m}$

## Answer
C

## Explanation
v-t 图像的面积表示位移。矩形面积 = 底 × 高 = $4 \times 5 = 20 \text{ m}$。选项 C 正确。常见错误是将速度值直接当作位移，或错误计算面积。
