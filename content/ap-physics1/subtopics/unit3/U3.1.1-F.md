A turntable rotates at a constant rate of 45 rpm (revolutions per minute). A coin is placed 0.10 m from the center. What is the linear speed of the coin?

## Options
A) 0.47 m/s
B) 4.7 m/s
C) 4.5 m/s
D) 0.14 m/s

## Answer
A

## Explanation
First convert: 45 rpm = 45/60 rev/s = 0.75 rev/s. Frequency f = 0.75 Hz. The linear speed v = 2πrf = 2π × 0.10 × 0.75 = 0.47 m/s. Option B forgets to convert rpm to rev/s (uses 45 directly). Option C confuses rpm with linear speed. Option D uses v = rf without the 2π factor. Key: always convert angular units properly, and remember that v = ωr = 2πrf.
