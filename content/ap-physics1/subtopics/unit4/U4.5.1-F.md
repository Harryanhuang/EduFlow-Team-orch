A person pulls a wagon with a force of 50 N directed at 30° above the horizontal. The wagon moves 10 m horizontally. How much work does the pulling force do?

## Options
A) 250 J
B) 433 J
C) 500 J
D) 100 J

## Answer
B

## Explanation
W = Fd cosθ = 50 × 10 × cos30° = 500 × 0.866 = 433 J. Only the horizontal component of the force (F cos30° = 43.3 N) does work because the displacement is horizontal. The vertical component (F sin30° = 25 N) does no work — it's perpendicular to the displacement. Option A (250 J) uses Fd sin30° instead of cos30°. Option C (500 J) ignores the angle entirely. Option D (100 J) uses some incorrect combination.
