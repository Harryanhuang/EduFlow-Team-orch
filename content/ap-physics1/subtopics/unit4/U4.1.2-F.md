A pendulum swings from its highest point to its lowest point. In the energy bar chart (LOL diagram), which correctly describes the change?

## Options
A) KE bars stay the same; PE bars decrease.
B) KE bars increase; PE bars decrease; total height stays the same.
C) KE bars increase; PE bars decrease; total height increases.
D) Both KE and PE bars increase.

## Answer
B

## Explanation
At the highest point: KE = 0 (pendulum momentarily stops), PE = maximum. At the lowest point: KE = maximum, PE = minimum (reference level). The total energy (sum of all bars) stays constant in a closed system. KE bars increase while PE bars decrease by the same amount. Option A is wrong — KE changes. Option C violates energy conservation (total energy can't increase). Option D violates PE decreasing as height drops.
