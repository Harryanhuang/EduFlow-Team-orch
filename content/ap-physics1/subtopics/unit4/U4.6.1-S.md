A car drives at constant speed 25 m/s on a level road. The total resistive force is 800 N. What is the engine's power output?

## Options
A) 0 W (constant speed means no acceleration)
B) 800 W
C) 32 W
D) 20,000 W

## Answer
D

## Explanation
At constant speed, the net force is zero, so the engine force equals the resistive force: F_engine = 800 N. Power: P = Fv = 800 × 25 = 20,000 W = 20 kW. Option A (0 W) incorrectly assumes no force means no power — the engine IS applying force, but it exactly balances drag. Option B (800 W) = F without multiplying by v. Option C (32 W) = F/v (inverted). The key insight: constant speed requires continuous power output to overcome resistive forces, even though there is no acceleration.
