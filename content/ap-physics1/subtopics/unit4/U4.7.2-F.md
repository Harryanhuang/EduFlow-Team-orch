A 1.0 kg cart moving at 4.0 m/s collides with a stationary 1.0 kg cart. They stick together. What is their common speed after the collision?

## Options
A) 0 m/s
B) 2.0 m/s
C) 4.0 m/s
D) 8.0 m/s

## Answer
B

## Explanation
Perfectly inelastic collision (stick together). Momentum conservation: m₁v₁ = (m₁+m₂)v_f. 1.0 × 4.0 = (1.0+1.0)v_f. v_f = 4.0/2.0 = 2.0 m/s. Equal masses → final speed is half the initial speed. Half the initial KE is lost: KE_i = ½(1)(16) = 8 J; KE_f = ½(2)(4) = 4 J; loss = 50%. Option A assumes they stop. Option C ignores the collision. Option D doubles instead of halving.
