A 2.0 kg book is on a shelf 1.5 m above the floor. If the reference level is chosen as the floor, what is the gravitational potential energy of the book-Earth system? (g = 10 m/s²)

## Options
A) 3.0 J
B) 15 J
C) 30 J
D) 0 J

## Answer
C

## Explanation
PE = mgh = 2.0 × 10 × 1.5 = 30 J. The reference level is the floor (h = 0), so the book at h = 1.5 m has PE = 30 J relative to this reference. Option A (3.0 J) = mg (weight, not energy). Option B (15 J) = mgh with g = 5 or h = 0.75 (wrong arithmetic). Option D (0 J) would be correct if the reference were at the shelf level. The key: PE depends on the chosen reference level, but ΔPE between two positions does not.
