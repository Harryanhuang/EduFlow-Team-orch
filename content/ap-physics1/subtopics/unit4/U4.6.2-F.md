An electric motor consumes 500 W of electrical power and delivers 350 W of mechanical power. What is the motor's efficiency?

## Options
A) 30%
B) 43%
C) 70%
D) 143%

## Answer
C

## Explanation
Efficiency: η = P_useful/P_input = 350/500 = 0.70 = 70%. The remaining 150 W (30%) is lost as heat due to friction and electrical resistance. Option A (30%) is the fraction lost, not the efficiency. Option B (43%) = 350/800 (wrong denominator). Option D (143%) > 100%, violating energy conservation — you can't get more energy out than you put in.
