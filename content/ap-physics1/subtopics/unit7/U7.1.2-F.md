A motor spins at 120 rpm (revolutions per minute). What is its angular velocity in rad/s?

## Options
A) 2π rad/s
B) 4π rad/s
C) 60π rad/s
D) 120π rad/s

## Answer
B

## Explanation
120 rpm = 120 rev/min. Convert: 120 rev/min × (2π rad/rev) × (1 min/60 s) = 120 × 2π / 60 = 4π rad/s. Option A (2π) would be 60 rpm. Option C (60π) forgets to divide by 60. Option D (120π) only converts rev→rad but not min→s.
