A solid disk (M = 4 kg, R = 0.5 m, I = ½MR²) rolls without slipping at v = 3 m/s. What is its total kinetic energy?

## Options
A) 9 J
B) 18 J
C) 27 J
D) 36 J

## Answer
C

## Explanation
Translational KE: K_trans = ½Mv² = ½(4)(9) = 18 J.
Rotational KE: K_rot = ½Iω² = ½(½MR²)(v/R)² = ½ × ½Mv² = ¼Mv² = ¼(4)(9) = 9 J.
Total KE = 18 + 9 = 27 J.

Note: for a solid disk, K_rot/K_trans = ½ (because I = ½MR²). The total KE = ½Mv² + ¼Mv² = ¾Mv² = ¾(4)(9) = 27 J. Option A (9) is only the rotational part. Option B (18) is only the translational part. Option D (36) uses Mv² (forgot all the ½ factors).
