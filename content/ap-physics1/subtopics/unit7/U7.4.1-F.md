A net torque of 12 N·m acts on a disk with moment of inertia 3 kg·m². What is the angular acceleration?

## Options
A) 4 rad/s²
B) 9 rad/s²
C) 36 rad/s²
D) 1/4 rad/s²

## Answer
A

## Explanation
τ_net = Iα → α = τ_net/I = 12/3 = 4 rad/s². Option B (9) uses I - τ or some other combination. Option C (36) multiplies τ × I instead of dividing. Option D (1/4) uses I/τ instead of τ/I.
