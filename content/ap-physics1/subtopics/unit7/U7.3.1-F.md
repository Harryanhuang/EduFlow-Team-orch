A force of 10 N is applied perpendicular to a wrench at a distance of 0.5 m from the bolt. What is the torque about the bolt?

## Options
A) 2 N·m
B) 5 N·m
C) 10 N·m
D) 20 N·m

## Answer
B

## Explanation
Since the force is perpendicular to the wrench: τ = rF sin 90° = rF = (0.5)(10) = 5 N·m. Option A (2) divides r/F or F/r. Option C (10) is just the force. Option D (20) uses r × F × 4 or doubles incorrectly. The perpendicular application maximizes torque — no sin reduction needed.
