A string is wrapped around a cylinder of radius R = 0.2 m. The cylinder starts from rest and accelerates at α = 5 rad/s². After 4 seconds, what is the linear speed and length of string unwound?

## Options
A) v = 4 m/s, L = 8 m
B) v = 10 m/s, L = 20 m
C) v = 20 m/s, L = 40 m
D) v = 1 m/s, L = 2 m

## Answer
A

## Explanation
After 4 s: ω = ω₀ + αt = 0 + (5)(4) = 20 rad/s. Linear speed of string: v = Rω = (0.2)(20) = 4 m/s.

Angular displacement: Δθ = ω₀t + ½αt² = 0 + ½(5)(16) = 40 rad. Length of string: L = RΔθ = (0.2)(40) = 8 m.

Alternatively: a_tangential = Rα = (0.2)(5) = 1 m/s². v = at = (1)(4) = 4 m/s. L = ½at² = ½(1)(16) = 8 m.

Option B (v=10, L=20) uses v = ω without multiplying by R. Option C (v=20) is just ω. Option D (v=1, L=2) uses a = α without multiplying by R.
