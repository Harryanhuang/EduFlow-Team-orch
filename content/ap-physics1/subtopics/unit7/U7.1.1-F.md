Which rotational quantity is analogous to linear velocity v?

## Options
A) Angular displacement θ
B) Angular velocity ω
C) Angular acceleration α
D) Torque τ

## Answer
B

## Explanation
The rotational-translational analogy maps: position x → angle θ, velocity v → angular velocity ω, acceleration a → angular acceleration α. Just as velocity is the rate of change of position (v = dx/dt), angular velocity is the rate of change of angle (ω = dθ/dt). Option A (θ) is analogous to position x. Option C (α) is analogous to acceleration a. Option D (τ, torque) is analogous to force F, not velocity.
