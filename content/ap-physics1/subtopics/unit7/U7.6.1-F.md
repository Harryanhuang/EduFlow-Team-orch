A flywheel with I = 10 kg·m² spins at 6 rad/s. What is its rotational kinetic energy?

## Options
A) 30 J
B) 60 J
C) 180 J
D) 360 J

## Answer
C

## Explanation
KE_rot = ½Iω² = ½(10)(6²) = ½(10)(36) = 180 J. Option A (30) uses ½Iω = ½(10)(6) = 30 (forgot to square ω). Option B (60) uses Iω = 60. Option D (360) forgets the ½: Iω² = 360.
