A disk with I = 4 kg·m² rotates at 5 rad/s. What is its angular momentum?

## Options
A) 0.8 kg·m²/s
B) 9 kg·m²/s
C) 20 kg·m²/s
D) 45 kg·m²/s

## Answer
C

## Explanation
L = Iω = (4)(5) = 20 kg·m²/s. Option A (0.8) uses I/ω. Option B (9) uses I + ω. Option D (45) uses I × ω² or something else. Simple multiplication: L = Iω.
