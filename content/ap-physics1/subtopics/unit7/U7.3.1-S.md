A force of 20 N is applied at the end of a 1 m rod at an angle of 30° to the rod. What is the torque about the pivot at the other end?

## Options
A) 5 N·m
B) 10 N·m
C) 17.3 N·m
D) 20 N·m

## Answer
B

## Explanation
τ = rF sin θ = (1)(20) sin 30° = 20 × 0.5 = 10 N·m. The component of force perpendicular to the rod is F⊥ = F sin 30° = 10 N. Only this perpendicular component creates torque. Option A (5) uses cos 30° or half of the correct answer. Option C (17.3 ≈ 10√3) uses cos 30° instead of sin 30°: τ = rF cos 30° = 20 × 0.866 ≈ 17.3. Option D (20) ignores the angle entirely (τ = rF).
