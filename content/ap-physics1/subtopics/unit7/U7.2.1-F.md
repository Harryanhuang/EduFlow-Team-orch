A wheel starts from rest and accelerates uniformly at 4 rad/s² for 3 s. What is its final angular velocity?

## Options
A) 4/3 rad/s
B) 7 rad/s
C) 12 rad/s
D) 36 rad/s

## Answer
C

## Explanation
Using ω = ω₀ + αt with ω₀ = 0: ω = 0 + (4)(3) = 12 rad/s. Option A (4/3) divides instead of multiplying. Option B (7) adds ω₀ + α + t. Option D (36) uses αt² = 4 × 9 = 36, which is the wrong formula (that's related to angular displacement, not velocity).
