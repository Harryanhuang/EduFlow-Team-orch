A solid sphere (I = ⅖mR²) rolls without slipping from rest down a ramp of height h. What is its speed at the bottom?

## Options
A) √(2gh)
B) √(10gh/7)
C) √(gh)
D) √(5gh)

## Answer
B

## Explanation
Energy conservation: mgh = ½mv² + ½Iω² = ½mv² + ½(⅖mR²)(v/R)² = ½mv² + ⅕mv² = (7/10)mv².
Solving: v² = 10gh/7 → v = √(10gh/7).
Option A (√(2gh)) is the speed for a sliding object (no rotation) — ignores rotational KE. Option C (√(gh)) is half of the sliding speed. Option D (√(5gh)) exceeds the sliding speed — impossible without additional energy input.
