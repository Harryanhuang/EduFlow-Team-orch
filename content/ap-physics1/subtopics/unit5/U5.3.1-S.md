A 2.0 kg cart moving at 3.0 m/s collides with a 1.0 kg cart moving at -3.0 m/s (opposite direction). After the collision, the 2.0 kg cart moves at -1.0 m/s. What is the 1.0 kg cart's final velocity?

## Options
A) 0 m/s
B) +3.0 m/s
C) +5.0 m/s
D) -3.0 m/s

## Answer
C

## Explanation
Momentum conservation: m₁v₁i + m₂v₂i = m₁v₁f + m₂v₂f. (2.0)(3.0) + (1.0)(-3.0) = (2.0)(-1.0) + (1.0)v₂f. 6.0 - 3.0 = -2.0 + v₂f. 3.0 = -2.0 + v₂f. v₂f = 5.0 m/s. The 1.0 kg cart reverses direction and speeds up to 5.0 m/s in the positive direction. Option A (0) forgets to include the 2.0 kg cart's final momentum. Option B (+3.0) assumes no change for the 1.0 kg cart. Option D (-3.0) doesn't change.
