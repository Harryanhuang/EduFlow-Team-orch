A 3 kg cart moving at 4 m/s collides with a 1 kg cart at rest. The two carts stick together. What is their common final speed?

## Options
A) 1 m/s
B) 2 m/s
C) 3 m/s
D) 4 m/s

## Answer
C

## Explanation
Perfectly inelastic collision: the carts stick together and move with a common final velocity. By momentum conservation: m₁v₁ + m₂v₂ = (m₁ + m₂)v_f. (3)(4) + (1)(0) = (3 + 1)v_f. 12 = 4v_f. v_f = 3 m/s. Option A (1 m/s) incorrectly divides by total mass but uses wrong numerator. Option B (2 m/s) might come from averaging speeds. Option D (4 m/s) ignores the second cart's mass entirely.
