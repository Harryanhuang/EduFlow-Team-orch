A buffer is prepared with equal concentrations of formic acid ($\text{HCOOH}$, $K_a = 1.8 \times 10^{-4}$) and sodium formate ($\text{HCOONa}$). What is the pH of this buffer?

## Options
A) $3.74$
B) $4.26$
C) $7.00$
D) $10.26$

## Answer
A

## Explanation
Using $\text{pH}=\text{p}K_a+\log\dfrac{[\text{A}^-]}{[\text{HA}]}$ with equal concentrations, the ratio is 1 and $\log(1)=0$, so $\text{pH}=\text{p}K_a$. Here $\text{p}K_a = -\log(1.8\times10^{-4}) = 3.74$. Therefore the buffer pH is $3.74$. Equal amounts of conjugate acid and base always give $\text{pH}=\text{p}K_a$.
