What is the pH of a $0.010 \text{ M}$ solution of $\text{HCl}$?

## Options
A) $1.0$
B) $2.0$
C) $10.0$
D) $12.0$

## Answer
B

## Explanation
$\text{HCl}$ is a strong acid that dissociates completely, so $[\text{H}^+] = 0.010 \text{ M} = 1.0 \times 10^{-2} \text{ M}$. Then $\text{pH} = -\log(1.0 \times 10^{-2}) = 2.0$. A pH of 2.0 is acidic, consistent with a dilute strong acid.
