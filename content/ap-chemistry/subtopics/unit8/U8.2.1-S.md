What is the pH of a $0.0050 \text{ M}$ solution of $\text{Ba(OH)}_2$ at $25\,^\circ\text{C}$?

## Options
A) $2.0$
B) $2.3$
C) $11.7$
D) $12.0$

## Answer
D

## Explanation
$\text{Ba(OH)}_2$ dissociates completely and provides 2 hydroxide ions per formula unit: $[\text{OH}^-] = 2 \times 0.0050 = 0.010 \text{ M}$. Then $\text{pOH} = -\log(0.010) = 2.0$, and $\text{pH} = 14.0 - 2.0 = 12.0$. Forgetting the factor of 2 gives $\text{pH} = 11.7$ (a common error), and confusing pOH with pH gives 2.0.
