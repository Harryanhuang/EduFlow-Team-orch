Which statement correctly describes the energy involved in breaking a chemical bond?

## Options
A) Breaking a bond releases energy and is exothermic.
B) Breaking a bond absorbs energy and is endothermic.
C) Breaking a bond neither absorbs nor releases energy.
D) Breaking a bond releases energy and is endothermic.

## Answer
B

## Explanation
Energy must always be supplied to pull bonded atoms apart, so breaking a bond absorbs energy and is endothermic (positive contribution to $\Delta H$). Conversely, forming a bond releases energy and is exothermic. This distinction is the basis for calculating reaction enthalpy from bond enthalpies.
