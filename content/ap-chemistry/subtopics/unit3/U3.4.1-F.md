Which of the following types of electromagnetic radiation has the highest energy per photon?

## Options
A) Microwave
B) Infrared
C) Visible light
D) Ultraviolet

## Answer
D

## Explanation
Photon energy follows $E = hc/\lambda$, so energy increases as wavelength decreases. Of the choices, ultraviolet radiation has the shortest wavelength (roughly 10–400 nm) and therefore the highest energy per photon. Microwaves have the longest wavelength and the lowest energy, followed by infrared and then visible light.
