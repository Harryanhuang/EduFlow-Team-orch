What is the percent by mass of hydrogen in water, $\mathrm{H_2O}$?

(Atomic masses: H = 1.01 g/mol, O = 16.00 g/mol)

## Options
A. 2.02%
B. 11.2%
C. 33.3%
D. 66.7%

## Answer
B

## Explanation
Step 1: Calculate molar mass of $\mathrm{H_2O}$:

$$M_{\mathrm{H_2O}} = 2(1.01) + 16.00 = 18.02 \text{ g/mol}$$

Step 2: Calculate mass percent of hydrogen:

$$\text{Mass \% H} = \frac{2(1.01 \text{ g/mol})}{18.02 \text{ g/mol}} \times 100\% = \frac{2.02}{18.02} \times 100\% = 11.2\%$$

Option A (2.02%) forgot to divide by total molar mass.
Option C (33.3%) incorrectly used atom ratio (2 H atoms out of 3 total atoms).
Option D (66.7%) incorrectly used 2/3 as the fraction.
