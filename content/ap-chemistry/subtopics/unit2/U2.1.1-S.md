Which compound has the greatest lattice energy?

## Options
A) NaCl
B) MgO
C) KBr
D) CaO

## Answer
B

## Explanation
Lattice energy ∝ q₁·q₂/r. MgO has ions with charges +2 and −2 (q product = 4) and small ionic radii (Mg²⁺ is smaller than Ca²⁺), giving it the highest charge density. CaO also has 2+/2− charges but larger Ca²⁺, so MgO > CaO. NaCl and KBr have only 1+/1− charges, giving much lower lattice energies.
