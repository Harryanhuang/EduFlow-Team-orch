Which of the following pairs of elements is most likely to form an ionic bond?

## Options
A) C and H
B) N and O
C) Na and Cl
D) S and O

## Answer
C

## Explanation
Na (EN ≈ 0.9) and Cl (EN ≈ 3.2) have ΔEN ≈ 2.3, which exceeds the ionic threshold of 1.7, making NaCl an ionic compound. The other pairs (C–H, N–O, S–O) all have ΔEN < 1.5, forming polar covalent bonds.
