Which of the following correctly ranks the melting points of these ionic solids from lowest to highest?

## Options
A) MgO < CaO < NaCl
B) NaCl < CaO < MgO
C) CaO < MgO < NaCl
D) NaCl < MgO < CaO

## Answer
B

## Explanation
Melting point correlates with lattice energy. NaCl has 1+/1− charges and larger ions → lowest lattice energy → lowest melting point. CaO and MgO both have 2+/2− charges, but Mg²⁺ is smaller than Ca²⁺, giving MgO a larger lattice energy than CaO. Therefore the correct order is NaCl < CaO < MgO.
