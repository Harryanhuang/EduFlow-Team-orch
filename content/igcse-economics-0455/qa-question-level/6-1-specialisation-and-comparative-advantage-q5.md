### Question Q-6.1-05
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: What are the risks of over-specialisation for a country?
**Answer**: Dependence on a single product or market makes the economy vulnerable to price shocks, changes in demand, or natural disasters affecting that sector. Lack of economic diversification reduces resilience. For example, oil-dependent countries face severe recessions when global oil prices fall sharply.
**Explanation**: Concentration risk reduces economic stability. A common error is assuming specialisation has no drawbacks.
**Tags**: over-specialisation, risks, diversification
