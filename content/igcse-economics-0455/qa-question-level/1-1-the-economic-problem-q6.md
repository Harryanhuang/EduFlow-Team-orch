### Question Q-1.1-06
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: A student has 3 hours to study for two exams. Explain the economic concepts involved in this situation.
**Answer**: Time is a scarce resource. The student faces a trade-off: more time spent on Exam A means less time for Exam B. The opportunity cost of studying one subject is the benefit forgone from not studying the other. The student must make a choice about how to allocate limited time to maximise overall results.
**Explanation**: Time is scarce, creating a trade-off and opportunity cost. A common error is saying the student can study both equally without acknowledging the trade-off involved.
**Tags**: time, scarcity, trade-off, opportunity-cost
