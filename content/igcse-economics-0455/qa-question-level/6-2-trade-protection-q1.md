### Question Q-6.2-01
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: What is trade protection?
**Answer**: Trade protection refers to government policies that restrict imports to protect domestic industries from foreign competition. Methods include tariffs, quotas, subsidies, and non-tariff barriers.
**Explanation**: Restricting imports to shield domestic producers. A common error is confusing trade protection with free trade.
