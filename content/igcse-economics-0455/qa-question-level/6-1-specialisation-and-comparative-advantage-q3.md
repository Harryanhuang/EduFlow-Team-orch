### Question Q-6.1-03
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Country A can produce 10 cars or 20 tonnes of wheat. Country B can produce 5 cars or 15 tonnes of wheat. Which country has a comparative advantage in cars?
**Answer**: Country A: OC of 1 car = 2 tonnes wheat. Country B: OC of 1 car = 3 tonnes wheat. Country A has lower OC and therefore has a comparative advantage in cars.
**Explanation**: A: 20/10 = 2 wheat per car. B: 15/5 = 3 wheat per car. A has lower OC. A common error is using absolute advantage instead of opportunity cost.
