### Question Q-3.5-02
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: What is an externality? Give one example.
**Answer**: An externality is a cost or benefit of an economic transaction that affects a third party who is not directly involved in the transaction. Example: air pollution from a factory is a negative externality affecting nearby residents.
**Explanation**: Third-party effect outside the transaction. A common error is confusing an externality with a cost or benefit experienced by the buyer or seller themselves.
