### Question Q-5.3-01
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: Define the term "development strategy" and give two examples.
**Answer**: A development strategy is a plan or set of policies adopted by a government or organisation to promote economic development and improve living standards. Examples include investing in education and healthcare, promoting foreign direct investment (FDI), building infrastructure, providing microfinance, and encouraging international trade.
**Explanation**: A strategy is a deliberate plan of action. A common error is giving vague answers without naming specific strategies.
