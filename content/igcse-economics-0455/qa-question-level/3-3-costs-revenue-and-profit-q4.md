### Question Q-3.3-04
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Calculate profit if total revenue is $12,000 and total cost is $9,000.
**Answer**: Profit = Total revenue - Total cost = $12,000 - $9,000 = $3,000.
**Explanation**: Profit = TR - TC. A common error is dividing TR by TC instead of subtracting.
