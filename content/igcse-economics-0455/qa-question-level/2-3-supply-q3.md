### Question Q-2.3-03
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Distinguish between a change in supply and a change in quantity supplied.
**Answer**: Change in quantity supplied: movement along the supply curve caused by a price change. Change in supply: a shift of the entire supply curve caused by non-price factors (costs, technology, etc.).
**Explanation**: Movement along vs shift. A common error is confusing the two.
**Tags**: supply, quantity-supplied, shift
