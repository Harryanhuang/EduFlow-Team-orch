### Question Q-2.4-07
**Difficulty**: Challenge
**Question**: Analyse the effect of a government subsidy on market equilibrium.
**Answer**: A subsidy shifts the supply curve right. Equilibrium price falls and quantity rises. Consumers benefit from lower prices; producers receive the market price plus the subsidy.
**Explanation**: Subsidy → supply right → lower price, higher quantity. A common error is price rises.
