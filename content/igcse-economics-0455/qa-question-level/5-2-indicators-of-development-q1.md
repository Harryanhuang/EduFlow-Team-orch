### Question Q-5.2-01
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: What is the Human Development Index (HDI)?
**Answer**: The HDI is a composite measure of development based on three dimensions: life expectancy at birth (health), education (mean years of schooling and expected years of schooling), and GNI per capita adjusted for purchasing power parity (standard of living).
**Explanation**: Health + education + income are the three dimensions. A common error is thinking the HDI measures only income.
