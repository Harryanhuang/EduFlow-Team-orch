### Question Q-1.1-05
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Explain why air is a free good but bottled water is an economic good.
**Answer**: Air is abundant and freely available — using it does not reduce availability for others (zero opportunity cost). Bottled water requires resources to produce, bottle, and transport — it has an opportunity cost. An economic good uses scarce resources and has an opportunity cost; a free good does not.
**Explanation**: Free good = zero opportunity cost. Economic good = uses scarce resources. A common error is saying air is not free because of pollution, or confusing "free of charge" with "free good."
