### Question Q-3.4-01
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: What characterises a perfectly competitive market?
**Answer**: Many buyers and sellers, identical (homogeneous) products, free entry and exit from the market, and perfect information. No single firm can influence the market price.
**Explanation**: Many firms, identical products, free entry. A common error is saying firms in perfect competition can set their own prices.
