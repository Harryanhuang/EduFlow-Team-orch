### Question Q-2.1-07
**Difficulty**: Challenge
**Question**: Evaluate whether a developing country should adopt a market or command economy.
**Answer**: Arguments for market: efficiency, innovation, foreign investment. Arguments for command: rapid industrialisation, addressing inequality. In practice, most developing countries use mixed economies.
**Explanation**: Both have pros and cons. Mixed is most common. A common error is advocating one without acknowledging trade-offs.
