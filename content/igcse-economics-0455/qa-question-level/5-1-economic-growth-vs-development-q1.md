### Question Q-5.1-01
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: Define economic growth.
**Answer**: Economic growth is an increase in a country's real GDP (gross domestic product) over time. It measures the increase in the total output of goods and services produced in an economy.
**Explanation**: Increase in real GDP. A common error is confusing real GDP with nominal GDP — real GDP is adjusted for inflation.
