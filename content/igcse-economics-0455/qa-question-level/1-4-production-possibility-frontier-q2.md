### Question Q-1.4-02
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: What does a point inside the PPF indicate about an economy?
**Answer**: A point inside the PPF indicates that resources are not being fully or efficiently used — there is unemployment, idle capital, or productive inefficiency. The economy could produce more of both goods without needing additional resources.
**Explanation**: Inside the curve = underutilisation of resources. A common error is saying it means the economy is saving resources for the future, or that it represents a desirable position.
**Tags**: ppf, inside, unemployment, inefficiency
