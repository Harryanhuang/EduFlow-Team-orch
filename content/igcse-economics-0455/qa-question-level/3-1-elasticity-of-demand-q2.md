### Question Q-3.1-02
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: If PED = 0.5, is demand elastic or inelastic?
**Answer**: Inelastic (PED < 1). Quantity demanded changes by a smaller percentage than the price change.
**Explanation**: < 1 = inelastic. A common error is saying elastic because 0.5 is a 'large' number.
**Tags**: ped, elastic, inelastic
