### Question Q-5.2-02
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: What does a high HDI value indicate about a country?
**Answer**: A high HDI value (close to 1.0) indicates that a country has high levels of health (long life expectancy), education (many years of schooling), and income (high GNI per capita). A low HDI value (close to 0) indicates poor development across these dimensions.
**Explanation**: The HDI scale runs from 0 to 1. A common error is interpreting HDI as a percentage rather than an index value.
