### Question Q-1.4-01
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: What does a production possibility frontier (PPF) show?
**Answer**: A PPF shows the maximum combinations of two goods (or categories of goods) that an economy can produce with its available resources and technology, assuming full and efficient use of all resources.
**Explanation**: Maximum output with given resources and technology. A common error is saying it shows what an economy wants to produce or what it actually produces — it shows what is possible at maximum capacity.
