### Question Q-1.1-02
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: Why do individuals have to make economic choices?
**Answer**: Because resources (income, time) are limited while wants are unlimited. People must decide which goods and services to buy and which to go without.
**Explanation**: Limited resources + unlimited wants = choice. A common error is saying choices are only about money — time and effort are also scarce resources.
