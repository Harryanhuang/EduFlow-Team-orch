### Question Q-1.1-01
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: Define scarcity in economics.
**Answer**: Scarcity means that resources are limited but human wants are unlimited. This creates the need to make choices about how resources are allocated.
**Explanation**: Scarcity is the fundamental economic problem. A common error is confusing scarcity with poverty — scarcity exists even in rich countries because wants always exceed available resources.
