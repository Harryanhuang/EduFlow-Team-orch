### Question Q-6.3-02
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: What is a trade deficit?
**Answer**: A trade deficit occurs when the value of a country's imports of goods and services exceeds the value of its exports over a given period. It is a component of the current account in the balance of payments.
**Explanation**: Imports exceed exports in value. A common error is confusing a trade deficit with a government budget deficit.
