### Question Q-2.2-03
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Distinguish between a change in demand and a change in quantity demanded.
**Answer**: Change in quantity demanded: movement along the demand curve caused by a price change. Change in demand: a shift of the entire demand curve caused by non-price factors (income, tastes, etc.).
**Explanation**: Movement along vs shift of curve. A common error is confusing the two.
**Tags**: demand, quantity-demanded, shift
