### Question Q-3.1-05
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: How does PED affect total revenue when price rises?
**Answer**: If demand is inelastic (PED < 1), total revenue increases when price rises. If demand is elastic (PED > 1), total revenue decreases when price rises.
**Explanation**: Inelastic demand + price up = revenue up. Elastic demand + price up = revenue down. A common error is always assuming revenue rises with price.
