### Question Q-2.4-04
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: What happens when supply decreases in a market?
**Answer**: The supply curve shifts left. At the original price, there is excess demand. Price rises and quantity traded falls until a new equilibrium is reached.
**Explanation**: Supply down → price up, quantity down. A common error is quantity up.
**Tags**: equilibrium, supply-decrease, price-change
