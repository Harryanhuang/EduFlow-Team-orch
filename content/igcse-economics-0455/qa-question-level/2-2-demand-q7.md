### Question Q-2.2-07
**Difficulty**: Challenge
**Question**: Analyse how advertising affects the demand for a product.
**Answer**: Successful advertising increases consumer awareness and desire for the product, shifting the demand curve to the right. It may also make demand more inelastic (less responsive to price changes) by building brand loyalty.
**Explanation**: Advertising shifts demand right and may affect elasticity. A common error is only saying 'more people buy it.'
