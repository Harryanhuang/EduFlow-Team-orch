### Question Q-3.2-06
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Why is supply more elastic in the long run than in the short run?
**Answer**: In the long run, firms can build new factories, enter or exit the market, adopt new technologies, and adjust all factors of production. In the short run, at least one factor is fixed.
**Explanation**: Long run = more flexibility to adjust. A common error is saying supply is always more elastic in the short run.
