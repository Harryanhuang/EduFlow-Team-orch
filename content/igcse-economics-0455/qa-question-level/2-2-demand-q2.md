### Question Q-2.2-02
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: State the law of demand.
**Answer**: As the price of a good rises, the quantity demanded falls, ceteris paribus (all other things being equal). There is an inverse relationship between price and quantity demanded.
**Explanation**: Inverse relationship. A common error is saying demand falls when price falls.
