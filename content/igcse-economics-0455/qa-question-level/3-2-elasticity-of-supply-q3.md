### Question Q-3.2-03
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: What factors determine price elasticity of supply?
**Answer**: 1) Time period (short run = inelastic, long run = elastic). 2) Spare capacity (more spare capacity = more elastic). 3) Stock levels (high stocks = more elastic). 4) Ease of switching production between goods.
**Explanation**: Time, capacity, stocks, flexibility. A common error is listing only time as a factor.
