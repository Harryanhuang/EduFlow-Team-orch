### Question Q-2.2-06
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Why does the demand curve slope downward?
**Answer**: 1) Substitution effect: as price falls, consumers switch to this good from alternatives. 2) Income effect: as price falls, consumers can afford more with the same income.
**Explanation**: Substitution and income effects. A common error is only giving one reason.
