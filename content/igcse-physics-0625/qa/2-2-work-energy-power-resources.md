# QA: 2.2 — Work, energy, power, energy resources and conversion

## Topic 名称
Work, energy, power, energy resources and conversion（功、能量、功率、能源与能量转化）

## Topic 定义
学习功的定义与计算、动能和重力势能、功率的概念、能量守恒定律和能量转化过程，以及可再生能源与不可再生能源的分类和特点。

## 关键知识点
- 功的定义：W = Fd（力与沿力方向移动距离的乘积），单位焦耳(J)
- 不做功的情况：有力无距离、力与运动方向垂直
- 重力势能：Ep = mgh
- 动能：Ek = ½mv²
- 能量守恒定律：能量不会凭空产生或消失，只能从一种形式转化为另一种形式
- 能量转化实例：自由落体（势能 → 动能）、发电（多种能源 → 电能）
- 功率定义：P = W/t = E/t，单位瓦特(W)，1 W = 1 J/s
- 功率的另一表达式：P = Fv
- 效率 = 有用能量输出 / 总能量输入 × 100% = 有用功率输出 / 总功率输入 × 100%
- 可再生能源：太阳能、风能、水能、地热能、潮汐能、生物质能
- 不可再生能源：化石燃料（煤、石油、天然气）、核能
- 各种能源的优缺点对比（成本、可靠性、环境影响、选址要求）

- 2.1（力、质量、重量）

## 常见错误
- 功的计算中用了总距离而非沿力方向的距离
- 混淆功率和能量（功率是做功的速率）
- 动能计算忘记 ½ 或速度忘记平方
- 效率计算时分子分母颠倒
- 认为核能是可再生能源
- 画能量流图时遗漏"浪费的能量"部分（通常以热能/声能形式散失）

## 可出题方向
- 功的计算（W = Fd）
- 势能和动能的计算
- 能量守恒应用题（如物体下落求速度）
- 功率计算（给定功和时间）
- 效率计算
- 能源分类与优缺点比较（简答题）
- 画能量流图/桑基图（Sankey diagram）

## Item-level prototype

### Question Q-2.2-01
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: A boy pushes a box with a force of 40 N across a floor for a distance of 5 m in the direction of the force. Calculate the work done.
**Answer**: 200 J
**Explanation**: W = Fd = 40 × 5 = 200 J. Work is measured in joules. A common error is using a force that is not in the direction of motion, or forgetting that the distance must be measured along the direction of the force.

**Tags**: work, W=Fd, calculation

### Question Q-2.2-02
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: A crane lifts a load of mass 500 kg vertically through a height of 12 m. Calculate the gravitational potential energy gained by the load. (g = 10 N/kg)
**Answer**: 60 000 J (60 kJ)
**Explanation**: Ep = mgh = 500 × 10 × 12 = 60 000 J. A common error is forgetting to multiply by g (using mass instead of weight), or forgetting the height, giving only 500 × 12 = 6000 J.

**Tags**: gravitational-potential-energy, Ep=mgh, calculation

### Question Q-2.2-03
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: A car of mass 1200 kg is travelling at 20 m/s. Calculate its kinetic energy.
**Answer**: 240 000 J (240 kJ)
**Explanation**: Ek = ½mv² = ½ × 1200 × 20² = ½ × 1200 × 400 = 240 000 J. A common error is forgetting the ½ or forgetting to square the velocity (using 20 instead of 400).

**Tags**: kinetic-energy, Ek=½mv², calculation

### Question Q-2.2-04
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: A motor does 6000 J of work in 15 seconds. Calculate the power output. If the motor uses 9000 J of electrical energy to do this work, what is its efficiency?
**Answer**: Power = 400 W. Efficiency = 66.7%.
**Explanation**: P = W/t = 6000/15 = 400 W. Efficiency = useful output / total input × 100% = 6000/9000 × 100% = 66.7%. A common error is confusing power with energy, or inverting the efficiency formula (input/output instead of output/input).

**Tags**: power, P=W/t, efficiency, calculation

### Question Q-2.2-05
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Classify each of the following energy resources as renewable or non-renewable: solar, natural gas, wind, nuclear, hydroelectric. State one advantage and one disadvantage of using solar panels for generating electricity.
**Answer**: Renewable: solar, wind, hydroelectric. Non-renewable: natural gas, nuclear. Advantage of solar: no fuel cost and no pollution during use. Disadvantage: only generates electricity during daylight; output depends on weather and season; high initial installation cost.
**Explanation**: Nuclear is non-renewable because uranium fuel is finite. A common error is classifying nuclear as renewable. For advantages/disadvantages, always give specific physical or economic reasons, not vague statements like "good for the environment."

**Tags**: energy-resources, renewable, non-renewable, solar
