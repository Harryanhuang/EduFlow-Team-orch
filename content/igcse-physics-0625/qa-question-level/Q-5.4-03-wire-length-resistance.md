# Question Q-5.4-03

**Difficulty**: Standard

**Question**: A wire of length 1 m has a resistance of 4 ohm. What would be the resistance of a wire of the same material with (a) length 2 m, (b) length 0.5 m?

**Answer**: (a) 8 ohm. (b) 2 ohm.

**Explanation**: Resistance is proportional to length. Doubling length doubles resistance; halving length halves resistance.
