# Question Q-4.1-09

**Difficulty**: Challenge

**Question**: A displacement-distance graph of a wave shows that the distance between consecutive peaks is 1.2 m and the maximum displacement is 0.3 m. If the wave speed is 6 m/s, calculate the frequency of the wave.

**Answer**: 5 Hz

**Explanation**: From the graph, wavelength λ = 1.2 m and amplitude = 0.3 m. Using v = fλ, rearrange to f = v/λ = 6/1.2 = 5 Hz. A common error is confusing the amplitude with the wavelength, or misreading the graph axes.
