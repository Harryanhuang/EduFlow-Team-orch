# Question Q-4.1-07

**Difficulty**: Challenge

**Question**: A radio station broadcasts at a frequency of 100 MHz. If the wavelength of these radio waves is 3 m, calculate the speed of the electromagnetic waves. Give your answer in standard form.

**Answer**: 3 × 10⁸ m/s

**Explanation**: v = fλ = (100 × 10⁶) × 3 = 3 × 10⁸ m/s. This confirms that radio waves travel at the speed of light. A common error is not converting MHz to Hz correctly, forgetting that 1 MHz = 10⁶ Hz.
