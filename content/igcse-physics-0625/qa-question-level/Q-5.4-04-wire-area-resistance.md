# Question Q-5.4-04

**Difficulty**: Standard

**Question**: A wire has a resistance of 10 ohm. What would be the resistance of a wire of the same material and length but with twice the cross-sectional area?

**Answer**: 5 ohm

**Explanation**: Resistance is inversely proportional to cross-sectional area. Doubling the area halves the resistance.
