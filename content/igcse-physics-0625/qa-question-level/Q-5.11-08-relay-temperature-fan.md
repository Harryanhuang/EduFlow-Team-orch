# Question Q-5.11-08

**Difficulty**: Challenge

**Question**: A relay coil is connected to a temperature sensor circuit. Explain how it could switch on a fan when temperature rises.

**Answer**: As temperature rises, the sensor circuit increases current through the relay coil. When the current is large enough, the electromagnet attracts the armature and closes the fan circuit.

**Explanation**: The relay links a sensor/control circuit to a separate output circuit.

**Tags**: relay, thermistor, fan-control
