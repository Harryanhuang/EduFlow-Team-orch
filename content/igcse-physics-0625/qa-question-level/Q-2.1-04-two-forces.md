# Question Q-2.1-04

**Difficulty**: Standard

**Question**: A 5 kg object is acted on by two forces: 30 N to the right and 10 N to the left. Calculate the acceleration and state its direction.

**Answer**: 4 m/s² to the right

**Explanation**: Resultant force = 30 - 10 = 20 N to the right. a = F/m = 20/5 = 4 m/s² to the right. A common error is not stating the direction of acceleration.
