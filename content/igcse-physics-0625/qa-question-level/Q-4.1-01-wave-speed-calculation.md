# Question Q-4.1-01

**Difficulty**: Foundation

**Question**: A wave has a frequency of 50 Hz and a wavelength of 4 m. Calculate its speed.

**Answer**: 200 m/s

**Explanation**: v = fλ = 50 × 4 = 200 m/s. A common error is dividing frequency by wavelength instead of multiplying.
