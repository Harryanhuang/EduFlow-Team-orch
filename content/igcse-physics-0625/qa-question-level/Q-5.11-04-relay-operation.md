# Question Q-5.11-04

**Difficulty**: Standard

**Question**: Describe how a relay uses an electromagnet to switch on a separate circuit.

**Answer**: A small current in the coil magnetises the core and attracts an iron armature. This closes contacts in a separate circuit, allowing a larger current to flow there.

**Explanation**: A relay allows a low-current control circuit to operate a high-current circuit.
