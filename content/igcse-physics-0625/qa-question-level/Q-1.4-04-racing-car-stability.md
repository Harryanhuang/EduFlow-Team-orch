# Question Q-1.4-04

**Difficulty**: Standard

**Question**: Why does a racing car have a low centre of gravity and a wide wheelbase?

**Answer**: A low centre of gravity and wide base increase stability, making the car less likely to topple when turning at high speed.

**Explanation**: Stability depends on both the height of the centre of gravity and the width of the base. A common error is only mentioning one factor.
