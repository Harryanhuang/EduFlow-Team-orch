# Question Q-5.6-05

**Difficulty**: Standard

**Question**: Sketch and describe how p.d. across a capacitor changes as it charges through a resistor from a d.c. supply.

**Answer**: The p.d. starts at 0 V, rises quickly at first, then rises more slowly and approaches the supply voltage.

**Explanation**: Charging is curved, not linear.
