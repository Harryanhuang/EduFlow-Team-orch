# Question Q-4.1-08

**Difficulty**: Challenge

**Question**: A wave generator produces 15 waves in 3 seconds. If the wavelength is 0.4 m, calculate: (a) the frequency, (b) the speed of the wave.

**Answer**: (a) 5 Hz, (b) 2 m/s

**Explanation**: (a) f = number of waves / time = 15/3 = 5 Hz. (b) v = fλ = 5 × 0.4 = 2 m/s. A common error in part (a) is using 15 Hz directly without dividing by time. In part (b), some students multiply frequency by wavelength incorrectly.
