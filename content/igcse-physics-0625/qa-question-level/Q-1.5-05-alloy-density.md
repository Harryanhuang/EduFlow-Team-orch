# Question Q-1.5-05

**Difficulty**: Standard

**Question**: An alloy is made by mixing 300 g of metal A (density 6 g/cm³) with 200 g of metal B (density 4 g/cm³). Calculate the density of the alloy.

**Answer**: 5 g/cm³

**Explanation**: Volume of A = 300/6 = 50 cm³. Volume of B = 200/4 = 50 cm³. Total mass = 500 g. Total volume = 100 cm³. Density = 500/100 = 5 g/cm³. A common error is averaging the densities (6+4)/2 = 5 — correct by coincidence but wrong method.
