# Question Q-5.3-01

**Difficulty**: Foundation

**Question**: Two resistors of 4 ohm and 6 ohm are connected in series. Calculate the total resistance.

**Answer**: 10 ohm

**Explanation**: In series, R_total = R1 + R2 = 4 + 6 = 10 ohm. A common error is using the parallel formula.
