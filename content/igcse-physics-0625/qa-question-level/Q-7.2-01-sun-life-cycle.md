# Question Q-7.2-01

**Difficulty**: Foundation

**Question**: Describe the life cycle of a star similar in mass to the Sun, listing all stages in order from formation to end stage.

**Answer**: Nebula → protostar → main-sequence star → red giant → planetary nebula → white dwarf → black dwarf.

**Explanation**: A star begins as a collapsing cloud of gas and dust (nebula), heats up as it contracts (protostar), stabilises with hydrogen fusion (main sequence), expands when hydrogen runs out (red giant), sheds its outer layers (planetary nebula), leaves a hot dense core (white dwarf), which eventually cools completely (black dwarf). A common error is missing stages or getting the order wrong.
