# Question Q-4.9-04

**Difficulty**: Standard

**Question**: Give two industrial uses of ultrasound.

**Answer**: (1) Detecting cracks or defects in metal structures (ultrasound reflects off internal defects). (2) Cleaning delicate items (ultrasound vibrations remove dirt from hard-to-reach areas).

**Explanation**: Both are practical applications. A common error is confusing industrial cleaning with medical imaging.
