# Question Q-4.8-05

**Difficulty**: Standard

**Question**: On an oscilloscope display, how would the trace change if (a) the sound is louder, (b) the pitch is higher?

**Answer**: (a) Louder sound: the amplitude (height) of the trace increases. (b) Higher pitch: the wavelength on the screen decreases (more waves fit in the same horizontal space).

**Explanation**: Amplitude ↔ loudness, frequency/wavelength ↔ pitch. A common error is saying louder makes more waves.
