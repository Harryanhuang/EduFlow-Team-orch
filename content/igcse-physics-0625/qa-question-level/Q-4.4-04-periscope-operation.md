# Question Q-4.4-04

**Difficulty**: Standard

**Question**: How does a periscope work?

**Answer**: A periscope uses two plane mirrors placed at 45° angles, parallel to each other. Light from the object reflects off the top mirror, then the bottom mirror, and into the viewer's eye, allowing viewing over obstacles.

**Explanation**: Two reflections at 45° redirect the light. A common error is saying it uses lenses.
