# Question Q-7.1-01

**Difficulty**: Foundation

**Question**: List the eight planets of the Solar System in order from closest to the Sun to furthest away.

**Answer**: Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, Neptune.

**Explanation**: The planets are ordered by increasing distance from the Sun. Pluto was reclassified as a dwarf planet in 2006 and is no longer counted among the major planets. A common error is including Pluto or listing the planets in the wrong order.
