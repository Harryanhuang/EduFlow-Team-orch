# Question Q-5.7-01

**Difficulty**: Foundation

**Question**: A 240 V mains supply powers a heater drawing a current of 8 A. Calculate the power of the heater.

**Answer**: 1920 W, or 1.92 kW.

**Explanation**: P = IV = 8 × 240 = 1920 W. Use P = IV because voltage and current are given.
