# Question Q-7.1-05

**Difficulty**: Standard

**Question**: Where is our Solar System located within the Milky Way galaxy?

**Answer**: Our Solar System is located in one of the spiral arms of the Milky Way (called the Orion Arm or Orion Spur), approximately two-thirds of the way from the galactic centre to the outer edge, about 26 000 light-years from the centre.

**Explanation**: We are not at the centre of the galaxy nor at the very edge. A common error is stating that we are "at the centre of the Milky Way" or confusing the Solar System with the galaxy itself.
