# Question Q-5.7-07

**Difficulty**: Challenge

**Question**: A 60 W lamp is replaced by a 9 W LED lamp. Both are used for 5 hours per day for 30 days. Electricity costs $0.20 per kWh. Calculate the monthly saving.

**Answer**: Old lamp energy = 0.060 × 150 = 9.0 kWh. LED energy = 0.009 × 150 = 1.35 kWh. Saving = 7.65 kWh, so cost saving = 7.65 × 0.20 = $1.53.

**Explanation**: Use total time and convert W to kW.
