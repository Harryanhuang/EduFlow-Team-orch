# Question Q-2.3-03

**Difficulty**: Standard

**Question**: A 2 kg ball is lifted to a height of 5 m. Calculate the gain in gravitational potential energy. (g = 10 N/kg)

**Answer**: 100 J

**Explanation**: Ep = mgh = 2 × 10 × 5 = 100 J. A common error is using just mg without the height.
