# Question Q-3.3-02

**Difficulty**: Foundation

**Question**: A bimetallic strip is made of brass and iron. When heated, which way does it bend? Explain. (Brass expands more than iron.)

**Answer**: It bends towards the iron side. Brass expands more than iron, so the brass side becomes longer, causing the strip to curve with the brass on the outside of the curve.

**Explanation**: The side that expands more is always on the outside of the bend. A common error is saying it bends towards the brass.
