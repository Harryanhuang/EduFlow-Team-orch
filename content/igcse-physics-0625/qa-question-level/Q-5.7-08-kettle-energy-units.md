# Question Q-5.7-08

**Difficulty**: Challenge

**Question**: A kettle is rated 2.2 kW and takes 3 minutes to boil water. Calculate the energy transferred in joules and in kWh.

**Answer**: Time = 180 s = 0.05 h. Energy = 2200 × 180 = 396000 J. In kWh, energy = 2.2 × 0.05 = 0.11 kWh.

**Explanation**: Use seconds with watts for joules and hours with kW for kWh.
