# Question Q-5.1-06

**Difficulty**: Standard

**Question**: Describe how you could demagnetise a bar magnet.

**Answer**: Heat it strongly, hammer it repeatedly, or place it inside a solenoid connected to an a.c. supply and slowly withdraw it.

**Explanation**: These methods disrupt the aligned magnetic domains. A common error is saying a magnet can be demagnetised simply by putting it near another magnet.
