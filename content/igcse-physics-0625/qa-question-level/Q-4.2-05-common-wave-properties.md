# Question Q-4.2-05

**Difficulty**: Standard

**Question**: What property do all waves (transverse and longitudinal) have in common?

**Answer**: All waves transfer energy without transferring matter. All waves can be reflected and have a speed, frequency, and wavelength.

**Explanation**: Energy transfer without matter transfer is the universal wave property. A common error is listing properties specific to one type.
