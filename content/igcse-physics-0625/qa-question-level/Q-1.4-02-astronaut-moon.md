# Question Q-1.4-02

**Difficulty**: Foundation

**Question**: An astronaut has a mass of 80 kg on Earth. What is her mass on the Moon? If g on the Moon is 1.6 N/kg, what is her weight there?

**Answer**: Mass = 80 kg (same everywhere). Weight = 80 × 1.6 = 128 N.

**Explanation**: Mass does not change with location. Weight changes because g is different. A common error is thinking mass also changes on the Moon.
