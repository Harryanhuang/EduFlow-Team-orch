# Question Q-4.7-06

**Difficulty**: Standard

**Question**: Microwaves have a wavelength of about 3 cm. Calculate their frequency. (c = 3 × 10⁸ m/s)

**Answer**: 10 GHz (or 10 × 10⁹ Hz)

**Explanation**: f = c/λ = 3 × 10⁸ / 0.03 = 10¹⁰ Hz = 10 GHz. A common error is not converting cm to metres (using 3 instead of 0.03).
