# Question Q-2.1-01

**Difficulty**: Foundation

**Question**: A force of 12 N acts on an object of mass 3 kg. Calculate the acceleration.

**Answer**: 4 m/s²

**Explanation**: F = ma, so a = F/m = 12/3 = 4 m/s². A common error is multiplying instead of dividing (12 × 3 = 36).
