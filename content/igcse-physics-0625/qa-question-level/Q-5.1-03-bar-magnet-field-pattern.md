# Question Q-5.1-03

**Difficulty**: Standard

**Question**: Draw the magnetic field pattern around a bar magnet, showing the direction of the field lines.

**Answer**: Field lines leave the N pole, curve around, and enter the S pole outside the magnet. They are closest together near the poles and never cross. Inside the magnet, field lines go from S to N.

**Explanation**: The direction outside the magnet is N to S. A common error is drawing field lines from S to N outside the magnet.
