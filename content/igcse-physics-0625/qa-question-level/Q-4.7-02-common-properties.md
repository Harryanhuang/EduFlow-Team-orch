# Question Q-4.7-02

**Difficulty**: Foundation

**Question**: State two properties that all electromagnetic waves have in common.

**Answer**: (1) All travel at the speed of light (3 × 10⁸ m/s) in a vacuum. (2) All are transverse waves. Also: all can travel through a vacuum, all transfer energy.

**Explanation**: Speed in vacuum and transverse nature are key. A common error is saying they all need a medium.
