# Question Q-4.1-03

**Difficulty**: Standard

**Question**: From a wave diagram, the distance between two adjacent crests is 0.8 m and 4 waves pass a point in 2 seconds. Calculate the wave speed.

**Answer**: 1.6 m/s

**Explanation**: Wavelength = 0.8 m. Frequency = 4/2 = 2 Hz. v = fλ = 2 × 0.8 = 1.6 m/s. A common error is using 4 waves as the frequency directly without dividing by time.
