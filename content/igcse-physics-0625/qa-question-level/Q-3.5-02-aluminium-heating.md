# Question Q-3.5-02

**Difficulty**: Standard

**Question**: A 0.5 kg block of aluminium is heated with a 50 W heater for 5 minutes. The temperature rises by 18°C. Calculate the specific heat capacity of aluminium.

**Answer**: 1667 J/(kg·°C)

**Explanation**: Energy supplied = Pt = 50 × 300 = 15 000 J. c = Q/(mΔT) = 15 000/(0.5 × 18) = 15 000/9 = 1667 J/(kg·°C). A common error is using 5 minutes instead of 300 seconds.
