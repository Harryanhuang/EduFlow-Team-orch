# Question Q-4.7-04

**Difficulty**: Standard

**Question**: Calculate the wavelength of a radio wave with a frequency of 100 MHz. (c = 3 × 10⁸ m/s)

**Answer**: 3 m

**Explanation**: λ = c/f = 3 × 10⁸ / (100 × 10⁶) = 3 × 10⁸ / 10⁸ = 3 m. A common error is not converting MHz to Hz.
