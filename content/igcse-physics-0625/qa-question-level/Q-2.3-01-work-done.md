# Question Q-2.3-01

**Difficulty**: Foundation

**Question**: A force of 20 N pushes a box 5 m along a horizontal surface. Calculate the work done.

**Answer**: 100 J

**Explanation**: W = Fs = 20 × 5 = 100 J. A common error is using W = F/s or giving the answer in N.
