# Question Q-5.4-02

**Difficulty**: Foundation

**Question**: What does it mean for a conductor to obey Ohm's law?

**Answer**: The current through the conductor is directly proportional to the p.d. across it, provided temperature remains constant. Its I-V graph is a straight line through the origin.

**Explanation**: The constant temperature condition matters.
