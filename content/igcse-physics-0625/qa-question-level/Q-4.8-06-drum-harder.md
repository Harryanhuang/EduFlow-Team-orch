# Question Q-4.8-06

**Difficulty**: Standard

**Question**: A drum is struck harder. Describe the changes to the sound produced in terms of amplitude and frequency.

**Answer**: Striking the drum harder increases the amplitude of vibration, making the sound louder. The frequency remains approximately the same (same pitch), as the drum's natural frequency is determined by its size and tension.

**Explanation**: Force affects amplitude, not frequency. A common error is thinking hitting harder also increases pitch.
