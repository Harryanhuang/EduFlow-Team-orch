# Question Q-3.2-01

**Difficulty**: Foundation

**Question**: Convert the following temperatures to Kelvin: (a) 0°C, (b) 25°C, (c) -40°C.

**Answer**: (a) 273 K, (b) 298 K, (c) 233 K

**Explanation**: T(K) = θ(°C) + 273. For -40°C: -40 + 273 = 233 K. A common error is subtracting 273 instead of adding.
