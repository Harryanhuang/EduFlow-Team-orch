# Question Q-2.4-09

**Difficulty**: Challenge

**Question**: An escalator lifts 60 people per minute, each of average mass 70 kg, through a vertical height of 5 m. The motor is 65% efficient. Calculate: (a) the useful power output of the motor, (b) the power input required. (g = 10 N/kg)

**Answer**: (a) Total mass per minute = 60 × 70 = 4200 kg. Work per minute = mgh = 4200 × 10 × 5 = 210 000 J. Useful power = 210 000/60 = 3500 W. (b) Power input = 3500/0.65 = 5385 W.

**Explanation**: Convert per-minute to per-second for power. A common error is using 60 people as the total mass (forgetting to multiply by 70 kg each), or using 60 seconds as the distance.
