# Question Q-1.5-04

**Difficulty**: Standard

**Question**: A liquid has a volume of 250 cm³ and a mass of 200 g. Will this liquid float on or sink in water? (Water density = 1 g/cm³)

**Answer**: Float. Density = 200/250 = 0.8 g/cm³. Since 0.8 < 1.0, the liquid will float on water.

**Explanation**: Density less than water means it floats. A common error is calculating the density correctly but then getting the float/sink decision backwards.
