# Question Q-5.4-09

**Difficulty**: Challenge

**Question**: A current-p.d. graph is straight through the origin up to 4 V, then curves and becomes less steep. Explain what this shows about resistance.

**Answer**: Up to 4 V the resistance is constant. Above 4 V the decreasing gradient means resistance increases, likely due to heating.

**Explanation**: For an I-V graph, gradient = I/V = 1/R.
