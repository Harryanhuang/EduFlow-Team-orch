# Question Q-5.7-03

**Difficulty**: Standard

**Question**: A family uses 450 kWh of electricity in one month. If the cost is $0.12 per kWh, calculate the total electricity bill.

**Answer**: $54

**Explanation**: Cost = 450 × 0.12 = $54. Electricity bills commonly use kWh directly.
