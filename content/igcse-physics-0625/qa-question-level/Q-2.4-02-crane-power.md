# Question Q-2.4-02

**Difficulty**: Foundation

**Question**: A crane lifts a 500 N load through a height of 10 m in 20 s. Calculate the power of the crane.

**Answer**: 250 W

**Explanation**: Work = Fs = 500 × 10 = 5000 J. Power = 5000/20 = 250 W. A common error is using the force directly without calculating work first.
