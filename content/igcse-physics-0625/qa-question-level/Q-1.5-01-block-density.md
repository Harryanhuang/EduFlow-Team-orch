# Question Q-1.5-01

**Difficulty**: Foundation

**Question**: A block has a mass of 240 g and dimensions 4 cm × 5 cm × 3 cm. Calculate its density.

**Answer**: 4 g/cm³

**Explanation**: Volume = 4 × 5 × 3 = 60 cm³. Density = mass/volume = 240/60 = 4 g/cm³. A common error is adding the dimensions instead of multiplying for volume.
