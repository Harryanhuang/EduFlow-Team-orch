# Question Q-1.4-05

**Difficulty**: Standard

**Question**: A spring balance reads 20 N when an object is hung from it on Earth. What would the same spring balance read for the same object on a planet where g = 5 N/kg? (Earth g = 10 N/kg)

**Answer**: 10 N

**Explanation**: Mass = W/g = 20/10 = 2 kg. Weight on the other planet = 2 × 5 = 10 N. A common error is halving the reading without showing the mass calculation.
