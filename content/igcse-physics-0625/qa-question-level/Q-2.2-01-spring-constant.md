# Question Q-2.2-01

**Difficulty**: Foundation

**Question**: A spring extends by 4 cm when a force of 8 N is applied. Calculate the spring constant.

**Answer**: 2 N/cm (or 200 N/m)

**Explanation**: k = F/x = 8/4 = 2 N/cm. In SI units: k = 8/0.04 = 200 N/m. A common error is not converting cm to m when using SI units.
