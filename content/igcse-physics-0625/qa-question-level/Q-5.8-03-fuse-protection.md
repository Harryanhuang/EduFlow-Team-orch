# Question Q-5.8-03

**Difficulty**: Standard

**Question**: Explain how a fuse protects an appliance and user.

**Answer**: A fuse contains a thin wire that melts if the current exceeds its rated value. This breaks the circuit and stops current flowing before wires overheat or the appliance case remains dangerous.

**Explanation**: The fuse is connected in the live wire so it disconnects the appliance from the dangerous supply.
