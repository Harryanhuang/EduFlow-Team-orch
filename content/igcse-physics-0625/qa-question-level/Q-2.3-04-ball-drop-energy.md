# Question Q-2.3-04

**Difficulty**: Standard

**Question**: A 0.5 kg ball is dropped from a height of 20 m. Assuming no air resistance, calculate its speed just before hitting the ground. (g = 10 m/s²)

**Answer**: 20 m/s

**Explanation**: By conservation of energy: Ep lost = Ek gained. mgh = ½mv². 0.5 × 10 × 20 = ½ × 0.5 × v². 100 = 0.25v². v² = 400. v = 20 m/s. A common error is not cancelling mass and over-complicating the calculation.
