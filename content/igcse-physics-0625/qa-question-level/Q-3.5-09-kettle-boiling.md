# Question Q-3.5-09

**Difficulty**: Challenge

**Question**: An electric kettle rated at 2400 W contains 1.5 kg of water at 20°C. Calculate the minimum time needed to bring the water to boiling point. State any assumption you make. (c_water = 4200 J/(kg·°C))

**Answer**: 210 s (3.5 min)

**Explanation**: Energy needed: Q = mcΔT = 1.5 × 4200 × (100 - 20) = 1.5 × 4200 × 80 = 504 000 J. Time = Q/P = 504 000/2400 = 210 s. Assumption: All the electrical energy is transferred to the water (no heat loss to surroundings, kettle, or air). In reality, some energy is wasted, so the actual time would be longer.
