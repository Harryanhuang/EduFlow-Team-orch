# Question Q-4.8-03

**Difficulty**: Standard

**Question**: Describe an experiment to measure the speed of sound in air.

**Answer**: Two people stand a known distance apart (e.g., 500 m). One person makes a loud sound (e.g., fires a starting pistol). The other person starts a stopwatch when they see the flash and stops it when they hear the sound. Speed = distance / time.

**Explanation**: Light travels much faster than sound, so the flash is seen almost instantly. A common error is starting the timer on the sound.
