# Question Q-2.1-02

**Difficulty**: Foundation

**Question**: What is the resultant force on a car of mass 1000 kg accelerating at 2.5 m/s²?

**Answer**: 2500 N

**Explanation**: F = ma = 1000 × 2.5 = 2500 N. A common error is giving the answer in kg·m/s instead of N.
