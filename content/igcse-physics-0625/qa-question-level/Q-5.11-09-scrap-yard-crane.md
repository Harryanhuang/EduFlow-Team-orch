# Question Q-5.11-09

**Difficulty**: Challenge

**Question**: Compare a permanent magnet and an electromagnet for use in a scrap-yard crane.

**Answer**: An electromagnet is better because it can be switched on to lift steel and switched off to release it. A permanent magnet would be difficult to release and its strength is less easily controlled.

**Explanation**: Control of magnetism is the key advantage.
