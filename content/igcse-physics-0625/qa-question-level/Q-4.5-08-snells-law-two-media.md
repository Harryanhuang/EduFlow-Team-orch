# Question Q-4.5-08

**Difficulty**: Challenge

**Question**: A ray of light passes from glass (n = 1.5) into water (n = 1.33). The angle of incidence in glass is 30°. Calculate the angle of refraction in water.

**Answer**: 34.3°

**Explanation**: Using Snell's law: n₁ sin i₁ = n₂ sin i₂. 1.5 × sin 30° = 1.33 × sin i₂. sin i₂ = (1.5 × 0.5) / 1.33 = 0.75 / 1.33 = 0.5639. i₂ = sin⁻¹(0.5639) = 34.3°. A common error is using n = sin i / sin r directly without accounting for both media.
