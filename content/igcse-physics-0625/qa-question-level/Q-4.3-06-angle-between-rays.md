# Question Q-4.3-06

**Difficulty**: Standard

**Question**: A ray of light is incident on a plane mirror at an angle of 45° to the normal. What is the angle between the incident ray and the reflected ray?

**Answer**: 90°

**Explanation**: Angle of incidence = 45°, angle of reflection = 45°. The angle between incident and reflected rays = 45° + 45° = 90°. A common error is answering 45° (the angle of incidence alone).
