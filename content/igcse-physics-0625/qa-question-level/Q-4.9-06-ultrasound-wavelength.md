# Question Q-4.9-06

**Difficulty**: Standard

**Question**: An ultrasound wave has a frequency of 2 MHz and travels through tissue at 1500 m/s. Calculate its wavelength.

**Answer**: 0.75 mm (or 7.5 × 10⁻⁴ m)

**Explanation**: λ = v/f = 1500 / (2 × 10⁶) = 1500 / 2000000 = 0.00075 m = 0.75 mm. A common error is not converting MHz to Hz correctly.
