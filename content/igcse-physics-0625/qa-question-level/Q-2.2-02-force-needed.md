# Question Q-2.2-02

**Difficulty**: Foundation

**Question**: A spring has a spring constant of 50 N/m. Calculate the force needed to extend it by 0.1 m.

**Answer**: 5 N

**Explanation**: F = kx = 50 × 0.1 = 5 N. A common error is dividing instead of multiplying.
