# Question Q-2.4-03

**Difficulty**: Standard

**Question**: A machine has a power input of 1000 W and a useful power output of 750 W. Calculate its efficiency as a percentage.

**Answer**: 75%

**Explanation**: Efficiency = useful output / total input × 100% = 750/1000 × 100% = 75%. A common error is giving 0.75 without the percentage sign.
