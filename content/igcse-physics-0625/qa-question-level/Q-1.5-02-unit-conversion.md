# Question Q-1.5-02

**Difficulty**: Foundation

**Question**: Convert a density of 2.7 g/cm³ into kg/m³.

**Answer**: 2700 kg/m³

**Explanation**: 1 g/cm³ = 1000 kg/m³. So 2.7 g/cm³ = 2.7 × 1000 = 2700 kg/m³. A common error is multiplying by 100 instead of 1000.
