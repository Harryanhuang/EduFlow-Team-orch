# Question Q-5.1-04

**Difficulty**: Standard

**Question**: A steel needle is stroked from end to end with the N pole of a bar magnet, always moving in the same direction. What pole is created at the end where the magnet finishes?

**Answer**: A south pole. The end where the stroking magnet finishes becomes the opposite pole to the stroking pole.

**Explanation**: This is the single-stroke method of magnetisation. A common error is thinking the finishing end becomes the same pole.
