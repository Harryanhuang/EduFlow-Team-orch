# Question Q-5.9-04

**Difficulty**: Standard

**Question**: Explain why the induced current reverses direction when a magnet is pulled out of a coil instead of pushed in.

**Answer**: Pulling the magnet out changes the magnetic field through the coil in the opposite way, so the induced emf and current reverse direction.

**Explanation**: The direction depends on whether flux linkage is increasing or decreasing.
