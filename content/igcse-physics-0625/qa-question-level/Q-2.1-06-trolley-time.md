# Question Q-2.1-06

**Difficulty**: Standard

**Question**: A trolley of mass 2 kg is pushed with a force of 10 N. If the friction is 2 N, how long will it take to reach a speed of 6 m/s from rest?

**Answer**: 1.5 s

**Explanation**: Resultant force = 10 - 2 = 8 N. a = 8/2 = 4 m/s². v = u + at: 6 = 0 + 4t, t = 6/4 = 1.5 s. Common error: forgetting to subtract friction before calculating acceleration.
