# Question Q-5.11-07

**Difficulty**: Challenge

**Question**: Explain how a loudspeaker produces sound from an alternating current signal.

**Answer**: The a.c. in the voice coil changes direction and size. In the magnetic field of a permanent magnet, the coil experiences a changing force, so it moves back and forth. The attached cone vibrates and produces sound waves.

**Explanation**: The frequency of vibration follows the frequency of the electrical signal.
