# Question Q-2.3-06

**Difficulty**: Standard

**Question**: A rollercoaster car of mass 500 kg starts from rest at a height of 30 m. Calculate its speed at the bottom, assuming no friction. (g = 10 m/s²)

**Answer**: 24.5 m/s

**Explanation**: mgh = ½mv². 500 × 10 × 30 = ½ × 500 × v². 150 000 = 250v². v² = 600. v = √600 ≈ 24.5 m/s. A common error is calculating ½ × 10 × 30 = 150 and stopping there.
