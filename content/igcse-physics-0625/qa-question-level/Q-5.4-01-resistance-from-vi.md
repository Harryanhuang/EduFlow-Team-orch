# Question Q-5.4-01

**Difficulty**: Foundation

**Question**: A resistor has a p.d. of 6 V across it and a current of 0.5 A flowing through it. Calculate its resistance.

**Answer**: 12 ohm

**Explanation**: R = V/I = 6/0.5 = 12 ohm. A common error is multiplying instead of dividing.
