# Question Q-3.2-03

**Difficulty**: Standard

**Question**: What is meant by thermal equilibrium?

**Answer**: Two objects in contact are in thermal equilibrium when they are at the same temperature and there is no net flow of thermal energy between them.

**Explanation**: The key is "no net" flow — energy still transfers both ways equally. A common error is saying "no energy transfer at all."
