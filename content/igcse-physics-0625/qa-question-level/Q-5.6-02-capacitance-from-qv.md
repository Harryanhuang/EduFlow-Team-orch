# Question Q-5.6-02

**Difficulty**: Foundation

**Question**: A capacitor stores 0.02 C of charge when connected to a 12 V supply. Calculate its capacitance.

**Answer**: 0.00167 F, or 1670 microF.

**Explanation**: C = Q/V = 0.02/12 = 0.00167 F. Convert to microfarads by multiplying by 10^6 if needed.
