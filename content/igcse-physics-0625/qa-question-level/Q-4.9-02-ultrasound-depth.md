# Question Q-4.9-02

**Difficulty**: Foundation

**Question**: An ultrasound probe sends a pulse into the body. The reflected echo is detected after 0.000 04 s. If the speed of ultrasound in tissue is 1500 m/s, calculate the depth of the reflecting surface.

**Answer**: 0.03 m (or 3 cm)

**Explanation**: Total distance = 1500 × 0.00004 = 0.06 m. Depth = 0.06 / 2 = 0.03 m = 3 cm. A common error is not dividing by 2 for the round trip.
