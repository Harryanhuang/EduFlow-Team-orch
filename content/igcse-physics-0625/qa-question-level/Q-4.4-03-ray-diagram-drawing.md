# Question Q-4.4-03

**Difficulty**: Standard

**Question**: Draw a ray diagram to show how an eye sees the image of a point object in a plane mirror.

**Answer**: Draw two rays from the object to the mirror, reflect them according to i = r, and extend the reflected rays backwards behind the mirror. They meet at the virtual image position.

**Explanation**: At least two rays are needed, with arrowheads showing direction. A common error is drawing rays from the image to the mirror.
