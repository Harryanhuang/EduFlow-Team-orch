# Question Q-5.10-04

**Difficulty**: Standard

**Question**: A wire of length 0.20 m carries a current of 3.0 A at right angles to a magnetic field of flux density 0.50 T. Calculate the force on the wire.

**Answer**: F = BIL = 0.50 × 3.0 × 0.20 = 0.30 N.

**Explanation**: This formula applies when the wire is perpendicular to the magnetic field.
