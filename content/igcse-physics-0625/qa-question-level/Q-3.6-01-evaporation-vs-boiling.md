# Question Q-3.6-01

**Difficulty**: Foundation

**Question**: What is the difference between evaporation and boiling?

**Answer**: Evaporation occurs only at the surface of a liquid and can happen at any temperature. Boiling occurs throughout the liquid and happens only at the boiling point.

**Explanation**: Both location and temperature conditions differ. A common error is saying they are the same process.
