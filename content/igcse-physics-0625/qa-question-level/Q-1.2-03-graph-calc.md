# Question Q-1.2-03

**Difficulty**: Standard

**Question**: A speed-time graph shows a straight line from (0, 0) to (10 s, 20 m/s). Calculate the acceleration and the total distance travelled.

**Answer**: Acceleration = 2.0 m/s², Distance = 100 m

**Explanation**: Acceleration = gradient = 20 / 10 = 2.0 m/s². Distance = area under graph = ½ × 10 × 20 = 100 m. A common error is using distance = speed × time with the final speed (20 × 10 = 200 m) instead of the area.
