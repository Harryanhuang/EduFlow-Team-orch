# Question Q-3.3-03

**Difficulty**: Standard

**Question**: Give two examples of where thermal expansion is a nuisance in everyday life and how it is dealt with.

**Answer**: (1) Railway tracks: gaps left between sections to allow for expansion in hot weather. (2) Bridges: expansion joints (rollers) allow the bridge to expand and contract without damage.

**Explanation**: Both examples need a problem and a solution. A common error is only listing the problem.
