# Question Q-3.3-01

**Difficulty**: Foundation

**Question**: Use the kinetic particle model to explain why a metal rod expands when heated.

**Answer**: When heated, the particles gain energy and vibrate more vigorously. The increased vibration pushes neighbouring particles further apart, increasing the average separation and causing the rod to expand.

**Explanation**: The particles themselves don't get bigger — their spacing increases. A common error is saying "the particles expand."
