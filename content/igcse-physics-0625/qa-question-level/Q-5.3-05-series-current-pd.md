# Question Q-5.3-05

**Difficulty**: Standard

**Question**: A 12 V battery is connected to two resistors in series: 3 ohm and 9 ohm. Calculate the current and the p.d. across each resistor.

**Answer**: Total R = 12 ohm. I = V/R = 12/12 = 1 A. p.d. across 3 ohm = 3 V; p.d. across 9 ohm = 9 V.

**Explanation**: In series, current is the same and voltage divides in proportion to resistance.
