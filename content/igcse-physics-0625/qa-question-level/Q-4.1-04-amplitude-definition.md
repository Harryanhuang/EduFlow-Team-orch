# Question Q-4.1-04

**Difficulty**: Standard

**Question**: What is the amplitude of a wave?

**Answer**: The maximum displacement of a particle from its rest (equilibrium) position, measured from the rest position to a crest or trough.

**Explanation**: Amplitude is half the crest-to-trough distance. A common error is saying it is the distance from crest to trough.
