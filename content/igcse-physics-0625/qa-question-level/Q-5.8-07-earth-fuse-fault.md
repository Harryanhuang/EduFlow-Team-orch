# Question Q-5.8-07

**Difficulty**: Challenge

**Question**: An appliance with a metal case develops a fault where the live wire touches the case. Explain how the earth wire and fuse protect the user.

**Answer**: The metal case becomes connected to earth through the earth wire, giving a low-resistance path for a large fault current. This large current melts the fuse and disconnects the live supply, so the case does not remain live.

**Explanation**: The protection depends on both the earth wire and fuse working together.
