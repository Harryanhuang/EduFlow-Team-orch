# Question Q-5.8-06

**Difficulty**: Standard

**Question**: Describe how double insulation protects a user.

**Answer**: A double-insulated appliance has two layers of insulating material between live parts and the user, so the external case cannot become live. It does not need an earth wire.

**Explanation**: Double insulation is used especially for appliances with plastic cases.
