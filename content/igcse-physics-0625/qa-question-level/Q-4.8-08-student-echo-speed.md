# Question Q-4.8-08

**Difficulty**: Challenge

**Question**: A student stands 165 m from a large wall and claps her hands. She hears the echo 1 second later. Calculate the speed of sound in air from this data.

**Answer**: 330 m/s

**Explanation**: Total distance travelled by sound = 2 × 165 = 330 m (to wall and back). Time = 1 s. Speed = distance/time = 330/1 = 330 m/s. A common error is using 165 m instead of 330 m (forgetting the round trip).
