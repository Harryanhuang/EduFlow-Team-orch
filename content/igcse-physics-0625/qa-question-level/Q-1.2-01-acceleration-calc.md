# Question Q-1.2-01

**Difficulty**: Foundation

**Question**: A car increases its speed from 10 m/s to 25 m/s in 5.0 s. Calculate its acceleration.

**Answer**: 3.0 m/s²

**Explanation**: Use acceleration = change in velocity / time. The change in velocity is 25 - 10 = 15 m/s. Then 15 / 5.0 = 3.0 m/s². Students often give the speed change without dividing by time, or omit the squared unit.
