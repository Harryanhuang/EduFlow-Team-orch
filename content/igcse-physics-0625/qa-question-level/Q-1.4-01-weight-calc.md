# Question Q-1.4-01

**Difficulty**: Foundation

**Question**: An object has a mass of 5 kg. Calculate its weight on Earth. (g = 10 N/kg)

**Answer**: 50 N

**Explanation**: W = mg = 5 × 10 = 50 N. A common error is giving the answer as 5 kg or confusing mass with weight.
