# Question Q-5.6-07

**Difficulty**: Challenge

**Question**: A 1000 microF capacitor is charged to 9 V and then discharged through a lamp. Explain how lamp brightness changes with time.

**Answer**: The lamp is brightest at first because capacitor p.d. and current are largest. As the capacitor discharges, p.d. and current fall, so the lamp gradually dims and goes out.

**Explanation**: Discharge is gradual rather than instant.
