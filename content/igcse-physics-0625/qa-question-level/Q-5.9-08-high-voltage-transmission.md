# Question Q-5.9-08

**Difficulty**: Challenge

**Question**: A transformer is used to transmit electrical power over long distances at high voltage. Explain why this reduces energy loss in cables.

**Answer**: For a given power, increasing voltage reduces current because P = IV. Cable heating loss is I^2R, so a smaller current greatly reduces energy lost as thermal energy.

**Explanation**: High voltage transmission is used to reduce current, not because high voltage itself saves energy.
