# Question Q-2.3-02

**Difficulty**: Foundation

**Question**: Calculate the kinetic energy of a 1000 kg car travelling at 20 m/s.

**Answer**: 200 000 J (or 200 kJ)

**Explanation**: Ek = ½mv² = ½ × 1000 × 20² = 500 × 400 = 200 000 J. A common error is forgetting the ½ or forgetting to square the velocity.
