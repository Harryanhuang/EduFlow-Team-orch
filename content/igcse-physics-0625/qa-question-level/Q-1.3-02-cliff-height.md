# Question Q-1.3-02

**Difficulty**: Foundation

**Question**: A stone is dropped from a cliff and takes 4 seconds to reach the ground. Calculate the height of the cliff. (g = 10 m/s²)

**Answer**: 80 m

**Explanation**: s = ½gt² = ½ × 10 × 4² = 5 × 16 = 80 m. A common error is forgetting the ½ or using v = gt instead.
