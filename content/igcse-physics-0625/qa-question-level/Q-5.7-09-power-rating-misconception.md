# Question Q-5.7-09

**Difficulty**: Challenge

**Question**: A student says, "The appliance with the highest power rating always costs the most to run." Explain why this is not necessarily true.

**Answer**: Cost depends on energy transferred, E = Pt, not power alone. A high-power appliance used briefly may use less energy than a low-power appliance used for many hours.

**Explanation**: Running time matters as well as power.

**Tags**: power, energy, cost, misconception
