# Question Q-1.3-01

**Difficulty**: Foundation

**Question**: An object is dropped from rest and falls for 3 seconds. Calculate its speed at this point. (g = 10 m/s²)

**Answer**: 30 m/s

**Explanation**: v = u + gt = 0 + 10 × 3 = 30 m/s. A common error is using s = ½gt² instead of v = gt when asked for speed.
