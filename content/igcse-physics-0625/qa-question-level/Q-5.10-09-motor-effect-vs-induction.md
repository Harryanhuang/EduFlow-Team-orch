# Question Q-5.10-09

**Difficulty**: Challenge

**Question**: Compare the motor effect and electromagnetic induction.

**Answer**: In the motor effect, a current in a magnetic field produces a force/motion. In electromagnetic induction, motion or changing magnetic field produces an emf/current. They are related but reverse processes.

**Explanation**: One converts electrical energy to mechanical motion; the other converts mechanical/change energy to electrical energy.
