# Question Q-3.1-05

**Difficulty**: Standard

**Question**: Explain why a balloon expands when placed in warm water.

**Answer**: The air particles inside the balloon gain kinetic energy and move faster. They collide with the balloon walls more frequently and with greater force, causing the balloon to expand.

**Explanation**: Temperature increase → faster particles → more forceful collisions. A common error is saying "air expands" without particle-level explanation.
