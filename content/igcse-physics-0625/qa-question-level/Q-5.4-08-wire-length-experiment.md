# Question Q-5.4-08

**Difficulty**: Challenge

**Question**: Describe an experiment to investigate how resistance of a wire depends on length, including how to make it a fair test.

**Answer**: Use a resistance wire, ammeter, voltmeter, power supply, switch, and sliding contact. Measure V and I for different lengths and calculate R = V/I. Keep material, thickness, and temperature constant; use low current or switch off between readings to reduce heating.

**Explanation**: Fair testing requires changing only length.
