# Question Q-4.8-01

**Difficulty**: Foundation

**Question**: A person shouts towards a cliff and hears the echo 3 seconds later. Calculate the distance to the cliff. (Speed of sound = 340 m/s)

**Answer**: 510 m

**Explanation**: Total distance = 340 × 3 = 1020 m. Distance to cliff = 1020 / 2 = 510 m (the sound travels there and back). A common error is not dividing by 2.
