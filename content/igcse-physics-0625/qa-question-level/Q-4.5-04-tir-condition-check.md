# Question Q-4.5-04

**Difficulty**: Standard

**Question**: Light travels from water (n = 1.33) into air. The angle of incidence is 50°. Will total internal reflection occur? (Critical angle for water ≈ 48.8°)

**Answer**: Yes. The light travels from a denser to a less dense medium and the angle of incidence (50°) is greater than the critical angle (48.8°), so total internal reflection occurs.

**Explanation**: Both conditions are met. A common error is comparing 50° to 90° instead of the critical angle.
