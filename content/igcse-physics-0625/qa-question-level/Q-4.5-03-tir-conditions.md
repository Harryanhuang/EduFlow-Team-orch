# Question Q-4.5-03

**Difficulty**: Foundation

**Question**: State the two conditions for total internal reflection to occur.

**Answer**: (1) Light must travel from a more dense (optically denser) medium to a less dense medium. (2) The angle of incidence must be greater than the critical angle.

**Explanation**: Both conditions are essential. A common error is stating only one condition or getting the density direction wrong.
