# Question Q-4.9-01

**Difficulty**: Foundation

**Question**: What is ultrasound? State its frequency range.

**Answer**: Ultrasound is sound with a frequency above 20 000 Hz (20 kHz), which is above the upper limit of human hearing.

**Explanation**: It's just high-frequency sound, not a different type of wave. A common error is saying it's "a different kind of wave."
