### Question Q-S2-04
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: A stem-and-leaf diagram shows: 1|2 3 5, 2|0 4 7 8, 3|1 6. How many data values are there? What is the median?
**Answer**: 10 values. Ordered: 12, 13, 15, 20, 24, 27, 28, 31, 36. Wait — 1|2 = 12. Values: 12,13,15,20,24,27,28,31,36. That's 9. Median = 5th value = 24.
**Explanation**: Read stem|leaf as full numbers. Median of 9 values = 5th. A common error is not reading the stem correctly.
