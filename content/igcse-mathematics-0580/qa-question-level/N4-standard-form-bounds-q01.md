### Question Q-N4-11
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Calculate (5 × 10⁻²) × (4 × 10⁻³). Give your answer in standard form.
**Answer**: 20 × 10⁻⁵ = 2 × 10⁻⁴
**Explanation**: 5×4 = 20, 10⁻² × 10⁻³ = 10⁻⁵. 20 × 10⁻⁵ = 2 × 10⁻⁴ (proper standard form). A common error is 20 × 10⁻⁵ (not adjusting coefficient).
