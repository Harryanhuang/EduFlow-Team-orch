### Question Q-M2-10
**Difficulty**: Challenge
**Question**: A solid is made by placing a hemisphere on top of a cylinder. Both have radius 3 cm. The cylinder has height 10 cm. Find the total volume.
**Answer**: Cylinder: π(3²)(10) = 90π. Hemisphere: (2/3)π(3³) = 18π. Total = 108π ≈ 339.3 cm³.
**Explanation**: Add cylinder volume and hemisphere volume. Hemisphere = (2/3)πr³. A common error is (4/3)πr³ (full sphere).
