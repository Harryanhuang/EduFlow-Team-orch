### Question Q-S4-02
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: A bag has 3 red and 2 blue balls. Two balls are picked without replacement. Use a tree diagram to find P(one of each colour).
**Answer**: P(RB) = 3/5 × 2/4 = 6/20. P(BR) = 2/5 × 3/4 = 6/20. P(one each) = 6/20 + 6/20 = 12/20 = 3/5.
**Explanation**: Two paths give one of each: RB and BR. Add the probabilities. A common error is only calculating one path.
