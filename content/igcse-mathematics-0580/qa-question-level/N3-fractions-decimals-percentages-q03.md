### Question Q-N3-02
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: Write 0.6 as a fraction in its simplest form.
**Answer**: 3/5
**Explanation**: 0.6 = 6/10. Simplify by dividing numerator and denominator by their HCF (2): 6/10 = 3/5. A common error is writing 6/10 without simplifying.
