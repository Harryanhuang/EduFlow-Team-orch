### Question Q-S2-09
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: A cumulative frequency graph shows that 40 students scored below 60 marks and 60 students scored below 80 marks. How many scored between 60 and 80?
**Answer**: 60 - 40 = 20 students.
**Explanation**: Cumulative frequency at 80 minus cumulative frequency at 60. A common error is 60 + 40 = 100.
