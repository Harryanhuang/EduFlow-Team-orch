### Question Q-M2-04
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Calculate the volume of a cone with radius 3 cm and height 7 cm. Use π = 3.14.
**Answer**: (1/3) × π × 3² × 7 = (1/3) × 3.14 × 9 × 7 = 65.94 cm³
**Explanation**: Volume = (1/3)πr²h. A common error is omitting the 1/3.
