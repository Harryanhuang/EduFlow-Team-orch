# Question: Question Q-M3-01
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: What is the formula for the arc length of a sector of a circle?
**Answer**: Arc length = (θ/360) × 2πr = rθ (when θ is in radians).
**Explanation**: Fraction of the circumference. A common error is using the diameter instead of the radius.
