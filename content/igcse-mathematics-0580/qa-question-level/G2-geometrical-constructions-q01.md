### Question Q-G2-09
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Construct a triangle with AB = 7 cm, angle A = 60°, and AC = 5 cm.
**Answer**: Draw AB = 7 cm. Construct 60° at A. Measure 5 cm along the 60° line to get C. Join BC.
**Explanation**: This is the SAS construction. Use compass for the 60° angle. A common error is not keeping compass radius constant.
