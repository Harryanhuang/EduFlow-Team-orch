### Question Q-N3-09
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Express 45 cm as a percentage of 1.5 m.
**Answer**: 30%
**Explanation**: Convert to the same unit: 1.5 m = 150 cm. (45/150) × 100 = 30%. A common error is using 1.5 instead of 150: (45/1.5) = 3000%.
