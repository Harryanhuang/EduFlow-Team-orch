### Question Q-M2-12
**Difficulty**: Challenge
**Question**: A cone has slant height 13 cm and radius 5 cm. Find its volume.
**Answer**: Height = √(13²-5²) = √(169-25) = √144 = 12 cm. Volume = (1/3)π(25)(12) = 100π ≈ 314 cm³.
**Explanation**: Pythagoras for height: h² = l² - r². Then volume formula. A common error is using slant height in the volume formula.
