### Question Q-M2-07
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Find the volume of a sphere with radius 3 cm. Give your answer in terms of π.
**Answer**: (4/3) × π × 3³ = (4/3) × π × 27 = 36π cm³
**Explanation**: Volume = (4/3)πr³. 27 × 4/3 = 36. A common error is 4π × 3² = 36π (using surface area formula).
