### Question Q-A3-07
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: A sequence starts 2, 5, 10, 17, 26. Find a formula for the nth term.
**Answer**: n² + 1
**Explanation**: First differences: 3, 5, 7, 9. Second difference = 2, so n². Check: n²+1: 1+1=2 ✓, 4+1=5 ✓, 9+1=10 ✓. A common error is 2n (not quadratic).
