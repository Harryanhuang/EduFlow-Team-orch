### Question Q-N2-12
**Difficulty**: Challenge
**Question**: The product of two negative integers is 48. Their sum is -14. Find the integers.
**Answer**: Factors of 48: (-6)×(-8) = 48, (-6)+(-8) = -14. Integers: -6 and -8.
**Explanation**: List factor pairs of 48 (both negative). Check which pair sums to -14. A common error is -4 and -12 (product = 48 but sum = -16).
