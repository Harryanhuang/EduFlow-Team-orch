### Question Q-N4-03
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Calculate (2 × 10⁵) × (3 × 10³). Give your answer in standard form.
**Answer**: 6 × 10⁸
**Explanation**: Multiply the coefficients: 2 × 3 = 6. Add the exponents: 5 + 3 = 8. Answer: 6 × 10⁸. A common error is multiplying the exponents: 2×3=6, 5×3=15, giving 6×10¹⁵.
