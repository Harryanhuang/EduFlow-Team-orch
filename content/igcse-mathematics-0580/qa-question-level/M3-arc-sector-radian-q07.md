# Question: Question Q-M3-07
**Difficulty**: Challenge
**Question**: The arc length of a sector is 12 cm and its area is 48 cm². Find the radius.
**Answer**: Area = (1/2) × r × arc length = 6r = 48 → r = 8 cm.
**Explanation**: Use area = (1/2) × r × arc length. A common error is using the circumference formula.
