### Question Q-N5-10
**Difficulty**: Challenge
**Question**: A and B are in the ratio 3:5. B and C are in the ratio 2:7. Find the ratio A:B:C.
**Answer**: A:B = 3:5 and B:C = 2:7. Make B the same: A:B = 6:10, B:C = 10:35. So A:B:C = 6:10:35.
**Explanation**: Make B equal in both ratios. LCM of 5 and 2 is 10. A:B = 6:10, B:C = 10:35. Combined: 6:10:35. A common error is writing 3:5:7.
