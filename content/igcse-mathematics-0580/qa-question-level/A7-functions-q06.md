# Question: Question Q-A7-06
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Describe the transformation that maps f(x) = x² to g(x) = (x − 3)² + 2.
**Answer**: A translation: shift 3 units right (x − 3) and 2 units up (+2). Vertex moves from (0, 0) to (3, 2).
**Explanation**: Inside brackets shift x, outside shift y. A common error is saying "3 units right" when the bracket says +3.
