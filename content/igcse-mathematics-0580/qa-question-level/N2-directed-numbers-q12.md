### Question Q-N2-13
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: Calculate: -3 + (-5) × (-2)
**Answer**: 7
**Explanation**: Follow the order of operations (BIDMAS). Multiplication before addition: (-5) × (-2) = 10 (negative times negative equals positive). Then -3 + 10 = 7. A common error is to work left to right: -3 + (-5) = -8, then -8 × (-2) = 16, which is wrong because addition was done before multiplication. Another common error is getting the sign wrong on the multiplication step (-5 × -2 = -10).
