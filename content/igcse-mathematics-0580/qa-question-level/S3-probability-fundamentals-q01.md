### Question Q-S3-11
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: A box contains 12 red and 8 blue pens. A pen is picked at random. What is the probability it is not blue?
**Answer**: P(not blue) = P(red) = 12/20 = 3/5.
**Explanation**: Not blue means red. 12 red out of 20 total. A common error is 8/20 = 2/5.
