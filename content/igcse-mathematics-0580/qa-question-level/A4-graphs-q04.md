### Question Q-A4-03
**Difficulty**: Challenge
**Question**: Sketch the graph of y = 2x + 1, stating the y-intercept.
**Answer**: Straight line through (0, 1) with gradient 2. For every 1 unit right, go up 2 units.
**Explanation**: y-intercept at (0,1), gradient 2. Passes through (1,3), (2,5), etc. A common error is plotting the gradient as the y-intercept.
