### Question Q-S4-10
**Difficulty**: Challenge
**Question**: Two cards are drawn from a pack of 52 without replacement. What is the probability they are both aces?
**Answer**: P(AA) = 4/52 × 3/51 = 12/2652 = 1/221.
**Explanation**: First ace: 4/52. Second ace (without replacement): 3/51. A common error is 4/52 × 4/52 = 16/2704.
