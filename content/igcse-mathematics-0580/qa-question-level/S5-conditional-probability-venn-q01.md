# Question: Question Q-S5-01
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: What is the formula for conditional probability P(A and B)?
**Answer**: P(A and B) = P(A) × P(B|A) = P(B) × P(A|B). "Multiply the probability of A by the probability of B given A has occurred."
**Explanation**: Conditional probability formula. A common error is just multiplying P(A) × P(B) (which only works if A and B are independent).
