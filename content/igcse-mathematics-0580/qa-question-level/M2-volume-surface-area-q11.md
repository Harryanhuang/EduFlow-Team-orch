### Question Q-M2-09
**Difficulty**: Challenge
**Question**: A hemisphere has radius 6 cm. Find its total surface area (including the base).
**Answer**: Curved surface: 2πr² = 2π(36) = 72π. Base: πr² = 36π. Total = 108π ≈ 339.3 cm².
**Explanation**: Total surface area of hemisphere = 3πr² (curved + flat base). A common error is 2πr² (curved only).
