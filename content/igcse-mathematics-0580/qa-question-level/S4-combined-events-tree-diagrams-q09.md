### Question Q-S4-08
**Difficulty**: Challenge
**Question**: A bag has 4 red, 3 blue, and 3 green balls. Three balls are picked without replacement. What is the probability all three are different colours?
**Answer**: P(RBG) = 4/10 × 3/9 × 3/8 = 36/720. There are 3! = 6 orderings. P = 6 × 36/720 = 216/720 = 3/10.
**Explanation**: Any ordering of R, B, G works. Each ordering has the same probability (numerator: 4×3×3, denominator: 10×9×8). A common error is one ordering only.
