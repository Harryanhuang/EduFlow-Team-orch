### Question Q-N5-09
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: 3 workers can complete a job in 8 days. How long would 6 workers take (working at the same rate)?
**Answer**: 4 days
**Explanation**: Inverse proportion: more workers = less time. 3×8 = 24 worker-days. 24÷6 = 4 days. A common error is direct proportion: 6/3 × 8 = 16 days (wrong — doubling workers halves time).
