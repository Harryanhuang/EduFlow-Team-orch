# Question: Question Q-S5-02
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: What does the notation P(A ∪ B) mean?
**Answer**: P(A ∪ B) is the probability of A or B or both occurring. P(A ∩ B) is the probability of both A and B occurring. P(A) + P(B) − P(A ∩ B).
**Explanation**: Union = OR. Intersection = AND. A common error is adding P(A) + P(B) without subtracting the overlap.
