### Question Q-N3-04
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: A shirt costs $48 after a 20% discount. What was the original price?
**Answer**: $60
**Explanation**: If 80% of original = $48, then original = $48 ÷ 0.8 = $60. A common error is calculating 20% of $48 and adding it back ($48 + $9.60 = $57.60), which is wrong because the 20% was taken from the original, not the sale price.
