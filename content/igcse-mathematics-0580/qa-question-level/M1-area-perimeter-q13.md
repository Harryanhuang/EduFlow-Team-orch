### Question Q-M1-13
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Find the area of an equilateral triangle with side 10 cm.
**Answer**: Height = √(10² - 5²) = √75 = 5√3. Area = ½ × 10 × 5√3 = 25√3 ≈ 43.3 cm².
**Explanation**: Split into two right triangles. Height = √(s² - (s/2)²). A common error is ½ × 10 × 10 = 50.
