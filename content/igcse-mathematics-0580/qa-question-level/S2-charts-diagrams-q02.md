### Question Q-S2-01
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: In a bar chart, the bar for 'Red' has height 15 and the bar for 'Blue' has height 10. How many more people chose Red than Blue?
**Answer**: 15 - 10 = 5 more people.
**Explanation**: Read the heights from the chart and find the difference. A common error is adding: 15 + 10 = 25.
