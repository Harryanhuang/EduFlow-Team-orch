### Question Q-A5-07
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: Solve: 4x + 3y = 25, 2x + 3y = 19.
**Answer**: Subtract: 2x = 6, x = 3. 12+3y=25, 3y=13, y=13/3.
**Explanation**: Subtracting eliminates y. x=3, y=13/3 = 4 1/3. A common error is y = 6 (25-19=6).
