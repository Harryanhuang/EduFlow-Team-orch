### Question Q-N5-07
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: If y is directly proportional to x and y = 15 when x = 3, find y when x = 7.
**Answer**: y = kx where k = 15/3 = 5. When x = 7: y = 5×7 = 35.
**Explanation**: Find constant of proportionality: k = y/x = 15/3 = 5. Then y = 5x. When x=7: y = 35. A common error is adding: 15+4 = 19 (not proportional).
