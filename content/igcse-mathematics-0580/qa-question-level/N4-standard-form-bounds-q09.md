### Question Q-N4-08
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Calculate (4.2 × 10⁻³) + (3.8 × 10⁻⁴). Give your answer in standard form.
**Answer**: 4.2 × 10⁻³ + 0.38 × 10⁻³ = 4.58 × 10⁻³
**Explanation**: Convert to the same power: 3.8 × 10⁻⁴ = 0.38 × 10⁻³. Then 4.2 + 0.38 = 4.58. Answer: 4.58 × 10⁻³. A common error is adding coefficients without adjusting powers: 8.0 × 10⁻⁷.
