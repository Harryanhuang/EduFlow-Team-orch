### Question Q-M2-06
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: A triangular prism has a triangular cross-section with base 6 cm, height 4 cm, and length 10 cm. Find the volume.
**Answer**: Area of triangle = ½ × 6 × 4 = 12 cm². Volume = 12 × 10 = 120 cm³.
**Explanation**: Volume = area of cross-section × length. A common error is 6 × 4 × 10 = 240 (not halving for the triangle).
