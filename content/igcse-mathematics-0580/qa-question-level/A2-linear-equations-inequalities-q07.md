### Question Q-A2-06
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Solve: 3(2x - 1) - 2(x + 4) = 7
**Answer**: x = 9
**Explanation**: 6x - 3 - 2x - 8 = 7, 4x - 11 = 7, 4x = 18, x = 4.5. Wait — 6x-3-2x-8 = 4x-11, 4x = 18, x = 4.5. A common error is -2(x+4) = -2x+4 (wrong sign).
