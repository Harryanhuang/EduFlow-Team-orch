### Question Q-S3-02
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: A bag contains 3 red balls and 7 blue balls. One ball is picked at random. What is the probability it is red?
**Answer**: P(red) = 3/10.
**Explanation**: Favourable (red) = 3, total = 10. A common error is 3/7 (red:blue instead of red:total).
**Tags**: probability, bag, single-event
