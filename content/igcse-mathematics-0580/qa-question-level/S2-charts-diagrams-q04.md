### Question Q-S2-03
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: A pictogram uses a symbol to represent 5 people. The row for 'Monday' has 3 full symbols and 1 half symbol. How many people on Monday?
**Answer**: 3.5 × 5 = 17.5. Since people must be whole numbers, this suggests 17 or 18 (likely 17 or 18 depending on context).
**Explanation**: Each symbol = 5. 3.5 symbols = 17.5. In real data this might round. A common error is 3 × 5 = 15 (ignoring the half symbol).
