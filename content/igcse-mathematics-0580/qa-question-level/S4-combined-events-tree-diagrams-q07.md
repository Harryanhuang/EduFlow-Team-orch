### Question Q-S4-06
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: A box contains 5 chocolates and 3 caramels. Sarah picks two at random without replacement. What is the probability both are the same type?
**Answer**: P(CC) = 5/8 × 4/7 = 20/56. P(AA) = 3/8 × 2/7 = 6/56. P(same) = 26/56 = 13/28.
**Explanation**: Both chocolates or both caramels. Add the two probabilities. A common error is only one path.
