### Question Q-N3-12
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: A car's value drops from $15,000 to $12,000. Find the percentage decrease.
**Answer**: Decrease = $3,000. Percentage = (3000/15000) × 100 = 20%.
**Explanation**: Percentage change = change/original × 100. A common error is 3000/12000 = 25%.
