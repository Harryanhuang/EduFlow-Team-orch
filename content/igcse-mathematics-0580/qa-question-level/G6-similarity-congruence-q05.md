### Question Q-G6-04
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Two similar shapes have areas 20 cm² and 80 cm². Find the linear scale factor.
**Answer**: Area scale factor = 80/20 = 4. Linear scale factor = √4 = 2.
**Explanation**: Area ratio = (linear ratio)². Linear ratio = √(area ratio). A common error is linear scale factor = 4 (not taking the square root).
