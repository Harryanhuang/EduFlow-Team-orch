### Question Q-A3-05
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Find the nth term of the quadratic sequence: 1, 4, 9, 16, 25, ...
**Answer**: n²
**Explanation**: These are square numbers. First differences: 3, 5, 7, 9. Second difference: 2 (constant). nth term = n². A common error is 2n-1 (confusing with odd numbers).
**Tags**: sequences, square-numbers, quadratic
