### Question Q-G6-03
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Triangle ABC is similar to triangle DEF. AB = 4 cm, DE = 6 cm, BC = 5 cm. Find EF.
**Answer**: Scale factor = 6/4 = 1.5. EF = 5 × 1.5 = 7.5 cm.
**Explanation**: Ratio of corresponding sides is constant. EF/BC = DE/AB = 6/4. A common error is EF = 5+2 = 7 (adding the difference).
