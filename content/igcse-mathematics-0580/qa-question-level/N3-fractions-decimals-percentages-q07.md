### Question Q-N3-06
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Calculate 1 1/2 × 2 2/3. Give your answer as a mixed number.
**Answer**: 4
**Explanation**: Convert to improper fractions: 1 1/2 = 3/2, 2 2/3 = 8/3. Multiply: (3×8)/(2×3) = 24/6 = 4. A common error is multiplying whole numbers and fractions separately.
