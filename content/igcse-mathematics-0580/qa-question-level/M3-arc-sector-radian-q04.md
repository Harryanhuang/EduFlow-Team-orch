# Question: Question Q-M3-04
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: A sector has radius 8 cm and arc length 6 cm. Find the angle in radians.
**Answer**: Arc length = rθ → 6 = 8θ → θ = 6/8 = 0.75 radians.
**Explanation**: Use arc length formula rθ. A common error is dividing the wrong way.
