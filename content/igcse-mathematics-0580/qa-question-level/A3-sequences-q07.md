### Question Q-A3-06
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Find the nth term of: 5, 8, 13, 20, 29, ...
**Answer**: n² + 4
**Explanation**: First differences: 3, 5, 7, 9. Second difference = 2. So it's quadratic with a = 2/2 = 1, giving n². n²+4: when n=1: 1+4=5 ✓. A common error is treating it as arithmetic.
