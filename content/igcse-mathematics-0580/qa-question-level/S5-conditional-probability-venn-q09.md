# Question: Question Q-S5-09
**Difficulty**: Challenge
**Question**: Two events A and B are such that P(A) = 3/4, P(B) = 2/3, and P(A ∩ B) = 1/2. Are A and B independent?
**Answer**: P(A) × P(B) = 3/4 × 2/3 = 1/2. P(A ∩ B) = 1/2. Since P(A) × P(B) = P(A ∩ B), A and B are independent.
**Explanation**: Independent if P(A ∩ B) = P(A) × P(B). A common error is assuming independence without checking.
