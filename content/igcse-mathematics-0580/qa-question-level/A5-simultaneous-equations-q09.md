**Difficulty**: Challenge
**Question**: Solve: x + 2y = 7, x² + y = 13.
**Answer**: From first: x = 7-2y. Substitute: (7-2y)² + y = 13, 49-28y+4y²+y=13, 4y²-27y+36=0. y=(27±√(729-576))/8 = (27±√153)/8.
