### Question Q-G6-08
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: A cone has radius 3 cm and height 8 cm. A similar cone has radius 4.5 cm. Find its height.
**Answer**: Scale factor = 4.5/3 = 1.5. Height = 8 × 1.5 = 12 cm.
**Explanation**: Corresponding lengths are in the same ratio. A common error is 8 + 1.5 = 9.5.
