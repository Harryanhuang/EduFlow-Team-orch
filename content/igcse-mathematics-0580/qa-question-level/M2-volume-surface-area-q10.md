### Question Q-M2-08
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: A cylinder has volume 200π cm³ and radius 5 cm. Find its height.
**Answer**: π × 25 × h = 200π, 25h = 200, h = 8 cm.
**Explanation**: πr²h = 200π, h = 200/(25) = 8. A common error is h = 200/5 = 40 (not squaring the radius).
