### Question Q-T3-03
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: A ship sails 12 km on a bearing of 045° from point A. How far north and east has it travelled?
**Answer**: North component = 12 cos 45° = 8.49 km. East component = 12 sin 45° = 8.49 km.
**Explanation**: North = d cos θ, East = d sin θ, where θ is the bearing measured clockwise from North. A common error is swapping the formulas.
