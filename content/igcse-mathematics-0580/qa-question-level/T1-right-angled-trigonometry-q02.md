### Question Q-T1-12
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: A right-angled triangle has hypotenuse 10 cm and one angle 35°. Find the area.
**Answer**: Opposite = 10×sin35° ≈ 5.74, adjacent = 10×cos35° ≈ 8.19. Area = ½×5.74×8.19 ≈ 23.5 cm².
**Explanation**: Find both legs using sin and cos, then ½×base×height. A common error is ½×10×10 = 50.
