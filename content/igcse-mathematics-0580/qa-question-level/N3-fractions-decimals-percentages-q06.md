### Question Q-N3-05
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Write 2/5, 0.45, and 42% in order from smallest to largest.
**Answer**: 2/5 (= 0.40), 42% (= 0.42), 0.45
**Explanation**: Convert all to decimals: 2/5 = 0.4, 42% = 0.42, 0.45 = 0.45. Order: 0.4, 0.42, 0.45. A common error is not converting to the same form before comparing.
**Tags**: ordering, fractions, decimals, percentages
