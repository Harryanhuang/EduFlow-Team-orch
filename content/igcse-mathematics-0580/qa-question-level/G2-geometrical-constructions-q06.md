### Question Q-G2-05
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Construct a 60° angle using only a compass and straightedge.
**Answer**: 1) Draw a line segment AB. 2) Set compass to AB. 3) Draw an arc from A. 4) From where the arc crosses AB, draw another arc crossing the first. 5) Join A to the intersection. The angle is 60°.
**Explanation**: This constructs an equilateral triangle (all angles 60°). A common error is not keeping the compass radius constant.
