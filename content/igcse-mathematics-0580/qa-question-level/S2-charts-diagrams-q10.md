### Question Q-S2-10
**Difficulty**: Challenge
**Question**: In a box plot, the lower quartile is 15, the median is 22, and the upper quartile is 30. What is the interquartile range?
**Answer**: IQR = 30 - 15 = 15
**Explanation**: IQR = upper quartile - lower quartile. A common error is (30+15)/2 = 22.5.
