### Question Q-A5-05
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Solve: 5x - 2y = 11, 3x + 2y = 13.
**Answer**: Add: 8x = 24, x = 3. 15-2y=11, y=2. Check: 3(3)+2(2)=13 ✓.
**Explanation**: Adding eliminates y (opposite signs). x=3, y=2. A common error is subtracting instead.
