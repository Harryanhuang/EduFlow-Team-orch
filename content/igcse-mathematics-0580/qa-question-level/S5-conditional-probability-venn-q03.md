# Question: Question Q-S5-03
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: A bag has 5 red and 3 blue balls. Two balls are drawn without replacement. Find P(both red).
**Answer**: P(first red) = 5/8. P(second red | first red) = 4/7. P(both red) = 5/8 × 4/7 = 20/56 = 5/14.
**Explanation**: Conditional probability without replacement. A common error is using 5/8 × 4/8 (with replacement).
