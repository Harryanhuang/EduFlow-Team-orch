### Question Q-M1-06
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Find the circumference of a circle with diameter 14 cm. Use π = 22/7.
**Answer**: πd = (22/7) × 14 = 44 cm
**Explanation**: Circumference = πd. With π = 22/7, the 7 cancels with 14. A common error is πr² (area formula).
