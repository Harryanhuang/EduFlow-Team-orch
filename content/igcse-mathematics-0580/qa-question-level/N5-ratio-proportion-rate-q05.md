### Question Q-N5-04
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: The ratio of boys to girls in a class is 5:4. There are 27 students. How many are boys?
**Answer**: 15 boys
**Explanation**: Total parts = 5+4 = 9. One part = 27÷9 = 3. Boys = 5×3 = 15. A common error is boys = 5/9 × 27 = 15 (correct method) but confusing which is which.
