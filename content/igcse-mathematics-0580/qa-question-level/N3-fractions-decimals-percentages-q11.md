### Question Q-N3-10
**Difficulty**: Challenge
**Question**: Simplify: (3/4) ÷ (2/5)
**Answer**: 15/8 or 1 7/8
**Explanation**: To divide fractions, multiply by the reciprocal: (3/4) × (5/2) = 15/8. A common error is dividing numerators and denominators: 3÷2 / 4÷5.
