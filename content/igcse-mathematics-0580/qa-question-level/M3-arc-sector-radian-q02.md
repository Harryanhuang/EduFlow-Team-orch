# Question: Question Q-M3-02
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: Calculate the area of a sector with radius 6 cm and angle 60°.
**Answer**: Area = (60/360) × π × 6² = (1/6) × 36π = 6π ≈ 18.85 cm².
**Explanation**: Fraction of full circle area. A common error is forgetting to square the radius.
