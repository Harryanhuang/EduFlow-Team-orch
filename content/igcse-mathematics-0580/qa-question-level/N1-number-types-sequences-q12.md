### Question Q-N1-15
**Difficulty**: Challenge
**Question**: Find the highest common factor (HCF) of 24 and 36.
**Answer**: 12
**Explanation**: List the factors of each number. Factors of 24: 1, 2, 3, 4, 6, 8, 12, 24. Factors of 36: 1, 2, 3, 4, 6, 9, 12, 18, 36. The largest factor common to both is 12. A common error is to find the LCM (72) instead of the HCF, or to stop at a smaller common factor like 6.
