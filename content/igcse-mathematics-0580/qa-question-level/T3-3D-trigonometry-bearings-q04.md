### Question Q-T3-04
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: From point A, point B is 8 km away on a bearing of 120°. Find the coordinates of B relative to A.
**Answer**: North displacement = 8 cos 120° = −4 km (i.e., 4 km South). East displacement = 8 sin 120° = 6.93 km East.
**Explanation**: cos bearing = north component, sin bearing = east component. Watch the sign: cos 120° is negative, so the north component is South.
