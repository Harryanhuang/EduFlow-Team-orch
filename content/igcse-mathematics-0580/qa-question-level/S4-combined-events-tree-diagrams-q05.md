### Question Q-S4-04
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: A spinner with 4 equal sections (1,2,3,4) is spun twice. What is the probability the sum is 5?
**Answer**: Pairs summing to 5: (1,4), (2,3), (3,2), (4,1). 4 out of 16. P = 4/16 = 1/4.
**Explanation**: Total outcomes = 4×4 = 16. List pairs summing to 5. A common error is 5/16.
