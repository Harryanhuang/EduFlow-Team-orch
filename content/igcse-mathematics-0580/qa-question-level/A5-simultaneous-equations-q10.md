### Question Q-A5-09
**Difficulty**: Challenge
**Question**: Solve: 3x - y = 8, x² - 2y = 5.
**Answer**: y = 3x-8. x²-2(3x-8)=5, x²-6x+16=5, x²-6x+11=0. Discriminant: 36-44=-8 < 0. No real solutions.
**Explanation**: Substitute, get quadratic. Discriminant < 0 means no real solutions. A common error is proceeding with complex numbers (not required at IGCSE).
