### Question Q-A4-14
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Find the coordinates where y = x² - 6x + 8 crosses the x-axis.
**Answer**: Set y = 0: x²-6x+8 = 0, (x-2)(x-4) = 0. Points: (2, 0) and (4, 0).
**Explanation**: x-intercepts: y=0, solve quadratic. A common error is finding the vertex instead.
