### Question Q-G1-11
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: A regular polygon has interior angles of 140°. How many sides does it have?
**Answer**: Exterior = 180° - 140° = 40°. n = 360°/40° = 9 sides (nonagon).
**Explanation**: Interior + exterior = 180°. n = 360°/exterior. A common error is 360°/140°.
