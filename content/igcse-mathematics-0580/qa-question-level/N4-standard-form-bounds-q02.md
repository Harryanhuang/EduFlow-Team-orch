### Question Q-N4-01
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: Write 45,000 in standard form.
**Answer**: 4.5 × 10⁴
**Explanation**: Move the decimal point 4 places left: 45,000 = 4.5 × 10⁴. The number must be between 1 and 10, multiplied by a power of 10. A common error is 45 × 10³ (45 is not between 1 and 10).
