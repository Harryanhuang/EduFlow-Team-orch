### Question Q-G3-11
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: In the diagram, lines PQ and RS are parallel. Angle PQR = 55° and angle QRS = 70°. Find angle QRS's alternate angle.
**Answer**: The alternate angle to QRS = 70° (alternate angles are equal when lines are parallel).
**Explanation**: Alternate (Z) angles are equal. A common error is 180°-70° = 110°.
