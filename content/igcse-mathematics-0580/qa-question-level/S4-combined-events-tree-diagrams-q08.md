### Question Q-S4-07
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: A die is thrown and a coin is flipped. What is the probability of getting a 6 and a head?
**Answer**: P(6 and H) = 1/6 × 1/2 = 1/12.
**Explanation**: Independent events: multiply. A common error is 1/6 + 1/2 = 2/3.
