### Question Q-A4-06
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Find the x-intercept and y-intercept of y = 2x - 6.
**Answer**: x-intercept: set y=0, 0=2x-6, x=3 → (3,0). y-intercept: set x=0, y=-6 → (0,-6).
**Explanation**: x-intercept: y=0, solve for x. y-intercept: x=0, read off y. A common error is swapping x and y intercepts.
