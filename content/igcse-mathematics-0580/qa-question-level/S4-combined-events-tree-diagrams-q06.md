### Question Q-S4-05
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Event A has probability 0.3 and event B has probability 0.5. If A and B are independent, find P(A and B).
**Answer**: P(A and B) = P(A) × P(B) = 0.3 × 0.5 = 0.15.
**Explanation**: For independent events, multiply probabilities. A common error is 0.3 + 0.5 = 0.8.
