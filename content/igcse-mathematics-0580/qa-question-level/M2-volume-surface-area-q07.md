### Question Q-M2-05
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Find the surface area of a sphere with radius 5 cm. Use π = 3.14.
**Answer**: 4 × π × 5² = 4 × 3.14 × 25 = 314 cm²
**Explanation**: Surface area = 4πr². A common error is 2πr² (hemisphere without base) or (4/3)πr³ (volume).
