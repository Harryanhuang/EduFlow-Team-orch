### Question Q-A5-02
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Solve: 3x + 2y = 16, x + 2y = 8.
**Answer**: Subtract: 2x = 8, x = 4. 4 + 2y = 8, y = 2. Check: 3(4)+2(2) = 16 ✓.
**Explanation**: Subtracting eliminates y. x=4, y=2. A common error is adding: 4x+4y=24 (not helpful).
