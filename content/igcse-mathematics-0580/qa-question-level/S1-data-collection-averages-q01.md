### Question Q-S1-11
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Five numbers have a median of 8, a mode of 10, and a mean of 7. Give a possible set of five numbers.
**Answer**: Mean = 35 (5×7). Median = 8 (3rd number). Mode = 10 (appears most). One possibility: 3, 5, 8, 10, 10. Sum = 36... no. 2, 6, 8, 10, 10 → sum = 36. Need 35. Try 2, 5, 8, 10, 10 → sum = 35 ✓.
**Explanation**: Work from the constraints. Sum must be 35, middle is 8, 10 appears twice. A common error is not checking all conditions.
