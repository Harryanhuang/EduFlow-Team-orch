### Question Q-A2-14
**Difficulty**: Challenge
**Question**: Rearrange y = (x+2)/(x-1) to make x the subject.
**Answer**: y(x-1) = x+2, yx-y = x+2, yx-x = y+2, x(y-1) = y+2, x = (y+2)/(y-1).
**Explanation**: Multiply both sides by (x-1), collect x terms, factor, divide. A common error is not collecting x terms on one side.
