### Question Q-N3-07
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: A price of $350 increases by 15%. Calculate the new price.
**Answer**: $402.50
**Explanation**: New price = $350 × 1.15 = $402.50. Alternatively: 15% of $350 = $52.50, new price = $350 + $52.50 = $402.50. A common error is subtracting instead of adding, or calculating 15% incorrectly.
