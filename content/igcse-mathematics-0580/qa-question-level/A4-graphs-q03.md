### Question Q-A4-02
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: Write down the gradient and y-intercept of y = 3x - 4.
**Answer**: Gradient = 3, y-intercept = -4
**Explanation**: In y = mx + c, m is the gradient and c is the y-intercept. A common error is y-intercept = 4 (ignoring the negative sign).
**Tags**: gradient, y-intercept, y=mx+c
