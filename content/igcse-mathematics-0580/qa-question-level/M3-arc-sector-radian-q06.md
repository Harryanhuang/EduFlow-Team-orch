# Question: Question Q-M3-06
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: What is the formula for the area of a segment of a circle?
**Answer**: Area of segment = Area of sector − Area of triangle = (1/2)r²(θ − sinθ) when θ is in radians.
**Explanation**: Sector minus triangle. A common error is forgetting to convert to radians.
