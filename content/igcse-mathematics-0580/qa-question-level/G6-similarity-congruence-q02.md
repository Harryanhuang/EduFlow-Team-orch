### Question Q-G6-01
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: State the four conditions for triangle congruence.
**Answer**: SSS (three sides equal), SAS (two sides and included angle), ASA/AAS (two angles and a side), RHS (right angle, hypotenuse, one other side).
**Explanation**: These are the four congruence tests. A common error is SSA (which is not sufficient for congruence).
