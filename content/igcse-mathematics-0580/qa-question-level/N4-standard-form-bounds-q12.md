### Question Q-N4-12
**Difficulty**: Challenge
**Question**: A number is rounded to 2 decimal places and gives 3.46. Find the lower bound.
**Answer**: 3.455
**Explanation**: If rounded to 2 d.p., the lower bound is 3.455 (anything from 3.455 rounds up to 3.46). A common error is 3.45.
