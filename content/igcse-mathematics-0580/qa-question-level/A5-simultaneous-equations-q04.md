### Question Q-A5-03
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Solve: y = 2x + 1, 3x + y = 11.
**Answer**: Substitute: 3x + (2x+1) = 11, 5x = 10, x = 2. y = 2(2)+1 = 5.
**Explanation**: Substitute y = 2x+1 into second equation. x=2, y=5. A common error is 3x + 2x = 11, forgetting the +1.
