### Question Q-N4-05
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Write 7.85 × 10⁶ as an ordinary number.
**Answer**: 7,850,000
**Explanation**: Move the decimal point 6 places right: 7.85 → 7,850,000. A common error is moving it 5 places (785,000) or 7 places (78,500,000).
