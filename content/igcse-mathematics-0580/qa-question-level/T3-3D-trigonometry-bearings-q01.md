### Question Q-T3-01
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: What is a bearing? Express a bearing as a three-digit number.
**Answer**: A bearing is a direction measured clockwise from North, written as 000° to 360°. North = 000°, East = 090°, South = 180°, West = 270°.
**Explanation**: Always write three digits. A common error is writing 90° instead of 090°.
