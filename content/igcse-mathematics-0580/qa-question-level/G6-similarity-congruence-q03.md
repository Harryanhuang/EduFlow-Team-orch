### Question Q-G6-02
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: Two triangles are similar. One has sides 3, 4, 5. The other has sides 6, 8, 10. What is the scale factor?
**Answer**: 6/3 = 8/4 = 10/5 = 2. Scale factor = 2.
**Explanation**: Corresponding sides are in the same ratio. A common error is 6-3 = 3 (using difference, not ratio).
