### Question Q-A6-11
**Difficulty**: Challenge
**Question**: Solve: (x+1)(x-2) = 4
**Answer**: x²-x-2 = 4, x²-x-6 = 0, (x-3)(x+2) = 0, x = 3 or x = -2
**Explanation**: Expand first, then bring all terms to one side. A common error is x+1=4 or x-2=4 (treating each bracket separately).
