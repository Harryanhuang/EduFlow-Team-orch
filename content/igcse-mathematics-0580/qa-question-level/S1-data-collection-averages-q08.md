### Question Q-S1-06
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: The heights of 5 students are: 155, 160, 162, 158, 165 cm. Find the median height.
**Answer**: Ordered: 155, 158, 160, 162, 165. Median = 160 cm.
**Explanation**: Order first, then find the middle (3rd of 5). A common error is the unsorted middle value.
