# Question: Question Q-M3-09
**Difficulty**: Challenge
**Question**: A frustum has radii 8 cm and 12 cm and height 10 cm. Find its volume.
**Answer**: V = (1/3)πh(R² + Rr + r²) = (1/3)π(10)(144 + 96 + 64) = (3040/3)π ≈ 3183 cm³.
**Explanation**: Frustum formula. A common error is using the wrong radii.
