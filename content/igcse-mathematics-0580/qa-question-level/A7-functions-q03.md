# Question: Question Q-A7-03
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Sketch the graph of f(x) = x² and describe its key features.
**Answer**: A parabola opening upwards, vertex at (0, 0), y ≥ 0, symmetrical about the y-axis, passes through (−1, 1), (1, 1), (2, 4), etc.
**Explanation**: Basic quadratic shape. A common error is forgetting the axis of symmetry.
