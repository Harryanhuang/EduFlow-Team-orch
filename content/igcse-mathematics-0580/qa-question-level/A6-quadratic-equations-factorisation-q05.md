### Question Q-A6-04
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Solve: x² - 3x - 10 = 0
**Answer**: (x-5)(x+2) = 0, x = 5 or x = -2
**Explanation**: Multiply to -10, add to -3: -5 and +2. (x-5)(x+2)=0. A common error is (x+5)(x-2) = x²+3x-10 (wrong sign on middle term).
