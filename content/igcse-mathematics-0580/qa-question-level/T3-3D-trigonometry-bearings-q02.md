### Question Q-T3-02
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: Sketch a diagram showing a bearing of 125° from point A. Describe the direction.
**Answer**: Draw a North line at A. Measure 125° clockwise from North. The direction is in the South-East quadrant, between South (180°) and East (090°).
**Explanation**: Clockwise from North. A common error is measuring anticlockwise instead.
