### Question Q-N3-03
**Difficulty**: Challenge
**Question**: Calculate 2/3 + 1/4. Give your answer as a fraction in its simplest form.
**Answer**: 11/12
**Explanation**: Find a common denominator: LCM of 3 and 4 is 12. 2/3 = 8/12, 1/4 = 3/12. 8/12 + 3/12 = 11/12. A common error is adding numerators and denominators directly: 2/3 + 1/4 = 3/7.
