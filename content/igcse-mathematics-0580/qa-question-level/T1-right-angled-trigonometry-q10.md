### Question Q-T1-08
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: A right-angled triangle has sides 5, 12, 13. Find the angle opposite the side of length 12.
**Answer**: sin θ = 12/13, θ = sin⁻¹(12/13) ≈ 67.4°.
**Explanation**: Opposite = 12, hypotenuse = 13. sin⁻¹(12/13). A common error is tan⁻¹(12/5).
