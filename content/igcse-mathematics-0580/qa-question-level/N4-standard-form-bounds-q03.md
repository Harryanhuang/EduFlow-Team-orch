### Question Q-N4-02
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: Write 0.0032 in standard form.
**Answer**: 3.2 × 10⁻³
**Explanation**: Move the decimal point 3 places right: 0.0032 = 3.2 × 10⁻³. The power is negative because the number is less than 1. A common error is 3.2 × 10³ (wrong sign on the power).
