### Question Q-N4-09
**Difficulty**: Challenge
**Question**: The number of bacteria in a culture is 2.4 × 10⁸. This is divided equally into 6000 petri dishes. How many bacteria per dish? Give your answer in standard form.
**Answer**: 2.4 × 10⁸ ÷ 6000 = 2.4 × 10⁸ ÷ (6 × 10³) = 0.4 × 10⁵ = 4 × 10⁴
**Explanation**: 6000 = 6 × 10³. (2.4 × 10⁸) ÷ (6 × 10³) = (2.4÷6) × 10⁽⁸⁻³⁾ = 0.4 × 10⁵ = 4 × 10⁴. A common error is 0.4 × 10⁵ without converting to proper standard form.
