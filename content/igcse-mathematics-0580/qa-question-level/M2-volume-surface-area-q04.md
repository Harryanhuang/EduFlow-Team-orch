### Question Q-M2-02
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Calculate the volume of a cylinder with radius 4 cm and height 10 cm. Use π = 3.14.
**Answer**: π × 4² × 10 = 3.14 × 16 × 10 = 502.4 cm³
**Explanation**: Volume = πr²h. A common error is 2πrh (curved surface area).
