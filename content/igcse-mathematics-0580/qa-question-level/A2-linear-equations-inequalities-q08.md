### Question Q-A2-07
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Solve the inequality: 2x - 5 < 9. Represent your answer on a number line.
**Answer**: 2x < 14, x < 7. Open circle at 7, arrow to the left.
**Explanation**: 2x < 14, x < 7. Open circle (not ≤). A common error is a filled-in circle at 7.
