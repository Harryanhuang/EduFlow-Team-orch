### Question Q-A3-03
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: The nth term of a sequence is 2n² + 1. Find the 5th term.
**Answer**: 2(25) + 1 = 51
**Explanation**: Substitute n=5: 2(5²) + 1 = 2(25) + 1 = 51. A common error is 2(5)² + 1 = 10² + 1 = 101 (not squaring 5 first).
