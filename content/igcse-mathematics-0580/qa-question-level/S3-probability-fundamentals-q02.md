### Question Q-S3-12
**Difficulty**: Challenge
**Question**: The probability that a student passes Maths is 0.7 and passes English is 0.8. The probability they pass both is 0.6. What is the probability they pass at least one?
**Answer**: P(M or E) = P(M) + P(E) - P(M and E) = 0.7 + 0.8 - 0.6 = 0.9.
**Explanation**: Inclusion-exclusion formula. A common error is 0.7 + 0.8 = 1.5 (not subtracting the overlap).
