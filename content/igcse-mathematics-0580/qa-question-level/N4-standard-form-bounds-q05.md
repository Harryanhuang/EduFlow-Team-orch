### Question Q-N4-04
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Calculate (6 × 10⁷) ÷ (2 × 10³). Give your answer in standard form.
**Answer**: 3 × 10⁴
**Explanation**: Divide the coefficients: 6 ÷ 2 = 3. Subtract the exponents: 7 - 3 = 4. Answer: 3 × 10⁴. A common error is dividing the exponents instead of subtracting.
