### Question Q-S4-01
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: A coin is flipped three times. Draw a tree diagram. What is the probability of exactly 2 heads?
**Answer**: Outcomes with exactly 2 heads: HHT, HTH, THH. Each has probability 1/8. P = 3/8.
**Explanation**: Tree has 2³ = 8 outcomes. Each outcome = (1/2)³ = 1/8. Three outcomes have exactly 2 heads. A common error is 2/8 = 1/4.
