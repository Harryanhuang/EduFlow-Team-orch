### Question Q-S4-09
**Difficulty**: Challenge
**Question**: A machine produces defective items with probability 0.1. What is the probability that in a sample of 3 items, at least one is defective?
**Answer**: P(at least 1 defective) = 1 - P(none) = 1 - 0.9³ = 1 - 0.729 = 0.271.
**Explanation**: None defective: 0.9³. At least one = 1 - none. A common error is 0.1 × 3 = 0.3.
