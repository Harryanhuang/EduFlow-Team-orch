### Question Q-N5-06
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Map scale is 1:50,000. Two towns are 8.5 cm apart on the map. Find the real distance in km.
**Answer**: 8.5 × 50,000 = 425,000 cm = 4,250 m = 4.25 km
**Explanation**: Real distance = 8.5 × 50,000 = 425,000 cm. Convert: ÷100 = 4,250 m, ÷1000 = 4.25 km. A common error is forgetting to convert cm to km.
