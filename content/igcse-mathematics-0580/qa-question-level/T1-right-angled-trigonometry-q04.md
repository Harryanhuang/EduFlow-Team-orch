### Question Q-T1-02
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: In a right-angled triangle, the adjacent side to angle θ is 4 and the opposite is 3. Find tan θ.
**Answer**: tan θ = 3/4 = 0.75
**Explanation**: TOA: tan θ = opposite/adjacent. A common error is adjacent/opposite (1/tan = cot).
