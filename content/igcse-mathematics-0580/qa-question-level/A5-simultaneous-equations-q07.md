### Question Q-A5-06
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: Solve: 2x + y = 9, x - 3y = 1.
**Answer**: From first: y = 9-2x. Substitute: x-3(9-2x)=1, x-27+6x=1, 7x=28, x=4. y=9-8=1.
**Explanation**: y = 9-2x, substitute into second. x=4, y=1. A common error is x-27+2x=1 (not distributing -3 correctly).
