### Question Q-N4-10
**Difficulty**: Challenge
**Question**: A rectangle has length 8.3 cm and width 5.7 cm, both correct to 1 decimal place. Find the lower bound of the area.
**Answer**: Lower bounds: length = 8.25, width = 5.65. Area lower bound = 8.25 × 5.65 = 46.6125 cm².
**Explanation**: For bounds of a product, use the lower bounds of each measurement. 8.25 × 5.65 = 46.6125. A common error is using 8.2 and 5.6 (subtracting 0.1 instead of 0.05).
