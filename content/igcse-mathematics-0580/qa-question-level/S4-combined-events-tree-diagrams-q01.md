### Question Q-S4-11
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: A bag has 6 red and 4 green balls. Two are picked with replacement. What is the probability they are different colours?
**Answer**: P(RG) = 6/10 × 4/10 = 24/100. P(GR) = 4/10 × 6/10 = 24/100. P(different) = 48/100 = 12/25.
**Explanation**: Two orderings: RG and GR. With replacement, probabilities stay the same. A common error is one path only.
