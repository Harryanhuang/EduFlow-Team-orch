### Question Q-G6-11
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Two similar rectangles have perimeters 20 cm and 30 cm. The smaller has area 24 cm². Find the area of the larger.
**Answer**: Linear ratio = 30/20 = 3/2. Area ratio = (3/2)² = 9/4. Area = 24 × 9/4 = 54 cm².
**Explanation**: Area scales by square of linear ratio. A common error is 24 × 3/2 = 36.
