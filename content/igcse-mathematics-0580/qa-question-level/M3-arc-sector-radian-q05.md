# Question: Question Q-M3-05
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Find the area of a sector with radius 10 cm and angle 2.5 radians.
**Answer**: Area = (1/2) × r² × θ = 0.5 × 100 × 2.5 = 125 cm².
**Explanation**: Formula (1/2)r²θ for radians. A common error is using degrees formula.
