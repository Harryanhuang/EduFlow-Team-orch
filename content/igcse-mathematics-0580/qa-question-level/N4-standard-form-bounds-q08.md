### Question Q-N4-07
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: A length is given as 12.5 cm correct to 1 decimal place. Find the upper bound.
**Answer**: 12.55 cm
**Explanation**: If rounded to 1 d.p., the actual value could be anything from 12.45 to just under 12.55. Upper bound = 12.55 cm (or 12.54999...). A common error is 12.6 (the next 1 d.p. value, not the bound).
