### Question Q-A6-09
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Solve: x² - 8x + 16 = 0
**Answer**: (x-4)² = 0, x = 4 (repeated root)
**Explanation**: Perfect square: (x-4)². One repeated root x=4. Discriminant = 64-64=0 confirms repeated root. A common error is x=4 and x=-4.
