### Question Q-A6-08
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Sketch the graph of y = x² - 4x + 3, stating the roots and the vertex.
**Answer**: Roots: (x-1)(x-3)=0, x=1 and x=3. Vertex: completing the square gives (x-2)²-1, vertex at (2, -1).
**Explanation**: Factorise for roots, complete the square for vertex. A common error is vertex at (4, 3) (reading coefficients).
