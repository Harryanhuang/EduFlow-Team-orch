### Question Q-A5-11
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Solve: 2x + 3y = 1, x - y = 3.
**Answer**: From second: x = y+3. Substitute: 2(y+3)+3y = 1, 5y+6=1, y=-1. x=2.
**Explanation**: x = y+3, substitute. y = -1, x = 2. Check: 2(2)+3(-1) = 1 ✓. A common error is sign errors.
