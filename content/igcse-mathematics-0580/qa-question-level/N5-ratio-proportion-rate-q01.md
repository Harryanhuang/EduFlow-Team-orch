### Question Q-N5-11
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: A map has scale 1:25,000. A field measures 4 cm by 3 cm on the map. Find the real area in km².
**Answer**: Real: 100,000 cm × 75,000 cm = 1 km × 0.75 km = 0.75 km².
**Explanation**: 4 cm × 25,000 = 100,000 cm = 1 km. 3 cm × 25,000 = 75,000 cm = 0.75 km. Area = 0.75 km². A common error is 12 cm² × 25,000 = 300,000 (not squaring the scale for area).
