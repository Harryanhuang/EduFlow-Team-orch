### Question Q-S1-03
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: Find the mode of: 3, 7, 8, 5, 12, 7, 3.
**Answer**: Bimodal: 3 and 7 (both appear twice).
**Explanation**: Mode is the most frequent value. Both 3 and 7 appear twice. A common error is saying there is no mode.
