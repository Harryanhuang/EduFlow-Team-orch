# Question: Question Q-S5-04
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: In a Venn diagram, n(A) = 20, n(B) = 15, n(A ∩ B) = 8, n(U) = 40. Find n(A ∪ B).
**Answer**: n(A ∪ B) = n(A) + n(B) − n(A ∩ B) = 20 + 15 − 8 = 27.
**Explanation**: Union formula for sets. A common error is forgetting to subtract the overlap.
