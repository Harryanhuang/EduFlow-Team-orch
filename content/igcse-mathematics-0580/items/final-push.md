### Question Q-N1-14
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Express 180 as a product of prime factors.
**Answer**: 180 = 2² × 3² × 5
**Explanation**: 180 = 2 × 90 = 2 × 2 × 45 = 2 × 2 × 3 × 15 = 2 × 2 × 3 × 3 × 5. A common error is 2 × 3 × 5 × 6 (6 is not prime).
**Tags**: prime-factors

### Question Q-A2-14

**Question**: Rearrange y = (x+2)/(x-1) to make x the subject.
**Answer**: y(x-1) = x+2, yx-y = x+2, yx-x = y+2, x(y-1) = y+2, x = (y+2)/(y-1).

**Question**: Find the area of an equilateral triangle with side 10 cm.

### Question Q-S2-10
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: In a box plot, the lower quartile is 15, the median is 22, and the upper quartile is 30. What is the interquartile range?
**Answer**: IQR = 30 - 15 = 15
**Explanation**: IQR = upper quartile - lower quartile. A common error is (30+15)/2 = 22.5.
