### Question Q-S1-01
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: Find the mean of: 3, 7, 8, 5, 12.
**Answer**: (3+7+8+5+12)/5 = 35/5 = 7
**Explanation**: Mean = sum of values ÷ number of values. A common error is not dividing by the count.
**Tags**: mean, averages

### Question Q-S1-02
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: Find the median of: 3, 7, 8, 5, 12.
**Answer**: Ordered: 3, 5, 7, 8, 12. Median = 7 (middle value).
**Explanation**: Must order first. With 5 values, the median is the 3rd. A common error is 8 (not ordering).
**Tags**: median, averages

### Question Q-S1-03

### Question Q-S1-05
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: The mean of 4, 7, x, and 9 is 6. Find x.
**Answer**: (4+7+x+9)/4 = 6, 20+x = 24, x = 4.
**Explanation**: Set up the mean equation and solve for x. A common error is 4+7+9+x = 6 (not multiplying by 4).

### Question Q-S1-06

### Question Q-S1-07
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: A frequency table shows: Score 1 (3 people), Score 2 (5 people), Score 3 (2 people). Find the mean score.
**Answer**: Mean = (1×3 + 2×5 + 3×2)/(3+5+2) = (3+10+6)/10 = 19/10 = 1.9
**Explanation**: Weighted mean: sum of (score × frequency) ÷ total frequency. A common error is (1+2+3)/3 = 2.
**Tags**: mean, frequency-table, weighted

### Question Q-S1-08
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Classify the data: the number of pets owned by each student in a class. Is it discrete or continuous?
**Answer**: Discrete. The number of pets is countable (0, 1, 2, 3, ...). It cannot take any value within a range.
**Explanation**: Discrete = countable, whole numbers. Continuous = measurable, any value. A common error is saying continuous.
**Tags**: data-types, discrete, classification

### Question Q-S1-09
**Difficulty**: Challenge
**Question**: The mean of 6 numbers is 10. One number is removed and the mean of the remaining 5 is 8. What number was removed?
**Answer**: Sum of 6 = 60. Sum of 5 = 40. Removed number = 60 - 40 = 20.
**Explanation**: Total before - total after = removed value. A common error is 10 - 8 = 2.
**Tags**: mean, removed-value, reasoning

### Question Q-S1-10
**Difficulty**: Challenge
**Question**: A group of 8 people has a mean age of 25. A group of 12 people has a mean age of 30. Find the combined mean age.
**Answer**: Total ages: 8×25 + 12×30 = 200 + 360 = 560. Total people: 20. Combined mean = 560/20 = 28.
**Explanation**: Combined mean = total of all values ÷ total count. A common error is (25+30)/2 = 27.5 (simple average of means).
**Tags**: mean, combined, weighted
