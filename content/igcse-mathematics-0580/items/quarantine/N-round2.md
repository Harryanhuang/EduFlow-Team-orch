### Question Q-N2-11
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Calculate: (-1)¹⁰⁰ + (-1)¹⁰¹
**Answer**: (-1)¹⁰⁰ = 1 (even power), (-1)¹⁰¹ = -1 (odd power). 1 + (-1) = 0.
**Explanation**: Even power of -1 = 1, odd = -1. A common error is both are 1 or both are -1.

### Question Q-N4-11

### Question Q-N5-11
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: A map has scale 1:25,000. A field measures 4 cm by 3 cm on the map. Find the real area in km².
**Answer**: Real: 100,000 cm × 75,000 cm = 1 km × 0.75 km = 0.75 km².
**Explanation**: 4 cm × 25,000 = 100,000 cm = 1 km. 3 cm × 25,000 = 75,000 cm = 0.75 km. Area = 0.75 km². A common error is 12 cm² × 25,000 = 300,000 (not squaring the scale for area).
**Tags**: ratio, scale, area

### Question Q-N1-13
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Find the HCF of 105 and 147.
**Answer**: 105 = 3×5×7, 147 = 3×7². HCF = 3×7 = 21.
**Explanation**: Prime factorise both. Common factors: 3 and 7. HCF = 21. A common error is 7 (only one common prime).
