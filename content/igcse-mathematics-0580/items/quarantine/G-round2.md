### Question Q-G1-11
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: A regular polygon has interior angles of 140°. How many sides does it have?
**Answer**: Exterior = 180° - 140° = 40°. n = 360°/40° = 9 sides (nonagon).
**Explanation**: Interior + exterior = 180°. n = 360°/exterior. A common error is 360°/140°.
**Tags**: interior-angles, regular-polygon

### Question Q-G2-09
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Construct a triangle with AB = 7 cm, angle A = 60°, and AC = 5 cm.
**Answer**: Draw AB = 7 cm. Construct 60° at A. Measure 5 cm along the 60° line to get C. Join BC.
**Explanation**: This is the SAS construction. Use compass for the 60° angle. A common error is not keeping compass radius constant.
**Tags**: construction, SAS, triangle

### Question Q-G3-11
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: In the diagram, lines PQ and RS are parallel. Angle PQR = 55° and angle QRS = 70°. Find angle QRS's alternate angle.
**Answer**: The alternate angle to QRS = 70° (alternate angles are equal when lines are parallel).
**Explanation**: Alternate (Z) angles are equal. A common error is 180°-70° = 110°.
**Tags**: angles, parallel-lines, alternate

### Question Q-G4-11
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: In a circle, AB is a diameter. C is on the circumference. Angle CAB = 35°. Find angle ABC.
**Answer**: Angle ACB = 90° (angle in semicircle). In triangle ABC: 35° + 90° + ABC = 180°. ABC = 55°.
**Explanation**: Angle in semicircle = 90°. Triangle angles sum to 180°. A common error is 35° (thinking it's isosceles).
**Tags**: circle-theorems, semicircle, triangle

### Question Q-G5-11
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Describe the transformation that maps y = x² to y = x² + 3.
**Answer**: Translation by vector (0, 3). The graph shifts up by 3 units.
**Explanation**: Adding to y shifts vertically. A common error is a horizontal shift.
**Tags**: transformations, translation, quadratic-graph

### Question Q-G6-11
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Two similar rectangles have perimeters 20 cm and 30 cm. The smaller has area 24 cm². Find the area of the larger.
**Answer**: Linear ratio = 30/20 = 3/2. Area ratio = (3/2)² = 9/4. Area = 24 × 9/4 = 54 cm².
**Explanation**: Area scales by square of linear ratio. A common error is 24 × 3/2 = 36.
**Tags**: similarity, area, perimeter
