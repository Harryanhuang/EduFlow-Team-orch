**Question**: Find the equation of the line perpendicular to y = 2x + 1 passing through (4, 3).

### Question Q-A6-13
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Solve: 4x² - 25 = 0.
**Answer**: 4x² = 25, x² = 25/4, x = ±5/2 = ±2.5. Or: (2x+5)(2x-5) = 0.
**Explanation**: Difference of squares: (2x+5)(2x-5) = 0. x = -5/2 or 5/2. A common error is x = 5/2 only.
**Tags**: quadratic, difference-of-squares
