A particle moves along a line with velocity v(t) = 3t squared minus 2 (in meters per second). The displacement of the particle from t = 0 to t = 2 is

## Options (if mcq)
A) 2 m
B) 4 m
C) 6 m
D) 8 m

## Answer
B

## Explanation
Displacement = integral from 0 to 2 of v(t) dt = integral of (3t squared minus 2) dt = [t cubed minus 2t] from 0 to 2 = (8 minus 4) minus 0 = 4 m.
