The volume of the solid obtained by rotating the region bounded by x = y squared, x = 4, and the y-axis (y is greater than or equal to 0) about the y-axis is

## Options (if mcq)
A) (32/5)pi
B) (64/5)pi
C) (128/5)pi
D) (256/5)pi

## Answer
C

## Explanation
Using washers perpendicular to y-axis: outer radius R = 4 (distance from y-axis to x = 4), inner radius r = y squared (distance from y-axis to x = y squared).
Bounds: y from 0 to 2 (since x = y squared, at x = 4, y = 2).
V = pi times integral from 0 to 2 of (R squared minus r squared) dy = pi times integral of (16 minus y to the fourth) dy = pi [16y minus y to the fifth / 5] from 0 to 2 = pi (32 minus 32/5) = (160 minus 32)/5 pi = (128/5) pi.
