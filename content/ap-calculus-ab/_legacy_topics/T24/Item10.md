The average value of f(x) = cosine x on the interval [0, pi] is

## Options (if mcq)
A) 0
B) 2/pi
C) 1/pi
D) pi/2

## Answer
A

## Explanation
Average value = (1/(pi minus 0)) times the integral from 0 to pi of cosine x dx = (1/pi)[sine x] from 0 to pi = (1/pi)(0 minus 0) = 0.
By symmetry (cosine is positive on [0, pi/2] and negative on [pi/2, pi]), the areas cancel out.
