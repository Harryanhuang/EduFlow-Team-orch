What is the derivative of f(x) = e^x?

## Options
A) e^x
B) x * e^(x-1)
C) ln(x)
D) e^x / x

## Answer
A) e^x

## Explanation
The exponential function e^x is its own derivative: d/dx[e^x] = e^x. This is a unique and fundamental property of the natural exponential function.
