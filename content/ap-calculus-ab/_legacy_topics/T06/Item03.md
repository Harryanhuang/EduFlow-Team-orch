What is the derivative of f(x) = sin(x)?

## Options
A) cos(x)
B) -cos(x)
C) -sin(x)
D) tan(x)

## Answer
A) cos(x)

## Explanation
This is a fundamental derivative rule: d/dx[sin(x)] = cos(x). This is one of the six basic trigonometric derivative results that AP Calculus students must know.
