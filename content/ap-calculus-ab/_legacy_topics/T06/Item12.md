What is the derivative of f(x) = 5e^x + 3*ln(x)?

## Options
A) 5e^x + 3/x
B) 5e^x + 3*ln(x)
C) 5x*e^(x-1) + 3/x
D) 5e^x - 3/x

## Answer
A) 5e^x + 3/x

## Explanation
d/dx[5e^x] = 5e^x and d/dx[3*ln(x)] = 3/x. So f'(x) = 5e^x + 3/x.
