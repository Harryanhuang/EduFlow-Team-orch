**Q:** A particle moves with velocity v(t)=sin(t). Using n=6, estimate total distance traveled from t=0 to t=π with a midpoint Riemann sum.

**A:** Δt = π/6. Midpoints: π/12, π/4, 5π/12, 7π/12, 3π/4, 11π/12
M₆ = (π/6)[sin(π/12)+sin(π/4)+sin(5π/12)+sin(7π/12)+sin(3π/4)+sin(11π/12)]
≈ (π/6)[0.259+0.707+0.966+0.966+0.707+0.259] ≈ (π/6)(3.864) ≈ 2.02
Exact total distance = 4 (since v>0 on (0,π))
