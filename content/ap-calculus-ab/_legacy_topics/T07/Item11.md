Let $f(x) = x^3 \sin(2x)$. What is the value of $f'(1.5)$?

## Options
A) $-5.730$
B) $-1.485$
C) $3.691$
D) $6.204$

## Answer
A

## Explanation
Using the product and chain rules: $f'(x) = 3x^2 \sin(2x) + 2x^3 \cos(2x)$. At $x = 1.5$: $f'(1.5) = 3(2.25)\sin(3) + 2(3.375)\cos(3) \approx 6.75(0.14112) + 6.75(-0.98999) \approx 0.9526 - 6.6824 = -5.730$ (calculator active).
