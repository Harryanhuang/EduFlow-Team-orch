The function $f(x) = x^2 \cos x$ has a horizontal tangent line at $x = \frac{\pi}{2}$. What is the value of $f'(0)$?

## Options
A) $0$
B) $1$
C) $-1$
D) $2$

## Answer
A

## Explanation
Using the product rule: $f'(x) = 2x \cos x - x^2 \sin x$. At $x = 0$: $f'(0) = 2(0)\cos(0) - (0)^2 \sin(0) = 0 - 0 = 0$.
