If y = ln(4x^2 - 1), what is dy/dx?

## Options
A) 1/(4x^2 - 1)
B) 8x/(4x^2 - 1)
C) 4x/(4x^2 - 1)
D) (4x^2 - 1)/(8x)

## Answer
B

## Explanation
Outer: ln(u), inner: u = 4x^2 - 1. d/dx[ln(u)] = 1/u * du/dx = 1/(4x^2 - 1) * 8x = 8x/(4x^2 - 1).
