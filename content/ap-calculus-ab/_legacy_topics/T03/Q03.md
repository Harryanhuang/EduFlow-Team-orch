## Question
Find the value of \( k \) that makes the following function continuous at \( x = 3 \):
\[
f(x) = \begin{cases}
\dfrac{x^2 - 9}{x - 3}, & x \neq 3 \\
k, & x = 3
\end{cases}
\]

## Answer
\[
\lim_{x \to 3} \frac{x^2 - 9}{x - 3} = \lim_{x \to 3} \frac{(x+3)(x-3)}{x-3} = \lim_{x \to 3} (x+3) = 6.
\]
So \( k = 6 \).
