A rectangle has width $x$ and height $y$, with $x > 0$ and $y > 0$. The area is 64 square units. What is the minimum possible perimeter?

A) 16 units
B) 32 units
C) 48 units
D) 64 units

## Answer
B

## Explanation
By AM-GM or calculus: For fixed area $A = xy = 64$, minimizing $P = 2x + 2y = 2(x + y)$ means maximizing $xy$ for fixed $x + y$, which occurs when $x = y = 8$. Then $P = 2(8 + 8) = 32$. Among rectangles with fixed area, the square has minimum perimeter.
