A farmer has 100 meters of fencing to enclose a rectangular pen against a long wall. If the side parallel to the wall has length $x$, which function gives the area enclosed?

A) $A = x(100 - x)$
B) $A = x(50 - x)$
C) $A = x(50 - x/2)$
D) $A = 50x - x^2/2$

## Answer
C

## Explanation
With the wall on one side, only three sides of fencing are needed. If the side parallel to the wall has length $x$, the two perpendicular sides have combined length $100 - x$, so each is $(100 - x)/2$. The area is $A = x \cdot \frac{100 - x}{2} = x(50 - x/2)$. Options C and D are equivalent.
