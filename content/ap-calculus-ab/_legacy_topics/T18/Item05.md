A company produces $x$ units of a product. The total revenue is $R(x) = 80x - 0.04x^2$ dollars and the total cost is $C(x) = 50x + 2000$ dollars. What is the maximum profit?

A) $3625
B) $6250
C) $11250
D) $18750

## Answer
A

## Explanation
Profit is $P(x) = R(x) - C(x) = (80x - 0.04x^2) - (50x + 2000) = 30x - 0.04x^2 - 2000$. To maximize, set $P'(x) = 30 - 0.08x = 0$, giving $x = 375$. Since $P''(x) = -0.08 < 0$, this is a maximum. Then $P(375) = 30(375) - 0.04(375)^2 - 2000 = 11250 - 5625 - 2000 = 3625$ dollars.
