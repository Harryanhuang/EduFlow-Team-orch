Let $f(x)$ be a differentiable function such that $f(1) = 3$ and $f'(1) = -2$. The linearization of $f$ at $x = 1$ is used to approximate $f(1.05)$. Which of the following is the approximation?

## Options
A) 2.90
B) 2.95
C) 3.05
D) 3.10

## Answer
A) 2.90

## Explanation
$L(x) = f(1) + f'(1)(x - 1) = 3 + (-2)(x - 1)$. So $f(1.05) \approx L(1.05) = 3 + (-2)(0.05) = 3 - 0.1 = 2.90$.
