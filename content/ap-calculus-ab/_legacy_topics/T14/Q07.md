Let $f(x) = \sqrt{x+1}$. Find the linearization at $x = 0$ and use it to estimate $\sqrt{0.05}$. Then classify the estimate as an overestimate or underestimate.
$L(x) = 1 + \frac{x}{2}$; $\sqrt{0.05} \approx 1.025$; underestimate (concave down, $f''(x) < 0$).
