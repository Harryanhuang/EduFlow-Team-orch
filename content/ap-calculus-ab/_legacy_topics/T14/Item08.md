Evaluate $\displaystyle \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{e^x - 1 - x}{x^2}$.

## Answer
$\dfrac{1}{2}$

## Explanation
Direct substitution gives $\frac{0}{0}$. Apply L'Hôpital's Rule:
$\displaystyle \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{e^x - 1}{2x}$, still $\frac{0}{0}$.
Apply L'Hôpital's Rule again:
$\displaystyle \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{e^x}{2} = \frac{1}{2}$.
