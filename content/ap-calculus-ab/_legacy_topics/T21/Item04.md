Evaluate: \(\displaystyle \int_0^2 6x^2\,dx\)

## Options
A) 12
B) 8
C) 16
D) 4

## Answer
C

## Explanation
Find the antiderivative: \(\int 6x^2\,dx = 6 \cdot \frac{x^3}{3} = 2x^3\).

Evaluate from 0 to 2:

\[
F(2) - F(0) = 2(2)^3 - 2(0)^3 = 2(8) - 0 = 16
\]
