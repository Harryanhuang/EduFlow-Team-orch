Water flows into a tank at a rate given by $R(t)$ gallons per hour, where $t$ is measured in hours. What are the units of $\displaystyle\int_0^4 R(t)\, dt$?

## Options
A) Gallons per hour
B) Hours
C) Gallons
D) Gallons per hour per hour

## Answer
C

## Explanation
The integral of a rate of change gives the net change in the quantity. Since $R(t)$ is in gallons per hour and $dt$ is in hours, the product $R(t)\, dt$ has units of gallons. The definite integral $\int_0^4 R(t)\, dt$ represents the total number of gallons of water that flowed into the tank from $t = 0$ to $t = 4$ hours.
