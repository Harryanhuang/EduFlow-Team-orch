A particle moves along the $x$-axis with position given by $s(t) = 3t^2 - 12t + 5$ for $t \geq 0$. At what time $t$ does the particle change direction?

## Options
A) $t = 1$
B) $t = 2$
C) $t = 3$
D) $t = 4$

## Answer
B

## Explanation
The particle changes direction when velocity changes sign. Velocity: $v(t) = s'(t) = 6t - 12$. Setting $v(t) = 0$: $6t - 12 = 0 \Rightarrow t = 2$. For $t < 2$, $v(t) < 0$ (moving left). For $t > 2$, $v(t) > 0$ (moving right). Since velocity changes from negative to positive at $t = 2$, the particle changes direction at $t = 2$.
