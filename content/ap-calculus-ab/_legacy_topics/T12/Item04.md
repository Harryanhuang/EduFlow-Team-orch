The position of a particle moving along a straight line is given by $s(t) = t^3 - 6t^2 + 9t$ for $t \geq 0$. What is the velocity of the particle at $t = 1$?

## Options
A) $-3$
B) $0$
C) $3$
D) $6$

## Answer
B

## Explanation
Velocity is the derivative of position: $v(t) = s'(t) = 3t^2 - 12t + 9$. At $t = 1$: $v(1) = 3(1) - 12(1) + 9 = 3 - 12 + 9 = 0$. The particle is momentarily at rest at $t = 1$.
