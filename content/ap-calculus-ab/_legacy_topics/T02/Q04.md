Evaluate $\displaystyle \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\dfrac{1}{x + 5} - \dfrac{1}{5}}{x}$.

Combine fractions: $\dfrac{1}{x + 5} - \dfrac{1}{5} = \dfrac{5 - (x + 5)}{5(x + 5)} = \dfrac{-x}{5(x + 5)}$.

$\dfrac{-x}{5x(x + 5)} = \dfrac{-1}{5(x + 5)}$ for $x \neq 0$.

$\lim\limits_{x \to 0} \dfrac{-1}{5(x + 5)} = -\dfrac{1}{25}$.
