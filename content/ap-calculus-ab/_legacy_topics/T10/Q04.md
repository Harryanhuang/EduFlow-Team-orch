Find all points on the curve x²y + y² = 5 where the tangent line is horizontal.

<answer>
(0, √5) and (0, -√5)
</answer>

<explanation>
Differentiate implicitly: 2xy + x²(dy/dx) + 2y(dy/dx) = 0. Solve: dy/dx = -2xy/(x² + 2y). Horizontal tangent when -2xy = 0: x = 0 (since y ≠ 0). Substitute into the curve: y² = 5, so y = ±√5. Points: (0, √5) and (0, -√5).
</explanation>
