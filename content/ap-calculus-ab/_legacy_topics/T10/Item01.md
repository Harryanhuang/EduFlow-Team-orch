What is dy/dx at the point (3, 4) on the curve x² + y² = 25?

## Options
A) -3/4
B) -4/3
C) 3/4
D) 4/3

## Answer
A) -3/4

## Explanation
Differentiate both sides implicitly with respect to x: 2x + 2y(dy/dx) = 0. Solve for dy/dx: dy/dx = -x/y. At (3, 4): dy/dx = -3/4.
