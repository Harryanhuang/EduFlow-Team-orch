If $f'(x) = (x + 2)^2(x - 4)$, find all critical points, intervals of increase/decrease, and classify all local extrema.
Critical points: $x = -2$ and $x = 4$. Increasing on $(4, \infty)$; decreasing on $(-\infty, 4)$. Local minimum at $x = 4$; no local extremum at $x = -2$.
