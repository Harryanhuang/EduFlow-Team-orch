Let $f(x) = x^3 - 3x^2 - 9x + 4$. Find all critical points, intervals of increase and decrease, all local extrema, and the absolute maximum and minimum on $[-2, 4]$.
Critical points: $x = -1, 3$. Increasing on $(-\infty, -1) \cup (3, \infty)$; decreasing on $(-1, 3)$. Local maximum at $x = -1$; local minimum at $x = 3$. Absolute maximum: $f(-1) = 9$ at $x = -1$; absolute minimum: $f(3) = -23$ at $x = 3$.
