Let $f(x) = 2x^2 - 3x + 1$ on the interval $[1, 3]$. What is the average rate of change of $f$ over this interval?

## Options
A) 5
B) 7
C) 9
D) 11

## Answer
B) 7

## Explanation
The average rate of change of $f$ on $[1, 3]$ is:
$(f(3) - f(1))/(3 - 1)$
$f(3) = 2(9) - 3(3) + 1 = 18 - 9 + 1 = 10$
$f(1) = 2(1) - 3(1) + 1 = 2 - 3 + 1 = 0$
Average rate of change = $(10 - 0)/2 = 5$

Correction: $f(3) = 2(9) - 9 + 1 = 18 - 9 + 1 = 10$, $f(1) = 2 - 3 + 1 = 0$. $(10 - 0)/2 = 5$.

Answer: A) 5.
