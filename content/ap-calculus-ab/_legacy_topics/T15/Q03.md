Rolle's Theorem is a special case of the Mean Value Theorem. What additional hypothesis does Rolle's Theorem add, and what conclusion does it draw?

Rolle's Theorem adds the hypothesis $f(a) = f(b)$ to the MVT hypotheses, and concludes that there exists at least one $c$ in $(a, b)$ such that $f'(c) = 0$.
