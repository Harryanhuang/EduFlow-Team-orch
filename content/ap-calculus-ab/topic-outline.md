## Item Format Notes
- Each topic has **15–20 items** with difficulty F:5 | S:7 | C:8
- Challenge items follow College Board FRQ style
- Items are marked: `[calc]` (calculator required) or `[no-calc]`
- C-class items require multi-step derivation
- Cross-unit C items integrate concepts from multiple units
