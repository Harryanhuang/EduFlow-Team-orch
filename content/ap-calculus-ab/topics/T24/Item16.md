The graph of y = f(x) consists of line segments from (negative 2, 0) to (0, 4), from (0, 4) to (2, 4), and from (2, 4) to (4, 0). The average value of f on [negative 2, 4] is

## Options (if mcq)
A) 8/3
B) 4/3
C) 2
D) 16/3

## Answer
A

## Explanation
Break into three intervals: [negative 2, 0], [0, 2], [2, 4].
On [negative 2, 0]: line from (negative 2, 0) to (0, 4). Slope = (4 minus 0) / (0 minus (negative 2)) = 4/2 = 2. Equation: y = 2(x + 2) = 2x + 4.
On [0, 2]: horizontal line y = 4.
On [2, 4]: line from (2, 4) to (4, 0). Slope = (0 minus 4) / (4 minus 2) = negative 2. Equation: y = negative 2(x minus 2) + 4 = negative 2x + 8.
Compute integrals:
Integral from negative 2 to 0 of (2x + 4) dx = [x squared + 4x] from negative 2 to 0 = (0) minus (4 minus 8) = 4.
Integral from 0 to 2 of 4 dx = 4 times 2 = 8.
Integral from 2 to 4 of (negative 2x + 8) dx = [negative x squared + 8x] from 2 to 4 = (negative 16 + 32) minus (negative 4 + 16) = 16 minus 12 = 4.
Total integral = 4 + 8 + 4 = 16.
Average value = (1/(4 minus (negative 2))) times 16 = (1/6) times 16 = 16/6 = 8/3.
