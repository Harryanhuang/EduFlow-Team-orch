The average value of the function f(x) = x squared on the interval [1, 3] is

## Options (if mcq)
A) 4/3
B) 13/3
C) 13/6
D) 4

## Answer
B

## Explanation
The average value of f on [a, b] is (1/(b-a)) times the integral from a to b of f(x) dx.
Integral of x squared from 1 to 3 = [x cubed / 3] from 1 to 3 = 27/3 - 1/3 = 26/3.
Average = (1/2) times (26/3) = 13/3.
