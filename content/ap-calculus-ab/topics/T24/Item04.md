The volume of the solid generated by revolving the region bounded by y = square root of x, x = 4, and the x-axis about the x-axis is found using the disk method. The integral that represents this volume is

## Options (if mcq)
A) pi times the integral from 0 to 4 of (square root of x) squared dx
B) 2pi times the integral from 0 to 4 of square root of x dx
C) pi times the integral from 0 to 4 of square root of x dx
D) pi times the integral from 0 to 4 of (square root of x) to the fourth power dx

## Answer
A

## Explanation
Disk method: V = pi times the integral of (radius) squared dx. The radius is y = square root of x, so radius squared = (square root of x) squared = x. The region goes from x = 0 to x = 4 (where y = square root of x = 0 at x = 0). So V = pi times integral from 0 to 4 of (square root of x) squared dx.
