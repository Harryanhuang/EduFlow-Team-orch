The average value of $f(x) = 6x^2$ on the interval $[1, 3]$ is

A) 6
B) 12
C) 26
D) 52

## Answer
C

## Explanation
The average value of a function $f$ on $[a, b]$ is given by:
$$\frac{1}{b-a}\int_a^b f(x) \, dx$$

For $f(x) = 6x^2$ on $[1, 3]$:
$$\text{Average value} = \frac{1}{3-1}\int_1^3 6x^2 \, dx = \frac{1}{2}\left[2x^3\right]_1^3$$

$$= \frac{1}{2}(2(27) - 2(1)) = \frac{1}{2}(54 - 2) = \frac{52}{2} = 26$$
