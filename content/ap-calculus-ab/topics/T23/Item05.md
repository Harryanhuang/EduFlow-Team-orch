A bacteria culture grows exponentially with growth constant $k = 0.25$ per hour. Starting with 100 bacteria, approximately how many are present after 6 hours?

A) 244

B) 250

C) 452

D) 500

## Answer
C

## Explanation
Using the continuous exponential growth model $P(t) = P_0 e^{kt}$: $P(6) = 100 e^{0.25 \cdot 6} = 100 e^{1.5}$. Since $e^{1.5} \approx 4.4817$, we get $P(6) \approx 100 \times 4.4817 \approx 448$, which rounds to the nearest option, 452.
