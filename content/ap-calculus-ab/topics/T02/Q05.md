Evaluate $\displaystyle \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\sqrt{x + 25} - 5}{x}$.

Multiply by conjugate: $\dfrac{\sqrt{x + 25} - 5}{x} \cdot \dfrac{\sqrt{x + 25} + 5}{\sqrt{x + 25} + 5} = \dfrac{x}{x(\sqrt{x + 25} + 5)} = \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{x + 25} + 5}$.

$\lim\limits_{x \to 0} \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{x + 25} + 5} = \dfrac{1}{10}$.
