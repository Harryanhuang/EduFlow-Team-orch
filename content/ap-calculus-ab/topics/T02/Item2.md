Evaluate the limit:

$\displaystyle \lim_{x \to 3} \frac{x^2 - 9}{x - 3}$

## Options
A) 0
B) 3
C) 6
D) 9

## Answer
C) 6

## Explanation
Factor the numerator: $x^2 - 9 = (x + 3)(x - 3)$.

For $x \neq 3$:
$$\frac{x^2 - 9}{x - 3} = \frac{(x + 3)(x - 3)}{x - 3} = x + 3$$

$$\lim_{x \to 3} (x + 3) = 3 + 3 = 6$$
