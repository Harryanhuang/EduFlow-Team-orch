Evaluate $\displaystyle \lim_{x \to 3} \frac{x^2 - 9}{x^2 + x - 12}$.

Factor both numerator and denominator:

Numerator: $x^2 - 9 = (x + 3)(x - 3)$

Denominator: $x^2 + x - 12 = (x + 4)(x - 3)$

$\dfrac{(x + 3)(x - 3)}{(x + 4)(x - 3)} = \dfrac{x + 3}{x + 4}$ for $x \neq 3$.

$\lim\limits_{x \to 3} \dfrac{x + 3}{x + 4} = \dfrac{6}{7}$.
