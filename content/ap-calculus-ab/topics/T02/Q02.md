Evaluate $\displaystyle \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\sqrt{x + 16} - 4}{x}$.

Multiply by conjugate: $\dfrac{\sqrt{x + 16} - 4}{x} \cdot \dfrac{\sqrt{x + 16} + 4}{\sqrt{x + 16} + 4} = \dfrac{x}{x(\sqrt{x + 16} + 4)} = \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{x + 16} + 4}$.

$\lim\limits_{x \to 0} \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{x + 16} + 4} = \dfrac{1}{8}$.
