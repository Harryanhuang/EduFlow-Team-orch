Use the Squeeze Theorem to evaluate $\displaystyle \lim_{x \to 0} x^2 \cos\left(\frac{1}{x}\right)$.

Since $-1 \leq \cos(1/x) \leq 1$ for all $x \neq 0$, and $x^2 \geq 0$:

$-x^2 \leq x^2 \cos(1/x) \leq x^2$.

$\lim\limits_{x \to 0} (-x^2) = 0$ and $\lim\limits_{x \to 0} x^2 = 0$.

By the Squeeze Theorem: $\lim\limits_{x \to 0} x^2 \cos(1/x) = 0$.
