Evaluate the limit:

$\displaystyle \lim_{x \to 1} \frac{x^3 - 1}{x - 1}$

## Options
A) 1
B) 2
C) 3
D) Does not exist

## Answer
C) 3

## Explanation
Use the difference of cubes formula: $x^3 - 1 = (x - 1)(x^2 + x + 1)$.

For $x \neq 1$:
$$\frac{x^3 - 1}{x - 1} = \frac{(x - 1)(x^2 + x + 1)}{x - 1} = x^2 + x + 1$$

$$\lim_{x \to 1} (x^2 + x + 1) = 1 + 1 + 1 = 3$$
