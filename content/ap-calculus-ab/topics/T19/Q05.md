**Q:** If a car travels with velocity v(t)=t²+1 mph, what does ∫₀³ v(t) dt represent and estimate with a midpoint sum (n=3)?

**A:** Represents total distance traveled from t=0 to t=3 hours.
M₃ = Δt[f(0.5)+f(1.5)+f(2.5)] = 1[1.25+3.25+7.25] = 11.75 miles.
(Exact = 12 miles by FTC: [t³/3 + t]₀³ = 12)
