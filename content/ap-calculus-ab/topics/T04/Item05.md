Evaluate $\displaystyle \lim_{x \to \infty} \frac{5x^2 + 3x - 1}{2x^2 - 7}$.

## Answer
$\displaystyle \frac{5}{2}$

## Explanation
Both numerator and denominator have degree 2. Divide through by $x^2$:
$$\lim_{x \to \infty} \frac{5 + 3/x - 1/x^2}{2 - 7/x^2} = \frac{5 + 0 - 0}{2 - 0} = \frac{5}{2}.$$
