A company's marginal cost function is given by $C'(x) = 3x^2 - 24x + 60$ dollars per unit, where $x$ is the number of units produced. If the fixed cost is $200$ dollars, what is the total cost of producing 5 units?

## Options
A) $325$ dollars
B) $375$ dollars
C) $425$ dollars
D) $500$ dollars

## Answer
A

## Explanation
Total cost is the fixed cost plus the accumulated marginal cost: $C(5) = C(0) + \int_0^5 C'(x)\, dx = 200 + \int_0^5 (3x^2 - 24x + 60)\, dx$. Evaluating the integral: $[x^3 - 12x^2 + 60x]_0^5 = 125 - 300 + 300 = 125$. Therefore $C(5) = 200 + 125 = 325$ dollars.
