A particle moves along the $x$-axis with position $s(t) = t^3 - 6t^2 + 9t + 2$ for $t \geq 0$.

(a) Find when the particle is at rest.
(b) Find when the particle is moving to the right.
(c) Find the acceleration at each time the particle is at rest.

## Answer
(a) $v(t) = s'(t) = 3t^2 - 12t + 9 = 3(t^2 - 4t + 3) = 3(t - 1)(t - 3)$. Set $v(t) = 0$: $t = 1$ and $t = 3$.

(b) Moving right when $v(t) > 0$: $3(t - 1)(t - 3) > 0$. Sign analysis: $v(t) > 0$ for $0 \leq t < 1$ and $t > 3$.

(c) $a(t) = v'(t) = 6t - 12$. At $t = 1$: $a(1) = -6$. At $t = 3$: $a(3) = 6$.
