Find the equation of the tangent line to $y = x \sec x$ at $x = \frac{\pi}{3}$.

Point: $(\frac{\pi}{3}, \frac{2\pi}{3})$ since $\sec(\frac{\pi}{3}) = 2$.

Slope: $f'(x) = \sec x + x \sec x \tan x$. At $x = \frac{\pi}{3}$: $f'(\frac{\pi}{3}) = 2 + \frac{\pi}{3} \cdot 2 \cdot \sqrt{3} = 2 + \frac{2\pi\sqrt{3}}{3}$.

Tangent line: $y - \frac{2\pi}{3} = \left(2 + \frac{2\pi\sqrt{3}}{3}\right)\left(x - \frac{\pi}{3}\right)$.
