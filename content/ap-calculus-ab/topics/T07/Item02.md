What is $\frac{d}{dx}[\cos x]$?

## Options
A) $\sin x$
B) $-\sin x$
C) $-\cos x$
D) $\cos x$

## Answer
B

## Explanation
The derivative of $\cos x$ is $-\sin x$. This is a standard trigonometric derivative that should be memorized for the AP exam.
