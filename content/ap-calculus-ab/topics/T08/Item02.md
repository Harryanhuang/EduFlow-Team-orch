What is the derivative of y = sin(5x)?

## Options
A) cos(5x)
B) 5 cos(5x)
C) -5 cos(5x)
D) 5 sin(5x)

## Answer
B

## Explanation
The outer function is sin(u) and the inner function is u = 5x. d/dx[sin(u)] = cos(u) * du/dx = cos(5x) * 5 = 5 cos(5x).
