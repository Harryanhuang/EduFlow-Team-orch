What is the value of $\displaystyle \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\sin x}{x}$?

## Options
A) 0
B) 1
C) Does not exist
D) $\infty$

## Answer
B) 1

## Explanation
This is a standard 0/0 indeterminate form. By L'Hôpital's Rule: $\displaystyle \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\sin x}{x} = \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\cos x}{1} = 1$.
