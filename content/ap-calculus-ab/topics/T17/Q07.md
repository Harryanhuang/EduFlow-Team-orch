If you are given only the graph of f'(x), how can you determine where f has points of inflection?
Points of inflection of f occur where the graph of f' changes from being concave up to concave down or vice versa. This means f'' changes sign, so look for points where the slope of f' is zero (horizontal tangent to f') or where the concavity of f' changes.
