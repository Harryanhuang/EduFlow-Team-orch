State the First Derivative Test. If $f'(c) = 0$, what conditions on $f'$ around $c$ give a local maximum, local minimum, or no local extremum?
If $f'$ changes from positive to negative at $c$: local maximum. If $f'$ changes from negative to positive at $c$: local minimum. If $f'$ does not change sign: no local extremum.
