Let $f(x) = \frac{x}{x^2 + 1}$. Find $f'(x)$, all critical points, intervals of increase/decrease, and classify all local extrema using the First Derivative Test.
$f'(x) = \frac{1 - x^2}{(x^2 + 1)^2}$. Critical points: $x = -1, 1$. Increasing on $(-1, 1)$; decreasing on $(-\infty, -1) \cup (1, \infty)$. Local minimum at $x = -1$; local maximum at $x = 1$.
