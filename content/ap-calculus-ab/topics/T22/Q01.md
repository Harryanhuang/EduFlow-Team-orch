Show that \(y = e^{3x}\) is a solution to the differential equation \(\dfrac{dy}{dx} = 3y\). Then find the general solution of the differential equation.
\(y = e^{3x}\) is a solution. The general solution is \(y = Ce^{3x}\), where \(C\) is any real constant.
