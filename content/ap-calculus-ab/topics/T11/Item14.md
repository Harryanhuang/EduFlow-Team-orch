The graph of $f'$ is shown below (described): $f'(x)$ is a parabola opening upward with vertex at $(3, 0)$ and $f'(1) = 4$. At what value of $x$ does the graph of $f$ have a point of inflection?

## Options
A) $x = 1$  B) $x = 3$  C) $x = 0$  D) $f$ has no point of inflection.

## Answer
B) $x = 3$

## Explanation
A point of inflection occurs where $f''$ changes sign, i.e., where $f'$ changes from increasing to decreasing or vice versa. Since $f'$ is a parabola with vertex at $(3, 0)$, $f'$ changes from decreasing to increasing at $x = 3$, meaning $f''$ changes sign there. Thus $f$ has a point of inflection at $x = 3$.
