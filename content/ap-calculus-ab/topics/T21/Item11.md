Evaluate: \(\displaystyle \int_0^{\pi/4} \sec^2(x)\,dx\)

## Options
A) 1
B) \(\frac{\pi}{4}\)
C) 0
D) \(\sqrt{2}\)

## Answer
A

## Explanation
The antiderivative of \(\sec^2(x)\) is \(\tan(x)\).

\[
\int_0^{\pi/4} \sec^2(x)\,dx = \left[\tan(x)\right]_0^{\pi/4} = \tan\left(\frac{\pi}{4}\right) - \tan(0) = 1 - 0 = 1
\]
