Find dy/dx at the point (3, 3) on the curve x³ + y³ = 6xy.

<answer>
dy/dx = -1
</answer>

<explanation>
Differentiate implicitly: 3x² + 3y²(dy/dx) = 6y + 6x(dy/dx). Solve: dy/dx = (6y - 3x²)/(3y² - 6x) = (2y - x²)/(y² - 2x). At (3, 3): dy/dx = (6 - 9)/(9 - 6) = -3/3 = -1.
</explanation>
