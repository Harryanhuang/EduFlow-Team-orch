Find the equation of the tangent line to the curve x² + 4y² = 25 at the point (3, 2).

<answer>
y = -(3/8)x + 25/8
</answer>

<explanation>
Differentiate implicitly: 2x + 8y(dy/dx) = 0, so dy/dx = -x/(4y). At (3, 2): dy/dx = -3/8. Tangent line: y - 2 = (-3/8)(x - 3), which simplifies to y = -(3/8)x + 25/8.
</explanation>
