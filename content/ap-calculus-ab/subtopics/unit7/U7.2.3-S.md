---What is the equilibrium solution to dP/dt = kP?

## Options
A. P = k
B. P = 0
C. P = 1/k
D. There is no equilibrium solution

## Answer
B

## Explanation
At equilibrium, dP/dt = 0, so kP = 0, giving P = 0. This is the only equilibrium solution. For k>0 it's unstable; for k<0 it's stable.
