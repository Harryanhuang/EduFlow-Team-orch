---Solve the initial value problem: dy/dx = 2xy with y(0) = 3.

## Options
A. y = e^{x^2} + 2
B. y = 3e^{x^2}
C. y = 3e^{x^2} - 2
D. y = e^{x^2+3}

## Answer
B

## Explanation
Separate: dy/y = 2x dx. Integrate: ln|y| = x^2 + C. Exponentiate: y = Ae^{x^2}. Using y(0)=3: 3 = A, so y = 3e^{x^2}.
