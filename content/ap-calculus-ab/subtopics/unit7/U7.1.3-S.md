---Solve dy/dx = 2y with y(0) = 5. What is y(1)?

## Options
A. 5e
B. 5e^2
C. 10e
D. 25e

## Answer
B

## Explanation
Separate: dy/(2y) = dx. Integrate: (1/2)ln|y| = x + C, so y = Ae^{2x}. Using y(0)=5: 5 = A, giving y = 5e^{2x}. At x=1: y = 5e^2.
