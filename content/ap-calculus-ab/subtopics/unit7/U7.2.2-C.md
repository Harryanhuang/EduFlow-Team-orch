A bacteria culture starts with 500 cells and triples every 4 hours. Which expression gives the population at time t hours?

## Options
A) P = 500 · 3^(t/4)
B) P = 500 · 3^t
C) P = 500 · e^(3t)
D) P = 1500 · e^(t/4)

## Answer
A

## Explanation
Tripling every 4 hours means P(4) = 3P₀. The model is P = P₀ · 3^(t/4). At t=0: P = 500 ✓. At t=4: P = 500·3^1 = 1500 ✓. Option B triples every 1 hour. Option C and D use base e with incorrect rates. Answer is A.
