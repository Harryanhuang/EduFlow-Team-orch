---A population grows exponentially according to dP/dt = 0.05P. If the initial population is 100, what is the population after 10 years?

## Options
A. 150
B. 165
C. 180
D. 200

## Answer
B

## Explanation
Using P(t) = P0 e^{kt} with P0=100, k=0.05: P(10) = 100e^{0.5} ≈ 100(1.6487) ≈ 164.87, which rounds to 165.
