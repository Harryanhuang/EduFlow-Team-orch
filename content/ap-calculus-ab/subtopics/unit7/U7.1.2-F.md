Which step is essential when solving dy/dx = xy by separation of variables?

## Options
A) Multiply both sides by x
B) Divide both sides by y and multiply both sides by dx
C) Take the derivative of both sides
D) Set dy/dx = 0

## Answer
B

## Explanation
To separate variables in dy/dx = xy, divide both sides by y (getting dy/y) and multiply by dx (getting x dx). This gives dy/y = x dx, which can then be integrated on both sides. The other options do not achieve separation of variables.
