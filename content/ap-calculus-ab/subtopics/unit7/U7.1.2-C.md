---Find the general solution to dy/dx = 1 + y^2.

## Options
A. y = tan(x + C)
B. y = tan(x) + C
C. y = cot(x + C)
D. y = sec(x + C)

## Answer
A

## Explanation
Separate: dy/(1+y^2) = dx. Integrate: arctan(y) = x + C. So y = tan(x + C). This is the general solution.
