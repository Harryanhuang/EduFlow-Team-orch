---A bacteria culture starts with 500 cells and grows at a rate proportional to its size with k=0.03. How long does it take to reach 1000 cells?

## Options
A. 10.0 years
B. 15.5 years
C. 23.1 years
D. 33.3 years

## Answer
C

## Explanation
Using P(t) = 500e^{0.03t}, set P(t)=1000: 1000 = 500e^{0.03t}, so 2 = e^{0.03t}, thus t = ln(2)/0.03 ≈ 23.1 years.
