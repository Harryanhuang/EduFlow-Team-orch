---A chemical decays according to dP/dt = -0.02P starting with 1000 grams. How much remains after 10 years?

## Options
A. 819
B. 800
C. 750
D. 700

## Answer
A

## Explanation
P(t) = 1000e^{-0.02t}. At t=10: P(10) = 1000e^{-0.2} ≈ 1000(0.8187) ≈ 818.7, which rounds to 819.
