What is the value of $\displaystyle\int_1^4 \frac{1}{\sqrt{x}}\,dx$?

## Options
A) 1
B) 2
C) 3
D) 4

## Answer
B

## Explanation
By FTC Part 2, $\int_1^4 \frac{1}{\sqrt{x}}\,dx = \int_1^4 x^{-1/2}\,dx = \Bigl[2x^{1/2}\Bigr]_1^4 = 2(2)-2(1)=2$.
