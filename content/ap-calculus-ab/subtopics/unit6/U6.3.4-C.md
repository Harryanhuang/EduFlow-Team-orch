$\displaystyle\int_0^3 x\sin(x^2)\,dx =$

## Options
A) $0.182$
B) $0.956$
C) $1.412$
D) $1.911$

## Answer
B

## Explanation
Let $u=x^2$, so $du=2x\,dx$ and $\tfrac12\,du=x\,dx$. When $x=0$, $u=0$; when $x=3$, $u=9$. The integral becomes
$$\frac12\int_0^9 \sin u\,du = \frac12\bigl[-\cos u\bigr]_0^9 = \frac12\bigl(1-\cos 9\bigr).$$
Using a calculator (radians), $\cos 9\approx -0.9111$, so the value is approximately $\tfrac12(1+0.9111)\approx 0.956$.
