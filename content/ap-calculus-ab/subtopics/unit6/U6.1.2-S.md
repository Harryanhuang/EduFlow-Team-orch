The function $f$ is even and $\displaystyle\int_0^3 f(x)\,dx = 8$. What is the value of $\displaystyle\int_{-3}^3 f(x)\,dx$?

## Options
A) $0$
B) $4$
C) $8$
D) $16$

## Answer
D

## Explanation
For an even function, $\int_{-a}^a f(x)\,dx = 2\int_0^a f(x)\,dx$. With $a=3$ and $\int_0^3 f(x)\,dx=8$, the value is $2\cdot 8 = 16$.
