$\displaystyle\int x^3\,dx =$

## Options
A) $\dfrac{x^4}{4}+C$
B) $\dfrac{x^4}{4}$
C) $3x^2+C$
D) $x^4+C$

## Answer
A

## Explanation
Applying the power rule for antiderivatives, $\int x^3\,dx = \frac{x^4}{4}+C$.
