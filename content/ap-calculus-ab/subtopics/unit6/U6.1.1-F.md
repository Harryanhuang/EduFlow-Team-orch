The function $f$ is defined on the interval $[0,4]$ with values $f(0)=2$, $f(1)=3$, $f(2)=5$, $f(3)=6$, and $f(4)=8$. What is the left Riemann sum approximation of $\int_0^4 f(x)\,dx$ using 4 subintervals of equal length?

## Options
A) 10
B) 12
C) 16
D) 20

## Answer
C

## Explanation
Using the left Riemann sum with 4 subintervals of width $\Delta x = \frac{4-0}{4}=1$, the left endpoints are $x=0,1,2,3$. The sum is $1\cdot f(0)+1\cdot f(1)+1\cdot f(2)+1\cdot f(3)=2+3+5+6=16$.
