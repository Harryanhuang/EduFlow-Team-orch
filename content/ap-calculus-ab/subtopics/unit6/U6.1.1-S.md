Let $f(x)=x^2+1$. What is the midpoint Riemann sum approximation of $\int_1^3 f(x)\,dx$ with $n=4$ subintervals of equal length?

## Options
A) 12.125
B) 14.125
C) 16.125
D) 18.125

## Answer
B

## Explanation
With $n=4$ subintervals on $[1,3]$, $\Delta x = \frac{3-1}{4}=0.5$. The midpoints are $x=1.25,1.75,2.25,2.75$. The midpoint sum is $0.5\bigl[f(1.25)+f(1.75)+f(2.25)+f(2.75)\bigr]=0.5(3.0625+5.0625+8.0625+12.0625)=0.5(28.25)=14.125$.
