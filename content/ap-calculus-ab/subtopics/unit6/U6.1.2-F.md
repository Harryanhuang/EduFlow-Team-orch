If $\displaystyle\int_0^3 f(x)\,dx = 5$ and $\displaystyle\int_0^3 g(x)\,dx = 7$, what is the value of $\displaystyle\int_0^3 \bigl[2f(x)-g(x)\bigr]\,dx$?

## Options
A) $-2$
B) $3$
C) $10$
D) $17$

## Answer
B

## Explanation
By linearity of the definite integral, $\int_0^3 \bigl[2f(x)-g(x)\bigr]\,dx = 2\int_0^3 f(x)\,dx - \int_0^3 g(x)\,dx = 2(5)-7=3$.
