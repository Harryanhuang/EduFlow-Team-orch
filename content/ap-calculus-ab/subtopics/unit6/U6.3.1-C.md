$\displaystyle\int \frac{x^3-8}{x-2}\,dx =$

## Options
A) $\dfrac{x^3}{3}+x^2+4x$
B) $\dfrac{x^3}{3}+x^2+4x+8$
C) $\dfrac{x^3}{3}+x^2+4x+C$
D) $\dfrac{x^3}{3}+2x^2+4x+C$

## Answer
C

## Explanation
First divide: $\frac{x^3-8}{x-2}=x^2+2x+4$ for $x\neq 2$. Then $\int (x^2+2x+4)\,dx = \frac{x^3}{3}+x^2+4x+C$.
