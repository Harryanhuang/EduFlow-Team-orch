$\displaystyle\int e^{3x}\,dx =$

## Options
A) $e^{3x}+C$
B) $3e^{3x}+C$
C) $\dfrac{1}{3}e^{3x}+C$
D) $\dfrac{1}{3}e^{x}+C$

## Answer
C

## Explanation
Since $\frac{d}{dx}(e^{3x})=3e^{3x}$, we have $\int e^{3x}\,dx = \frac{1}{3}e^{3x}+C$.
