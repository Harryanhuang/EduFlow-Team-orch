What is the value of $\displaystyle\int_{-2}^2 |x|\,dx$?

## Options
A) 0
B) 2
C) 3
D) 4

## Answer
D

## Explanation
Split the integral: $\int_{-2}^2 |x|\,dx = \int_{-2}^0 (-x)\,dx + \int_0^2 x\,dx = \Bigl[-\frac{x^2}{2}\Bigr]_{-2}^0 + \Bigl[\frac{x^2}{2}\Bigr]_0^2 = (0+2)+(2-0)=4$.
