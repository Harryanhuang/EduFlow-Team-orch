The function $f$ is odd and $\displaystyle\int_1^3 f(x)\,dx = 5$. What is the value of $\displaystyle\int_{-3}^1 f(x)\,dx$?

## Options
A) $-10$
B) $-5$
C) $0$
D) $5$

## Answer
B

## Explanation
Because $f$ is odd, $\int_{-a}^a f(x)\,dx = 0$. With $a=3$, $\int_{-3}^3 f(x)\,dx = 0$. By interval additivity, $0 = \int_{-3}^1 f(x)\,dx + \int_1^3 f(x)\,dx$, so $\int_{-3}^1 f(x)\,dx = -5$.
