A rectangular garden is to be fenced with 100 meters of fencing. What is the maximum possible area of the garden?

## Options
A) 100
B) 312.5
C) 625
D) 25 by 25

## Answer
C

## Explanation
Let the rectangle have width w and length l. Given 2w+2l=100, so w+l=50, thus l=50-w. Area A=w(50-w)=50w-w^2. A'(w)=50-2w=0 gives w=25. Then l=25. The maximum area is 25×25=625 square meters. Option C is correct. Option A gives the perimeter. Option B gives half the maximum area. Option D gives the dimensions but not the maximum area value.
