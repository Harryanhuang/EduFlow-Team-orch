A closed cylindrical can must have volume 16π cubic inches. What is the minimum total surface area?

## Options
A) 12π
B) 16π
C) 24π
D) 32π

## Answer
C

## Explanation
V = πr²h = 16π gives h = 16/r². Surface area S = 2πr² + 2πrh = 2πr² + 32π/r. Then S' = 4πr − 32π/r² = 0 gives r³ = 8, so r = 2 and h = 4. The minimum surface area is S = 2π(4) + 2π(2)(4) = 8π + 16π = 24π. Option C is correct.
