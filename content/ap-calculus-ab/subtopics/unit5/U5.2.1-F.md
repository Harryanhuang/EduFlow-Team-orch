Find all critical points of f(x)=x^3-3x^2.

## Options
A) x=0 only
B) x=0 and x=1
C) x=0 and x=2
D) x=0, x=2, and x=3

## Answer
C

## Explanation
Critical points occur where f'(x)=0 or where f'(x) is undefined. For f(x)=x^3-3x^2, f'(x)=3x^2-6x=3x(x-2), so f'(x)=0 at x=0 and x=2. The derivative exists everywhere. Therefore the critical points are x=0 and x=2. Option C is correct. Option A misses x=2. Option B incorrectly includes x=1 where f'(1)=-3≠0. Option D incorrectly includes x=3 which is not in the domain of consideration and is not a critical point.
