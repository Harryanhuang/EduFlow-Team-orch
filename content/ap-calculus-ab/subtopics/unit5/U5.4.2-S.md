A square sheet of cardboard with side length 20 inches has squares of side length x cut from each corner. The sides are folded up to form an open box. What is the maximum volume of the box?

## Options
A) 8000/27
B) 16000/27
C) 4000/9
D) 2000/3

## Answer
B

## Explanation
Let the box have side length x cut from each corner. The dimensions become (20-2x) by (20-2x) by x. Volume V=x(20-2x)^2=x(400-80x+4x^2)=4x^3-80x^2+400x. V'(x)=12x^2-160x+400=4(3x^2-40x+100)=4(3x-10)(x-10). Setting V'(x)=0 gives x=10/3 or x=10. x=10 is impossible (would make the base zero), so x=10/3. V''(x)=24x-160, V''(10/3)=80-160=-80<0, confirming a maximum. The maximum volume is V=(10/3)(20-20/3)^2=(10/3)(40/3)^2=(10/3)(1600/9)=16000/27 cubic inches. Option B is correct.
