The function $f$ is defined for all real numbers. The table below gives selected values of $f$.

| $x$ | 1.9 | 1.99 | 1.999 | 2.001 | 2.01 | 2.1 |
|-----|-----|------|-------|-------|------|-----|
| $f(x)$ | 3.61 | 3.9601 | 3.996001 | 4.004001 | 4.0401 | 4.41 |

What is a reasonable estimate for $\lim_{x \to 2} f(x)$?

## Options
A) 3.6
B) 4.0
C) 4.4
D) The limit does not exist.

## Answer
B

## Explanation
As $x$ approaches 2 from the left (1.9, 1.99, 1.999), $f(x)$ approaches 4.0. As $x$ approaches 2 from the right (2.001, 2.01, 2.1), $f(x)$ also approaches 4.0. Since the left-hand and right-hand values both converge to 4, a reasonable estimate is $\lim_{x \to 2} f(x) = 4$. The pattern suggests $f(x) = x^2$, so the limit is exactly 4.
