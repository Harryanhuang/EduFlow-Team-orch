---Find the area between the curves y = 2x and y = x from x = 0 to x = 2.

## Options
A. 1
B. 2
C. 3
D. 4

## Answer
B

## Explanation
Area = ∫[0,2] (2x - x)dx = ∫[0,2] x dx = [x²/2][0,2] = 2. Alternatively, it's a triangle with base 2 and height 2, so area = (1/2)(2)(2) = 2.
