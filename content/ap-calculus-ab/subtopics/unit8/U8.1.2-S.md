---Find the area between y = x² and y = 2x + 3.

## Options
A. 9
B. 10
C. 32/3
D. 12

## Answer
C

## Explanation
Intersection: x² = 2x+3, so x²-2x-3=0, (x-3)(x+1)=0, x=-1 or x=3. Area = ∫[-1,3] (2x+3 - x²)dx = [x²+3x-x³/3][-1,3] = (9+9-9) - (1-3+1/3) = 9 - (-5/3) = 32/3.
