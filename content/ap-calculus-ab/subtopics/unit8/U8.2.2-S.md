---For v(t) = 2t - 4 on [0, 2], compare total distance traveled vs net displacement.

## Options
A. Both are 4
B. Total distance is 4, net displacement is -4
C. Total distance is 4, net displacement is 0
D. Both are 0

## Answer
B

## Explanation
v(t) = 2t-4. On [0,2], v(t) ≤ 0. Total distance = ∫[0,2] |2t-4|dt = ∫[0,2] (4-2t)dt = [4t-t²][0,2] = 8-4 = 4. Net displacement = ∫[0,2] (2t-4)dt = [t²-4t][0,2] = 4-8 = -4.
