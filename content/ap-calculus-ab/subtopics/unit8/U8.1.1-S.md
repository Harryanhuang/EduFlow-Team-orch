---What is the average value of f(x) = x³ + 1 on [0, 2]?

## Options
A. 1
B. 2
C. 2.5
D. 3

## Answer
D

## Explanation
Average value = (1/(2-0)) ∫[0,2] (x³+1)dx = (1/2)[x⁴/4 + x][0,2] = (1/2)(4+2) = 3.
