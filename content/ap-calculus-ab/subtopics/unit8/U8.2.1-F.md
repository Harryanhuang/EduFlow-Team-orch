---A particle moves with velocity v(t) = 2t m/s. What is its displacement from t=0 to t=3?

## Options
A. 3 meters
B. 9 meters
C. 18 meters
D. 27 meters

## Answer
B

## Explanation
Displacement = ∫[0,3] v(t)dt = ∫[0,3] 2t dt = [t²][0,3] = 9. The particle is 9 meters from the starting point.
