---A particle moves with velocity v(t) = t + 1. What is the displacement from t=0 to t=4?

## Options
A. 12
B. 16
C. 20
D. 24

## Answer
A

## Explanation
Accumulation = ∫[0,4] (t+1)dt = [t²/2 + t][0,4] = 8 + 4 = 12. This represents the total change in position (displacement) over [0,4].
