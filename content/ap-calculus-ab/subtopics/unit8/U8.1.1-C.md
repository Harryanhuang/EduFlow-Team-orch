If ∫₀⁴ f(x)dx = 24, what is the average value of f on [0, 4]?

## Options
A) 6
B) 8
C) 24
D) 96

## Answer
A

## Explanation
Average value = (1/(b−a)) ∫ₐᵇ f(x)dx = (1/4)(24) = 6. Option A is correct.
