For v(t) = t − 2 on [0, 4], what are the net displacement and total distance?

## Options
A) Net displacement = 0, total distance = 4
B) Net displacement = 4, total distance = 4
C) Net displacement = 0, total distance = 8
D) Net displacement = −4, total distance = 4

## Answer
A

## Explanation
Net displacement = ∫₀⁴ (t−2)dt = [t²/2 − 2t]₀⁴ = (8−8)−0 = 0. Total distance: v(t)=0 at t=2. ∫₀² (2−t)dt = [2t−t²/2]₀² = 2, ∫₂⁴ (t−2)dt = [t²/2−2t]₂⁴ = 2. Total = 2+2 = 4. Answer is A.
