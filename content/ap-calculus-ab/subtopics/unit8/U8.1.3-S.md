---Find the area enclosed by x = y² and x = 2y.

## Options
A. 4/3
B. 2/3
C. 8/3
D. 2

## Answer
A

## Explanation
Curves: x = y² and x = 2y. Intersection: y² = 2y, so y=0 or y=2. Area = ∫[0,2] (2y - y²)dy = [y² - y³/3][0,2] = 4 - 8/3 = 4/3.
