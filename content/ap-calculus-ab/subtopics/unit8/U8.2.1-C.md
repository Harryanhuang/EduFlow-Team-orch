A particle moves with velocity v(t) = 3t² − 2t. If s(0) = 5, what is s(2)?

## Options
A) 9
B) 10
C) 13
D) 17

## Answer
A

## Explanation
s(2) = s(0) + ∫₀² v(t)dt = 5 + ∫₀² (3t² − 2t)dt = 5 + [t³ − t²]₀² = 5 + (8 − 4) = 9. Option A is correct.
