---For v(t) = t² - 1 on [0, 3], find total distance and net displacement.

## Options
A. Total distance = 6, net displacement = 6
B. Total distance = 22/3, net displacement = 22/3
C. Total distance = 20/3, net displacement = 6
D. Total distance = 22/3, net displacement = 6

## Answer
D

## Explanation
v(t) = t²-1 = 0 at t=1. On [0,1]: v≤0, on [1,3]: v≥0. Total distance = ∫[0,1] (1-t²)dt + ∫[1,3] (t²-1)dt = [t-t³/3][0,1] + [t³/3-t][1,3] = (1-1/3) + (9-3 - 1/3+1) = 2/3 + 20/3 = 22/3. Net displacement = ∫[0,3] (t²-1)dt = [t³/3-t][0,3] = 9-3 = 6.
