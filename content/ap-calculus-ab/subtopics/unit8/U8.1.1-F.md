---What is the average value of f(x) = x on the interval [0, 4]?

## Options
A. 1
B. 1.5
C. 2
D. 4

## Answer
C

## Explanation
Average value = (1/(b-a)) ∫[a,b] f(x)dx. Here: (1/(4-0)) ∫[0,4] x dx = (1/4)[x²/2][0,4] = (1/4)(8) = 2.
