---Find the area enclosed by x = y² - 1 and x = 1 - y².

## Options
A. 2/3
B. 4/3
C. 2
D. 8/3

## Answer
D

## Explanation
Curves: x = y²-1 and x = 1-y². Intersection: y²-1 = 1-y², so 2y²=2, y=±1. Area = ∫[-1,1] [(1-y²) - (y²-1)]dy = ∫[-1,1] (2-2y²)dy = [2y - 2y³/3][-1,1] = (2-2/3) - (-2+2/3) = 4/3 + 4/3 = 8/3.
