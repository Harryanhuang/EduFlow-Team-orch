Find the area enclosed between y = x² and y = x on [0, 1].

## Options
A) 1/6
B) 1/3
C) 1/2
D) 2/3

## Answer
A

## Explanation
On [0,1], x ≥ x². Area = ∫₀¹ (x − x²)dx = [x²/2 − x³/3]₀¹ = 1/2 − 1/3 = 1/6. Answer is A.
