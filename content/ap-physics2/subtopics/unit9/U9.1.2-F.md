What is the gauge pressure at a depth of $3 \text{ m}$ below the surface of a freshwater lake? ($\rho_{\text{water}} = 1000 \text{ kg/m}^3$, $g = 10 \text{ m/s}^2$)

## Options
A) $3{,}000 \text{ Pa}$
B) $10{,}000 \text{ Pa}$
C) $30{,}000 \text{ Pa}$
D) $130{,}000 \text{ Pa}$

## Answer
C

## Explanation
Gauge pressure is the pressure due to the fluid alone, excluding atmospheric pressure: $P_{\text{gauge}} = \rho g h = 1000 \times 10 \times 3 = 30{,}000 \text{ Pa}$. Option D gives the absolute pressure ($P_0 + \rho g h \approx 100{,}000 + 30{,}000 = 130{,}000 \text{ Pa}$), but the question asks for gauge pressure.
