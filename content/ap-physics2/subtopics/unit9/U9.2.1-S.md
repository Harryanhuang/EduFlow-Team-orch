A wooden block with density $600 \text{ kg/m}^3$ floats in water ($\rho = 1000 \text{ kg/m}^3$). What fraction of the block's volume is below the water surface?

## Options
A) $0.3$
B) $0.5$
C) $0.6$
D) $0.8$

## Answer
C

## Explanation
For a floating object in equilibrium, $F_b = mg$, so $\rho_{\text{fluid}} V_{\text{sub}} g = \rho_{\text{object}} V_{\text{total}} g$. Therefore $V_{\text{sub}} / V_{\text{total}} = \rho_{\text{object}} / \rho_{\text{fluid}} = 600 / 1000 = 0.6$. The block is $60\%$ submerged.
