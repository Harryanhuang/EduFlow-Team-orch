An AC generator produces electricity by:

## Options
A) Creating charge from nothing
B) Rotating a coil in a magnetic field, changing the flux
C) Using a battery to push current through the coil
D) Heating the coil to produce electrons

## Answer
B

## Explanation
An AC generator works by rotating a coil (or magnet) in a magnetic field. As the coil rotates, the magnetic flux through it changes continuously, inducing an alternating EMF by Faraday's law. The mechanical energy of rotation is converted to electrical energy.
