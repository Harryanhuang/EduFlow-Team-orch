A magnetic dipole with moment $\mu = 0.50 \text{ A·m}^2$ is in a $0.20 \text{ T}$ field at an angle of $60°$. What is the torque?

## Options
A) $0.050 \text{ N·m}$
B) $0.087 \text{ N·m}$
C) $0.10 \text{ N·m}$
D) $0.17 \text{ N·m}$

## Answer
B

## Explanation
$\tau = \mu B \sin\theta = (0.50)(0.20)(\sin 60°) = 0.10 \times 0.866 = 0.087 \text{ N·m}$.
