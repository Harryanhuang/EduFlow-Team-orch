A $2.0 \text{ H}$ inductor carries $3.0 \text{ A}$. How much energy is stored in its magnetic field?

## Options
A) $3.0 \text{ J}$
B) $6.0 \text{ J}$
C) $9.0 \text{ J}$
D) $12 \text{ J}$

## Answer
C

## Explanation
$U = \frac{1}{2}LI^2 = \frac{1}{2}(2.0)(3.0)^2 = \frac{1}{2}(2.0)(9.0) = 9.0 \text{ J}$.
