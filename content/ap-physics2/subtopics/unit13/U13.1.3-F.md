A $0.50 \text{ m}$ wire carrying $4.0 \text{ A}$ is placed perpendicular to a $0.20 \text{ T}$ magnetic field. What is the force on the wire?

## Options
A) $0.10 \text{ N}$
B) $0.20 \text{ N}$
C) $0.40 \text{ N}$
D) $1.0 \text{ N}$

## Answer
C

## Explanation
$F = ILB\sin 90° = (4.0)(0.50)(0.20)(1) = 0.40 \text{ N}$.
