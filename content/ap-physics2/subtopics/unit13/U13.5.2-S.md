A transformer has $500$ primary turns and $2{,}000$ secondary turns. If the primary voltage is $120 \text{ V}$, what is the secondary voltage?

## Options
A) $30 \text{ V}$
B) $120 \text{ V}$
C) $480 \text{ V}$
D) $2{,}400 \text{ V}$

## Answer
C

## Explanation
$V_s = V_p \times (N_s/N_p) = 120 \times (2000/500) = 120 \times 4 = 480 \text{ V}$. This is a step-up transformer (more secondary turns).
