Two parallel wires carry equal currents in the same direction. At the midpoint between them, the magnetic fields from each wire:

## Options
A) Add together
B) Cancel each other
C) Are perpendicular to each other
D) Cannot be determined

## Answer
B

## Explanation
By the right-hand rule, the field from each wire at the midpoint points in opposite directions (one up, one down, or one into the page and one out). Since the currents are equal and the distances are equal, the magnitudes are equal. The fields cancel.
