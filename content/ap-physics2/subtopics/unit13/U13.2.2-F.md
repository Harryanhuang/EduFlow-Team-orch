Which material is paramagnetic?

## Options
A) Copper
B) Aluminum
C) Gold
D) Water

## Answer
B

## Explanation
Aluminum has unpaired electrons in its 3p subshell, making it paramagnetic (weakly attracted to magnetic fields). Copper, gold, and water are diamagnetic (weakly repelled).
