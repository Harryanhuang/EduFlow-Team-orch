A ferromagnetic material has a wide hysteresis loop with high remanence and high coercivity. This material is best suited for:

## Options
A) A temporary electromagnet core
B) A permanent magnet
C) A transformer core
D) A magnetic shield

## Answer
B

## Explanation
High remanence means the material retains strong magnetization after the field is removed. High coercivity means it resists demagnetization. These are ideal properties for a permanent magnet. A transformer core needs low hysteresis loss (narrow loop, low coercivity — "soft" magnetic material).
