The current in a $0.50 \text{ H}$ inductor increases from $2.0 \text{ A}$ to $5.0 \text{ A}$ in $0.30 \text{ s}$. What is the magnitude of the induced EMF?

## Options
A) $1.5 \text{ V}$
B) $3.0 \text{ V}$
C) $5.0 \text{ V}$
D) $10 \text{ V}$

## Answer
C

## Explanation
$|\varepsilon| = L|dI/dt| = 0.50 \times (5.0 - 2.0)/0.30 = 0.50 \times 10 = 5.0 \text{ V}$.
