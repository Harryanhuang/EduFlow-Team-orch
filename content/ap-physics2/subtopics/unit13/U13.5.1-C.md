A $0.40 \text{ m}$ metal rod slides at $3.0 \text{ m/s}$ on rails perpendicular to a $0.50 \text{ T}$ field. What is the induced EMF? If the circuit is completed by a $10 \text{ Ω}$ resistor, what current flows?

## Options
A) $0.06 \text{ V}$, $0.006 \text{ A}$
B) $0.60 \text{ V}$, $0.06 \text{ A}$
C) $6.0 \text{ V}$, $0.60 \text{ A}$
D) $6.0 \text{ V}$, $6.0 \text{ A}$

## Answer
B

## Explanation
$\varepsilon = BLv = (0.50)(0.40)(3.0) = 0.60 \text{ V}$. $I = \varepsilon/R = 0.60/10 = 0.06 \text{ A}$.
