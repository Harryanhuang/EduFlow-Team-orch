A rectangular coil with $20$ turns, area $0.01 \text{ m}^2$, and current $3.0 \text{ A}$ is in a $0.50 \text{ T}$ magnetic field. The plane of the coil is parallel to the field. What is the torque on the coil?

## Options
A) $0 \text{ N·m}$
B) $0.15 \text{ N·m}$
C) $0.30 \text{ N·m}$
D) $0.60 \text{ N·m}$

## Answer
C

## Explanation
When the plane is parallel to the field, the normal is perpendicular: $\theta = 90°$. $\tau = NIAB\sin 90° = (20)(3.0)(0.01)(0.50)(1) = 0.30 \text{ N·m}$.
