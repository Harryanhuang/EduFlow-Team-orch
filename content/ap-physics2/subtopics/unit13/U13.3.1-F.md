Which of the following is a scalar field?

## Options
A) Electric field
B) Magnetic field
C) Electric potential
D) Gravitational field

## Answer
C

## Explanation
Electric potential has only magnitude at each point — no direction. It is a scalar field. Electric field, magnetic field, and gravitational field all have both magnitude and direction at each point, making them vector fields.
