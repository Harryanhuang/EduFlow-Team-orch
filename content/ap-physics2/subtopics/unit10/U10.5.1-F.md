Which of the following processes occurs spontaneously?

## Options
A) Heat flowing from a cold object to a hot object
B) A gas expanding to fill its container
C) A broken egg reassembling itself
D) All of the above

## Answer
B

## Explanation
The second law states that entropy increases in spontaneous processes. A gas expanding to fill its container increases the number of accessible microstates (higher entropy). Heat flowing from cold to hot (A) decreases entropy and does not occur spontaneously. A broken egg reassembling (C) would require a decrease in entropy.
