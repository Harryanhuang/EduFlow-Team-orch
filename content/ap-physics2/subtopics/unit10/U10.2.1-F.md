A container holds $2.0 \text{ mol}$ of an ideal gas at $300 \text{ K}$ and a pressure of $1.0 \times 10^5 \text{ Pa}$. What is the volume of the gas? ($R = 8.314 \text{ J/(mol·K)}$)

## Options
A) $2.5 \times 10^{-3} \text{ m}^3$
B) $4.99 \times 10^{-2} \text{ m}^3$
C) $0.50 \text{ m}^3$
D) $5.0 \text{ m}^3$

## Answer
B

## Explanation
From $PV = nRT$: $V = nRT/P = (2.0)(8.314)(300) / (1.0 \times 10^5) = 4988.4 / 100{,}000 = 4.99 \times 10^{-2} \text{ m}^3$.
