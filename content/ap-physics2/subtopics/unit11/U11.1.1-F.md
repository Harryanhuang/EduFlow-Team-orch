Two point charges, $q_1 = +3 \text{ nC}$ and $q_2 = -5 \text{ nC}$, are separated by $0.20 \text{ m}$. What is the magnitude of the electrostatic force between them? ($k = 8.99 \times 10^9 \text{ N·m}^2/\text{C}^2$)

## Options
A) $3.4 \times 10^{-6} \text{ N}$
B) $3.4 \times 10^{-3} \text{ N}$
C) $6.7 \times 10^{-7} \text{ N}$
D) $7.5 \times 10^{-5} \text{ N}$

## Answer
A

## Explanation
$F = k|q_1 q_2|/r^2 = (8.99 \times 10^9)(3 \times 10^{-9})(5 \times 10^{-9}) / (0.20)^2 = (8.99 \times 15 \times 10^{-9}) / 0.04 = 1.349 \times 10^{-7} / 0.04 = 3.37 \times 10^{-6} \text{ N} \approx 3.4 \times 10^{-6} \text{ N}$.
