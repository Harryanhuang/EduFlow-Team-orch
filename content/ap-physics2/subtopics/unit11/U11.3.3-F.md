A $-3 \text{ nC}$ charge is placed in a uniform electric field of $5{,}000 \text{ N/C}$ pointing to the right. What is the force on the charge?

## Options
A) $1.5 \times 10^{-5} \text{ N}$ to the right
B) $1.5 \times 10^{-5} \text{ N}$ to the left
C) $6.7 \times 10^{-13} \text{ N}$ to the left
D) $1.5 \times 10^{-5} \text{ N}$ upward

## Answer
B

## Explanation
$F = qE = (3 \times 10^{-9})(5{,}000) = 1.5 \times 10^{-5} \text{ N}$. Since the charge is negative, the force is opposite to the field direction — to the left.
