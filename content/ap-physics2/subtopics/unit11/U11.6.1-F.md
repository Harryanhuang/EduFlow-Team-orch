What is the electric potential at a point $0.30 \text{ m}$ from a point charge of $+6 \text{ nC}$?

## Options
A) $+18 \text{ V}$
B) $+180 \text{ V}$
C) $+54 \text{ V}$
D) $+540 \text{ V}$

## Answer
B

## Explanation
$V = kq/r = (8.99 \times 10^9)(6 \times 10^{-9})/0.30 = 53.94/0.30 = 179.8 \text{ V} \approx 180 \text{ V}$.
