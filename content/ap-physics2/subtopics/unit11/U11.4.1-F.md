What is the electric field at a distance of $0.20 \text{ m}$ from a point charge of $+4 \text{ nC}$?

## Options
A) $90 \text{ N/C}$, radially outward
B) $900 \text{ N/C}$, radially outward
C) $900 \text{ N/C}$, radially inward
D) $9.0 \times 10^{3} \text{ N/C}$, radially outward

## Answer
B

## Explanation
$E = k|q|/r^2 = (8.99 \times 10^9)(4 \times 10^{-9})/(0.20)^2 = 35.96/0.04 = 899 \text{ N/C} \approx 900 \text{ N/C}$. Positive charge → field points radially outward.
