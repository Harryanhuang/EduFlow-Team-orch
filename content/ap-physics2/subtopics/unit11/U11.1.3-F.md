A neutral metal sphere is placed in a uniform external electric field pointing to the right. Which charge distribution results?

## Options
A) Positive charge on the left, negative on the right
B) Negative charge on the left, positive on the right
C) Positive charge on the top, negative on the bottom
D) No charge redistribution occurs

## Answer
B

## Explanation
In an electric field pointing right, free electrons (negative) in the conductor are pushed to the left (against the field), and positive ions remain on the right. This creates a separation: negative charge on the left (near the field source) and positive charge on the right (away from the field source).
