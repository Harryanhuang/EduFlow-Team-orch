A parallel-plate capacitor has a voltage of $120 \text{ V}$ across plates separated by $4.0 \text{ mm}$. What is the electric field between the plates?

## Options
A) $3.0 \times 10^{1} \text{ N/C}$
B) $3.0 \times 10^{3} \text{ N/C}$
C) $3.0 \times 10^{4} \text{ N/C}$
D) $4.8 \times 10^{5} \text{ N/C}$

## Answer
C

## Explanation
$E = V/d = 120 / (4.0 \times 10^{-3}) = 30{,}000 \text{ N/C} = 3.0 \times 10^4 \text{ N/C}$.
