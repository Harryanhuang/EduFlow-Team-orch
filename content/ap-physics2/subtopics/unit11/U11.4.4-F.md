In a correct electric field line diagram for two equal positive charges, which feature is correctly described?

## Options
A) Field lines connect the two charges.
B) Field lines from each charge point toward each other.
C) Field lines from each charge point outward and repel each other near the midpoint.
D) Field lines form closed loops between the charges.

## Answer
C

## Explanation
For two equal positive charges, field lines originate from each charge and point outward. Near the midpoint between them, the fields partially cancel, so the lines curve away from each other — they do not cross, connect, or form loops. The midpoint is a null point where $E = 0$.
