A metal sphere gains $2.0 \times 10^{12}$ excess electrons. What is the net charge on the sphere? ($e = 1.6 \times 10^{-19} \text{ C}$)

## Options
A) $+320 \text{ nC}$
B) $-320 \text{ nC}$
C) $+0.32 \text{ nC}$
D) $-0.32 \text{ nC}$

## Answer
B

## Explanation
Each electron has charge $-e = -1.6 \times 10^{-19} \text{ C}$. Total charge: $q = ne = (2.0 \times 10^{12})(-1.6 \times 10^{-19}) = -3.2 \times 10^{-7} \text{ C} = -320 \text{ nC}$.
