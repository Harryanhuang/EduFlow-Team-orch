A $10 \text{ kΩ}$ potentiometer is connected across a $5.0 \text{ V}$ source. The wiper is set at $40\%$ from the bottom. What is the output voltage?

## Options
A) $2.0 \text{ V}$
B) $3.0 \text{ V}$
C) $4.0 \text{ V}$
D) $5.0 \text{ V}$

## Answer
A

## Explanation
The potentiometer acts as a voltage divider. With the wiper at $40\%$ from the bottom, the bottom portion has resistance $0.40 \times 10 \text{ kΩ} = 4 \text{ kΩ}$. $V_{\text{out}} = V_{\text{in}} \times (R_{\text{bottom}} / R_{\text{total}}) = 5.0 \times 0.40 = 2.0 \text{ V}$.
