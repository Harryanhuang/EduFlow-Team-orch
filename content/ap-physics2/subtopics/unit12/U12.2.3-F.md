Two resistors of $6 \text{ Ω}$ and $12 \text{ Ω}$ are connected in parallel. What is the equivalent resistance?

## Options
A) $18 \text{ Ω}$
B) $4 \text{ Ω}$
C) $8 \text{ Ω}$
D) $2 \text{ Ω}$

## Answer
B

## Explanation
$1/R_{\text{eq}} = 1/6 + 1/12 = 2/12 + 1/12 = 3/12 = 1/4$. $R_{\text{eq}} = 4 \text{ Ω}$.
