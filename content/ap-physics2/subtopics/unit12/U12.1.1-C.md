A wire narrows from diameter $4.0 \text{ mm}$ to $2.0 \text{ mm}$. The current in the wide section is $3.0 \text{ A}$ and the drift velocity is $v_1$. What is the drift velocity in the narrow section?

## Options
A) $v_1$
B) $2v_1$
C) $4v_1$
D) $8v_1$

## Answer
C

## Explanation
Current is the same everywhere: $I = nqA_1 v_1 = nqA_2 v_2$. $v_2 = v_1 \times (A_1/A_2) = v_1 \times (\pi(2)^2 / \pi(1)^2) = v_1 \times (4/1) = 4v_1$. The drift velocity increases by the ratio of areas (which is the square of the diameter ratio).
