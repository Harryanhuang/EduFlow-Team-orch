A Wheatstone bridge has $R_1 = 3 \text{ Ω}$, $R_2 = 6 \text{ Ω}$, $R_3 = 5 \text{ Ω}$, and unknown $R_4$. The bridge is balanced (no current through the galvanometer). What is $R_4$?

## Options
A) $2.5 \text{ Ω}$
B) $5.0 \text{ Ω}$
C) $10 \text{ Ω}$
D) $15 \text{ Ω}$

## Answer
C

## Explanation
Balanced condition: $R_1/R_2 = R_3/R_4$. $3/6 = 5/R_4$. $0.5 = 5/R_4$. $R_4 = 10 \text{ Ω}$.
