Three resistors of $2 \text{ Ω}$, $3 \text{ Ω}$, and $5 \text{ Ω}$ are connected in series. What is the equivalent resistance?

## Options
A) $0.96 \text{ Ω}$
B) $6 \text{ Ω}$
C) $10 \text{ Ω}$
D) $30 \text{ Ω}$

## Answer
C

## Explanation
In series: $R_{\text{eq}} = 2 + 3 + 5 = 10 \text{ Ω}$.
