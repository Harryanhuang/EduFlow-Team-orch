A $10 \text{ μF}$ capacitor in series with a $100 \text{ kΩ}$ resistor is connected to a $12 \text{ V}$ battery. What is the time constant?

## Options
A) $0.10 \text{ s}$
B) $1.0 \text{ s}$
C) $10 \text{ s}$
D) $100 \text{ s}$

## Answer
B

## Explanation
$\tau = RC = (100 \times 10^3)(10 \times 10^{-6}) = 1.0 \text{ s}$.
