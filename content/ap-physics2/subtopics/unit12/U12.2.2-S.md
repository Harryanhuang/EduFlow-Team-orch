A $24 \text{ V}$ battery is connected to three series resistors: $R_1 = 4 \text{ Ω}$, $R_2 = 6 \text{ Ω}$, $R_3 = 2 \text{ Ω}$. What is the voltage across $R_2$?

## Options
A) $4 \text{ V}$
B) $8 \text{ V}$
C) $12 \text{ V}$
D) $18 \text{ V}$

## Answer
C

## Explanation
$R_{\text{eq}} = 4 + 6 + 2 = 12 \text{ Ω}$. $I = V/R_{\text{eq}} = 24/12 = 2.0 \text{ A}$. $V_2 = IR_2 = 2.0 \times 6 = 12 \text{ V}$.
