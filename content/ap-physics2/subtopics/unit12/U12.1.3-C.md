A $1{,}500 \text{ W}$ heater runs for $3.0 \text{ hours}$. If electricity costs $\$0.12 \text{ per kWh}$, what is the cost?

## Options
A) $\$0.36$
B) $\$0.54$
C) $\$1.08$
D) $\$5.40$

## Answer
B

## Explanation
Energy: $E = Pt = 1.5 \text{ kW} \times 3.0 \text{ h} = 4.5 \text{ kWh}$. Cost: $4.5 \times \$0.12 = \$0.54$.
