A $3 \text{ μF}$ capacitor and a $6 \text{ μF}$ capacitor are connected in parallel. What is the equivalent capacitance?

## Options
A) $2 \text{ μF}$
B) $9 \text{ μF}$
C) $18 \text{ μF}$
D) $4.5 \text{ μF}$

## Answer
B

## Explanation
In parallel, capacitances add: $C_{\text{eq}} = 3 + 6 = 9 \text{ μF}$. Note: this is the opposite of resistors in parallel.
