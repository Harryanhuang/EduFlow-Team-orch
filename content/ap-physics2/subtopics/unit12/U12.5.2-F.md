A circuit has a $12 \text{ V}$ battery supplying $2.0 \text{ A}$ to two resistors: $R_1 = 3 \text{ Ω}$ and $R_2 = 3 \text{ Ω}$ in series. What is the power supplied by the battery?

## Options
A) $6.0 \text{ W}$
B) $12 \text{ W}$
C) $24 \text{ W}$
D) $48 \text{ W}$

## Answer
C

## Explanation
$P_{\text{battery}} = \varepsilon I = 12 \times 2.0 = 24 \text{ W}$. Check: $P_{R1} + P_{R2} = I^2R_1 + I^2R_2 = (4)(3) + (4)(3) = 12 + 12 = 24 \text{ W}$. Power is conserved.
