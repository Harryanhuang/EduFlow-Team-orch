Which statement about superconductors is correct?

## Options
A) Superconductors have very low resistance at all temperatures.
B) Superconductors have zero resistance below a critical temperature $T_c$.
C) All materials become superconductors near absolute zero.
D) Superconductors cannot carry large currents.

## Answer
B

## Explanation
A superconductor transitions to zero resistivity ($\rho = 0$) when cooled below its critical temperature $T_c$. Above $T_c$, it behaves as a normal conductor. Not all materials become superconductors (C is wrong), and superconductors can carry very large currents without dissipation (D is wrong).
