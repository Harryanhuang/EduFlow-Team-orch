A single-loop circuit has a $12 \text{ V}$ battery and two resistors: $R_1 = 4 \text{ Ω}$ and $R_2 = 2 \text{ Ω}$ in series. Using Kirchhoff's loop rule, what is the current?

## Options
A) $1.0 \text{ A}$
B) $2.0 \text{ A}$
C) $3.0 \text{ A}$
D) $6.0 \text{ A}$

## Answer
B

## Explanation
Loop rule (clockwise): $+12 - I(4) - I(2) = 0$. $12 = 6I$. $I = 2.0 \text{ A}$.
