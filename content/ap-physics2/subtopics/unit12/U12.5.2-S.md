A battery with EMF $10 \text{ V}$ and internal resistance $5 \text{ Ω}$ is connected to a variable load resistor $R$. For what value of $R$ is the power delivered to the load maximized?

## Options
A) $0 \text{ Ω}$
B) $2.5 \text{ Ω}$
C) $5 \text{ Ω}$
D) $10 \text{ Ω}$

## Answer
C

## Explanation
Maximum power transfer occurs when $R_{\text{load}} = r_{\text{internal}} = 5 \text{ Ω}$. At this point: $I = 10/(5+5) = 1.0 \text{ A}$, $P = I^2 R = (1.0)^2 \times 5 = 5.0 \text{ W}$.
