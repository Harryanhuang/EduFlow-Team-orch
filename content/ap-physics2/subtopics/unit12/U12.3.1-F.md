A capacitor stores $6.0 \times 10^{-6} \text{ C}$ of charge when connected to a $12 \text{ V}$ battery. What is its capacitance?

## Options
A) $72 \text{ μF}$
B) $2.0 \text{ μF}$
C) $0.50 \text{ μF}$
D) $0.083 \text{ μF}$

## Answer
C

## Explanation
$C = Q/V = (6.0 \times 10^{-6}) / 12 = 5.0 \times 10^{-7} \text{ F} = 0.50 \text{ μF}$.
