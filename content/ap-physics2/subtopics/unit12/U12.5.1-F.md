At a junction, current $I_1 = 3 \text{ A}$ enters from the left and $I_2 = 1 \text{ A}$ enters from the top. What is the current $I_3$ leaving to the right?

## Options
A) $1 \text{ A}$
B) $2 \text{ A}$
C) $3 \text{ A}$
D) $4 \text{ A}$

## Answer
D

## Explanation
$I_{\text{in}} = I_{\text{out}}$. $I_1 + I_2 = I_3$. $3 + 1 = 4 \text{ A}$.
