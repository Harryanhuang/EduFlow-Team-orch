A battery has EMF $\varepsilon = 9.0 \text{ V}$ and internal resistance $r = 1.0 \text{ Ω}$. It is connected to an external resistor $R = 8.0 \text{ Ω}$. What is the terminal voltage of the battery?

## Options
A) $9.0 \text{ V}$
B) $8.0 \text{ V}$
C) $7.2 \text{ V}$
D) $1.0 \text{ V}$

## Answer
B

## Explanation
$I = \varepsilon / (R + r) = 9.0 / (8.0 + 1.0) = 1.0 \text{ A}$. Terminal voltage: $V = \varepsilon - Ir = 9.0 - 1.0 \times 1.0 = 8.0 \text{ V}$. Alternatively: $V = IR = 1.0 \times 8.0 = 8.0 \text{ V}$.
