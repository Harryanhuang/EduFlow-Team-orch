A $12 \text{ V}$ battery is connected to two parallel resistors: $R_1 = 4 \text{ Ω}$ and $R_2 = 6 \text{ Ω}$. What is the current through $R_2$?

## Options
A) $1.0 \text{ A}$
B) $2.0 \text{ A}$
C) $3.0 \text{ A}$
D) $5.0 \text{ A}$

## Answer
B

## Explanation
In parallel, voltage is the same: $V = 12 \text{ V}$ across both. $I_2 = V/R_2 = 12/6 = 2.0 \text{ A}$.
