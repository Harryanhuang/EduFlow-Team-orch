$3.0 \times 10^{18}$ electrons pass through a wire cross-section in $2.0 \text{ s}$. What is the current? ($e = 1.6 \times 10^{-19} \text{ C}$)

## Options
A) $0.24 \text{ A}$
B) $1.5 \text{ A}$
C) $2.4 \text{ A}$
D) $6.0 \times 10^{18} \text{ A}$

## Answer
A

## Explanation
Total charge: $q = ne = (3.0 \times 10^{18})(1.6 \times 10^{-19}) = 0.48 \text{ C}$. Current: $I = q/t = 0.48 / 2.0 = 0.24 \text{ A}$.
