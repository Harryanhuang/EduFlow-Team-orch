A $12 \text{ Ω}$ resistor carries a current of $2.0 \text{ A}$. What is the power dissipated?

## Options
A) $6.0 \text{ W}$
B) $24 \text{ W}$
C) $48 \text{ W}$
D) $96 \text{ W}$

## Answer
C

## Explanation
$P = I^2R = (2.0)^2 \times 12 = 4 \times 12 = 48 \text{ W}$.
