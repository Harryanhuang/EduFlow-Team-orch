Rank the penetrating power of alpha, beta, and gamma radiation from least to greatest.

## Options
A) Alpha < Beta < Gamma
B) Gamma < Beta < Alpha
C) Beta < Alpha < Gamma
D) Alpha < Gamma < Beta

## Answer
A

## Explanation
Alpha particles are stopped by paper or skin. Beta particles penetrate paper but are stopped by thin metal. Gamma rays are highly penetrating and require thick lead or concrete to stop.
