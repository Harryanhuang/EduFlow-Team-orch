The Compton effect provided strong evidence for:

## Options
A) The wave nature of light
B) The particle nature of light
C) The existence of the electron
D) Nuclear structure

## Answer
B

## Explanation
Compton's experiment showed that X-ray photons scatter off electrons like particles, with momentum $p = h/\lambda$ (and energy $E = hf$). The wavelength shift matched the prediction using photon momentum and energy-momentum conservation, providing direct evidence for the particle nature of EM radiation.
