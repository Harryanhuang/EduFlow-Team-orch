A $10 \text{ W}$ laser emits green light at $\lambda = 550 \text{ nm}$. How many photons per second does it emit? ($h = 6.63 \times 10^{-34} \text{ J·s}$, $c = 3.0 \times 10^8 \text{ m/s}$)

## Options
A) $2.8 \times 10^{19} \text{ photons/s}$
B) $2.8 \times 10^{18} \text{ photons/s}$
C) $2.8 \times 10^{20} \text{ photons/s}$
D) $1.1 \times 10^{19} \text{ photons/s}$

## Answer
A

## Explanation
$E_{\text{photon}} = hc/\lambda = (6.63 \times 10^{-34})(3.0 \times 10^8)/(550 \times 10^{-9}) = 3.62 \times 10^{-19} \text{ J}$. $N/t = P/E_{\text{photon}} = 10/(3.62 \times 10^{-19}) = 2.76 \times 10^{19} \text{ photons/s} \approx 2.8 \times 10^{19}$.
