An electron is accelerated from rest through a $100 \text{ V}$ potential. What is its de Broglie wavelength?

## Options
A) $0.12 \text{ nm}$
B) $1.2 \text{ nm}$
C) $12 \text{ nm}$
D) $120 \text{ nm}$

## Answer
A

## Explanation
$\lambda = h/p = h/\sqrt{2meV}$. Numerically: $\lambda(\text{nm}) = 1.23/\sqrt{V(\text{V})} = 1.23/\sqrt{100} = 1.23/10 = 0.123 \text{ nm} \approx 0.12 \text{ nm}$.
