In alpha decay, the daughter nucleus has:

## Options
A) Mass number 4 less, atomic number 2 less
B) Mass number 2 less, atomic number 4 less
C) Mass number 4 more, atomic number 2 more
D) Same mass number, atomic number 2 less

## Answer
A

## Explanation
An alpha particle is ${}^{4}_{2}\text{He}$. When emitted, the daughter has $A' = A - 4$ and $Z' = Z - 2$.
