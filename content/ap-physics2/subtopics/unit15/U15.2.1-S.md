${}^{238}_{92}\text{U}$ undergoes a decay series involving 8 alpha decays and 6 beta decays. What is the final stable product?

## Options
A) ${}^{206}_{82}\text{Pb}$
B) ${}^{210}_{84}\text{Po}$
C) ${}^{222}_{86}\text{Rn}$
D) ${}^{234}_{90}\text{Th}$

## Answer
A

## Explanation
8 alpha decays: $A = 238 - 8(4) = 206$, $Z = 92 - 8(2) = 76$. But 6 beta decays (${}^{0}_{-1}e$): $Z = 76 + 6(1) = 82$. Final: ${}^{206}_{82}\text{Pb}$, lead-206, which is stable.
