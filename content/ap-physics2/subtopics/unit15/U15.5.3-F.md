A hydrogen atom transitions from $n = 4$ to $n = 2$. What is the energy of the emitted photon? ($E_n = -13.6/n^2 \text{ eV}$)

## Options
A) $0.85 \text{ eV}$
B) $2.55 \text{ eV}$
C) $13.6 \text{ eV}$
D) $12.75 \text{ eV}$

## Answer
B

## Explanation
$E_4 = -13.6/16 = -0.85 \text{ eV}$. $E_2 = -13.6/4 = -3.40 \text{ eV}$. $\Delta E = -0.85 - (-3.40) = 2.55 \text{ eV}$. This corresponds to the H-beta line of the Balmer series (visible blue-green).
