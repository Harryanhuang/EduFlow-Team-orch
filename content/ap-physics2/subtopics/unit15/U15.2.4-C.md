A wooden artifact has a ${}^{14}\text{C}/{}^{12}\text{C}$ ratio of $1/8$ the atmospheric value. How old is the artifact? ($T_{1/2} = 5730$ years)

## Options
A) $5{,}730$ years
B) $11{,}460$ years
C) $17{,}190$ years
D) $45{,}840$ years

## Answer
C

## Explanation
$1/8 = (1/2)^3$ means 3 half-lives have passed. Age $= 3 \times 5{,}730 = 17{,}190$ years.
