A sample has half-life 5.0 years. How many years does it take for the sample to decay to $12.5\%$ of its original amount?

## Options
A) $5.0$ years
B) $7.5$ years
C) $10$ years
D) $15$ years

## Answer
D

## Explanation
$12.5\% = 1/8 = (1/2)^3$. So 3 half-lives: $3 \times 5.0 = 15$ years.
