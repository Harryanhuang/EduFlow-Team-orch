Electrons are accelerated through a $150 \text{ V}$ potential. Their de Broglie wavelength is approximately:

## Options
A) $0.05 \text{ nm}$
B) $0.1 \text{ nm}$
C) $0.5 \text{ nm}$
D) $1.0 \text{ nm}$

## Answer
B

## Explanation
$\lambda(\text{nm}) \approx 1.23/\sqrt{V} = 1.23/\sqrt{150} = 1.23/12.25 = 0.10 \text{ nm}$. This wavelength is comparable to atomic spacings, allowing diffraction from crystals.
