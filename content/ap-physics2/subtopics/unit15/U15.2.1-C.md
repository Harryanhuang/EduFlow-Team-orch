In the alpha decay ${}^{212}_{84}\text{Po} \to {}^{208}_{82}\text{Pb} + {}^{4}_{2}\text{He}$, the Q-value is $8.95 \text{ MeV}$. What is the kinetic energy of the alpha particle?

## Options
A) $8.95 \text{ MeV}$
B) $8.78 \text{ MeV}$
C) $4.48 \text{ MeV}$
D) $0.17 \text{ MeV}$

## Answer
B

## Explanation
By momentum conservation: $m_{\alpha}v_{\alpha} = m_{\text{Pb}}v_{\text{Pb}}$. The alpha gets most of the energy because it's much lighter: $K_{\alpha} = Q \times m_{\text{Pb}}/(m_{\alpha} + m_{\text{Pb}}) = 8.95 \times 208/212 = 8.78 \text{ MeV}$.
