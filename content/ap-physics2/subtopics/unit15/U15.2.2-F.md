In beta-minus decay of ${}^{14}_{6}\text{C}$, the daughter nucleus is:

## Options
A) ${}^{14}_{5}\text{B}$
B) ${}^{14}_{7}\text{N}$
C) ${}^{13}_{6}\text{C}$
D) ${}^{15}_{7}\text{N}$

## Answer
B

## Explanation
Beta-minus: $Z$ increases by 1 ($6 \to 7$), $A$ unchanged ($14$). The daughter is ${}^{14}_{7}\text{N}$ (nitrogen-14).
