What is the energy of a photon with wavelength $500 \text{ nm}$? ($h = 6.63 \times 10^{-34} \text{ J·s}$, $c = 3.0 \times 10^8 \text{ m/s}$)

## Options
A) $1.3 \times 10^{-19} \text{ J}$
B) $4.0 \times 10^{-19} \text{ J}$
C) $3.0 \times 10^{-19} \text{ J}$
D) $1.3 \times 10^{-27} \text{ J}$

## Answer
B

## Explanation
$E = hc/\lambda = (6.63 \times 10^{-34})(3.0 \times 10^8)/(500 \times 10^{-9}) = 1.989 \times 10^{-25}/5.0 \times 10^{-7} = 3.98 \times 10^{-19} \text{ J} \approx 4.0 \times 10^{-19} \text{ J}$.
