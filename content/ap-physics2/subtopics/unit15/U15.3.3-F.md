A photon scatters at $90°$ from a free electron. What is the wavelength shift? ($\lambda_C = 2.43 \text{ pm}$)

## Options
A) $0 \text{ pm}$
B) $2.43 \text{ pm}$
C) $4.86 \text{ pm}$
D) $1.21 \text{ pm}$

## Answer
B

## Explanation
$\Delta\lambda = \lambda_C(1 - \cos\theta) = 2.43(1 - \cos 90°) = 2.43(1 - 0) = 2.43 \text{ pm}$.
