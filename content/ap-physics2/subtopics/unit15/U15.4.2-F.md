Which experiment demonstrates the wave nature of light?

## Options
A) Photoelectric effect
B) Compton scattering
C) Double-slit interference
D) Blackbody radiation peak

## Answer
C

## Explanation
The double-slit interference pattern shows wave behavior (interference fringes). The photoelectric effect and Compton scattering show particle behavior (photons with discrete energy/momentum).
