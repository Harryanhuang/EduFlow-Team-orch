A sample contains $1.0 \times 10^{12}$ nuclei with decay constant $\lambda = 3.0 \times 10^{-8} \text{ s}^{-1}$. What is the activity?

## Options
A) $3.0 \times 10^{4} \text{ Bq}$
B) $3.0 \times 10^{4} \text{ decays/s}$
C) $3.0 \times 10^{20} \text{ Bq}$
D) $0.30 \text{ Bq}$

## Answer
B

## Explanation
$A = \lambda N = (3.0 \times 10^{-8})(1.0 \times 10^{12}) = 3.0 \times 10^4$ decays/s = $3.0 \times 10^4 \text{ Bq}$.
