The Sun's spectrum shows dark absorption lines first studied by Fraunhofer. These lines:

## Options
A) Show the Sun's composition
B) Indicate the Sun is moving toward or away from us (via Doppler shift)
C) Both A and B
D) Show only hydrogen

## Answer
C

## Explanation
Fraunhofer lines are absorption lines caused by the Sun's cooler outer atmosphere absorbing specific wavelengths. Their positions identify chemical elements (composition), and their shift from lab wavelengths reveals the Sun's motion (Doppler shift).
