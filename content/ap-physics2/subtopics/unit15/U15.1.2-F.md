In the notation ${}^{14}_{7}\text{N}$, how many neutrons does the nitrogen nucleus have?

## Options
A) $7$
B) $14$
C) $21$
D) $8$

## Answer
A

## Explanation
Neutrons $= A - Z = 14 - 7 = 7$.
