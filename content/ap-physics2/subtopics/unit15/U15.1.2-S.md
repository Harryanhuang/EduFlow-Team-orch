Complete: ${}^{238}_{92}\text{U} \to {}^{234}_{90}\text{Th} + {}$

## Options
A) ${}^{4}_{2}\text{He}$
B) ${}^{0}_{-1}e$
C) ${}^{1}_{0}n$
D) ${}^{0}_{+1}e$

## Answer
A

## Explanation
Conserve $A$: $238 = 234 + A$, so $A = 4$. Conserve $Z$: $92 = 90 + Z$, so $Z = 2$. The particle with $A = 4$, $Z = 2$ is ${}^{4}_{2}\text{He}$, an alpha particle. This is alpha decay.
