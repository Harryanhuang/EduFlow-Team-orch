A radioactive sample has a half-life of 10 days. What fraction remains after 30 days?

## Options
A) $1/3$
B) $1/8$
C) $1/10$
D) $1/30$

## Answer
B

## Explanation
30 days = 3 half-lives. Fraction remaining $= (1/2)^3 = 1/8$.
