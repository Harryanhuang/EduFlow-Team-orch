A photon has energy $3.2 \times 10^{-19} \text{ J}$. What is this in electron-volts?

## Options
A) $1.0 \text{ eV}$
B) $2.0 \text{ eV}$
C) $3.2 \text{ eV}$
D) $5.0 \text{ eV}$

## Answer
B

## Explanation
$E(\text{eV}) = E(\text{J})/e = (3.2 \times 10^{-19})/(1.6 \times 10^{-19}) = 2.0 \text{ eV}$.
