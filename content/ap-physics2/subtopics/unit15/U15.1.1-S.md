At the scale of an atomic nucleus, which force is dominant?

## Options
A) Gravitational
B) Electromagnetic
C) Strong nuclear
D) Weak nuclear

## Answer
C

## Explanation
At nuclear distances ($\sim 10^{-15}$ m), the strong nuclear force is approximately $100$ times stronger than the electromagnetic force and $10^{38}$ times stronger than gravity. It dominates nuclear structure despite its very short range.
