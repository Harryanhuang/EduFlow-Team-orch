Which fundamental force is responsible for holding protons and neutrons together in the nucleus?

## Options
A) Gravitational force
B) Electromagnetic force
C) Strong nuclear force
D) Weak nuclear force

## Answer
C

## Explanation
The strong nuclear force binds protons and neutrons (nucleons) together in the nucleus. It is the strongest of the four fundamental forces but acts only over very short distances ($\sim 10^{-15} \text{ m}$).
