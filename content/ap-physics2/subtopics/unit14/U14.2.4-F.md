An open organ pipe of length $1.5 \text{ m}$ is open at both ends. Speed of sound $= 340 \text{ m/s}$. What is the fundamental frequency?

## Options
A) $57 \text{ Hz}$
B) $113 \text{ Hz}$
C) $227 \text{ Hz}$
D) $340 \text{ Hz}$

## Answer
B

## Explanation
Open pipe: $f_1 = v/(2L) = 340/(2 \times 1.5) = 340/3.0 = 113 \text{ Hz}$.
