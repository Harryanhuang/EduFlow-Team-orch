Two coherent sources produce waves of wavelength $0.50 \text{ m}$. At a point, the path difference is $1.25 \text{ m}$. What type of interference occurs?

## Options
A) Constructive
B) Destructive
C) Neither
D) Cannot be determined

## Answer
B

## Explanation
$\Delta x / \lambda = 1.25 / 0.50 = 2.5 = 2 + \frac{1}{2}$. This is $(n + \frac{1}{2})\lambda$ with $n = 2$, so destructive interference.
