A diffraction grating has $500 \text{ lines/mm}$. What is the grating spacing $d$?

## Options
A) $5.0 \times 10^{-3} \text{ mm}$
B) $2.0 \times 10^{-3} \text{ mm}$
C) $5.0 \times 10^{-4} \text{ mm}$
D) $2.0 \times 10^{-6} \text{ m}$

## Answer
B

## Explanation
$d = 1/N = 1/500 \text{ mm} = 2.0 \times 10^{-3} \text{ mm} = 2.0 \text{ μm}$.
