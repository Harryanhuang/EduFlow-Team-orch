A coin lies at the bottom of a pool $2.0 \text{ m}$ deep ($n_{\text{water}} = 1.33$). What is the apparent depth when viewed from directly above?

## Options
A) $1.3 \text{ m}$
B) $1.5 \text{ m}$
C) $2.0 \text{ m}$
D) $2.7 \text{ m}$

## Answer
B

## Explanation
$d' = d/n = 2.0/1.33 = 1.50 \text{ m}$. The pool appears shallower than it actually is.
