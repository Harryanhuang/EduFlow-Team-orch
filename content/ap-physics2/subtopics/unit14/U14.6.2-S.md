An object is $20 \text{ cm}$ in front of a diverging lens with $f = -10 \text{ cm}$. Where is the image?

## Options
A) $20 \text{ cm}$ on the opposite side
B) $20 \text{ cm}$ on the same side (virtual)
C) $6.7 \text{ cm}$ on the same side (virtual)
D) $6.7 \text{ cm}$ on the opposite side

## Answer
C

## Explanation
$1/d_i = 1/f - 1/d_o = 1/(-10) - 1/20 = -1/10 - 1/20 = -2/20 - 1/20 = -3/20$. $d_i = -20/3 = -6.7 \text{ cm}$. Negative → virtual image on the same side as the object, 6.7 cm from the lens.
