A grating with $d = 3.0 \text{ μm}$ is illuminated with $\lambda = 600 \text{ nm}$ light. What is the highest order visible?

## Options
A) $3$
B) $4$
C) $5$
D) $6$

## Answer
C

## Explanation
$m_{\text{max}} = \lfloor d/\lambda \rfloor = \lfloor 3000/600 \rfloor = \lfloor 5 \rfloor = 5$. At $m = 5$: $\sin\theta = 5 \times 600/3000 = 1.0$, meaning $\theta = 90°$ (grazing). This is technically the limit.
