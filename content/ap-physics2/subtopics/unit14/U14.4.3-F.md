What is the critical angle for light traveling from water ($n = 1.33$) to air?

## Options
A) $37°$
B) $42°$
C) $49°$
D) $53°$

## Answer
C

## Explanation
$\sin\theta_c = n_{\text{air}}/n_{\text{water}} = 1.00/1.33 = 0.752$. $\theta_c = \arcsin(0.752) = 48.8° \approx 49°$.
