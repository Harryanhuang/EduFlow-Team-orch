An object is placed $30 \text{ cm}$ in front of a concave mirror with focal length $10 \text{ cm}$. Where is the image?

## Options
A) $7.5 \text{ cm}$ in front of the mirror
B) $15 \text{ cm}$ in front of the mirror
C) $15 \text{ cm}$ behind the mirror
D) $30 \text{ cm}$ in front of the mirror

## Answer
B

## Explanation
$1/d_i = 1/f - 1/d_o = 1/10 - 1/30 = (3-1)/30 = 2/30 = 1/15$. $d_i = 15 \text{ cm}$. Positive $d_i$ → real image, in front of the mirror.
