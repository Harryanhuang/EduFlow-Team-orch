What is the speed of sound in air at $35°\text{C}$?

## Options
A) $331 \text{ m/s}$
B) $343 \text{ m/s}$
C) $352 \text{ m/s}$
D) $365 \text{ m/s}$

## Answer
C

## Explanation
$v = 331 + 0.6T = 331 + 0.6(35) = 331 + 21 = 352 \text{ m/s}$.
