You walk toward a plane mirror at $2.0 \text{ m/s}$. At what speed does your image approach you?

## Options
A) $1.0 \text{ m/s}$
B) $2.0 \text{ m/s}$
C) $4.0 \text{ m/s}$
D) $0 \text{ m/s}$

## Answer
C

## Explanation
You approach the mirror at $2.0 \text{ m/s}$; your image also approaches the mirror at $2.0 \text{ m/s}$ from the other side. The relative speed of approach between you and your image is $2.0 + 2.0 = 4.0 \text{ m/s}$.
