A grating with $10{,}000$ lines is illuminated in the second order ($m = 2$) with $\lambda = 500 \text{ nm}$. What is the minimum wavelength difference it can resolve?

## Options
A) $0.025 \text{ nm}$
B) $0.050 \text{ nm}$
C) $0.10 \text{ nm}$
D) $0.25 \text{ nm}$

## Answer
A

## Explanation
$R = mN = 2 \times 10{,}000 = 20{,}000$. $\Delta\lambda = \lambda/R = 500/20{,}000 = 0.025 \text{ nm}$.
