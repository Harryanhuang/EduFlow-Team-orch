Unpolarized light of intensity $I_0$ passes through a polarizer, then through a second polarizer whose axis is at $45°$ to the first. What is the transmitted intensity?

## Options
A) $I_0/4$
B) $I_0/2$
C) $I_0 \cos^2(45°) = I_0/2$
D) $I_0/8$

## Answer
A

## Explanation
After the first polarizer: $I_1 = I_0/2$ (unpolarized → polarized reduces by half). After the second at $45°$: $I_2 = I_1 \cos^2(45°) = (I_0/2)(1/2) = I_0/4$.
