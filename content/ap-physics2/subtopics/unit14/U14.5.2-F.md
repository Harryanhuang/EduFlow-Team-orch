A convex mirror:

## Options
A) Converges light to a focal point
B) Always forms a virtual, diminished image
C) Can form real images of distant objects
D) Has a positive focal length

## Answer
B

## Explanation
A convex mirror diverges light and always forms virtual, upright, and diminished (smaller) images regardless of object position. This is why convex mirrors are used as security mirrors and in vehicle side mirrors (objects are closer than they appear).
