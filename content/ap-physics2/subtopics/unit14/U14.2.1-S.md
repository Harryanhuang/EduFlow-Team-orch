A string with linear density $\mu = 0.005 \text{ kg/m}$ is under tension $T = 20 \text{ N}$. What is the wave speed?

## Options
A) $10 \text{ m/s}$
B) $40 \text{ m/s}$
C) $63 \text{ m/s}$
D) $100 \text{ m/s}$

## Answer
C

## Explanation
$v = \sqrt{T/\mu} = \sqrt{20/0.005} = \sqrt{4000} = 63.2 \text{ m/s} \approx 63 \text{ m/s}$.
