A string has a fundamental frequency of $100 \text{ Hz}$. At which driving frequency will the string NOT resonate?

## Options
A) $100 \text{ Hz}$
B) $200 \text{ Hz}$
C) $250 \text{ Hz}$
D) $300 \text{ Hz}$

## Answer
C

## Explanation
A string fixed at both ends resonates at integer multiples of the fundamental: $100, 200, 300, 400, \ldots$ Hz. $250 \text{ Hz}$ is not an integer multiple ($250/100 = 2.5$), so the string will not resonate at this frequency.
