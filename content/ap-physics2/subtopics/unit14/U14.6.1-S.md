An object is placed $30 \text{ cm}$ in front of a converging lens with focal length $10 \text{ cm}$. Where is the image?

## Options
A) $10 \text{ cm}$ on the same side as the object
B) $15 \text{ cm}$ on the opposite side
C) $30 \text{ cm}$ on the opposite side
D) $20 \text{ cm}$ on the opposite side

## Answer
B

## Explanation
$1/d_i = 1/f - 1/d_o = 1/10 - 1/30 = (3-1)/30 = 2/30 = 1/15$. $d_i = 15 \text{ cm}$. Positive → real image on the opposite side.
