An ambulance siren emits $800 \text{ Hz}$ and approaches you at $30 \text{ m/s}$. You are stationary. Speed of sound $= 340 \text{ Hz}$. What frequency do you hear?

## Options
A) $737 \text{ Hz}$
B) $800 \text{ Hz}$
C) $876 \text{ Hz}$
D) $941 \text{ Hz}$

## Answer
C

## Explanation
Source approaching, observer stationary: $f' = f \cdot v/(v - v_s) = 800 \times 340/(340 - 30) = 800 \times 340/310 = 800 \times 1.097 = 877 \text{ Hz} \approx 876 \text{ Hz}$.
