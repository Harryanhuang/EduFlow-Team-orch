Which of the following is an electromagnetic wave?

## Options
A) Sound wave
B) Water wave
C) X-ray
D) Seismic wave

## Answer
C

## Explanation
X-rays are electromagnetic waves — they consist of oscillating electric and magnetic fields and travel through vacuum. Sound, water, and seismic waves are mechanical waves that require a medium.
