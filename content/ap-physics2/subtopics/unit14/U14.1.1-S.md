Which electromagnetic wave has the highest frequency?

## Options
A) Radio waves
B) Microwaves
C) Ultraviolet light
D) Gamma rays

## Answer
D

## Explanation
Gamma rays have the highest frequency (and shortest wavelength) in the EM spectrum. Frequency increases from radio waves → microwaves → infrared → visible → ultraviolet → X-rays → gamma rays.
