Light travels from air ($n_1 = 1.00$) to glass ($n_2 = 1.50$) at an angle of incidence of $30°$. What is the angle of refraction?

## Options
A) $19°$
B) $30°$
C) $45°$
D) $49°$

## Answer
A

## Explanation
$\sin\theta_2 = (n_1/n_2)\sin\theta_1 = (1.00/1.50)\sin 30° = (2/3)(0.5) = 0.333$. $\theta_2 = \arcsin(0.333) = 19.5° \approx 19°$.
