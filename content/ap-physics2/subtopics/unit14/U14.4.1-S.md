Two plane mirrors are placed at $60°$ to each other. How many images are formed of an object placed between them?

## Options
A) $3$
B) $5$
C) $6$
D) $11$

## Answer
B

## Explanation
$n = (360°/\alpha) - 1 = (360/60) - 1 = 6 - 1 = 5$ images.
