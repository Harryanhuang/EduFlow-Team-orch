A magnifying glass with $f = 5.0 \text{ cm}$ is used by a person with a near point of $25 \text{ cm}$. What is the angular magnification?

## Options
A) $1 \times$
B) $3 \times$
C) $5 \times$
D) $25 \times$

## Answer
C

## Explanation
For relaxed-eye angular magnification: $M = N/f = 25/5.0 = 5 \times$. The image appears at the near point ($25 \text{ cm}$), magnified 5 times the angular size.
