A piece of white paper scatters light in all directions. This is an example of:

## Options
A) Specular reflection
B) Diffuse reflection
C) Total internal reflection
D) Absorption

## Answer
B

## Explanation
White paper has a rough surface at the microscopic level. Each ray obeys the law of reflection locally, but the varied surface orientation scatters light in many directions. This is diffuse reflection, which allows us to see the paper from any angle.
