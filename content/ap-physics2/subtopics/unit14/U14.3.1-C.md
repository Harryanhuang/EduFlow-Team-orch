Two coherent waves have amplitudes $A$ and $2A$. What is the ratio $I_{\text{max}}/I_{\text{min}}$?

## Options
A) $2$
B) $4$
C) $9$
D) $16$

## Answer
C

## Explanation
$I_{\text{max}} \propto (A + 2A)^2 = 9A^2$. $I_{\text{min}} \propto (2A - A)^2 = A^2$. Ratio $= 9A^2/A^2 = 9$.
