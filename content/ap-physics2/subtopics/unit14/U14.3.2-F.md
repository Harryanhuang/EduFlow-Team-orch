In a double-slit experiment, slit separation $d = 0.20 \text{ mm}$, screen distance $L = 2.0 \text{ m}$, wavelength $\lambda = 600 \text{ nm}$. What is the fringe spacing?

## Options
A) $1.0 \text{ mm}$
B) $3.0 \text{ mm}$
C) $6.0 \text{ mm}$
D) $12 \text{ mm}$

## Answer
C

## Explanation
$\Delta y = \lambda L / d = (600 \times 10^{-9})(2.0)/(0.20 \times 10^{-3}) = 1.2 \times 10^{-6} / 2.0 \times 10^{-4} = 6.0 \times 10^{-3} \text{ m} = 6.0 \text{ mm}$.
