The intensity of a wave at distance $r$ from a point source is $I$. At distance $3r$, the intensity is:

## Options
A) $I/3$
B) $I/9$
C) $I/27$
D) $3I$

## Answer
B

## Explanation
For a point source, $I \propto 1/r^2$. At $3r$: $I' = I/(3)^2 = I/9$.
