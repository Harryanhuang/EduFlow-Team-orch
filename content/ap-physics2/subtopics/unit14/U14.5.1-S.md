When you raise your right hand in front of a plane mirror, the image raises its:

## Options
A) Right hand (no inversion)
B) Left hand (lateral inversion)
C) Right foot
D) Both hands

## Answer
B

## Explanation
A plane mirror produces lateral inversion — left and right are swapped from the viewer's perspective. When you raise your right hand, the image appears to raise its left hand.
