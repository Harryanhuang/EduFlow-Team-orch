A photon of green light has wavelength $550 \text{ nm}$. What is its energy? ($h = 6.63 \times 10^{-34} \text{ J·s}$, $c = 3.0 \times 10^8 \text{ m/s}$)

## Options
A) $1.2 \times 10^{-19} \text{ J}$
B) $2.4 \times 10^{-19} \text{ J}$
C) $3.6 \times 10^{-19} \text{ J}$
D) $4.8 \times 10^{-19} \text{ J}$

## Answer
C

## Explanation
$E = hc/\lambda = (6.63 \times 10^{-34})(3.0 \times 10^8)/(550 \times 10^{-9}) = 1.989 \times 10^{-25}/(5.5 \times 10^{-7}) = 3.62 \times 10^{-19} \text{ J}$.
