Light travels from glass ($n = 1.50$) to air. What is the critical angle for total internal reflection?

## Options
A) $30°$
B) $42°$
C) $48°$
D) $60°$

## Answer
B

## Explanation
$\sin\theta_c = n_2/n_1 = 1.00/1.50 = 0.667$. $\theta_c = \arcsin(0.667) = 41.8° \approx 42°$.
