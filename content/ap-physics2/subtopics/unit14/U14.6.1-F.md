A convex lens is used to:

## Options
A) Diverge light rays
B) Form virtual images of distant objects
C) Converge parallel light to a focal point
D) Always produce real images

## Answer
C

## Explanation
A convex lens is a converging lens — it brings parallel light rays to a focal point on the far side. This property is used in magnifying glasses, cameras, and eyeglasses for farsighted people.
