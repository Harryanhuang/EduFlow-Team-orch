A double-slit apparatus is submerged in water ($n = 1.33$). Compared to air, the fringe spacing:

## Options
A) Increases by a factor of $1.33$
B) Decreases by a factor of $1.33$
C) Stays the same
D) Doubles

## Answer
B

## Explanation
In water, $\lambda_n = \lambda_0 / n = \lambda_0 / 1.33$. Since $\Delta y = \lambda L / d$, the fringe spacing decreases by a factor of $n = 1.33$.
