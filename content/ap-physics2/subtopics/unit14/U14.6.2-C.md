A person can see nearby objects clearly but not distant ones. This person is:

## Options
A) Farsighted (hyperopic) — needs convex lens
B) Nearsighted (myopic) — needs concave lens
C) Farsighted — needs concave lens
D) Nearsighted — needs convex lens

## Answer
B

## Explanation
A nearsighted (myopic) eye focuses light from distant objects in front of the retina (eyeball too long or lens too strong). A concave (diverging) lens pre-spreads the light so the eye's lens can focus it on the retina.
