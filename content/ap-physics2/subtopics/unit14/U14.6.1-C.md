A $+5.0$ diopter lens is placed in contact with a $-2.0$ diopter lens. What is the combined focal length?

## Options
A) $1/3 \text{ m} = 0.33 \text{ m}$
B) $1/7 \text{ m} \approx 0.14 \text{ m}$
C) $1/3.0 \text{ m} = 0.33 \text{ m}$
D) $7.0 \text{ m}$

## Answer
A

## Explanation
$P_{\text{total}} = P_1 + P_2 = 5.0 + (-2.0) = 3.0$ diopters. $f = 1/P = 1/3.0 \text{ m} = 0.33 \text{ m}$.
