A telescope has a $10 \text{ cm}$ diameter objective lens. What is the minimum angular separation it can resolve for green light ($550 \text{ nm}$)?

## Options
A) $3.4 \times 10^{-4} \text{ rad}$
B) $6.7 \times 10^{-4} \text{ rad}$
C) $1.2 \times 10^{-3} \text{ rad}$
D) $5.5 \times 10^{-3} \text{ rad}$

## Answer
B

## Explanation
$\theta_{\text{min}} = 1.22\lambda/D = 1.22 \times 550 \times 10^{-9} / 0.10 = 6.71 \times 10^{-6} / 0.10 = 6.71 \times 10^{-4} \text{ rad}$.
