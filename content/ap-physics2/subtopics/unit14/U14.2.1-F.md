A sound wave of frequency $680 \text{ Hz}$ travels through air at $340 \text{ m/s}$. What is its wavelength?

## Options
A) $0.25 \text{ m}$
B) $0.50 \text{ m}$
C) $1.0 \text{ m}$
D) $2.0 \text{ m}$

## Answer
B

## Explanation
$\lambda = v/f = 340/680 = 0.50 \text{ m}$.
