A sound has intensity $1.0 \times 10^{-6} \text{ W/m}^2$. What is its intensity level in decibels?

## Options
A) $30 \text{ dB}$
B) $60 \text{ dB}$
C) $90 \text{ dB}$
D) $120 \text{ dB}$

## Answer
B

## Explanation
$\beta = 10 \log_{10}(I/I_0) = 10 \log_{10}(10^{-6}/10^{-12}) = 10 \log_{10}(10^6) = 10 \times 6 = 60 \text{ dB}$.
