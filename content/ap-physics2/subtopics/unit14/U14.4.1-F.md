A light ray strikes a plane mirror at an angle of $35°$ to the normal. What is the angle of reflection?

## Options
A) $35°$
B) $55°$
C) $70°$
D) $145°$

## Answer
A

## Explanation
By the law of reflection, the angle of reflection equals the angle of incidence. Both are measured from the normal: $\theta_r = \theta_i = 35°$.
