An object is $15 \text{ cm}$ from a converging lens ($f = 10 \text{ cm}$). The image is $30 \text{ cm}$ from the lens on the opposite side. What is the magnification?

## Options
A) $0.5$
B) $2.0$
C) $-2.0$
D) $-0.5$

## Answer
C

## Explanation
$m = -d_i/d_o = -(30)/15 = -2.0$. The magnitude is $2$ (image is twice the size of the object) and the negative sign indicates an inverted (real) image.
