A fiber has core $n_1 = 1.48$ and cladding $n_2 = 1.45$. What is the critical angle at the core-cladding boundary?

## Options
A) $76°$
B) $79°$
C) $82°$
D) $85°$

## Answer
B

## Explanation
$\sin\theta_c = n_2/n_1 = 1.45/1.48 = 0.9797$. $\theta_c = \arcsin(0.9797) = 78.5° \approx 79°$.
