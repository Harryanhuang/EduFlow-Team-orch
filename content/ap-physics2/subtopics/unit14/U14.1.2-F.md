A wave has a frequency of $50 \text{ Hz}$ and a wavelength of $0.80 \text{ m}$. What is its speed?

## Options
A) $16 \text{ m/s}$
B) $40 \text{ m/s}$
C) $63 \text{ m/s}$
D) $50 \text{ m/s}$

## Answer
B

## Explanation
$v = f\lambda = 50 \times 0.80 = 40 \text{ m/s}$.
