An object is placed $0.50 \text{ m}$ in front of a plane mirror. Where is the image?

## Options
A) $0.25 \text{ m}$ in front of the mirror
B) $0.50 \text{ m}$ in front of the mirror
C) $0.50 \text{ m}$ behind the mirror
D) $1.0 \text{ m}$ behind the mirror

## Answer
C

## Explanation
The image in a plane mirror is located the same distance behind the mirror as the object is in front. Object distance = image distance = $0.50 \text{ m}$.
