A $0.80 \text{ m}$ string fixed at both ends has a wave speed of $40 \text{ m/s}$. What is the fundamental frequency?

## Options
A) $12.5 \text{ Hz}$
B) $25 \text{ Hz}$
C) $50 \text{ Hz}$
D) $100 \text{ Hz}$

## Answer
B

## Explanation
$f_1 = v/(2L) = 40/(2 \times 0.80) = 40/1.60 = 25 \text{ Hz}$.
