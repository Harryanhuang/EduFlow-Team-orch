A single slit of width $0.10 \text{ mm}$ is illuminated with light of wavelength $500 \text{ nm}$. What is the angular position of the first dark fringe?

## Options
A) $0.14°$
B) $0.29°$
C) $0.57°$
D) $1.15°$

## Answer
B

## Explanation
$\sin\theta = m\lambda/a = (1)(500 \times 10^{-9})/(0.10 \times 10^{-3}) = 5.0 \times 10^{-3}$. $\theta = \arcsin(0.005) = 0.286° \approx 0.29°$.
