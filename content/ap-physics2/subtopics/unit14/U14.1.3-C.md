Two tuning forks vibrate at $440 \text{ Hz}$ and $444 \text{ Hz}$. What beat frequency is heard?

## Options
A) $4 \text{ Hz}$
B) $884 \text{ Hz}$
C) $442 \text{ Hz}$
D) $221 \text{ Hz}$

## Answer
A

## Explanation
$f_{\text{beat}} = |f_1 - f_2| = |440 - 444| = 4 \text{ Hz}$. Four amplitude pulsations per second are heard.
