A sound source radiates $0.10 \text{ W}$ uniformly in all directions. What is the intensity at $5.0 \text{ m}$?

## Options
A) $3.2 \times 10^{-4} \text{ W/m}^2$
B) $6.4 \times 10^{-4} \text{ W/m}^2$
C) $2.0 \times 10^{-3} \text{ W/m}^2$
D) $0.10 \text{ W/m}^2$

## Answer
A

## Explanation
$I = P/(4\pi r^2) = 0.10/(4\pi(5.0)^2) = 0.10/(314.2) = 3.18 \times 10^{-4} \text{ W/m}^2 \approx 3.2 \times 10^{-4} \text{ W/m}^2$.
