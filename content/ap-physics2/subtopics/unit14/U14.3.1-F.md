Two waves with amplitudes $3 \text{ cm}$ and $4 \text{ cm}$ arrive at a point in phase. What is the resultant amplitude?

## Options
A) $1 \text{ cm}$
B) $5 \text{ cm}$
C) $7 \text{ cm}$
D) $12 \text{ cm}$

## Answer
C

## Explanation
In phase → constructive interference. Resultant amplitude $= 3 + 4 = 7 \text{ cm}$.
