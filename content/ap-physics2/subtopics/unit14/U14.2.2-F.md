Sound waves in air are:

## Options
A) Transverse
B) Longitudinal
C) Both transverse and longitudinal
D) Neither

## Answer
B

## Explanation
Sound waves in air are longitudinal — the air molecules oscillate back and forth parallel to the direction of wave propagation, creating compressions and rarefactions.
