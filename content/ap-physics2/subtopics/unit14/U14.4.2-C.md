When white light passes through a glass prism, violet light is deviated more than red light because:

## Options
A) Violet light travels faster in glass
B) The refractive index of glass is higher for violet light
C) Violet light has a longer wavelength
D) The prism absorbs red light

## Answer
B

## Explanation
The refractive index of glass increases with decreasing wavelength: $n_{\text{violet}} > n_{\text{red}}$. By Snell's law, a higher $n$ means more bending, so violet light is deviated more than red light. This is dispersion.
