### Question Q-1.4-06
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: The owner contributes an additional $20,000 cash to the business. State the effect on the accounting equation.
**Answer**: Assets (cash) increase by $20,000 and Capital increases by $20,000. The equation remains balanced: both sides increase by the same amount. Liabilities are unaffected.
**Explanation**: Capital introduction increases both assets and capital. A common error is recording this as a liability — it is the owner's investment, not a loan, so it increases capital not liabilities.
