### Question Q-3.3-03
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: Write the correcting journal entries for each error: (a) A $300 purchase on credit was debited to Purchases $300 but credited to Trade receivables $300 instead of Trade payables. (b) A sale of $150 to Kavi was omitted entirely.
**Answer**: (a) Debit Trade receivables $300, Credit Trade payables $300, Narration: "Correction — credit on sale wrongly posted to Trade receivables instead of Trade payables." The Purchases debit was correct; only the credit side needs moving from the wrong account to the right one. (b) Debit Trade receivables (Kavi) $150, Credit Sales $150, Narration: "Correction — credit sale of $150 to Kavi omitted." The entire transaction was missing, so both sides must be entered.
**Explanation**: For (a), one side of the original entry was correct (Purchases debit $300) — only the credit needs correcting by removing it from Trade receivables and posting it to Trade payables. For (b), since nothing was recorded, both debit and credit must be added. A common error is also debiting Purchases again in (a) — that would double-count the purchase.
