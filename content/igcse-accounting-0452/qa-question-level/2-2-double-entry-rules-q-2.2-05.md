### Question Q-2.2-05
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Write the double entry for: a business sells goods for $800 on credit to Ahmed.
**Answer**: Debit Ahmed (trade receivables) $800 (asset increases — Ahmed owes the business money). Credit Sales $800 (revenue increases).
**Explanation**: A credit sale creates a receivable (debit) and increases sales revenue (credit). A common error is debiting cash — no cash has been received yet; the customer owes the money.
