### Question Q-2.3-08
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Describe the process of posting from the sales day book to the ledger.
**Answer**: Individual entries in the sales day book are posted daily to the personal accounts of customers in the sales ledger (debit each customer's account). At the end of the period (usually monthly), the total of the sales day book is posted to the sales account in the nominal ledger (credit sales). This ensures both individual customer balances and the total sales figure are updated.
**Explanation**: There are two levels of posting: individual entries to customer accounts (debit) and the total to the sales account (credit). A common error is only posting the total and forgetting individual customer accounts, or only posting individuals and forgetting the total.
