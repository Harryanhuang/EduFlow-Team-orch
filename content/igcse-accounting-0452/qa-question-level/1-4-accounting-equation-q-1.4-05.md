### Question Q-1.4-05
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: A business buys a machine for $10,000 cash. State the effect on the accounting equation.
**Answer**: One asset (machinery) increases by $10,000 and another asset (cash) decreases by $10,000. Total assets remain unchanged. Liabilities and capital are unaffected. The equation remains balanced.
**Explanation**: This is an asset exchange transaction — total assets do not change, only the composition changes. A common error is thinking the equation is disrupted because cash goes down — the increase in machinery offsets it.
