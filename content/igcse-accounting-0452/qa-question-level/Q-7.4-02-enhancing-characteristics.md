# Question Q-7.4-02

**Difficulty**: Foundation

**Question**: What are the four enhancing qualitative characteristics of financial information?

**Answer**: 1) **Comparability** — information can be compared across periods (trend analysis) and across entities (inter-firm comparison). Consistency in accounting policies supports comparability. 2) **Verifiability** — different knowledgeable and independent observers would reach consensus that the information is a faithful representation. 3) **Timeliness** — information is available to decision-makers in time to influence their decisions. 4) **Understandability** — information is presented clearly so that users with reasonable knowledge of business and accounting can comprehend it. Complex information should not be excluded — it should be presented as clearly as possible. These enhance usefulness of information that is already relevant and faithfully represented.

**Explanation**: Enhancing characteristics improve usefulness but cannot make irrelevant or misrepresenting information useful. A common error is treating all characteristics as equally important.
