### Question Q-2.4-03
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: What is "posting" in the context of the ledger?
**Answer**: Posting is the process of transferring (copying) transaction amounts from the books of original entry (day books, cash book) into the appropriate accounts in the ledger. For each transaction, the debit amount is posted to the debit side of one account and the credit amount is posted to the credit side of another account, maintaining the double entry.
**Explanation**: Posting moves data from chronological records (day books) to classified records (ledger accounts). A common error is posting to the wrong side or to the wrong account.
