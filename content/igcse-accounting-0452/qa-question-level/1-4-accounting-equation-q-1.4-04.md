### Question Q-1.4-04
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: A business has assets of $80,000 and capital of $55,000. Calculate the liabilities.
**Answer**: From A = L + C, rearranging: L = A - C = $80,000 - $55,000 = $25,000.
**Explanation**: The equation can be rearranged to find any one unknown. A common error is writing L = A + C — this would give $135,000, which would make the equation unbalanced ($80,000 ≠ $135,000 + $55,000).
