### Question Q-2.6-03
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: How is the reimbursement amount calculated under the imprest system?
**Answer**: The reimbursement amount equals the total of all petty cash payments (supported by vouchers/receipts) made during the period. When the business reimburses petty cash, it pays an amount equal to what was spent, restoring the fund to the original imprest amount. Formula: Reimbursement = Total petty cash payments = Opening imprest amount - Closing cash balance.
**Explanation**: The reimbursement restores the fund to its fixed level. A common error is reimbursing the difference between the imprest amount and the closing balance without verifying against vouchers — this could mask missing cash.
