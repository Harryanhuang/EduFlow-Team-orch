### Question Q-1.4-08
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: A business repays a bank loan of $15,000 by cheque. State the effect on the accounting equation.
**Answer**: Assets (bank balance) decrease by $15,000 and Liabilities (bank loan) decrease by $15,000. Capital is unaffected. The equation remains balanced: both sides decrease by $15,000.
**Explanation**: Repaying a liability reduces both assets and liabilities. A common error is thinking the repayment is an expense that reduces capital — loan repayment is not an expense (interest would be, but the principal repayment is a balance sheet transaction only).
