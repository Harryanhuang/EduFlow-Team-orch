### Question Q-2.3-03
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: What type of transactions are recorded in the purchases day book (purchases journal)?
**Answer**: The purchases day book records credit purchases of goods only. Each entry shows the date, the supplier's name, the invoice number, and the amount. Cash purchases are NOT recorded in the purchases day book — they go directly into the cash book. The total of the purchases day book is posted to the purchases account in the ledger.
**Explanation**: Like the sales day book, the purchases day book only records credit purchases. A common error is including cash purchases — they are recorded in the cash book, not the purchases day book.
