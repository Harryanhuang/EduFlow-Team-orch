# Topic 5.2 — Sum to Infinity and Convergence
## Items File

**Item 1 [F]**
Question: State whether the geometric series 2 + 1 + 1/2 + ... converges or diverges.
Answer: Converges (|r| = 1/2 < 1)
Difficulty: F
Topic: 5.2
Explanation: This is a geometric series with a = 2 and r = 1/2. Since |r| < 1, the series converges to a finite sum.
Tags: geometric series, convergence, geometric sequence
**Item 2 [F]**
Question: Find the sum to infinity of 4 + 2 + 1 + 1/2 + ...
Answer: 8
Difficulty: F
Topic: 5.2
Explanation: a = 4, r = 1/2. S_∞ = a/(1−r) = 4/(1−1/2) = 8.
Tags: sum to infinity, geometric series, convergence
**Item 3 [S]**
Question: Geometric series: a = 6, S_∞ = 18. Find r.
Answer: r = 2/3
Difficulty: S
Topic: 5.2
Explanation: S_∞ = a/(1−r) = 18 → 6/(1−r) = 18 → 1−r = 6/18 = 1/3 → r = 1 − 1/3 = 2/3. Since |r| < 1, the series converges.
Tags: sum to infinity, geometric series, finding ratio
**Item 4 [S]**
Question: Express 0.333... as a fraction.
Answer: 1/3
Difficulty: S
Topic: 5.2
Explanation: 0.333... = 3/10 + 3/100 + 3/1000 + ... = geometric series with a = 3/10, r = 1/10. S_∞ = (3/10)/(1−1/10) = (3/10)/(9/10) = 3/9 = 1/3.
Tags: repeating decimal, geometric series, sum to infinity
**Item 5 [S]**
Question: a = 3, r = 1/3. Find S_∞.
Answer: 9/2
Difficulty: S
Topic: 5.2
Explanation: S_∞ = a/(1−r) = 3/(1−1/3) = 3/(2/3) = 3 × 3/2 = 9/2.
Tags: sum to infinity, geometric series

**Item 7 [C]**
Question: A geometric series has S_∞ = 8 and first term a = 2. Find S₅.
Answer: 781/128
Difficulty: C
Topic: 5.2
Explanation: S_∞ = a/(1−r) = 8 → 2/(1−r) = 8 → 1−r = 2/8 = 1/4 → r = 3/4. Then S₅ = a(r⁵−1)/(r−1) = 2((3/4)⁵−1)/(3/4−1) = 2((243/1024)−1)/(−1/4) = 2(−781/1024) × (−4) = 2 × 781/256 = 781/128.
Tags: sum to infinity, geometric series, partial sum
**Item 8 [C]**
Question: A convergent geometric series has sum = 4 and first term = 1. Find r.
Answer: r = 3/4
Difficulty: C
Topic: 5.2
Explanation: S_∞ = a/(1−r) = 4 → 1/(1−r) = 4 → 1−r = 1/4 → r = 3/4. Since |r| < 1, the series converges. Verify: S_∞ = 1/(1−3/4) = 4 ✓.
Tags: sum to infinity, geometric series, finding ratio
**Item 9 [C]**
Question: Express 2.3454545... (repeating 45) as a fraction in simplest form.
Answer: 129/55
Difficulty: C
Topic: 5.2
Explanation: x = 2.3454545... has one non-repeating digit and two repeating digits (45). Multiply by 10: 10x = 23.4545... Multiply by 1000: 1000x = 2345.4545.... Subtract: 1000x − 10x = 990x = 2345.4545... − 23.4545... = 2322. So x = 2322/990 = 387/165. Verify: 387 ÷ 165 = 2.3454545... ✓. Simplify: GCD(387, 165) = 3, so 387/165 = 129/55.
Tags: repeating decimal, geometric series, fraction, mixed repeating
