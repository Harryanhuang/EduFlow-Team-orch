# Topic 1.1 — Simultaneous Equations and Elimination
## QA Question Level File

**1. [F]** Solve by elimination: 2x + 3y = 7, x − y = 4
**2. [F]** Solve by substitution: y = 3x − 2, 2x + y = 8
**3. [S]** Solve: 3x + 4y = 18, 5x − 2y = 4
**4. [S]** Solve: 2x/3 + y/2 = 5/6, x/4 − y/3 = 1/12
**5. [S]** Two numbers have a sum of 23 and a difference of 7. Find the numbers.
**6. [S]** A shop sells pens at $3 each and notebooks at $5 each. A customer buys 8 items for $30. How many of each?
**7. [C]** Solve the simultaneous equations: 2x + y = 4, x² + y = 7
**8. [C]** Solve: 2x + y = 5, x² + y = 8
**9. [C]** Find the value of k for which the simultaneous equations 3x + 2y = 7 and 6x + ky = 14 have infinitely many solutions.
