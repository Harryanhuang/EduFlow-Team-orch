# Topic 9.1 — Equation of a Straight Line and Circle
## QA Question Level File

**1. [F]** Find the equation of the line through (1, 3) and (4, 11).

**2. [F]** Write the equation of the circle with centre (2, −3) and radius 5.

**3. [S]** Find the equation of the line perpendicular to y = 2x + 1 passing through (3, 4).

**4. [S]** Find the centre and radius of the circle x² + y² − 4x + 6y − 3 = 0.

**5. [S]** Find the equation of the circle passing through (1, 1), (2, 2), and (0, 2).

**6. [S]** Find where the line y = 2x + 1 intersects the circle (x − 1)² + (y − 2)² = 9.

**7. [C]** Find the equation of the tangent to the circle x² + y² = 25 at the point (3, 4).

**8. [C]** Find the length of the chord of the circle x² + y² = 25 cut off by the line y = x + 1.

**9. [C]** Prove that the angle in a semicircle is a right angle using coordinate geometry.
