# Topic 7.1 — Trigonometric Functions and Identities
## QA Question Level File

**1. [F]** Without using a calculator, find the exact value of sin 30° + cos 60°.
**2. [F]** Convert 150° to radians, giving your answer in terms of π.
**3. [S]** Without a calculator, find tan 60° × sin 30° × sec 45°.
**4. [S]** Express 5π/6 radians in degrees.
**5. [S]** Given that sin θ = 3/5 and θ is acute, find cos θ.
**6. [S]** Prove that sin²θ/(1 − sin²θ) = tan²θ.
**7. [C]** Solve the equation 2 sin²θ + cos θ = 1 for 0° ≤ θ ≤ 360°.
**8. [C]** Given that cos x = −1/3 and x is in the second quadrant, find the exact value of sin 2x.
**9. [C]** Prove the identity: (sec θ − tan θ)(sec θ + tan θ) = 1.
