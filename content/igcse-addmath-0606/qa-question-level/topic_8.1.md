**1. [F]** Using first principles, find the derivative of f(x) = x².

**3. [S]** Find dy/dx for (a) y = 3x⁴ − 2x² + 7x (b) y = (2x + 1)(x − 3)

**4. [S]** Differentiate using the quotient rule: y = (x² + 1)/(x − 1)

**5. [S]** If y = (3x − 2)⁵, find dy/dx.

**6. [S]** Find the equation of the tangent to y = x² − 4x at x = 3.

**8. [C]** Differentiate: y = sin(2x² + 3x). Find dy/dx.
