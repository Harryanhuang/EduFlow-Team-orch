# Topic 9.2 — Parametric Equations and Loci
## QA Question Level File

**1. [F]** Convert to Cartesian: x = 3t, y = t² + 1.

**2. [F]** Find the locus of a point P(x, y) such that its distance from A(1, 2) equals its distance from B(4, 6).

**3. [S]** Find the Cartesian equation of the curve given by x = 2 cos θ, y = 3 sin θ.

**4. [S]** Describe the locus of points satisfying |z − 2i| = 3 (complex plane).

**5. [S]** Convert: x = t + 1/t, y = t − 1/t (t ≠ 0). Find Cartesian equation.

**6. [S]** Find the equation of the locus of a point P equidistant from the line y = 3 and the point (0, −1).

**7. [C]** The parametric equations of a curve are x = 2 sin θ, y = cos 2θ. Find dy/dx in terms of θ.

**8. [C]** Find the equation of the tangent to x = t², y = t³ at the point where t = 2.

**9. [C]** Find the equation of the locus of a point P such that the product of its distances from (a, 0) and (−a, 0) is a².
