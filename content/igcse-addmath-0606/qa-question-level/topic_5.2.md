# Topic 5.2 — Sum to Infinity and Convergence
## QA Question Level File

**1. [F]** State whether the geometric series 2 + 1 + 1/2 + ... converges or diverges.
**2. [F]** Find the sum to infinity of the geometric series 4 + 2 + 1 + 1/2 + ...
**3. [S]** A geometric series has first term 6 and sum to infinity 18. Find the common ratio.
**4. [S]** Express 0.333... as a fraction.
**5. [S]** A geometric series has a = 3 and r = 1/3. Find S_∞.
**6. [S]** Express 0.171717... as a fraction.
**7. [C]** A geometric series has sum to infinity of 8 and first term 2. Find the sum of the first 5 terms.
**8. [C]** The sum of a convergent geometric series is 4. The first term is 1. Find the common ratio.
**9. [C]** Express 2.3454545... (repeating 45) as a fraction in simplest form.
