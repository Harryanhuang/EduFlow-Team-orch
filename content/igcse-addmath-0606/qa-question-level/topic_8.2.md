# Topic 8.2 — Applications of Differentiation
## QA Question Level File

**1. [F]** Find the equation of the tangent to y = x² at x = 1.

**2. [F]** Find the stationary points of y = x² − 4x.

**3. [S]** Find the maximum value of f(x) = 2x³ − 9x² + 12x.

**4. [S]** A rectangle has perimeter 20 cm. Find the maximum area.

**5. [S]** Find the equation of the normal to y = x³ − 3x at the point (1, −2).

**6. [S]** Find the stationary points of y = 2x³ − 3x² − 36x and determine their nature.

**7. [C]** A cone has volume V. Find the radius and height of the cone with minimum surface area for a fixed volume.

**8. [C]** Find the point on the line y = 2x + 3 closest to the origin.

**9. [C]** Prove that the minimum value of x + 4/x for x > 0 is 4.
