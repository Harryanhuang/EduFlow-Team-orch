# Topic 1.1 — Simultaneous Equations and Elimination

## Basic Info
- **Topic ID:** 1.1
- **Domain:** Algebra
- **Core/Supplement:** Core
- **Weight in exam:** Medium (Paper 1 & 2)

## Knowledge Points
1. Solution of two linear equations in two unknowns by elimination
2. Solution of two linear equations in two unknowns by substitution
3. Solution involving one linear and one quadratic equation
4. Word problems leading to simultaneous equations
5. Interpretation of solution sets (unique, infinite, no solution)

## Key Formulas
- Elimination: add/subtract equations after multiplying to align coefficients
- Substitution: express one variable in terms of the other, substitute back
- Determinant method: x = (b₁c₂ - b₂c₁)/(a₁b₂ - a₂b₁), y = (a₂c₁ - a₁c₂)/(a₁b₂ - a₂b₁)

## Common Errors
- Forgetting to multiply both sides when aligning coefficients
- Incorrect sign changes during elimination
- Extraneous solutions when squaring (not applicable here but watch for)

## Prerequisite Topics
- Basic algebraic manipulation (1.2)

## Difficulty Band: Easy (F:2|S:4|C:3)
- F: direct 2-linear equation elimination, clean integer solutions
- S: one equation needs multiplying, fractional solutions
- C: linear + quadratic, interpretation of intersection point
