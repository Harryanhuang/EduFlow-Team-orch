# Topic 7.2 — Trigonometric Equations and Double-Angle Formulas

## Basic Info
- **Topic ID:** 7.2
- **Domain:** Trigonometry
- **Core/Supplement:** Core
- **Weight in exam:** Medium-High (Paper 1 and Paper 2)

## Knowledge Points
1. Double-angle formulas: sin 2A = 2 sin A cos A
2. Double-angle: cos 2A = cos²A − sin²A = 2 cos²A − 1 = 1 − 2 sin²A
3. Double-angle: tan 2A = 2 tan A / (1 − tan²A)
4. Solving trigonometric equations involving double angles
5. Using identities to simplify equations before solving
6. General solutions of trig equations

## Key Formulas
- sin 2A = 2 sin A cos A
- cos 2A = cos²A − sin²A = 2 cos²A − 1 = 1 − 2 sin²A
- tan 2A = 2 tan A / (1 − tan²A)

## Common Errors
- Using the wrong double-angle identity for cos
- Forgetting to consider the range when solving equations
- Losing solutions when dividing by a trigonometric expression
- Mixing up general solution formulas for sin/cos/tan

## Difficulty Band: F:2|S:4|C:3
- F: recall double-angle formulas, evaluate using given info
- S: solve equations using double-angle identities
- C: general solutions, proving identities with double angles
