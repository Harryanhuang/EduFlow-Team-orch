# Topic 8.2 — Applications of Differentiation

## Basic Info
- **Topic ID:** 8.2
- **Domain:** Calculus
- **Core/Supplement:** Core
- **Weight in exam:** High (Paper 1 and Paper 2)

## Knowledge Points
1. Equation of tangent: y − y₁ = m(x − x₁), m = f'(x₁)
2. Equation of normal: perpendicular to tangent, m_normal = −1/m_tangent
3. Rates of change: dy/dx = rate of y with respect to x
4. Connected rates of change problems
5. Maximum and minimum values: stationary points (f'(x) = 0)
6. Second derivative test: f''(x) > 0 → minimum; f''(x) < 0 → maximum
7. Optimization problems (maximise/minimise quantities)
8. Curve sketching using derivatives

## Key Formulas
- Tangent at (x₁, y₁): y − y₁ = f'(x₁)(x − x₁)
- Normal: y − y₁ = −1/f'(x₁) · (x − x₁)
- Stationary point: f'(x) = 0
- Nature: f''(x) > 0 (min), f''(x) < 0 (max), f''(x) = 0 (inflection possible)

## Common Errors
- Forgetting to find y-coordinate at the point for tangent equation
- Confusing the sign when finding the normal
- Not checking second derivative when classifying stationary points
- Setting up optimization problems incorrectly

## Difficulty Band: F:2|S:4|C:3
- F: find equation of tangent, identify stationary points
- S: connected rates, use second derivative test, optimisation
- C: complex optimisation, points of inflection, practical problems
