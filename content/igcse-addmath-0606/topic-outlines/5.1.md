# Topic 5.1 — Arithmetic and Geometric Sequences and Series

## Basic Info
- **Topic ID:** 5.1
- **Domain:** Sequences and Series
- **Core/Supplement:** Core
- **Weight in exam:** Medium (Paper 1 and Paper 2)

## Knowledge Points
1. Arithmetic sequences: definition, common difference d
2. nth term of arithmetic sequence: a_n = a + (n−1)d
3. Arithmetic series: sum of n terms S_n = n/2(a + l) or S_n = n/2[2a + (n−1)d]
4. Geometric sequences: definition, common ratio r
5. nth term of geometric sequence: a_n = ar^(n−1)
6. Geometric series: sum of n terms S_n = a(r^n − 1)/(r − 1) for r ≠ 1
7. Arithmetic mean and geometric mean

## Key Formulas
- Arithmetic: a_n = a + (n−1)d
- Arithmetic sum: S_n = n/2(a + l) = n/2[2a + (n−1)d]
- Geometric: a_n = ar^(n−1)
- Geometric sum: S_n = a(r^n − 1)/(r − 1)

## Common Errors
- Confusing arithmetic and geometric formulas
- Forgetting the condition r ≠ 1 in geometric series formula
- Incorrectly identifying the first term in a shifted sequence
- Mixing up a and r in geometric sequences

## Difficulty Band: F:2|S:4|C:3
- F: find nth term, find sum of arithmetic/geometric series
- S: determine parameters of sequences, use arithmetic/geometric mean
- C: combined sequences, proof of sum formulas, applications
