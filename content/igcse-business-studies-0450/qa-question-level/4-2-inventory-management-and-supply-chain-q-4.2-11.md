### Question Q-4.2-11
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Explain how inventory management and supply chain can affect business performance.
**Answer**: Inventory Management And Supply Chain affects business performance by influencing key operational decisions, resource allocation, and strategic planning. Businesses must consider these factors when making day-to-day and long-term decisions. The impact varies depending on the size of the business, the market it operates in, and the competitive environment.
**Explanation**: This tests application and analysis skills. A common error is describing rather than explaining the link between inventory management and supply chain and business performance.
