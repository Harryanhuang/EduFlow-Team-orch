### Question Q-2.3-03
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Distinguish between internal and external recruitment. Give one advantage of each.
**Answer**: Internal recruitment fills vacancies with existing employees (e.g., promotion, transfer). Advantage: cheaper, faster, and motivates current staff. External recruitment hires from outside the business. Advantage: brings new skills, ideas and experience into the business.
**Explanation**: Each method suits different situations. A common error is claiming internal recruitment brings 'new ideas' — that is an external recruitment advantage.
