### Question Q-4.5-01
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: What is outsourcing? Give an example.
**Answer**: Outsourcing is when a business contracts out a non-core activity or function to an external third-party provider rather than performing it in-house. Examples include a manufacturer outsourcing its cleaning services, an airline outsourcing its IT helpdesk, or a retailer outsourcing its delivery logistics to a courier company.
**Explanation**: Outsourcing allows the business to focus on its core activities. A common error is confusing outsourcing with redundancy — outsourcing transfers work externally; redundancy eliminates the role.
