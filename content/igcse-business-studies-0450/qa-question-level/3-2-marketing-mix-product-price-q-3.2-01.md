### Question Q-3.2-01
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: What is meant by the 'marketing mix'?
**Answer**: The marketing mix is the combination of four elements — product, price, promotion, and place — that a business uses to satisfy customer needs and wants.
**Explanation**: The 4Ps framework is fundamental to marketing strategy. A common error is adding 'people' or 'process' — those belong to the extended 7Ps model, not the core 4Ps.
