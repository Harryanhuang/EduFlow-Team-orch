### Question Q-2.4-02
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: What is redundancy?
**Answer**: Redundancy occurs when an employee is dismissed because the job they do is no longer needed — for example, the business is closing, downsizing, or the work is being done by machines.
**Explanation**: Redundancy is about the role disappearing, not the person's performance. A common error is treating redundancy as a form of punishment.
