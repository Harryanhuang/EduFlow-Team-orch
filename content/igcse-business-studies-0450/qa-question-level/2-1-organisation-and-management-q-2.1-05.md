### Question Q-2.1-05
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: What is the difference between line authority and staff authority?
**Answer**: Line authority gives managers the power to direct the work of subordinates and make decisions (e.g., production manager). Staff authority gives specialists the power to advise and support but not to command (e.g., HR advisor, legal counsel).
**Explanation**: Line managers make decisions; staff specialists advise. A common error is assuming staff managers have the same command authority as line managers.
