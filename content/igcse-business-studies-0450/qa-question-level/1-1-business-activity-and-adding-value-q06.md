### Question Q-1.1-06
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: How does branding help a business add value?
**Answer**: A strong brand allows a business to charge higher prices because customers associate the brand with quality, trust, or status. The bought-in costs remain the same, so value added increases.
**Explanation**: Brand → higher price → more value added. A common error is saying branding reduces costs.
**Tags**: branding, adding-value, premium-price
