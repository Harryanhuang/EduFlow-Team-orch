### Question Q-4.1-04
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Explain the disadvantages of batch production.
**Answer**: 1) Work-in-progress inventory builds up between batches. 2) Equipment must be cleaned and re-set between batches, causing downtime. 3) Per-unit cost is higher than flow production because batches are smaller. 4) Storage space is needed for raw materials and finished batches. 5) Workers may repeat the same tasks within a batch, reducing motivation.
**Explanation**: Batch is a compromise between job and flow. A common error is listing only advantages — batch production carries storage costs and setup delays that flow production avoids.
