### Question Q-1.2-04
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Give two advantages of being a sole trader.
**Answer**: 1) Complete control over decisions. 2) Keep all profits. 3) Easy and cheap to set up. 4) Personal relationship with customers.
**Explanation**: Control, profits, ease of setup. A common error is listing unlimited liability as an advantage.
