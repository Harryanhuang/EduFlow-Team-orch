### Question Q-1.4-06
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: What is the difference between an entrepreneur and an intrapreneur?
**Answer**: An entrepreneur starts their own business and bears the risk. An intrapreneur innovates within an existing organisation, using the company's resources and bearing less personal risk.
**Explanation**: Own business vs within company. A common error is treating them as the same.
