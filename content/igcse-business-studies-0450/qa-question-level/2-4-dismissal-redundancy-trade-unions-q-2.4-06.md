### Question Q-2.4-06
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: What rights do employees have against unfair dismissal?
**Answer**: Employees generally have the right to: a written reason for dismissal, a notice period, appeal against the decision, and (after a qualifying period) the right to claim unfair dismissal at an employment tribunal. Employers must follow a fair procedure including investigation and hearing.
**Explanation**: Rights vary by jurisdiction. A common error is claiming all dismissals are automatically unfair — some are lawful if proper procedure is followed.
