### Question Q-2.3-02
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: What is a job description?
**Answer**: A job description is a document that outlines the main features and responsibilities of a job, including the tasks to be performed, working conditions, and to whom the employee reports.
**Explanation**: It helps candidates understand what the job involves. A common error is confusing job description with person specification — the former describes the job, the latter describes the ideal person.
