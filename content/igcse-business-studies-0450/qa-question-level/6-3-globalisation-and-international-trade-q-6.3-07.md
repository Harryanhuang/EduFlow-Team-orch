### Question Q-6.3-07
**Difficulty**: Challenge
**Question**: Evaluate the impact of an MNC setting up a factory in a developing country on the host country's economy.
**Answer**: Positive impacts: 1) Job creation and skills transfer through training. 2) Infrastructure development (roads, power) that benefits the wider community. 3) Tax revenue for the government. 4) Technology transfer and knowledge spillovers to local firms. 5) Increased exports improving the balance of payments. Negative impacts: 1) Profits may be repatriated to the home country rather than reinvested locally. 2) Environmental damage if regulations are weak. 3) Local businesses may be unable to compete. 4) Exploitation of low-wage labour with poor working conditions. 5) The MNC may have disproportionate political influence. The net impact depends on the strength of local institutions and the terms negotiated with the MNC.
**Explanation**: MNC investment is not automatically beneficial. A common error is focusing only on job creation without considering profit repatriation and environmental costs.
