### Question Q-2.2-02
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: Define 'financial reward'. Give two examples.
**Answer**: A financial reward is a monetary benefit given to employees. Examples: wages/salary, bonus, commission, profit sharing, share options, fringe benefits.
**Explanation**: Financial rewards directly satisfy economic needs. A common error is listing non-financial rewards such as praise or promotion.
