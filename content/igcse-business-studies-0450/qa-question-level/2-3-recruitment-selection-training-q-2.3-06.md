### Question Q-2.3-06
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Explain why training is important for a business.
**Answer**: Training develops employee skills, knowledge and competence. Benefits: higher productivity and quality, fewer accidents, improved employee motivation and retention, easier adoption of new technology, and a stronger employer brand for recruitment.
**Explanation**: Training is an investment, not just a cost. A common error is listing only one benefit — exam answers should give multiple linked points.
