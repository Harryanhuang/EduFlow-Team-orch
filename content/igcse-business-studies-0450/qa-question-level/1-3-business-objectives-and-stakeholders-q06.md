### Question Q-1.3-06
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: How do employees' objectives differ from owners' objectives?
**Answer**: Employees want job security, good wages, and working conditions. Owners want profit, growth, and return on investment. These may conflict — higher wages reduce profits.
**Explanation**: Employees vs owners: different priorities. A common error is saying they always align.
**Tags**: stakeholders, employees, owners, conflict
