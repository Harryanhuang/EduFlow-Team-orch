### Question Q-3.5-07
**Difficulty**: Challenge
**Question**: Evaluate the limitations of sales forecasting for a new business with no sales history.
**Answer**: Without historical data, trend-based forecasting is impossible. New businesses must rely on market research, competitor benchmarks, and expert judgment — all of which carry higher uncertainty. Consumer behaviour may differ from predictions, market conditions can change rapidly, and initial forecasts often overestimate demand. However, forecasting is still necessary for securing finance and planning. New businesses should use scenario planning (optimistic, realistic, pessimistic) rather than a single forecast.
**Explanation**: Forecasts for new businesses are inherently uncertain. A common error is presenting a single forecast without sensitivity analysis.
