### Question Q-3.2-06
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Explain the role of branding in the product element of the marketing mix.
**Answer**: Branding creates a distinct identity for a product through a name, logo, design, or combination. It helps customers recognise and trust the product, supports premium pricing, builds loyalty, and differentiates from competitors.
**Explanation**: Branding is a long-term investment. A common error is confusing branding with advertising — branding is the identity, advertising is the communication.
