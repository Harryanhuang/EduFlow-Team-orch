### Question Q-3.3-03
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Distinguish between above the line and below the line promotion.
**Answer**: Above the line promotion uses paid media where the business has no direct control over content (e.g., TV ads, billboards). Below the line promotion uses methods the business fully controls (e.g., sales promotion, direct mail, social media, loyalty schemes).
**Explanation**: The distinction is about control and cost. A common error is thinking 'above the line' means higher quality.
