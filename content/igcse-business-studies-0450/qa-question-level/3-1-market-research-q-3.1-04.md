### Question Q-3.1-04
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: What is a questionnaire? Give one advantage and one disadvantage.
**Answer**: A questionnaire is a written set of questions given to a sample of people to gather their opinions. Advantage: can collect a lot of data quickly and cheaply, especially online. Disadvantage: response rates can be low, and questions may be misunderstood without clarification.
**Explanation**: Questionnaires are the most common primary research method. A common error is claiming questionnaires always give reliable results — biased questions ruin data quality.
