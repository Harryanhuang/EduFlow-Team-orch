### Question Q-1.3-05
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: How can a business's objectives change over time?
**Answer**: A new business may focus on survival, then shift to profit, then growth. Objectives change due to market conditions, competition, economic climate, and business size.
**Explanation**: Objectives evolve with business lifecycle. A common error is saying objectives never change.
