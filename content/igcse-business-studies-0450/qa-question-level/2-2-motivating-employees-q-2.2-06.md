### Question Q-2.2-06
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Describe Taylor's view of what motivates workers.
**Answer**: Taylor believed workers are primarily motivated by money. He advocated piece-rate pay — paying workers per unit produced — to maximize output. He viewed workers as economically driven and needing close supervision.
**Explanation**: Taylor's theory is useful in manufacturing but ignores social and psychological needs. A common error is attributing hierarchy-of-needs theory to Taylor.
