### Question Q-2.1-06
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: What is a matrix organisation structure? Give one advantage and one disadvantage.
**Answer**: A matrix structure combines functional and project-based reporting — employees report to both a functional manager and a project manager. Advantage: flexible use of skills across projects. Disadvantage: dual reporting can cause conflict and confusion over priorities.
**Explanation**: Matrix structures suit project-based industries. A common error is thinking employees have only one manager in a matrix.
