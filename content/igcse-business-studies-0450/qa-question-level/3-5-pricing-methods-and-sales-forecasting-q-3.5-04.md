### Question Q-3.5-04
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: What is sales forecasting? Why is it important?
**Answer**: Sales forecasting is estimating future sales revenue over a given period based on past data, market research, or expert judgment. It is important because it helps businesses plan production, manage cash flow, set budgets, allocate resources, and make staffing decisions.
**Explanation**: Forecasts are never perfect but provide a basis for planning. A common error is treating a forecast as a guarantee rather than an estimate.
