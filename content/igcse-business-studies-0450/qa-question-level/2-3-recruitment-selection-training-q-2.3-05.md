### Question Q-2.3-05
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: What is an interview? Give one advantage and one disadvantage as a selection method.
**Answer**: An interview is a face-to-face (or virtual) meeting where the employer asks questions to assess a candidate's suitability. Advantage: allows direct assessment of communication skills and personality. Disadvantage: can be subjective — the interviewer may be influenced by first impressions rather than actual ability.
**Explanation**: Interviews are the most common selection method. A common error is claiming interviews are always objective.
