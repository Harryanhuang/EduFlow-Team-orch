### Question Q-6.2-11
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Explain how economic climate and business decisions can affect business performance.
**Answer**: Economic Climate And Business Decisions affects business performance by influencing key operational decisions, resource allocation, and strategic planning. Businesses must consider these factors when making day-to-day and long-term decisions. The impact varies depending on the size of the business, the market it operates in, and the competitive environment.
**Explanation**: This tests application and analysis skills. A common error is describing rather than explaining the link between economic climate and business decisions and business performance.
