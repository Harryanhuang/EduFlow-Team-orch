### Question Q-3.4-08
**Difficulty**: Challenge
**Question**: A clothing retailer has identified two segments: teenagers seeking trendy fashion and professionals seeking smart casual wear. Analyse how it should adapt its marketing mix for each segment.
**Answer**: Teenagers: product — trendy, seasonal items with bold designs; price — lower to match limited budgets; promotion — social media, influencer marketing, student discounts; place — online stores and high-street locations near schools. Professionals: product — classic, higher-quality materials; price — mid to premium reflecting quality; promotion — targeted email campaigns, quality-focused advertising; place — premium shopping centres and quality online shopping experience. The two mixes differ across all 4Ps to match distinct needs.
**Explanation**: Each segment needs a tailored mix. A common error is changing only promotion while leaving product and price the same.
