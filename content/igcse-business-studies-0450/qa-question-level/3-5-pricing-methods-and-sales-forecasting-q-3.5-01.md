### Question Q-3.5-01
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: What is a pricing method?
**Answer**: A pricing method is a systematic approach a business uses to set the price of its products or services. Common methods include cost-plus pricing, mark-up pricing, penetration pricing, price skimming, competitive pricing, and psychological pricing.
**Explanation**: Pricing methods ensure prices cover costs and align with business strategy. A common error is confusing pricing method with pricing strategy — method is the calculation; strategy is the market positioning.
