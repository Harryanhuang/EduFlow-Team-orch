### Question Q-3.2-02
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: Define 'product' as an element of the marketing mix.
**Answer**: The product is the good or service being offered to customers. It includes features, design, quality, branding, packaging, and after-sales service.
**Explanation**: Product is more than the physical item — it includes the total customer experience. A common error is only describing the physical good.
