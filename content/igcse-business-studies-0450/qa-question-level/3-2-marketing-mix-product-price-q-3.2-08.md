### Question Q-3.2-08
**Difficulty**: Challenge
**Question**: Analyse how a business should adapt its product strategy when a product reaches the maturity stage of the product life cycle.
**Answer**: At maturity, sales peak and competition intensifies. Strategies include: 1) Product improvements or extensions to refresh interest. 2) Finding new market segments or uses. 3) Reducing costs to maintain margins as prices fall. 4) Planning for replacement with new products. 5) Heavy promotion to defend market share.
**Explanation**: Extension strategies can prolong maturity. A common error is suggesting 'reduce price' as the only response — this triggers price wars.
