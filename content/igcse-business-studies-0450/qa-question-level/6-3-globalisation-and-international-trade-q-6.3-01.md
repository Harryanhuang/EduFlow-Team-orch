### Question Q-6.3-01
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: What is globalisation?
**Answer**: Globalisation is the process by which economies, businesses, and cultures around the world become increasingly interconnected through trade, investment, technology, and the movement of people. It leads to the integration of markets, production, and financial systems across national borders.
**Explanation**: Globalisation is a long-term trend, not a single event. A common error is equating globalisation with international trade — trade is one component; globalisation also covers investment, technology transfer, and cultural exchange.
