### Question Q-4.2-07
**Difficulty**: Challenge
**Question**: Evaluate the costs and benefits of holding large inventories for a retail business.
**Answer**: Benefits: 1) No stock-outs — customers find what they want, protecting sales and reputation. 2) Ability to take advantage of bulk discounts from suppliers. 3) Buffer against supply chain disruptions or seasonal demand spikes. 4) Display of full product range attracts more customers. Costs: 1) High storage, insurance, and security expenses. 2) Risk of obsolescence, especially for fashion or technology products. 3) Capital tied up in stock cannot be used for investment or marketing. 4) Deterioration, theft, or damage. For most retailers, the costs of excessive inventory outweigh the benefits — lean inventory with reliable restocking is preferable.
**Explanation**: The optimal level balances service and cost. A common error is presenting only one side — both costs and benefits must be weighed for the specific business context.
