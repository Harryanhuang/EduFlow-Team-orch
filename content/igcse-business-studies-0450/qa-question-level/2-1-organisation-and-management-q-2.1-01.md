### Question Q-2.1-01
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: What is meant by 'organisation structure'?
**Answer**: Organisation structure is the framework of authority and responsibility that shows how tasks, power and reporting relationships are arranged within a business. It is usually shown as an organisational chart.
**Explanation**: The structure clarifies who reports to whom. A common error is confusing organisation structure with organisational culture.
