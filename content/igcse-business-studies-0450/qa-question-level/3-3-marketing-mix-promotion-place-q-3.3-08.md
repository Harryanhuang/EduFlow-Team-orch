### Question Q-3.3-08
**Difficulty**: Challenge
**Question**: Analyse the factors a business should consider when choosing its distribution channels.
**Answer**: Factors include: 1) Product type — perishable goods need short channels. 2) Cost — intermediaries add margin but reduce the business's distribution costs. 3) Customer expectations — where do target customers shop? 4) Competition — do rivals use certain channels effectively? 5) Business size — small businesses may lack resources for direct distribution.
**Explanation**: Channel choice is strategic, not just logistical. A common error is choosing channels based only on cost.
