### Question Q-3.5-05
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Describe two methods of sales forecasting.
**Answer**: 1) Trend extrapolation — using past sales data to project future sales by extending the trend line. 2) Market research — surveying customers or testing the market to estimate demand. 3) Salesforce estimates — aggregating predictions from the sales team who interact with customers. 4) Delphi method — consulting a panel of experts to reach a consensus forecast.
**Explanation**: Different methods suit different situations. A common error is relying on a single method — combining approaches improves accuracy.
