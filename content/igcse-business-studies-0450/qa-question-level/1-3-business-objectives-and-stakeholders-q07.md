### Question Q-1.3-07
**Difficulty**: Challenge
**Question**: Evaluate whether profit maximisation should be the main objective of all businesses.
**Answer**: Arguments for: profit is essential for survival, funds growth, rewards owners for risk, and attracts investment. Arguments against: short-term profit maximisation may harm long-term success by cutting quality, employee welfare, or environmental standards. Social enterprises and non-profits prioritise social goals over profit. Many successful businesses balance profit with customer satisfaction, employee welfare, and corporate social responsibility. The importance of profit maximisation depends on the type of business and its stage of development.
**Explanation**: Profit is important but not the only objective. A common error is saying profit is always the only goal or ignoring other legitimate objectives.
