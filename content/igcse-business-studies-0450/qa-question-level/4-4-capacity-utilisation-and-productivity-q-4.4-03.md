### Question Q-4.4-03
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Describe three ways a business can increase its capacity utilisation.
**Answer**: 1) Increase demand through marketing, sales promotions, or entering new markets. 2) Reduce capacity by closing underused facilities or selling idle equipment — raising the percentage by lowering the denominator. 3) Offer discounts or special deals to fill unused capacity (e.g., off-peak pricing for hotels). 4) Diversify the product range to use existing capacity for different products. 5) Accept contract work from other businesses to fill spare capacity.
**Explanation**: Both raising output and lowering capacity can improve the percentage. A common error is only considering ways to increase demand — reducing capacity is also a valid strategy.
