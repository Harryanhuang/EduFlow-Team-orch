### Question Q-2.4-07
**Difficulty**: Challenge
**Question**: Evaluate the benefits and disadvantages of trade unions for a business.
**Answer**: Benefits: single point of contact for workforce concerns, can improve communication, may help resolve disputes efficiently, and can represent workers in grievances. Disadvantages: industrial action disrupts production, collective bargaining may increase labour costs, unions may resist changes the business needs. The overall impact depends heavily on the relationship between management and the union — a cooperative partnership can benefit both sides, while an adversarial relationship damages productivity and morale. For most businesses, the benefits of constructive union engagement outweigh the disadvantages of having no union representation at all.
**Explanation**: The relationship quality matters more than union existence. A common error is only listing negatives.
