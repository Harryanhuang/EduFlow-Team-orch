### Question Q-2.1-02
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: Define 'span of control'.
**Answer**: Span of control is the number of subordinates directly reporting to a manager. A wide span means one manager supervises many people; a narrow span means fewer subordinates per manager.
**Explanation**: Span of control affects how closely managers can supervise. A common error is confusing it with chain of command.
