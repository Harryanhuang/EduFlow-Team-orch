### Question Q-4.5-03
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Explain the advantages and disadvantages of outsourcing a business function.
**Answer**: Advantages: 1) Cost savings — external providers may achieve economies of scale. 2) Access to specialist expertise and technology. 3) Allows the business to focus on core activities. 4) Flexibility — easier to scale up or down without hiring or firing. Disadvantages: 1) Loss of direct control over quality and timing. 2) Dependency on the supplier — if they fail, the business is disrupted. 3) Confidentiality and data security risks. 4) Possible negative public perception, especially if outsourcing leads to domestic job losses.
**Explanation**: Outsourcing is a trade-off between cost and control. A common error is listing only advantages — the loss of control is a significant risk.
