### Question Q-3.5-06
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: A business currently uses cost-plus pricing. Explain two advantages and two disadvantages of this method.
**Answer**: Advantages: 1) Simple to calculate and apply — ensures every sale covers costs and generates profit. 2) Stable prices — customers are not confused by frequent changes. Disadvantages: 1) Ignores demand — if customers are willing to pay more, the business leaves money on the table. 2) Ignores competition — rivals may charge significantly less, making the product uncompetitive. 3) May incentivise inefficiency if costs are not controlled.
**Explanation**: Cost-plus is simple but market-blind. A common error is listing only advantages without acknowledging the method's key weakness — it ignores what customers will actually pay.
