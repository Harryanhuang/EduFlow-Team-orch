### Question Q-3.1-09
**Difficulty**: Challenge
**Question**: A small bakery wants to launch a new range of gluten-free cakes. Recommend a market research plan and justify your choices.
**Answer**: 1) Secondary: research industry reports on gluten-free food trends and market size — cheap starting point. 2) Primary qualitative: run a focus group with existing customers to understand preferences — deep insights. 3) Primary quantitative: distribute an online questionnaire to a sample of local residents to measure demand — cost-effective numeric data. 4) Test marketing: sell a small batch at the bakery to observe real purchase behaviour. Justification: a small business needs low-cost methods first, then validation through actual sales.
**Explanation**: Recommendations should be proportionate to business size and budget. A common error is recommending expensive methods (e.g., large-scale surveys) for a small business.
