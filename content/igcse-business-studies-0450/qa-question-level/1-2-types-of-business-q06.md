### Question Q-1.2-06
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: What is a business plan? Why is it important?
**Answer**: A business plan sets out business goals, strategies, market research, financial forecasts, and resources needed. It is important for securing finance and guiding the business.
**Explanation**: Roadmap for the business. A common error is saying it is only needed for banks.
