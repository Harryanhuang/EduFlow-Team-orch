### Question Q-3.2-04
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: What is product differentiation? Why is it important?
**Answer**: Product differentiation is making a product distinct from competitors' offerings through features, design, quality, branding, or service. It helps a business stand out in the market, reduce price competition, and build customer loyalty.
**Explanation**: Differentiation supports premium pricing. A common error is confusing differentiation with diversification — the former makes existing products unique, the latter enters new markets.
