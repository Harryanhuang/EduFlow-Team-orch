### Question Q-2.1-07
**Difficulty**: Challenge
**Question**: Evaluate the advantages and disadvantages of a wide span of control.
**Answer**: Advantages: fewer managers reduce costs; subordinates gain more autonomy and develop skills; faster communication with fewer levels. Disadvantages: managers have less time per subordinate, which may reduce supervision quality; risk of overload for managers; subordinates may feel unsupported. Best suited to experienced, self-motivated teams.
**Explanation**: The ideal span depends on task complexity and employee capability. A common error is claiming one span size is always better.
