### Question Q-1.2-05
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Give two disadvantages of a partnership.
**Answer**: 1) Profits must be shared. 2) Disagreements between partners. 3) Unlimited liability (each partner liable for all debts). 4) Decisions take longer.
**Explanation**: Shared profits, disagreements, unlimited liability. A common error is listing advantages.
**Tags**: partnership, disadvantages
