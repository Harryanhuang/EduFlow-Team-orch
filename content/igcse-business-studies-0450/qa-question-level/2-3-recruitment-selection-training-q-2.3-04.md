### Question Q-2.3-04
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Describe three methods a business can use to recruit externally.
**Answer**: 1) Advertising in newspapers, magazines or online job boards. 2) Using recruitment agencies or headhunters. 3) Recruiting through social media and professional networks (e.g., LinkedIn). Other methods include job fairs, school or university partnerships.
**Explanation**: Online recruitment is increasingly popular. A common error is listing 'promotion' as an external method — promotion is internal.
