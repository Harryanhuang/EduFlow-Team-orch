### Question Q-3.1-03
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Compare primary and secondary research.
**Answer**: Primary research is collected first-hand, specific to the business's needs, and up-to-date, but is expensive and time-consuming. Secondary research uses existing data, is cheaper and faster, but may be outdated or not specific enough. Most businesses use both.
**Explanation**: The choice depends on budget and urgency. A common error is claiming one is always better.
