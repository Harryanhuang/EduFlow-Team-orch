### Question Q-3.2-03
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Describe three pricing strategies and when each is appropriate.
**Answer**: 1) Cost-plus: adding a markup to cost — simple, ensures profit, suits small businesses. 2) Penetration: setting a low initial price to gain market share — suits new entrants to competitive markets. 3) Price skimming: setting a high initial price then lowering it — suits innovative or unique products. 4) Competitive: matching rivals' prices — suits oligopoly markets.
**Explanation**: Choice depends on product type, competition, and business objectives. A common error is describing only the strategy without when to use it.
