### Question Q-6.3-03
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: What are the advantages and disadvantages of globalisation for businesses?
**Answer**: Advantages: 1) Access to larger markets and more customers. 2) Lower production costs through offshoring and global supply chains. 3) Economies of scale from producing for a global market. 4) Access to new technology and expertise. 5) Diversification of risk across multiple markets. Disadvantages: 1) Increased competition from foreign firms. 2) Cultural and language barriers in new markets. 3) Political and currency risks in foreign countries. 4) Complexity of managing operations across time zones. 5) Negative public perception regarding labour practices or environmental impact in host countries.
**Explanation**: Globalisation creates both opportunities and threats. A common error is presenting it as universally positive — small domestic firms may struggle against global competitors.
