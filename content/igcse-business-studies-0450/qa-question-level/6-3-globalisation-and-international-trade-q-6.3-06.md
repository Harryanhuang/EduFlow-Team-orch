### Question Q-6.3-06
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: What are the advantages and disadvantages of protectionism for a country's economy?
**Answer**: Advantages: 1) Protects infant industries until they are strong enough to compete globally. 2) Protects domestic jobs in industries threatened by cheap imports. 3) Reduces dependency on foreign suppliers for essential goods (national security). 4) Can reduce a trade deficit. Disadvantages: 1) Consumers face higher prices and less choice. 2) Protected industries may become inefficient without competition. 3) Other countries may retaliate with their own trade barriers, reducing exports. 4) Global output is reduced because countries do not fully specialise according to comparative advantage.
**Explanation**: Protectionism has short-term benefits but long-term costs. A common error is assuming protecting one industry helps the whole economy — the costs are borne by consumers and export industries.
