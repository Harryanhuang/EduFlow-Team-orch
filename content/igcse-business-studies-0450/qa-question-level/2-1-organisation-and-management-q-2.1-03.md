### Question Q-2.1-03
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Distinguish between a tall and a flat organisational structure.
**Answer**: A tall structure has many levels of hierarchy and a long chain of command, with narrow spans of control. A flat structure has few hierarchy levels, wider spans of control, and shorter chains of command.
**Explanation**: Tall structures allow close supervision but slower communication. Flat structures enable faster decisions but managers have less time per subordinate.
