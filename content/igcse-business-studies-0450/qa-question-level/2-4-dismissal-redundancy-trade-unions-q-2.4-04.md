### Question Q-2.4-04
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Explain the difference between voluntary and compulsory redundancy.
**Answer**: Voluntary redundancy is when employees choose to leave in exchange for a redundancy package. Compulsory redundancy is when the employer selects employees to leave. Voluntary redundancy is generally preferred as it reduces resentment and legal risk.
**Explanation**: Both are legitimate business practices. A common error is thinking voluntary redundancy means the employee was not truly redundant.
