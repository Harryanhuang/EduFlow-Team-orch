### Question Q-2.1-04
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Explain what is meant by 'delegation'. Give one advantage and one disadvantage.
**Answer**: Delegation is giving lower-level employees authority and responsibility to carry out tasks. Advantage: saves senior managers' time and motivates junior staff. Disadvantage: tasks may be done poorly if the employee lacks skills, and the manager remains accountable for failures.
**Explanation**: Delegation is not abdication — the manager retains accountability. A common error is thinking delegation removes responsibility from the manager.
