### Question Q-4.4-04
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: Describe four ways a business can improve labour productivity.
**Answer**: 1) Invest in training to improve workers' skills and efficiency. 2) Introduce better technology or machinery to speed up production. 3) Improve motivation through financial rewards (bonuses, piece-rate pay) or non-financial methods (teamwork, job enrichment). 4) Improve the organisation and management of work — better layout, clearer targets, reduced idle time. 5) Hire more skilled or experienced workers.
**Explanation**: Productivity improvements come from people, technology, and processes. A common error is assuming productivity gains only come from working harder — better methods and tools are often more effective.
