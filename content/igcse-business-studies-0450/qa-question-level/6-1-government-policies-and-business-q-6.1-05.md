### Question Q-6.1-05
**Difficulty**: Standard
**Question**: How does government regulation affect business operations? Give two examples of regulations and their impact.
**Answer**: Government regulation sets legal standards that businesses must comply with. Examples: 1) Employment law — minimum wage, working time limits, health and safety requirements increase labour costs but protect workers. 2) Environmental regulation — emissions standards, waste disposal rules increase production costs but reduce pollution. 3) Consumer protection law — requirements for product safety, truthful advertising, and refund rights increase compliance costs but build consumer trust. Regulation can create barriers to entry for small firms but also levels the playing field through consistent standards.
**Explanation**: Regulation creates costs but also benefits. A common error is presenting regulation as purely negative — it creates certainty and protects businesses from unfair competition.
