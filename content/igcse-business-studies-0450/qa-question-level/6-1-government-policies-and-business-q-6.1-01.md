### Question Q-6.1-01
**Difficulty**: Foundation
**Question**: What is fiscal policy?
**Answer**: Fiscal policy is the use of government spending and taxation to influence the economy. Expansionary fiscal policy (increased spending or lower taxes) aims to boost economic growth and reduce unemployment. Contractionary fiscal policy (reduced spending or higher taxes) aims to reduce inflation and government debt.
**Explanation**: Fiscal policy is set by the government, not the central bank. A common error is confusing fiscal policy with monetary policy — fiscal is taxes and spending; monetary is interest rates and money supply.
