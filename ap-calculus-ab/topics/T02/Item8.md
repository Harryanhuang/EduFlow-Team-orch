Evaluate the limit:

$\displaystyle \lim_{x \to 9} \frac{\sqrt{x} - 3}{x - 9}$

## Options
A) $0$
B) $\dfrac{1}{6}$
C) $\dfrac{1}{3}$
D) $1$

## Answer
B) $\dfrac{1}{6}$

## Explanation
Rationalize the numerator by multiplying by the conjugate:

$$\frac{\sqrt{x} - 3}{x - 9} \cdot \frac{\sqrt{x} + 3}{\sqrt{x} + 3} = \frac{x - 9}{(x - 9)(\sqrt{x} + 3)}$$

For $x \neq 9$:
$$= \frac{1}{\sqrt{x} + 3}$$

$$\lim_{x \to 9} \frac{1}{\sqrt{x} + 3} = \frac{1}{3 + 3} = \frac{1}{6}$$
