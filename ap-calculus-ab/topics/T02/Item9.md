Evaluate the limit:

$\displaystyle \lim_{h \to 0} \frac{(2 + h)^2 - 4}{h}$

## Options
A) 0
B) 2
C) 4
D) 8

## Answer
C) 4

## Explanation
Expand the numerator:

$(2 + h)^2 - 4 = 4 + 4h + h^2 - 4 = 4h + h^2 = h(4 + h)$

$$\lim_{h \to 0} \frac{h(4 + h)}{h} = \lim_{h \to 0} (4 + h) = 4$$
